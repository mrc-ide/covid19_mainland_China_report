30 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection in Jiangsu
Release date: 2020-01-30 08:44:50 Views:

At 04:00 on January 29, 2020, Jiangsu Province reported 30 new confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. Among them, 5 were in Nanjing, 1 in Wuxi, 7 in Xuzhou, 2 in Changzhou, 4 in Suzhou, 3 in Nantong, 3 in Lianyungang, 4 in Huai'an, and 1 in Taizhou.

Of the newly confirmed cases, 11 had a history of living in Hubei, 9 had a history of travel in Wuhan, and 7 were close contacts of previously confirmed cases. The oldest is 67 years old and the youngest is 7 years old. There are 12 women and 18 men. All have been treated in designated hospitals and are in stable condition. 401 close contacts have been traced, all of whom are under medical observation in isolation.

As of 24:00 on January 29, Jiangsu Province had reported a total of 129 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, including 1 severe case and 1 discharged case. Among the confirmed cases, there were 19 cases in Nanjing, 10 in Wuxi, 15 in Xuzhou, 10 in Changzhou, 24 in Suzhou, 9 in Nantong, 6 in Lianyungang, 7 in Huai'an, 6 in Yancheng, and Yangzhou. There were 8 cases in Shanghai, 2 cases in Zhenjiang, 9 cases in Taizhou, and 4 cases in Suqian. Among the severe cases, 1 was in Zhenjiang; and among the discharged cases, 1 was in Suzhou.

At present, Jiangsu Province has tracked 2,069 close contacts, 117 medical observations have been lifted, and 1952 are still receiving medical observations.

Experts remind: Please come to Wuhan from Jiangsu, have visited Wuhan, and come to Jiangsu via Wuhan to register with the community (village) committee as soon as possible, and take a temperature test. If there are no symptoms, it is best to perform medical observation at home with your family for 14 days, pay attention to your physical condition, and take a temperature measurement every morning and evening (including your family) and record it in the book. If you have respiratory symptoms or fever during this period, you should call 12320 for information on nearby medical institutions with fever clinics and go to the fever clinics. Avoid using public transportation, you can call 120 or drive to the car. When driving, pay attention to open the windows to maintain ventilation and wear medical protective masks.
