Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 30, 2020
Release time: 2020-01-31 source: this website

From 04:00 to 14:00 on January 30, 2020, Hebei Province reported 17 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 6 in Cangzhou, 4 in Tangshan, 4 in Baoding, 2 in Xingtai, and Handan. 1 city. There were 51 new suspected cases, including 14 in Shijiazhuang, 11 in Qinhuangdao, 8 in Tangshan, 8 in Zhangjiakou, 6 in Xingtai, 2 in Langfang, 1 in Cangzhou, and 1 in Dingzhou.

As of 24:00 on January 30, Hebei Province has reported a total of 82 confirmed cases, including 1 death, 7 severe cases, and no discharged patients. Among the confirmed cases, there were 18 cases in Cangzhou City, 11 cases in Shijiazhuang City, 11 cases in Baoding City, 9 cases in Langfang City, 7 cases in Handan City, 7 cases in Xingtai City, 7 cases in Tangshan City, 6 cases in Zhangjiakou City, 4 cases in Hengshui City, and Chengde City. There were 2 cases in Cities; 1 case in Cangzhou City; 2 cases in Cangzhou City, 2 cases in Shijiazhuang City, 1 case in Handan City, 1 case in Hengshui City and 1 case in Zhangjiakou City among severe cases. There are 78 suspected cases.

At present, 1899 close contacts have been tracked. Of the 109 people who were separated from medical observation on the same day, 1698 people are currently under medical observation.

Hebei Provincial Health Committee

January 31, 2020
