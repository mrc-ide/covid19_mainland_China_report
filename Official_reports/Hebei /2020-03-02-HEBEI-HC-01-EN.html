0At 04:00 on March 1, 2020, Hebei Province reported no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia. Eleven new cases were cured and discharged, including 6 in Tangshan, 3 in Cangzhou, and 2 in Zhangjiakou. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on March 1, a total of 318 confirmed cases were reported in Hebei Province, of which 18 were treated in hospital, 3 were severe cases, 294 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 41 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui.
