/ February 24, 2020 Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-25 Source: This website /
/
///////
/

0On February 24, 2020, at 04:00, Hebei Province reported no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia. Twenty new cases were cured and discharged, including 7 in Tangshan, 4 in Zhangjiakou, 3 in Baoding, 3 in Cangzhou, 1 in Shijiazhuang, 1 in Langfang, and 1 in Handan. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 24, Hebei Province has reported a total of 311 confirmed cases, of which 64 were treated in hospital, 5 were currently with severe cases, 241 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 34 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 3 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Qinhuangdao City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among the severe cases, 3 were in Tangshan City, 1 in Shijiazhuang City, and 1 in Cangzhou City. Among the discharged patients, there were 32 cases in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 31 in Cangzhou, 28 in Langfang, 27 in Tangshan, 27 in Handan, 22 in Shijiazhuang, 19 in Xingtai, and 8 in Qinhuangdao. 8 cases in Hengshui City and 7 cases in Chengde City. There are 0 suspected cases.

At present, 10,602 close contacts have been tracked. 42 people were separated from medical observation on the same day, and 342 people are currently under medical observation.

The health of the Hebei Provincial Health Commission is the same as that of the Hebei Provincial Health Commission. The health of the Hebei Province is the same as that of the Communist Party of China.
