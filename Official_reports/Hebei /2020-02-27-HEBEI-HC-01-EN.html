/ Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province on February 26, 2020
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-27 Source: This website /
/
///////
/

0 On February 26, 2020, at 04:00, Hebei Province reported 5 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia (Zhangjiakou City). Thirteen new cases were cured and discharged, including 6 in Tangshan, 2 in Langfang, 2 in Cangzhou, 2 in Xingtai, and 1 in Handan. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 26, Hebei Province has reported a total of 317 confirmed cases, of which 50 were treated in hospital, 5 were currently with severe cases, 261 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 40 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 3 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Qinhuangdao City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among severe cases, 3 were in Tangshan City, 1 in Zhangjiakou City, and 1 in Cangzhou City. Of the discharged patients, 36 were from Tangshan, 35 from Cangzhou, 32 from Zhangjiakou, 32 from Baoding, 30 from Langfang, 29 from Handan, 22 from Shijiazhuang, 22 from Xingtai, and 8 from Qinhuangdao. 8 cases in Hengshui City and 7 cases in Chengde City. There are 0 suspected cases.

At present, 10,675 close contacts have been tracked, 63 people were separated from medical observation on the same day, and 278 people are currently under medical observation.
