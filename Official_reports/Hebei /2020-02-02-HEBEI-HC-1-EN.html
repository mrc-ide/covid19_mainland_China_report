Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on February 1, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-02 Source: This website

From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 1, 2020, Hebei Province reported 8 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, including 3 in Tangshan City, 2 in Baoding City, 1 in Shijiazhuang City, 1 in Zhangjiakou City, and Handan 1 city. Sixty new suspected cases were added, of which 23 were in Shijiazhuang, 21 in Xingtai, 8 in Qinhuangdao, 3 in Zhangjiakou, 3 in Tangshan, 1 in Handan, and 1 in Dingzhou.

As of 24:00 on February 1, a total of 104 confirmed cases were reported in Hebei Province, including 1 death, 9 existing severe cases, and 3 discharged from hospital. Among the confirmed cases, there were 19 cases in Cangzhou, 13 in Shijiazhuang, 13 in Baoding, 11 in Tangshan, 11 in Langfang, 11 in Handan, 10 in Xingtai, 9 in Zhangjiakou, 4 in Hengshui, and Chengde. 2 cases in Qinhuangdao, 1 case in Qinhuangdao; 1 case in Cangzhou among deaths; 2 cases in Shijiazhuang, 2 in Xingtai, 1 in Chengde, 1 in Zhangjiakou, 1 in Langfang, 1 in Cangzhou There were 1 case in Handan City; 2 cases were discharged in Baoding City and 1 case was in Langfang City. There are 104 suspected cases.

A total of 3,132 close contacts have been tracked. Of the 176 people who were released from quarantine medical observation on the same day, 2,540 are currently undergoing quarantine medical observation.

Hebei Provincial Health Committee

February 2, 2020
