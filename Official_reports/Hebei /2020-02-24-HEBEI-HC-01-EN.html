/ Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province on February 23, 2020
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-24 Source: This website /
/
///////
/

0 On February 23, 2020, at 04:00 and 24 hours, Hebei Province reported no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia. 17 new cases were cured and discharged, of which 4 in Handan, 2 in Shijiazhuang, 2 in Zhangjiakou, 2 in Qinhuangdao, 2 in Langfang, 2 in Cangzhou, 1 in Chengde, 1 in Hengshui, 1 case in Xingtai City. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 23, Hebei Province has reported a total of 311 confirmed cases, of which 84 were treated in hospital, 7 were severe cases, 221 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 34 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 3 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Qinhuangdao City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among the severe cases, 3 were in Shijiazhuang City, 3 were in Tangshan City, and 1 was in Cangzhou City. Of the discharged patients, there were 29 cases in Baoding, 28 in Zhangjiakou, 28 in Cangzhou, 27 in Langfang, 26 in Handan, 21 in Shijiazhuang, 20 in Tangshan, 19 in Xingtai, and 8 in Qinhuangdao 8 cases in Hengshui City and 7 cases in Chengde City. There are 0 suspected cases.

A total of 10,561 close contacts have been tracked. Of the 420 people who were released from quarantine medical observation on that day, 343 are currently undergoing quarantine medical observation.
