Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 25, 2020
Release time: 2020-01-26 source: this website

At 04:00 on January 25, 2020, Hebei Province reported 5 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 2 in Handan City, 2 in Baoding City, and 1 in Shijiazhuang City. Handan City reported the first confirmed case. There were 5 new suspected cases, including 2 in Chengde, 1 in Qinhuangdao, and 2 in Langfang.

As of 24:00 on January 25, Hebei Province had reported a total of 13 confirmed cases, including 1 death. Among the confirmed cases, there were 5 cases in Shijiazhuang, 3 in Baoding, 2 in Cangzhou, 2 in Handan, and 1 in Chengde. Among the deaths, 1 was in Cangzhou. A total of 5 suspected cases were reported.

At present, 222 close contacts have been traced, all of whom are under observation in isolation.
