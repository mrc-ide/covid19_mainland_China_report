The first batch of 3 patients with new type of coronavirus pneumonia in Hebei were cured and discharged
Release time: 2020-02-03 Source: People's Network

The first batch of 3 patients with new-type coronavirus pneumonia in Hebei Province, after careful treatment, have met the cure standards and were officially discharged.

At 10 am on February 2, two patients with new-type coronavirus pneumonia were formally cured and discharged from Baoding People's Hospital. The two patients were mother and son. The mother was 30 years old and the son was 9 years old. They lived in Wuhan for a long time. They were originally from Baoding Anguo City and returned to Baoding Anguo from Wuhan on January 19, 2020. He was admitted to the People's Hospital of Baoding for treatment on January 24. On January 25, the mother and child were simultaneously diagnosed with a new coronavirus-infected pneumonia case. At present, the clinical symptoms of the mother and the child have disappeared, and various laboratory indicators have returned to normal.

Another confirmed patient was discharged from the Third People's Hospital of Langfang. The patient of Langfang, male, 48 years old, worked in a property company. He went to the hospital for treatment on January 24 due to fever and cough, and was admitted to the Third People's Hospital of Langfang on January 25. On January 26, a group of pneumonia patients diagnosed with a new coronavirus infection was confirmed by the expert group. After treatment, the clinical symptoms disappeared, and various laboratory indicators returned to normal.

It is reported that during the hospitalization of the above cases, the principle of "one person, one team" and "one person, one plan" was adhered to. Experts at all levels carefully formulated the treatment plan, insisted that both Chinese and western medicine be used as early as possible, and applied Chinese medicine therapy as soon as possible to effectively promote the smooth recovery of patients. After evaluation by the provincial and municipal expert groups, they met the criteria for isolation and discharge of pneumonia diagnosed with new coronavirus infection from the National Health and Health Commission, and recovered and discharged.
