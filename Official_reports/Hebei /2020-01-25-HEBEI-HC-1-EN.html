Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province
Release time: 2020-01-25 Source: This website

At 04:00 on January 24, 2020, Hebei Province reported 6 new confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. Among them: one in Baoding and one in Chengde, the first confirmed cases reported; other newly confirmed cases in Shijiazhuang City and one in Cangzhou City.

As of 24:00 on January 24, Hebei Province had reported 8 confirmed cases and 1 death. Among the confirmed cases, there were 4 cases in Shijiazhuang, 2 in Cangzhou, 1 in Baoding, and 1 in Chengde. Among the deaths, 1 was in Cangzhou.

At present, 172 close contacts have been tracked, all of whom are under observation in isolation.
