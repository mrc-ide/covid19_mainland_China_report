Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 29, 2020
Release time: 2020-01-30 source: this website

From 04:00 to 14:00 on January 29, 2020, Hebei Province reported 17 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, of which 5 were in Zhangjiakou City, 4 in Cangzhou City, 2 in Handan City, 2 in Xingtai City, and Shijiazhuang There was 1 case in Baoding City, 1 case in Baoding City, 1 case in Chengde City, and 1 case in Hengshui City. There were 38 new suspected cases, of which 13 were in Shijiazhuang, 7 in Xingtai, 7 in Qinhuangdao, 4 in Tangshan, 3 in Zhangjiakou, 2 in Handan, 1 in Langfang, and 1 in Xinji.

As of 24:00 on January 29, a total of 65 confirmed cases were reported in Hebei Province, including 1 death, 8 existing severe cases, and no discharged patients. Among the confirmed cases, 12 were in Cangzhou, 11 in Shijiazhuang, 9 in Langfang, 7 in Baoding, 6 in Zhangjiakou, 6 in Handan, 5 in Xingtai, 4 in Hengshui, 3 in Tangshan, and Chengde. There were 2 cases in Cities; 1 case in Cangzhou City; 3 cases in Cangzhou City, 2 cases in Shijiazhuang City, 1 case in Langfang City, 1 case in Hengshui City and 1 case in Zhangjiakou City among the severe cases. There are 68 suspected cases.

At present, 1581 close contacts have been tracked, 49 people were released from medical observation on the same day, and 1495 people are currently receiving medical observation.

Hebei Provincial Health Committee January 30, 2020
