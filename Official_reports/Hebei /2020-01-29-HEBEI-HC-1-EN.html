Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 28, 2020
Release time: 2020-01-29 Source: This website

At 04:00 to January 24, 2020, Hebei Province reported 15 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 3 in Baoding, 3 in Cangzhou, 3 in Langfang, 2 in Tangshan, and Shijiazhuang. There was 1 case in Hengshui City, 1 case in Hengshui City, 1 case in Zhangjiakou City, and 1 case in Xingtai City. Zhangjiakou City reported the first confirmed case. 45 new cases were suspected, of which 12 were in Xingtai, 8 in Shijiazhuang, 6 in Tangshan, 5 in Qinhuangdao, 5 in Cangzhou, 3 in Chengde, 3 in Zhangjiakou, 2 in Handan, and Hengshui 1 city.
As of 24:00 on January 28, Hebei Province had reported a total of 48 confirmed cases, including 1 death and 4 severe cases. Among the confirmed cases, there were 10 cases in Shijiazhuang, 9 in Langfang, 8 in Cangzhou, 6 in Baoding, 4 in Handan, 3 in Xingtai, 3 in Hengshui, 3 in Tangshan, 1 in Chengde, and Zhangjiakou. Among the deaths, 1 was in Cangzhou; among the severe cases, 2 were in Shijiazhuang, 1 was in Langfang, and 1 was in Hengshui. There are 56 suspected cases.
At present, 1,040 close contacts have been traced, all of whom are under observation in isolation.
