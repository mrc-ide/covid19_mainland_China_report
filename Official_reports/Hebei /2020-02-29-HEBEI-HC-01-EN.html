/ Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province on February 28, 2020
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-29 Source: This website /
/
///////
/

0 On February 28, 2020, at 04:00, Hebei Province reported no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia. Three new cases were cured and discharged, including two in Cangzhou and one in Tangshan. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 28, Hebei Province has reported a total of 318 confirmed cases, of which 35 were treated in hospital, 5 were severe cases, 277 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 41 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 3 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Qinhuangdao City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among the severe cases, 3 were in Tangshan City, 1 in Zhangjiakou City, and 1 in Cangzhou City. Of the discharged patients, 41 were in Tangshan, 41 in Cangzhou, 33 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 30 in Langfang, 30 in Handan, 24 in Shijiazhuang, 22 in Xingtai, and 9 in Qinhuangdao. 8 cases in Hengshui City and 7 cases in Chengde City. There are 0 suspected cases.

At present, 10,897 close contacts have been tracked. 13 people were separated from medical observation on the same day, and 470 people are receiving medical observation.
