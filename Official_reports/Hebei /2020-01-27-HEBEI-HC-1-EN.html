Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 26, 2020
Release time: 2020-01-27 source: this website
At 04:00 on January 26, 2020, Hebei Province reported 5 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, including 2 in Langfang City, 2 in Shijiazhuang City, and 1 in Xingtai City. The first confirmed cases were reported in Langfang and Xingtai. Eleven suspected cases were added, of which 3 were in Cangzhou, 3 in Xingtai, 2 in Langfang, 2 in Hengshui, and 1 in Handan.
As of 24:00 on January 26, Hebei Province had reported a total of 18 confirmed cases and 1 death. Among the confirmed cases, 7 were in Shijiazhuang, 3 were in Baoding, 2 were in Cangzhou, 2 were in Handan, 2 were in Langfang, 1 was in Chengde, and 1 was in Xingtai. Among the deaths, 1 was in Cangzhou. A total of 16 suspected cases were reported.
At present, 353 close contacts have been traced, all of whom are under observation in isolation.
