Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on February 2, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-03 source: this website

From February 2nd, 2020 to February 24th, Hebei Province reported 9 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, of which 4 were in Xingtai City, 3 in Shijiazhuang City, 1 in Langfang City, and 1 in Qinhuangdao City. 50 new suspected cases were added, of which 15 were in Shijiazhuang, 9 in Qinhuangdao, 8 in Xingtai, 4 in Zhangjiakou, 4 in Tangshan, 3 in Handan, 3 in Chengde, 3 in Cangzhou, and Hengshui 1 city.

As of 24:00 on February 2, a total of 113 confirmed cases were reported in Hebei Province, including 1 death, 12 existing severe cases, and 3 discharged from hospital. Among the confirmed cases, there were 19 cases in Cangzhou, 16 in Shijiazhuang, 14 in Xingtai, 13 in Baoding, 12 in Langfang, 11 in Tangshan, 11 in Handan, 9 in Zhangjiakou, 4 in Hengshui, and Chengde. 2 in Qinhuangdao City, 2 in Qinhuangdao City; 1 in Cangzhou City among deaths; 2 in Shijiazhuang City, 2 in Xingtai City, 2 in Tangshan City, 2 in Zhangjiakou City, 1 in Chengde City, and 1 in Langfang City There were 1 case in Cangzhou City and 1 in Handan City. Among the discharged patients, there were 2 in Baoding City and 1 in Langfang City. There are 108 suspected cases.

At present, 3,796 close contacts have been tracked. Of the 501 people who were released from quarantine medical observation on the same day, 2797 are currently undergoing quarantine medical observation.

Hebei Provincial Health Committee

February 3, 2020
