Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 31, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-01 source: this website

On January 31, 14 new cases were confirmed in our province, including 3 in Xingtai, 3 in Handan, 2 in Zhangjiakou, 2 in Langfang, 1 in Shijiazhuang, 1 in Cangzhou, and 1 in Tangshan. 1 case in Qinhuangdao City. Qinhuangdao reported the first confirmed case. There were 47 new suspected cases, including 16 in Shijiazhuang, 8 in Qinhuangdao, 8 in Tangshan, 5 in Zhangjiakou, 3 in Xingtai, 3 in Dingzhou, 2 in Baoding, and 2 in Handan.

As of 24:00 on January 31, Hebei Province has reported a total of 96 confirmed cases, including 1 death, 8 severe cases, and no discharged patients. Among the confirmed cases, there were 19 cases in Cangzhou, 12 in Shijiazhuang, 11 in Langfang, 11 in Baoding, 10 in Xingtai, 10 in Handan, 8 in Zhangjiakou, 8 in Tangshan, 4 in Hengshui, and Chengde. There were 2 cases in Qinhuangdao City, 1 case in Qinhuangdao City; 1 case in Cangzhou City among death cases; 2 cases in Shijiazhuang City, 2 cases in Cangzhou City, 1 case in Zhangjiakou City, 1 case in Chengde City, 1 case in Hengshui City, and 1 in Handan City example. There are 88 suspected cases.

At present, 2354 close contacts have been tracked, 52 of them were released from medical observation on the same day, and 2045 people are currently undergoing medical observation.
