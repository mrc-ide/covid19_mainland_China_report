Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hebei Province on January 27, 2020
Release time: 2020-01-28 source: this website

At 04:00 on January 27, 2020, Hebei Province reported 15 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 4 in Langfang, 3 in Cangzhou, 2 in Handan, 2 in Shijiazhuang, and Hengshui. There were 2 cases in city, 1 case in Tangshan city and 1 case in Xingtai city. Hengshui and Tangshan reported the first confirmed cases. There were 38 new suspected cases, including 24 in Shijiazhuang, 3 in Cangzhou, 3 in Zhangjiakou, 3 in Baoding, 2 in Tangshan, 1 in Hengshui, 1 in Langfang and 1 in Xingtai.

As of 24:00 on January 27, Hebei Province had reported a total of 33 confirmed cases, including 1 death. Among the confirmed cases, 9 were in Shijiazhuang, 6 were in Langfang, 5 were in Cangzhou, 4 were in Handan, 3 were in Baoding, 2 were in Xingtai, 2 were in Hengshui, 1 was in Chengde, and 1 was in Tangshan. Among the cases, one was from Cangzhou. A total of 54 suspected cases were reported.

At present, 644 close contacts have been traced, all of whom are under observation in isolation.
