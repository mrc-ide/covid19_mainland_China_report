/ February 27, 2020 Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-28 Source: This website /
/

/

0From February 0 to February 24, 2020, Hebei Province reported one new confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia (Zhangjiakou City). Thirteen new cases were cured and discharged, including 4 in Tangshan, 4 in Cangzhou, 2 in Shijiazhuang, 1 in Zhangjiakou, 1 in Qinhuangdao, and 1 in Handan. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 27, Hebei Province has reported a total of 318 confirmed cases, of which 38 were treated in hospital, 5 were currently with severe cases, 274 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 41 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 3 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Qinhuangdao City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among the severe cases, 3 were in Tangshan City, 1 in Zhangjiakou City, and 1 in Cangzhou City. Among the discharged patients, there were 40 cases in Tangshan City, 39 cases in Cangzhou City, 33 cases in Zhangjiakou City, 32 cases in Baoding City, 30 cases in Langfang City, 30 cases in Handan City, 24 cases in Shijiazhuang City, 22 cases in Xingtai City, and 9 cases in Qinhuangdao City. 8 cases in Hengshui City and 7 cases in Chengde City. There are 0 suspected cases.

At present, 10,864 close contacts have been tracked. Of the 16 people who were separated from medical observation on the same day, 450 people are currently under medical observation.
