/ Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province on February 25, 2020
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-26 Source: This website /

0On February 25, 2020, from 04:00 to 24:00, Hebei Province reported one new confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia (Zhangjiakou City). Seven new cases were cured and discharged, including three in Tangshan, two in Cangzhou, one in Xingtai, and one in Handan. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 25, Hebei Province has reported a total of 312 confirmed cases, of which 58 were treated in hospital, 5 were severe cases, 248 were cured and discharged, and 6 died. Of the confirmed cases, 58 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 35 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 32 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 29 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 3 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Qinhuangdao City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among the severe cases, 3 were in Tangshan City, 1 in Shijiazhuang City, and 1 in Cangzhou City. Of the discharged patients, 33 were from Cangzhou, 32 from Zhangjiakou, 32 from Baoding, 30 from Tangshan, 28 from Langfang, 28 from Handan, 22 from Shijiazhuang, 20 from Xingtai, and 8 from Qinhuangdao. 8 cases in Hengshui City and 7 cases in Chengde City. There are 0 suspected cases.

At present, 10,627 close contacts have been tracked, 69 people were separated from medical observation on the same day, and 298 people are currently receiving medical observation from isolated.
