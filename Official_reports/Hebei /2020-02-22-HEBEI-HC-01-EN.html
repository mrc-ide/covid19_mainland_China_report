0From February 0 to February 24, 2020, Hebei Province reported one new confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia (Shijiazhuang City). Fourteen new cases were cured and discharged, including three in Tangshan, two in Baoding, two in Xingtai, two in Handan, one in Shijiazhuang, one in Chengde, one in Zhangjiakou, and one in Qinhuangdao. 1 case in Langfang City.
