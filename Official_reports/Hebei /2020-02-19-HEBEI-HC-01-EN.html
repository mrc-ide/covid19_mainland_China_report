/ February 18, 2020 Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Hebei Province
/
/
/ Release time: 2020-02-19 Source: This website /
/
///////
/

0 On February 18, 2020, from 04:00 to February 24, Hebei Province reported 4 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia (Tangshan City). Thirteen new cases were cured and discharged, including 6 in Zhangjiakou, 2 in Langfang, 2 in Handan, 1 in Qinhuangdao, 1 in Tangshan, and 1 in Baoding. No new suspected cases.

As of 24:00 on February 18, Hebei Province has reported a total of 306 confirmed cases, including 4 deaths, 20 existing severe cases, and a total of 136 patients discharged from hospital. Of the confirmed cases, 55 were in Tangshan, 48 in Cangzhou, 34 in Zhangjiakou, 32 in Baoding, 31 in Handan, 30 in Langfang, 28 in Shijiazhuang, 23 in Xingtai, 10 in Qinhuangdao, and Hengshui. There were 8 cases in Chengde City and 7 cases in Chengde City. Among the deaths, 2 were in Cangzhou City, 1 in Tangshan City, and 1 in Xingtai City. Among the severe cases, 4 were in Cangzhou City, 3 were in Shijiazhuang City, 3 were in Qinhuangdao City, and 3 were in Tangshan City. Cases, 2 cases in Langfang City, 2 cases in Handan City, 1 case in Baoding City, 1 case in Hengshui City, 1 case in Xingtai City; among the discharged patients, 27 cases were in Baoding City, 18 cases were in Langfang City, 18 cases were in Cangzhou City, and 15 cases were in Zhangjiakou City. There were 12 cases in Handan City, 11 cases in Shijiazhuang City, 11 cases in Tangshan City, 10 cases in Xingtai City, 6 cases in Hengshui City, 4 cases in Chengde City and 4 cases in Qinhuangdao City. There are 0 suspected cases.

At present, a total of 10,500 close contacts have been tracked. 358 people were separated from medical observation on that day, and 2027 people were currently receiving medical observation from isolation.

The health committees of the Hebei Province and the Ministry of Health are the same as those of the Hebei Provincial Health Committee.
