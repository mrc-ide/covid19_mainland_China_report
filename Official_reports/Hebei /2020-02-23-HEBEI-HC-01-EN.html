0From February 0 to February 24, 2020, Hebei Province reported two new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, including one in Tangshan City and one in Handan City. Twenty new cases were cured and discharged, including 4 in Shijiazhuang, 4 in Cangzhou, 3 in Langfang, 3 in Xingtai, 3 in Handan, 1 in Tangshan, 1 in Zhangjiakou, and 1 in Hengshui. There were no new deaths. No new suspected cases.
