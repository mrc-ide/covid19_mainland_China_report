Hebei takes effective measures to do a good job in epidemic prevention and control
Release time: 2020-01-26 Source: Hebei News Network

As of January 24, eight confirmed cases of pneumonia of imported new coronavirus infection were found in Hebei. In order to prevent the spread of the epidemic and ensure the health and safety of the people, Hebei has made epidemic prevention and control a top priority, initiated a first-level emergency response to major public health emergencies in accordance with laws and regulations, and effectively and effectively completed various prevention and control measures Work, resolutely win the battle of epidemic prevention and control, and act as a political "moat" in the capital.

After the outbreak, the Hebei Provincial Party Committee and Provincial Government resolutely implemented the spirit of the important instructions of General Secretary Jinping and the decision-making and deployment of the Party Central Committee and the State Council. Make instructions, arrange meetings of the Standing Committee of the Provincial Party Committee, special meetings of the provincial Party committee, and executive meetings of the provincial government to make arrangements. On January 21, the Provincial Party Committee and the Provincial Government convened a teleconference on the provincial epidemic prevention and control work, set up a provincial epidemic prevention and control work group, and issued an "urgent notice on further improving the prevention and control of pneumonia outbreaks of new coronavirus infection." . On January 24, Hebei once again held a video conference on epidemic prevention and control. Wang Dongfeng and Xu Qin made arrangements for further scientific and effective epidemic prevention and control.

In the past few days, all relevant departments in various places have acted quickly and responded with all their strength. All provinces, cities, and counties have launched a joint prevention and control work mechanism, conducted a comprehensive survey of all residents in the area, set up temperature detection points, carried out key screening, and worked together to do a good job in epidemic prevention and control. The Hebei Provincial Health and Health Committee transferred 64 experts to establish a provincial-level medical treatment and epidemic prevention and control expert group, identified 14 provincial and municipal designated hospitals, and 157 county-level febrile patients screening designated hospitals, closely monitored the epidemic situation, and fully implemented medical treatment. Effectively strengthen the management of fever clinics, pre-checks and triages in medical institutions, increase the monitoring and investigation of fever patients, resolutely prevent the spread of the epidemic, and ensure that cases do not go out of the province or into Beijing. Health and health, industrial credit, development and reform, and market supervision departments at all levels actively organize the production, transfer, and reserve of emergency supplies, and have a total of 200 negative-pressure wards and 491 ordinary negative-pressure beds. At the same time, an epidemic day report, zero report and daily briefing system were established to timely release epidemic information in strict accordance with procedures.

The public security and transportation department launched the "three defense lines" checkpoint to carry out epidemic prevention inspections for personnel entering Beijing and provinces. A total of 8,560 people were inspected, and those who found fever were sent to the hospital for treatment in a timely manner. A total of 11,912 people received fever clinics in the province, including 149 fever patients from Wuhan, all of whom were isolated for observation.

According to reports, in the next step, Hebei will further strengthen joint prevention and control, adhere to the five-level linkage of provinces, cities, counties, townships (streets), and villages (communities), and continue to conduct in-depth and comprehensive investigations and file all residents and returnees in the jurisdiction. Lika, without missing a person, for full coverage. Strengthen coordination and cooperation among regions and departments, and provide full coverage of body temperature screening for personnel entering Hebei from key areas. Focus on screening and strict inspection of all vehicles and personnel entering Beijing from Hubei Province. Take measures immediately if fever symptoms are found; All returning students, migrant workers, and personnel of government agencies, enterprises, and institutions are classified and registered. Fever personnel are screened to prevent cross-infection, and personnel-intensive places are checked one by one. The province has suspended the travel of domestic and overseas travel agencies, organized large-scale "non-essential" events and large-scale meetings of party and government organs, and canceled mass gatherings and other activities.

Hebei will also continue to do a good job in material reserves, strengthen communication and docking with production enterprises and storage units, ensure that adequate testing reagents, medical treatment drugs, medical equipment, medical masks, disinfection drugs, clinical blood and case transfer vehicles are fully provided to ensure emergency response. demand. Further strengthen medical treatment, in accordance with the "four centralized" principle of centralized cases, centralized experts, centralized resources, and centralized treatment, with a professional treatment team, organize expert teams and designated hospitals to fully treat patients diagnosed, strengthen monitoring of quarantined staff, once again When an epidemic situation is discovered, treatment is carried out as soon as possible. At the same time, further improve the working mechanism, do a good job in emergency response, adhere to and improve the daily report and zero report system, once suspected and confirmed cases are promptly treated, and report immediately, never report or report delayed.
