Good news! Four more patients with new coronary pneumonia discharged from Qinghai Province

/

/

Source: February 12, 2020: Provincial Command Medical Treatment Team

/
/

At 14:30 pm on February 12, four patients with neo-coronary pneumonia cured in our province were discharged from the Fourth People's Hospital of the province. This is the third batch of patients discharged from our province. It is understood that the Provincial Fourth People's Hospital has admitted a total of 15 patients with new crown pneumonia, and a total of 7 patients were discharged. So far, 9 of the 18 patients diagnosed in Qinghai have been discharged, and medical treatment has achieved periodic results.

//

The four patients discharged this time, Chen Moumou, was the first confirmed patient in our province. He was admitted on January 23 and was diagnosed on the 25th. Qi Moumou was admitted on January 27 and was diagnosed the same day. Ma Moumou was transferred to the Fourth People's Hospital of Henan Province on January 26 after staying at the Hui Nationality Hospital in Xining and was diagnosed on the same day. Han Moumou was transferred from the Menyuan County People's Hospital to the Provincial Fourth People's Hospital for treatment on February 3.

//

On February 11, according to the national "Pneumonitis Diagnosis and Treatment Program for the New Coronavirus Infection (Trial Implementation of the Fifth Revised Edition)" discharge standard, which was evaluated by provincial multidisciplinary experts and met discharge standards. After being discharged from the hospital, a single person and single room isolation will be completed for another 14 days in accordance with the arrangements of the medical and health institution in the place of residence.

//

Since the admission of the four patients, our province adheres to the principle of “centralized cases, centralized experts, centralized resources, and centralized treatment” in accordance with the national diagnosis and treatment plan, and mobilizes disciplines such as respiratory, critical illness, hospital sense, imaging, inspection, pharmacy, and Chinese medicine The most elite force, the implementation of integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine, one person, one plan, one person, one team, scientific and orderly medical treatment, actively contact the National Union of Peking Union Hospital, China-Japan Friendship Hospital online consultation, and constantly adjust and optimize the treatment plan. The Fourth People's Hospital of the Province organized daily experts to evaluate the disease and give full treatment, and encouraged the four patients to actively cooperate with the treatment through psychological counseling, nutrition support, humanistic care, doctor-patient communication, and health education, and increased confidence and courage to overcome the disease. Earned time for an early cure and discharge.

//

The director of the Fourth People's Hospital of Zhejiang Province, Ai Qi said, as the only provincial infectious disease specialist hospital in the province, since the outbreak, the staff of the hospital has always put the patient's life and health in the first place, and responded with calmness. Challenge and challenge, the charge is at the forefront of the fight against epidemic prevention, never shrinks, no complaints, and strive to use their "hard index" in exchange for the "safety index" of the masses. The medical staff of the quarantine ward, Xiao Xiaojia, for everyone, took the initiative to give up the opportunity to take vacations, and stayed at work at all hours of the day and night. Most of them are post-80s or post-90s. They are all old and young, but in order to overcome the epidemic, they entrust their children to the elderly or friends. Many people have not returned home for more than 20 days. In order to cure the patients, they are not afraid of the risk of infection, wear sealed protective clothing, shuttle between the beds day and night, do their best to treat the patients, and give the patients the most careful treatment and the most delicate care. During this period, in order to save protective materials, everyone dared not drink more water and eat more, just afraid that the use of toilets would delay time and waste protective materials. It is precisely because of their silent dedication, adherence day and night, and return to the rehabilitation of each patient, which further strengthened our confidence and determination to win the epidemic prevention and control fight. I like them.
