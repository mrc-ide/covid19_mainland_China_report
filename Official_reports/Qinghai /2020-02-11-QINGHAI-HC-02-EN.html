Qinghai medical team and 3 patients with new coronary pneumonia successfully treated were discharged from hospital

/

/

February 11, 2020 Source: Provincial Command Office (Health Promotion Group)

/
/

At 14:06 on February 10, Shen, a 43-year-old man with a new crown pneumonia in Xinzhou District of Wuhan City, was discharged from the People's Hospital of Xinzhou District. It is understood that this is the first case of critically ill patients with pneumonitis infected by a new type of coronavirus that was successfully treated by the Qinghai Medical Team and the local medical department. At 11:02 on the same day, Zhou, a 48-year-old patient, and Luo, a 28-year-old patient, also successfully discharged from the Xinzhou District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital.

//

On January 26, Shen was admitted to the Department of Infectious Diseases of Xinzhou District People's Hospital due to fever and chest tightness, and transferred to the ICU on January 31. Members of the Qinghai expert group Sun Bin, Hou Ming, Xi Xiaobin, Sun Xiaolin and doctors of Xinzhou District People's Hospital Zeng Xinghong, Qiu Shiming, Wang Xiaofen, and Chen Ming set up a joint medical team to treat them urgently. On February 2, the patient was released from critical condition. Since then, with the full treatment and careful care of the expert group and the medical team, Shen's condition continued to improve, respiratory symptoms improved, and pulmonary imaging showed that the lesions were clearly absorbed. The body temperature was normal for 4 consecutive days, and all laboratory indicators were normal. Nucleic acid tests were negative on February 6th and 9th. After evaluation by the expert group, Shen met the criteria for de-segregation and discharge of confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia from the National Health and Health Commission, and was discharged smoothly on February 10.

//

Hou Ming, an expert from the Qinghai Medical Team, said: "Mr. Shen can recover smoothly. Thanks to the cooperation between the Qinghai medical team and the critical team of the Xinzhou District People's Hospital, careful treatment, coupled with Mr. Shen's stubborn willpower, is also very cooperative with the treatment. OK, so after 11 days of treatment, he has now reached the clinical discharge standard, congratulations. This is the first patient we have discharged from the ward after being converted to the ward on January 31. It is a serious illness since our establishment. The patient's first discharged patient recovered well. "

//

"Mr. Shen was discharged today. Everyone is very happy. During the treatment, Mr. Shen cooperated with the medical team's nursing work. I hope Mr. Shen will continue to strengthen his rehabilitation training at home. Mr. Shen cheers, Wuhan cheers, China Come on, we all come together! "

//

Qing Xiaobin, an expert from the Qinghai Medical Team, said: "Mr. Shen is the first group of patients after our Qinghai medical team came to Xinzhou to set up the critical care department. It is also our first patient who was discharged from the hospital with serious illness. Discharge is the result of everyone's hard work and the expectations of all of us. I believe that with this first case, there will be more second and third cases ... I think Wuhan will be better, It will be just like Mr. Shen! "

//

Out of the inpatient building, Shen thanked the party and the government and thanked the medical staff continuously: "The medical team in Qinghai is very concerned about us. We will go to see us in the morning, noon and night, and treat me technically. Encourage me not to be afraid I adjusted my mindset and told me to do nothing. I should eat and sleep. The doctor told me how to do it. Now I am recovering well and everything is normal. "
