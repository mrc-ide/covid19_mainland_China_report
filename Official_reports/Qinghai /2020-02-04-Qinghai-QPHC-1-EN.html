Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Qinghai Province on February 3, 2020

/

/

February 04, 2020 Source: Qinghai Provincial Health Committee

/
/

At 04:00 on February 3, 2020, Qinghai Province reported 2 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 0 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 0 new discharged cases. Of which: 2 new confirmed cases were reported in Haibei Prefecture, and no other cases were reported in other cities and prefectures.

//

As of 24:00 on February 3, Qinghai Province had reported a total of 15 confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection, including: 1 severe case, 0 death cases, and 0 discharged cases. Among the confirmed cases, there were 12 cases in Xining City and 3 cases in Haibei Prefecture.

//

At present, 284 close contacts have been tracked, 3 were released from medical observation on the same day, and 281 were still receiving medical observation.
