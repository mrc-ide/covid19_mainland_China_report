Expert team of our province inspects and guides medical institutions for new coronary pneumonia nucleic acid testing

/

/

February 25, 2020 Source: Medical Administration, Hospital Authority

/
/

Recently, the Provincial Health and Health Committee invited Qinghai scholar Professor Zhao Shengxiu to organize experts to inspect and supervise the medical and health institutions that carry out the new crown pneumonia nucleic acid test in our province.

//

The expert team went to the Provincial Center for Disease Control and Prevention, the Provincial People's Hospital, the Qinghai Red Cross Hospital, the Affiliated Hospital of Qinghai University, the Provincial Fourth People's Hospital, and the First People's Hospital of Xining to hear reports on the development of nucleic acid testing during the epidemic and inspected them Nucleic acid testing laboratory operation and suggestions on the next step

//

The expert group requested,One isMedical and health institutions should effectively strengthen the quality control of nucleic acid testing and enhance testing capabilities;two isProvincial Centers for Disease Control and Prevention strengthen training of personnel in medical institutions and disease prevention and control centers at various levels to carry out high-quality nucleic acid testing;Three isStrengthen investment in nucleic acid testing laboratories to achieve "should complete inspection, receivable and exhaustion, cure and cure";Four isAll medical institutions attach great importance to laboratory protection requirements, strengthen training, strengthen self-protection of medical personnel, and ensure the safety of medical personnel themselves.

//

Immediately, various medical institutions rectified the questions raised by the expert group in a timely manner, ensuring that the nucleic acid testing laboratory operated in strict accordance with national standards and regulations.
