Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Qinghai Province on February 26, 2020

/

/

February 27, 2020 Source: Qinghai Provincial Health and Health Committee

/
/

At 04:00 on February 26, 2020, there were no new confirmed and suspected cases of new coronavirus pneumonia in Qinghai Province.

//

As of 24:00 on February 26, Qinghai Province has reported 18 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia (including 15 in Xining City and 3 in Haibei Prefecture), and all of them have been cured and discharged.

//

At present, a total of 437 close contacts have been tracked, and all medical observations have been lifted.
