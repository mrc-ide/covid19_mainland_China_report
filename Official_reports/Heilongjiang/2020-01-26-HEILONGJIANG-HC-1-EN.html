Latest outbreak report

Release source: Heilongjiang Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-01-26 Hits: 10467

At 04:00 on January 25, 2020, Heilongjiang Province reported 6 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, including 1 severe case. Qiqihar City reported the first confirmed case. 10 new suspected cases were added.

As of 24:00 on January 25, Heilongjiang Province had reported 15 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, including 8 in Harbin, 1 in Qiqihar, 1 in Mudanjiang, 3 in Daqing, and 2 in Suihua. One death was confirmed in Suihua. A total of 17 suspected cases were reported.

The number of hot clinics in the province on that day was 3,122. At present, 507 close contacts have been tracked, 64 have been released from medical observation, and 443 are still receiving medical observation.
