Latest outbreak report

Release source: Heilongjiang Provincial Health CommitteeRelease time: 2020-02-19Clicks: 14961

At 02:00 on February 18, 2020, Heilongjiang Province reported 6 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia. 23 new cases were cured and discharged. One new death was reported. 21 new suspected cases were added.

As of 24:00 on February 18, Heilongjiang Province reported 470 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, of which 192 were in Harbin, 52 in Shuangyashan, 47 in Suihua, 46 in Jixi, 43 in Qiqihar, and 23 in Daqing. There were 17 cases in Qitaihe City, 15 cases in Jiamusi City, 14 cases in Heihe City, 13 cases in Mudanjiang City, 5 cases in Hegang City, 2 cases in Daxinganling District, and 1 case in Yichun City. There were 72 cases of severe cases. There were 12 dead cases, including 4 confirmed cases in Suihua City, 1 confirmed case in Daqing City, 3 confirmed cases in Shuangyashan City, 1 confirmed case in Qiqihar City, and 3 confirmed cases in Harbin City. 108 cured cases were discharged, including 10 cases of Provincial Infectious Disease Prevention and Control Hospital, 36 cases of Harbin Infectious Disease Hospital, 4 cases of Shuangyashan People's Hospital, 12 cases of Jiamusi Infectious Disease Hospital, 16 cases of Suihua First Hospital, Mudanjiang 1 case of People's Hospital, 2 cases of Hegang Infectious Diseases Hospital, 3 cases of Jixi Infectious Diseases Hospital, 6 cases of Qiqihar City Seventh Hospital, 4 cases of Qiqihar City First Hospital, 7 cases of Daqing City Second Hospital, Qitaihe There were 6 cases in the Municipal People's Hospital and 1 case in the Greater Xing'an Mountains. There are 75 suspected cases.

The number of hot clinics in the province was 2,236. At present, 15,474 close contacts have been traced, 11,909 medical observations have been lifted, and 3,442 people are still receiving medical observations.
