Update on Xinjiang (including Corps) New Coronavirus Pneumonia Epidemic Situation as of 24:00 on February 9

Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee February 10, 2020 08:35 Source: Click: [ ]

At 04:00 on February 9, 2020, Xinjiang (including the Corps) reported 4 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new deaths, and 0 new discharges. among them:

The 4th Division of the Corps reported the first confirmed case, 3 cases; among the other newly confirmed cases, 1 case was in Changji Prefecture.

As of 24:00 on February 9, Xinjiang (including the Corps) had reported 49 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia (38 in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and 11 in Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps), of which:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 20 cases in Urumqi, 10 in Ili, 3 in Changji, 1 in Turpan, 3 in Bazhou, 1 in Aksu, 3 in the 4th Division of the Corps, and 1 in Wujiaqu, the 6th Division of the Corps. Cases, 1 case of the 7th Division of the Corps, 1 case of Shihezi City of the 8th Division of the Corps, 4 cases of the 9th Division of the Corps, 1 case of the 12th Division of the Corps; 12 existing severe cases, 4 critical cases; cumulative deaths 0 Cases, a total of 0 discharged patients.

There are still 3278 people under medical observation.
