Update on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region as of 24:00 on January 26

Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee January 27, 2020 09:16 Source: Click: [ ]

At 04:00 on January 26, 2020, the autonomous region reported one new confirmed case of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus infection, one new severe case, zero new deaths, and zero new discharged cases. Among the newly confirmed cases, one was in Urumqi; among the newly added severe cases, one was in Urumqi.

Patient Liu, male, 44 years old, Urumqi. He traveled to the Mainland in early January and returned to Xinjiang on the 22nd. Due to chest tightness and shortness of breath, he was admitted to the hospital for isolation and treatment on the 25th. On the 26th, the virus was tested positive by the Autonomous Region Center for Disease Control and Prevention. On the same day, the diagnosis and expert group of the Epidemic Prevention and Control Leading Group of the Autonomous Region Health and Health Commission evaluated and confirmed that the case was a confirmed case of pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. At present, the patient continues to be treated in isolation at a local designated hospital with stable condition.

As of 24:00 on January 26, the autonomous region had reported 5 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 1 severe case, 0 dead cases, and 0 discharged patients. Among the confirmed cases, 4 were in Urumqi and 1 in Yili. Among the severe cases, 1 was in Urumqi.

At present, a total of 204 close contacts have been tracked, 0 have been released from medical observation, and 204 people are currently undergoing medical observation.
