From February 6 to 12:00 on February 21, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 132 confirmed cases.

The 132nd patient, female, 45 years old, now lives in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin City, has no travel or work history in Wuhan, and is the wife of the 82nd confirmed case. The patient underwent medical observation at a centralized isolation point on February 8. She developed fever on the 19th. She was sent to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on the 20th. She was diagnosed with a suspected case and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment. The city's expert group confirmed the 132nd case in our city, which is a common type. He is currently being transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and quarantine medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts. The investigation and epidemiological investigations of other close contacts are in progress.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 21, 2020
