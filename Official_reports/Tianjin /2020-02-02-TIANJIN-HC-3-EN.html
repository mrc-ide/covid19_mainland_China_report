As of 6:00 on February 2, 4 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to the number of confirmed cases to 45

Date: 2020-02-02 09:49
source: Commission office
Views:

As of 6:00 on February 2nd, 4 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection have been added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases has increased to 45.

The 42nd patient, female, 64 years old, lived in Heping District, Tianjin. No Wuhan travel or work history. The patient developed fever on January 27 and coughed on January 30. She treated herself symptomatically. On February 1st, he was diagnosed in the General Hospital of Medical University and was diagnosed as the 42nd case in our city by the expert group of the city, which is a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. The patient denied contacting patients with pneumonia diagnosed or suspected of new coronavirus infection, but the patient's family is large, so the family history of the source of the epidemic and the contact history of the case are under investigation. The patient's close-contact staff is currently following up.

The 43rd patient, female, 35 years old, was a salesperson in the department store of Baodi District, Tianjin, living in Baodi District, Tianjin. She was a colleague with the 34th and 37th confirmed patients. The patient developed fever on January 21, and had several visits to symptomatic treatment in a community hospital. On January 27, she was seen in the People's Hospital of Baodi District. Her viral and nucleic acid tests were negative twice. On January 30, he was diagnosed at the Municipal Chest Hospital due to fever again. On the same day, he was transferred to the General Hospital of Medical University for isolation and treatment. On January 31, the virus nucleic acid test was negative. On February 1, the fourth virus nucleic acid test was positive. The 43rd case in our city is severe. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. Close contacts are being tracked down.

The 44th patient, male, 75 years old, lived in Hedong District and was a relative of the 20th and 35th cases. The patient developed fever on January 30 and was seen at the Third Central Hospital of the City on February 1. The city expert group confirmed the 44th case in our city, which is a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. The case was isolated and managed as a close contact on January 27, and there are currently no close contacts.

The 45th patient, female, 61 years old, lived in Hedong District, Tianjin. The patient lived in Xiaogan City, Hubei Province from November 2019 to January 24, 2020, and was isolated at home after returning to Tianjin on January 24. Fever appeared on January 25. He was diagnosed at the Third Central Hospital of the City on January 28. The virus nucleic acid test was positive on February 1. The city expert group confirmed the 45th case in our city, which is a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. Close contacts are currently being investigated.

So far, Tianjin has reported 45 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 28 males and 17 females; 3 critically ill, 14 severe, and 28 common; and 124 suspected cases. A total of 301 close contacts have been investigated, of which 12 have been diagnosed, 13 have been released from medical observation, and 276 are still receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
