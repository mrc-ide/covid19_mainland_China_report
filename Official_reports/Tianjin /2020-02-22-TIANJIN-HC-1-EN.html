As of 22:00 on February 21, Tianjin has newly added one confirmed case of new coronary pneumonia, with a total of 133 confirmed cases.

The 133th patient, female, 47 years old, now lives in Bamen Town, Baodi District, Tianjin City, has no travel or work history in Wuhan, and has been in close contact with a couple in the same village who has been shopping in the department store in Baodi District. The patient developed symptoms of dry cough and fever on February 4 and was told to go to a fever clinic when he went to the Bamen City Hospital. However, the patient refused and he improved after taking the medicine on his own. On February 9, she developed fever and cough again. She recovered after taking her own medicine. On the 10th, she went to the Baodi District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for a hot clinic. On the morning of the 11th, she was transferred to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a hot clinic. . The results of the three nucleic acid tests on February 11, 13, and 17 were all negative, and the results of the two nucleic acid tests on 19 and 21 were positive for a single target. According to the comprehensive judgment of the city expert group, it was the 133th confirmed case in our city, which was normal. Type, has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Identified close contacts are currently under medical observation in isolation, and other close contacts and sources of infection are under investigation.

According to preliminary epidemiological investigations, the patient played cards with Yin and Liu from the same village several times from January 24 to 27. The couple visited the Baodi District Department Store on January 20. Among them, Yin had symptoms such as runny nose and sneezing on January 24, which improved after taking the medication; Liu had symptoms of cough and sneezing on January 29, which improved after 2-3 days. Both did not see a doctor after the onset of illness. The couple reportedly separated from home on February 2 but failed to report registration as required, and concentrated on medical observation on February 21.

As of now, Tianjin has reported a total of 133 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, including 71 males and 62 females; 2 critically ill, 20 severe, 45 normal, and 1 mild; 62 were discharged from hospital, and died 3 example. There were 127 suspected cases. A total of 2054 close contacts were investigated, and 699 were still under medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
