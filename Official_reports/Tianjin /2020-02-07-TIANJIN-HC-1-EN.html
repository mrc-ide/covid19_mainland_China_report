At 00:00 on February 6, 2020, Tianjin reported 10 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 3 new critical cases, 8 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, No additional cases were discharged. among them:

Beichen District reported the first confirmed case. Six other cases were confirmed in Baodi District, 1 in Xiqing District, 1 in Dongli District and 1 in Binhai New District.

As of 24:00 on February 6, a total of 79 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 7 severe cases, 28 severe cases, 1 death case, and 2 discharged patients were reported in Tianjin. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 21 cases in Baodi District, 10 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 4 cases in Dongli District, 4 cases in Xiqing District, and 3 in Nankai District. There were 3 cases in Binhai New Area, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Beichen District, and 6 cases from outside Tianjin. Among the discharged patients, there were 1 case in Hedong District and 1 case in Hongqiao District.

At present, 599 close contacts have been traced, 62 people have been released from medical observation, and 516 people are still receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 7, 2020
