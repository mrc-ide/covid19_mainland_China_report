As of 23:00 on February 11, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 106 confirmed cases.

The 106th patient, female, 27 years old, individual merchant, lived in Wangdingdi, Nankai District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient had contact with relatives who had fever symptoms during a family visit in Hejian City, Hebei Province, from January 23 to 30 (now in isolation and observation at a hospital in Cangzhou). The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 11, and was confirmed by the city's expert group as the 106th case in our city, which is a common type. The patient is currently being transferred to Haihe Hospital. Isolation medical observations are being conducted on identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.



Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 12, 2020
