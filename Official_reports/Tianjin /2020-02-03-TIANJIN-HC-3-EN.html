As of 10:00 on February 3, Tianjin has newly added 2 new cases of coronavirus-infected pneumonia and a total of 56 confirmed cases

Date: 2020-02-03 14:10
source: Commission office
Views:

As of 10:00 on February 3, 2 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were newly added in Tianjin, with a total of 56 confirmed cases.

The 55th patient, female, 56 years old, lived in Nankai District, Tianjin, and was the family member of the 9th and 21st confirmed cases, who were close contacts. Since January 24, he has been isolated and observed at home. During the period, he did not contact other personnel. During the isolation period, the patient's temperature was normal and there were no symptoms. There was no obvious cause of weakness on February 1st, and the nucleic acid test was positive on February 2nd. On February 3, the 55th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city's expert group, which was of ordinary type, and the vital signs were stable. At present, the Nankai District Center for Disease Control and Prevention has basically eliminated close contacts and terminally disinfected its residence.

The 56th patient, female, 32 years old, residing in Lutai Town, Ninghe District, Tianjin, was the family member of the 38th confirmed case and was a close contact. The patient and the 38th patient had close contact with the 32nd confirmed case on January 24. After the 32nd diagnosis, the patient underwent home isolation medical observation. Fever symptoms appeared in the early morning of February 1, and he went to the Ninghe District Hospital for a fever clinic. On February 2, a nucleic acid test was performed. On the 3rd, the city expert group confirmed the 56th case in Tianjin, which was a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, the Ninghe District Center for Disease Control and Prevention conducts isolated medical observations on identified close contacts and terminal disinfection of their residences.

As of now, Tianjin has reported a total of 56 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 30 males and 26 females; 3 critically ill, 15 severe, 37 common, no deaths, and 1 discharged.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 3, 2020
