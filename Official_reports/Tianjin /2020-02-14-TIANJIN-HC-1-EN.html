At 04:00 on February 13, 2020, Tianjin reported 7 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new critical cases, 0 new severe cases, 1 new death case, and new hospital discharge. 10 cases. among them:

Three new cases were confirmed in Baodi District, 2 in Nankai District, 1 in Hedong District, and 1 in Beichen District.

As of 24:00 on February 13, Tianjin had reported a total of 119 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 4 severe cases, 27 severe cases, 3 death cases, and 21 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 46 cases in Baodi District, 14 cases in Hedong District, 12 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Nankai District, 6 cases in Beichen District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, and 4 cases in Dongli District. 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Wuqing District, and 6 cases came from outside Tianjin.

Among the discharged patients, there were 4 cases in Hedong District, 3 in Hebei District, 3 in Baodi District, 2 in Heping District, 2 in Hexi District, 2 in Xiqing District, 1 in Hongqiao District, 1 in Dongli District and 1 in Beichen District There were 1 case in Wuqing District and 1 case in Tianjin.

At present, 1,240 close contacts have been traced, 474 medical observations have been lifted, and 730 are still receiving medical observations.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
