Between February 6 and 18:00 on February 15, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 122 confirmed cases.

The 122nd patient, female, 63 years old, now living in Yuhua Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, was the wife of the 110th confirmed case, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient went to the Baodi District Department Store for shopping on January 22, and developed cough, runny nose, fever, and joint pain and abdominal pain. On February 6, he went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic and was diagnosed with a suspected case that day. She was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment; the nucleic acid test result was positive on the 15th. The city expert group confirmed the 122nd confirmed case in Tianjin, and the classification was ordinary. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital and his close contacts are being investigated.

As of now, Tianjin has reported a total of 122 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 65 males and 57 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 15, 2020
