From February 6 to 11:00 on February 14, one new case of new coronavirus pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 120 confirmed cases.

The 120th patient, female, 49 years old, lived in Lugu Port, Wuqing District, Tianjin, had no travel or work history in Wuhan, was the sister of the 109th confirmed case, and was a close contact. On February 12, the patient was transferred from 120 first aid to Wuqing District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for a fever diagnosis and isolation checkup; on the 14th, he was transferred to the Second People's Hospital of Wuqing District. His body temperature was normal. The nucleic acid test result was positive that day. The 120th case was diagnosed in our city and was of ordinary type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital, and the identified close contacts are being observed in isolation for medical observation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been terminally disinfected.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 14, 2020
