From 18:00 on February 5th to 12:00 on February 6, 9 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection were added in Tianjin, with a total of 78 confirmed cases.

The 70th patient, male, 34 years old, lived in Beichen District, Tianjin, and had no history of tourism and work in Wuhan. The patient developed symptoms such as fever, chills, sputum, and fatigue on January 22, and returned to Baoding, Hebei, for the Spring Festival that day. He was infused at two clinics in Linbao Village, Luxian County, Baoding, Hebei Province from January 23 to 27; On the 28th, she went to the Shexian People's Hospital for a diagnosis of pneumonia, but returned to her local home without treatment; on January 31, she returned to Tianjin and on February 1 to the Wuqing District People's Hospital for treatment. For pneumonia suspected of being infected by a new type of coronavirus, the city's expert group was diagnosed as the 70th case in our city, which was severe. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Eleven close contacts who have been judged so far have been isolated for medical observation, and other close contacts are being investigated.

The 71st patient, male, 55 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin City. On January 24, he went to the men's clothing area on the first floor of the department store in Baodi District to shop. The patient took the medicine after feeling uncomfortable on January 30; he went to the clinic in his community from February 1 to 2; he drove to the People's Hospital of Baodi District with a son on February 4 for a hot clinic. On February 6, the 71st case was confirmed by the city expert group and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. At present, 12 close contacts who have been judged have undergone isolated medical observation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 72nd patient, female, 65 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin, and had no history of tourism and work in Wuhan. The patient developed symptoms of dry cough and fever on February 1st and drove to the Baodi District People's Hospital with a fever clinic on February 5th. On February 6, the 72nd case of the city was confirmed by the city expert group and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Its possible source of infection is being further investigated. At present, 12 close contacts who have been judged have undergone isolated medical observation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 73rd patient, female, 60 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin. On January 20, she went shopping in the clothing area of the Department Store in Baodi District. The patient went shopping at the Koudong Agricultural Products Market in Baodi District on January 22. She had a conscious cold with headache on January 23, and subsequently developed fever. Her symptoms did not improve until February 3, and she went to the People's Hospital of Baodi District on February 4. Outpatient visit. On February 6, the city's expert group confirmed the 73rd case in our city and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Nine close contacts have been identified for medical observation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been disinfected.

The 74th patient, male, 53 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin. He and his wife visited the Baodi District Department Store on January 20th. The patient consciously experienced general soreness on January 26, and relieved after taking the medicine on his own. The symptoms worsened on January 28. He went to the community hospital on February 1 and sent a fever consultation to the People's Hospital of Baodi District on February 4. On February 6, the city's expert group confirmed the 74th case in our city and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Eight judged close contacts have been isolated for medical observation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been disinfected.

The 75th patient, male, 45 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin, and his wife visited the shoe area of the department store in Baodi District on January 20. On February 6, the city's expert group confirmed the 75th case in our city, and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. At present, the three close contacts identified have been observed in isolation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 76th patient, male, 30 years old, lives in Xiqing District, Tianjin. The 78th patient, female, 56 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin.
