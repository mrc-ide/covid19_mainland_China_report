Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on February 3, 2020

Date: 2020-02-03 09:04
source: Commission office
Views:

At 04:00 to 24:00 on February 2, 2020, Tianjin reported 7 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 0 new critical cases, 2 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, and One more case was discharged. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases in each district, 3 were in Hedong District, 2 in Baodi District, 1 in Heping District, and 1 in Ninghe District. Among the newly-discharged cases, 1 was in Hongqiao District.

As of 24:00 on February 2nd, Tianjin has reported a total of 48 confirmed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection, 3 severe cases, 15 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 1 discharged case. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 9 cases in Hebei District, 9 cases in Hedong District, 5 cases in Baodi District, 4 cases in Heping District, 3 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Dongli District, 3 cases in Ninghe District, 2 cases in Nankai District, and 2 cases in Xiqing District. There were 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Binhai New District, and 4 cases of Laijin in the field. Among the discharged patients, 1 case was in Hongqiao District.

At present, 356 close contacts have been traced, 15 people have been released from medical observation, and 327 people are still receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 3, 2020
