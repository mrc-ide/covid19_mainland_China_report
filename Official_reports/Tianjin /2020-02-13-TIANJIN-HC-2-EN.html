As of 6:00 on February 13th, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 113 confirmed cases.

The 113th patient, male, 85 years old, now living in Ruijing Street, Beichen District, Tianjin, has no travel or work history in Wuhan. He is the husband of the 100th confirmed case, the father-in-law of 103 confirmed cases, and 108 of the confirmed cases. father. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 12, and the city's expert group was diagnosed as the 113th case in our city, which is a common type. The patient became ill during the period of intensive quarantine. At present it is determined that there is no close contact, and his home has been terminally disinfected.

As of now, 113 cases of new coronavirus pneumonia have been reported in our city, including 61 males and 52 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 13, 2020
