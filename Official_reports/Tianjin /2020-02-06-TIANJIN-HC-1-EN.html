At 04:00 on February 5, 2020, Tianjin reported 2 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 0 new critical cases, 3 new severe cases, 1 new death case, No additional cases were discharged. among them:

Two new cases were confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 5th, Tianjin has reported a total of 69 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 4 severe cases, 20 severe cases, 1 death case, and 2 discharged cases. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 15 cases in Baodi District, 10 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Nankai District, 3 cases in Dongli District, and 3 cases in Xiqing District. There were 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Binhai New District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Tianjin outside the hospital. Among the discharged patients, 1 case was in Hongqiao District and 1 case was in Hedong District.

At present, 528 close contacts have been traced, 47 people have been released from medical observation, and 460 people are still receiving medical observation.



Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 6, 2020
