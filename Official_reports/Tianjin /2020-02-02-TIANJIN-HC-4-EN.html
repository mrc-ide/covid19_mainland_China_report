As of 11:00 on February 2nd, 3 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to the number of confirmed cases in Tianjin.

Date: 2020-02-02 13:18
source: Commission office
Views:

As of 12:00 on February 2, three new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new-type coronavirus infection were added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases increased to 48.

The 46th patient, female, 71 years old, lived in Ninghe District. This patient was a relative of the 32nd confirmed case and was a close contact. No Wuhan residence history. The patient ate with the 32nd case at the restaurant on January 23, and then went to the 32nd case at home. After the 32nd case was confirmed, the patient was sent to Ninghe District Hospital for screening on the afternoon of January 31, and was diagnosed and reported on February 1 with suspected new-type coronavirus infection. On February 2nd, he was transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and was confirmed as the 46th case in our city by the expert group of the city, which is a common type. At present, the vital signs are stable. The Ninghe District Center for Disease Control and Prevention has carried out an epidemiological investigation, carried out isolated medical observations on four close contacts, and continued to follow up and investigate the close contacts.

The 47th patient, male, 59 years old, now lives in Hedong District and is an employee of the Tianjin Railway Coach Section. The patient had contact with the 6 confirmed cases and the 2 confirmed cases. Fever appeared on the evening of January 23, and was sent to the Third Central Hospital of the City for fever isolation and treatment in the early morning of the 24th. The nucleic acid test was negative. He returned home at night on the 25th to continue his home medical isolation observation. He continued to have fever and went to the third on the 28th The central hospital sent a fever consultation and was treated in an isolation ward. The nucleic acid test was still negative. Due to high suspicion, the nucleic acid test was positive again on February 2. The city expert group confirmed the 47th case in our city, which was severe. Currently, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. The Hedong District Center for Disease Control and Prevention is continuing medical observation of its relatives (1 person) in isolation medical observation and terminal disinfection of their residence.

The 48th patient, female, 26 years old, now lives in Baodi District and is a salesperson at Zhengxin Pharmacy in Baodi District. From 15:00 to 18:00 on January 23, the patient had visited the department store shoes area (43th confirmed case work area), clothing area and jewelry area (37th confirmed case work area) for shopping. The patient developed fever, dizziness, dry cough and other symptoms on January 29. She took antipyretic medication to relieve her symptoms. She then went to the Baodi District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital for a fever clinic and returned. On February 1st, she went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic for symptoms that did not resolve. She was diagnosed with pneumonia suspected of a new coronavirus infection and reported. On February 2, the city's expert group confirmed the 48th case in our city, which is a common type. Currently, the Baodi District Center for Disease Control is investigating its close contacts.

Up to now, a total of 48 cases of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus infection have been found in Tianjin, including 29 cases of males and 19 cases of females; 3 of them are critically ill, 15 are severe, 30 are common, and there are no deaths.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 2, 2020
