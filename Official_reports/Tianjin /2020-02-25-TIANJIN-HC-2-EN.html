On February 25, four more patients with new coronary pneumonia were discharged from Tianjin Haihe Hospital, including a 90-year-old patient with new coronary pneumonia. At present, 91 patients with Tianjin New Crown Pneumonia have been cured.

Li Jian, a doctor of the medical team of Tianjin First Central Hospital in Haihe Hospital and chief physician of ICU of the First Central Hospital, said that the 90-year-old patient was admitted on February 19 and was in good condition when he was admitted. The clinical classification is ordinary. Consider By this time, the patient was old and the overall classification was heavy. The patient had no history of underlying disease, and the oxygenation index and blood oxygen saturation were not low. The general condition was good. The medical staff used antiviral therapy and combined Chinese medicine therapy. The patient recovered quickly. According to the discharge procedure, the medical staff reviewed the CT, Nucleic acid tests and other tests all met discharge standards.

It is understood that this elderly patient is a close contact of the confirmed case and his family members are also undergoing treatment in the hospital. In order not to worry the elderly, the medical staff often tells his family about the treatment of the patient to reassure the elderly, and the patient himself is very cheerful and optimistic. Life can take care of themselves, and actively cooperate with treatment, take the initiative to communicate and chat with medical staff.

On the afternoon of the 25th, the grandfather walked out of the isolation ward accompanied by medical staff. The elderly were very talkative, thanked the medical staff, and told the medical staff that if they went to Baodi, they could go to him. The grandfather said, "I am old and take good care of me."

When everyone was boasting that Grandpa's mentality was good, Grandpa amused everyone with a sentence: "Can I not live to such an age?"

The medical staff is also very happy to be able to treat patients of such an advanced age. Li Jian said that with the support of the First Central Hospital and Haihe Hospital, we have successfully carried out our work. I believe that with the joint efforts of all parties, we can definitely treat more Many patients with new coronary pneumonia.
