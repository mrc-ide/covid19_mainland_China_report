As of 20:00 on February 12, two new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 112 confirmed cases.

The 111th patient, female, 54 years old, went shopping in the department store of Baodi District on January 22, and now lives in Shigezhuang Town, Baodi District, Tianjin. She has no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on February 2 and stayed in the hospital for observation. The nucleic acid test results were negative on the 3rd, 5th, 7th, 8th, and 10th days, and the nucleic acid test results were positive on the 12th The city's expert group confirmed the 111th case in our city, and the classification was ordinary. At present, he has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital of the city for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.

The 112th patient, female, 42 years old, had been in contact with the 51st confirmed case on January 24, and now lives in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, without a history of travel or work in Wuhan. The patient developed fever and respiratory symptoms on February 6 and went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic and stayed in the hospital for observation. The nucleic acid test results on the 6th, 8th, and 10th days were all negative, and the nucleic acid test results were positive on the 12th. The city's expert group confirmed the 112th case in our city, and the classification was ordinary. At present, he has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital of the city for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.

So far, Tianjin has reported 112 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, including 60 males and 52 females.

Today, another patient with new coronary pneumonia was cured and discharged from Tianjin Haihe Hospital. Up to now, 11 cases have been cured in Tianjin.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 12, 2020
