As of 2 o'clock on February 1, 3 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to the number of confirmed cases in Tianjin

Date: 2020-02-02 00:06
source: Commission office
Views:

As of 22:00 on February 1, three new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases increased to 41.

The 39th case, male, 52 years old, lived in Dongli District, Tianjin, and had no travel history in Wuhan. From January 20 to 26, he returned to his mother's house in Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province to celebrate the Chinese New Year, and returned to Tianjin by car with his family on the 26th. The patient reported that she had eaten with a confirmed coronavirus infection in Wenzhou. He developed fever on January 27 and treated himself symptomatically. He was diagnosed in the Airport Hospital of the General Hospital of Medical University on January 31 and was diagnosed as the 39th case in Tianjin by the city expert group on February 1. At present, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

The 40th case, female, 46 years old, lived in Dongli District, Tianjin, and had no travel history in Wuhan. The case was the 39th family member of the confirmed case. They returned to Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province for the Chinese New Year from January 20th to 26th, and returned to Tianjin by car on the 26th. On January 31st, she was diagnosed in the General Hospital of Tianjin Medical University Airport Hospital because of cough and pharyngeal discomfort. On February 1st, the city's expert group confirmed the 40th case in Tianjin, which was a common type. At present, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

The 41st case, female, 21 years old, lives in Dongli District, Tianjin, and has no travel history in Wuhan. The case was the family member of the 39th and 40th confirmed cases. They returned to Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province for the Chinese New Year from January 20th to 26th, and returned to Tianjin by self-driving on the 26th. He was diagnosed with fever in the General Hospital of Tianjin Medical University Airport on January 31, and was diagnosed as the 41st case in Tianjin by the expert team on February 1 as a normal type. At present, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

An epidemiological investigation showed that the above three cases occurred in a family of three and were clustered. From January 26 to 31, the three said they had stayed at home and had no contact with others.

As of now, 41 cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection have been found in Tianjin, including 27 males and 14 females; 3 critical cases, 13 severe cases, and 25 common cases; no deaths.



Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
