Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on February 2, 2020

Date: 2020-02-02 09:44
source: Commission office
Views:

At 04:00 on February 1, 2020, Tianjin reported 9 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 3 new critical cases, 1 new severe case, and 0 new death case. No additional cases were discharged. among them:

The first confirmed cases were reported in Dongli District and Baodi District. Among the newly confirmed cases in other districts, one was from Hebei, one from Hedong, and one from Ninghe.

As of 24:00 on February 1st, Tianjin has reported a total of 41 confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection, 3 severe cases, 13 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 0 discharged cases. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 9 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Hedong District, 3 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Heping District, 3 cases in Baodi District, 3 cases in Dongli District, 2 cases in Nankai District, 2 cases in Xiqing District, and 2 in Hongqiao District. There were 2 cases in Ninghe District, 2 cases in Binhai New Area, and 4 cases in Tianjin outside.

At present, 301 close contacts have been traced, 13 have been released from medical observation, and 276 are still receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 2, 2020
