At 9:00 on January 25, 2020, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection was added in Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases increased to nine

Date: 2020-01-25 14:25
source: Commission office

At 9:00 on January 25, 2020, a new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection was added in our city, and the number of confirmed cases increased to nine.

The ninth patient, Du Moumou, male, 57 years old, lived in Hedong District. From Wuhan to Tianjin on January 17, fever occurred on January 24. He went to Tianjin First Central Hospital for a fever clinic. The city expert group confirmed the 9th case in our city, which was a severe case. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable.
The patient was in close contact with Huang, the third confirmed case in this city.
The situation of the previous 8 confirmed patients is as follows:

In the first case, Zheng Moumou, female, 59 years old, lives in Yangliuqing Town, Xiqing District, and has a history of tourism in Wuhan. Onset on January 14th, he went to the Xiqing Hospital and the City Haihe Hospital for treatment. On January 21st, the diagnosis group of the National Health and Health Commission confirmed a confirmed case of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection. It is a severe case and is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital. The vital signs are stable.

In the second case, Yang Moumou, male, 57 years old, lives in Hedong District, works in the Tianjin motor train and passenger bus section, and has a history of business travel in Wuhan. He developed symptoms on January 18, and successively visited the Affiliated Hospital of the Chinese Academy of Research, the Armed Police Hospital, the General Hospital of Medical University, and the Haihe Hospital of the City. On January 21, the diagnosis group of the National Health and Health Commission confirmed the confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection. He is currently undergoing treatment at Haihe Hospital, and his condition is worse than before. He is still a severe case with stable vital signs.

The third case, Huang Moumou, female, 68 years old, lives in Nankai District and works in Wuhan. He developed symptoms on January 14 and successively visited the Municipal People's Hospital and the Municipal Haihe Hospital. On January 21, the diagnosis was confirmed by the municipal expert group. He was a severe case and is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

The fourth case, Yu Moumou, male, 40 years old, lives in Heping District and has a working history in Wuhan. He developed symptoms on January 14 and successively visited the People's Hospital of Hubei Province, the General Hospital of Medical University, and the Haihe Hospital of the City. He was diagnosed by the expert team on January 21 as a mild case. He is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

In the fifth case, Chen Moumou, male, 46 years old, lives in Hebei District, and works in the Tianjin motor train and bus section. Pharyngeal discomfort occurred on January 15th. Since January 17th, he has visited the Beining Street Community Health Service Center and the Fourth Central Hospital of the city. On January 23rd, the diagnosis was confirmed by the city expert group. It is a severe case and is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital. The vital signs are stable.

In the sixth case, Shimou, male, 56 years old, had a history of hypertension for more than 10 years, and lived in Hebei District. Fever appeared on January 19th, and went to the Fourth Central Hospital of the city on January 23, and was confirmed by the city expert group on January 24. It was a severe case and is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

The seventh case, Kang Moumou, female, 29 years old, lives in Binhai New Area, works in Wuhan, and came to Tianjin on January 23. On January 24, he developed fever and went to the Fifth Central Hospital for treatment. He was diagnosed by the city expert group as a mild case. He is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

In the eighth case, Cui, a 39-year-old male, lives in Hedong District and is a staff member of the Tianjin Railway Passenger Car Depot. I visited Wuhan from January 19 to 22, and returned to Tianjin on January 23. He developed fever on the evening of January 23, and went to the Third Central Hospital of the City on January 24. He was diagnosed by the city expert group as a severe case. He is currently being treated in Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

At present, a total of 9 cases of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus infection have been found in our city, including 6 males and 3 females; 7 severe cases and 2 mild cases; no critical cases. As of today, 85 close contacts have been tracked, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 83 are still receiving medical observation.
