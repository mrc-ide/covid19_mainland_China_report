Epidemiological situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on January 27, 2020

Date: 2020-01-27 11:56
source: Commission office

At 04:00 on January 26, 2020, Tianjin reported 4 new confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, 4 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 0 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 3 cases in Hebei District and 1 case in Tianjin.

As of 24:00 on January 26, Tianjin had reported a total of 14 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 11 severe cases, 0 deaths, and 0 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 5 cases in Hebei District, 2 in Hedong District, 2 in Nankai District, 1 in Xiqing District, 1 in Heping District, 1 in Binhai New District, and 2 in Laijin.

At present, 178 close contacts have been traced, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 173 are still receiving medical observation.
