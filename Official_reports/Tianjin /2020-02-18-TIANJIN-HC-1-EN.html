At 04:00 on February 17, 2020, Tianjin reported 1 new confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia, 1 new critically ill case, 0 new severe case, 0 new death case, and new discharge. 1 case. among them:

One new case was confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 17, Tianjin had reported 125 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 4 critical cases, 22 severe cases, 3 dead cases, and 46 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 50 cases in Baodi District, 15 cases in Hedong District, 12 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Nankai District, 6 cases in Beichen District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, and 4 cases in Dongli District. 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Laijin District.

Among the discharged patients, there were 12 cases in Baodi District, 7 cases in Hebei District, 7 cases in Hedong District, 4 cases in Heping District, 3 cases in Xiqing District, 2 cases in Hexi District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 1 case in Nankai District, and 1 in Dongli District. There were 1 case in Beichen District, 1 case in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Binhai New District, and 3 cases outside Tianjin.

At present, 1646 close contacts have been traced, 920 people have been released from medical observation, and 686 people are still receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 18, 2020
