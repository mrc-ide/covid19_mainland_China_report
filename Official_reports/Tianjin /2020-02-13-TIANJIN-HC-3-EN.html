From February 6 to 15:00 on February 13, 4 new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, and a total of 117 confirmed cases.

The 114th patient, female, 57 years old, lived in Wangdingdi Street, Nankai District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient arrived in Tianjin Binhai International Airport from Hohhot on January 20, and her daughter (115th confirmed case) picked up and returned home by taking subway lines 2 and 6; she went to the city's first central hospital on February 12 The outpatient visit was diagnosed as a suspected case and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment. The nucleic acid test result was positive on the 13th and was confirmed by the city's expert group as the 114th confirmed case in our city. The classification was ordinary. He is currently being transferred to Haihe Hospital, and his possible source of infection is being further investigated. Isolation medical observations of the identified close contacts are underway, and other close contacts are under investigation.

The 115th patient, female, 28 years old, lived in Wangdingdi Street, Nankai District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient took the Metro Line 2 and 6 to Tianjin Binhai International Airport on January 20 and met with his mother (the 114th confirmed case) on the subway. He returned to the city's No. 1 Central Hospital for a hot consultation on February 12 He was diagnosed as a suspected case and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment. He is currently being transferred to Haihe Hospital, and his possible source of infection is being further investigated. Isolation medical observations of the identified close contacts are underway, and other close contacts are under investigation.
