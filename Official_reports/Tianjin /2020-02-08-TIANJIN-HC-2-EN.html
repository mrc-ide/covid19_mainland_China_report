From 18:00 on February 7 to 6:00 on February 8, there were 7 newly confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin, and a total of 88 confirmed cases.

The 82nd patient, male, 47 years old, was a middle school teacher in Baodi District, living in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin. He had no history of travel or work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping and staying in the department store in Baodi District. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 8. The 82nd case was confirmed by the city expert group and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. The possible source of infection is being further investigated. Three identified close contacts have been quarantined for medical observation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 83rd patient, female, 42 years old, was a salesperson in the clothing and footwear section of the department store in Baodi District, and lives in Baodi District, Tianjin. He has no travel or work history in Wuhan. He is the 43th confirmed case and the 53th confirmed case colleague. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 8 and was diagnosed by the city's expert group as the 83rd case in our city. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observation has been conducted on one of the close contacts identified.

The 84th patient, male, 36 years old, was an employee of a building materials company in Tianjin. He lived in Yuhua Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. He was the family member of the 43th confirmed case. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 8 and was diagnosed by the city's expert group as the 84th case in our city. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment.

The 85th patient, female, 56 years old, retired, lived in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin City, had no travel or work history in Wuhan, and was a close contact with the 72 confirmed cases. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 8 and was diagnosed by the city's expert group as the 85th case in our city. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. At present, the five close contacts identified have been isolated for medical observation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been disinfected.

The 86th patient, male, 65 years old, retired, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin City, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. He was the 72nd confirmed case and the 85th confirmed case relative. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 8 and was diagnosed as the 86th case in our city by the expert group of the city. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. At present, the two close contacts who have been judged have undergone isolated medical observation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been disinfected at the end.

The 87th patient, female, 43 years old, lived in Koudong Town, Baodi District, Tianjin City, had no travel or work history in Wuhan, and went shopping in the Baodi District Department Store on January 20th. He was the 75th patient with close contact with confirmed cases. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 8 and was diagnosed as the 87th case in our city by the expert group of the city. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. At present, the three close contacts identified have been observed in isolation. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 88th patient, male, 42 years old, lived in Wuqing District, Tianjin, had no travel or work history in Wuhan, and was the brother of the 70th confirmed case. The patient had a positive nucleic acid test on February 7, and was confirmed as the 88th case in our city by the expert group on February 8. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. At present, five close contacts identified have been observed in isolation, and other close contacts are being investigated.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 88 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 49 males and 39 females; 5 critically ill, 32 severe, and 48 common; 2 were discharged from hospital and 1 died. There were 302 suspected cases. A total of 621 close contacts were investigated, of which 21 were diagnosed, 82 were released from medical observation, and 518 were receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 8, 2020
