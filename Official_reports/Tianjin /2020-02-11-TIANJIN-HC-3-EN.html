From February 6 to 10:00 on February 11, two new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 102 confirmed cases.

The 101st patient, female, 50 years old, was the wife of the 95th confirmed case and was a close contact. The patient lived in Dazhigu Street, Hedong District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient was diagnosed as the 101st case in Tianjin on February 11 by the city expert group and was of ordinary type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Six identified close contacts have been identified for medical observation.

The 102nd patient, male, 49 years old, lived in Shuangkou Town, Beichen District, Tianjin, had no travel or work history in Wuhan, and denied exposure to fever, cough or pneumonia. The patient was positive on the 10th through the preliminary nucleic acid test of the Xiqing District Center for Disease Control and Prevention on the 11th, and the result of the retest on the 11th was positive, and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 102nd case in Tianjin. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observation has been performed on the four close contacts who have been identified.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 102 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 55 males and 47 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 11, 2020
