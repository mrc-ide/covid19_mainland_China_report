As of 18:00 on February 20, Tianjin has newly added 1 confirmed case of new coronary pneumonia, a total of 131 confirmed cases, and 5 discharged from hospital.

The 131th patient, female, 49 years old, lives at Haibin Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, and has no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient went shopping in the clothing area of the department store in Baodi District on January 23, went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on February 8, was diagnosed as a suspected case, and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment; the fourth nucleic acid test result was positive on the 20th The city expert group confirmed the 131st case in our city, and the classification was ordinary. At present, she has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.

As of now, Tianjin has reported a total of 131 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 71 males and 60 females.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 20, 2020
