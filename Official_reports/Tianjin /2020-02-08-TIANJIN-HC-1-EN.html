At 04:00 on February 7, 2020, Tianjin reported 2 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 0 new critical cases, 4 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, and No additional cases were discharged. among them:

Two new cases were confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 7th, Tianjin has reported 81 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 5 critical cases, 32 severe cases, 1 death case, and 2 discharged cases. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 23 cases in Baodi District, 10 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 4 cases in Dongli District, 4 cases in Xiqing District, and 3 in Nankai District There were 3 cases in Binhai New Area, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Beichen District, and 6 cases from outside Tianjin. Among the discharged patients, there were 1 case in Hedong District and 1 case in Hongqiao District.

At present, 621 close contacts have been traced, 82 people have been released from medical observation, and 518 people are still receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 8, 2020
