As of 10:00 on February 1, 3 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to 37 cases

Date: 2020-02-01 13:24
source: Commission office

As of 10:00 on February 1, three new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases increased to 37.

The 35th patient, female, 74 years old, who lived in Hedong District, Tianjin, was a relative of the 20th confirmed case. She has been isolated and managed as a close contact. No relatives or friends have visited her or went out. The patient developed fever on January 30 and was seen in the Third Central Hospital of the City on the 31st. The 35th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city expert team and was a general case. At present, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs.

The 36th patient, male, 53 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin, and was a relative of the 34th confirmed case. The patient developed fever symptoms on January 28. She recovered after taking the medicine on her own. She developed fever again on January 30. She was referred to the People's Hospital of Baodi District. On February 1st, the 36th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city's expert group, which was a general case. . At present, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs. Currently identified close contacts of patients are undergoing isolated medical observation, and other close contacts are being investigated.

The 37th patient, female, 43 years old, living in Baodi District, Tianjin, was the colleague of the 34th confirmed case. He had no history of travelling in Wuhan before the onset. He went to Beijing to purchase from January 16-17, and went to a jewelry store in Beijing. Close contact with a hot customer. The patient developed fever from the self-measured body temperature on the 25th, and her symptoms eased after taking the medicine on her own. She re- fevered after she stopped taking the drug on the 30th. She was diagnosed at the Baodi District People's Hospital on the 31st. The nucleic acid test was positive. , For common cases. At present, the patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital with stable vital signs. In this case, it has been confirmed that close contacts are undergoing isolation medical observation, and other close contacts are being investigated.

Up to now, a total of 37 cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection have been found in Tianjin, including 25 males and 12 females; 15 severe cases and 22 common cases; and no critical and death cases.
