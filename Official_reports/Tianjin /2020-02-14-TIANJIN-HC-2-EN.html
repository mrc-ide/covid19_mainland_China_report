As of 24:00 on February 13, two new cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 119 confirmed cases.

The 118th patient, male, 10 years old, currently resides in Koudong Town, Baodi District, Tianjin. He has no history of travel and work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi District. It is the 75th and 87th confirmed cases. Son. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 13 and was diagnosed by the city expert group as the 118th confirmed case in Tianjin, which is a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital and close contacts are being investigated.

The 119th patient, female, 31 years old, now lives in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin. He has no history of travel and work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi District. He is the 93th close contact with confirmed cases. The patient shared meals with the 93rd and 107th confirmed cases several times from January 26 to February 2; the nucleic acid test result was positive on February 13th, and the city expert group confirmed the 119th confirmed case in Tianjin , For ordinary type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital, and the identified close contacts will be observed in isolation. Other close contacts are being investigated.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
