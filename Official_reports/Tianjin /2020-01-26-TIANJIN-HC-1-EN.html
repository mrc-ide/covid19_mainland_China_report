Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on January 26, 2020

Date: 2020-01-26 09:27
source: Commission office

At 04:00 on January 25, 2020, Tianjin reported 2 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 1 new severe case, 0 new deaths, and 0 new discharged cases.

As of 24:00 on January 25, Tianjin has reported 10 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 7 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 0 discharged cases.

Among the confirmed cases, there was 1 case in Heping District, 1 case in Nankai District, 3 cases in Hedong District, 2 cases in Hebei District, 1 case in Xiqing District, 1 case in Binhai New District, and 1 case in Laijin.

At present, 86 close contacts have been traced, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 81 people are still receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

January 26, 2020
