From February 6 to 15:00 on February 12, 3 new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, and a total of 110 cases were confirmed.

The 108th case, female, 55 years old, was the daughter of the 100th confirmed case and the wife of the 103th confirmed case, living in Wanghailou Street, Hebei District, without Wuhan travel or work history. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 12, and was confirmed by the city's expert group as the 108th case in our city. At present, she has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital of the city for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts. The investigation and epidemiological investigations of other close contacts are in progress.

The 109th case was a female, 40 years old, living in Shuangkou Town, Beichen District. The wife of the 102nd confirmed case had no history of travel or work in Wuhan. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 12, and was confirmed by the city's expert group as the 109th case in our city, which was classified as severe. At present, she has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital of the city for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts. The investigation and epidemiological investigation of other close contacts are in progress.

The 110th patient, male, 63 years old, lived in Yuhua Street, Baodi District, Tianjin City, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient developed fever symptoms on February 2 and took self-administered antipyretic drugs for 3 days without remission. He then went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic and was hospitalized for observation. The nucleic acid test result was negative on the 6th and weak on the 8th. Positive; transferred to the Haihe Hospital on the 9th for a fever clinic and stayed in the hospital for observation; on the 12th, the nucleic acid test result was positive, and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 110th case in our city and the 41st case in Baodi District. An epidemiological investigation showed that his wife had been shopping at the Breguet Department Store. She had fever symptoms and was being treated in isolation. Her first nucleic acid test was negative. Isolation medical observations have been conducted on the identified close contacts, and other close contact investigations and epidemiological investigations are ongoing.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
