As of 6 o'clock on February 18, Tianjin had two new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, and a total of 127 confirmed cases.

The 126th patient, male, 64 years old, now lives in Haibin Street, Baodi District, Tianjin. He has no history of travel and work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi. The patient went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on February 5th, and both nucleic acid test results were negative; on the 8th, he was kept in isolation; on the 15th, he was transferred to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic and was admitted to an isolation ward. The nucleic acid test results It was negative; the fourth nucleic acid test result was positive on the 17th, and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 126th case in our city. At present, she has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.

The 127th patient, female, 54 years old, currently resides in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin City, without a history of travel and work in Wuhan, and has no history of shopping or staying in a department store in Baodi District. It is the 93rd, 107th, 119th in our city Cases, 121 close contacts. The patient was transferred to an isolation point for medical observation on February 10; the nucleic acid test result was positive on the 17th, and was confirmed by the city's expert group as the 127th case in our city. At present, she has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.

As of now, the city has reported a total of 127 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 68 males and 59 females.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
