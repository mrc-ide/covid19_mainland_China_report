Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on February 4, 2020

From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 3, 2020, Tianjin reported 15 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, 0 new critical cases, 1 new severe case, 0 new death case, and No additional cases were discharged. among them:

The newly confirmed cases in each district included 2 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Baodi District, 1 case in Hedong District, 1 case in Hebei District, 1 case in Nankai District, 1 case in Ninghe District, and 1 case in Xiqing District; 2 cases came from outside Tianjin.

As of 24:00 on February 3, Tianjin has reported a total of 63 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 3 critical cases, 16 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 1 discharged case. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 11 cases in Baodi District, 10 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 3 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Nankai District, 3 cases in Dongli District, and 3 cases in Xiqing District. There were 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Binhai New District, and 6 cases of Laijin in the field. Among the discharged patients, 1 case was in Hongqiao District.

At present, 410 close contacts have been tracked, 23 have been released from medical observation, and 369 are still receiving medical observation.




Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 4, 2020
