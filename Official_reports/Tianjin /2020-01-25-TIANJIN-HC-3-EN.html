One new case of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection in Tianjin, the number of confirmed cases increased to 10

Date: 2020-01-25 18:33
source: Commission office

At 16:00 on January 25, 2020, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection was added in Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases increased to 10.

The 10th patient, Hu Moumou, male, 30 years old, was born in Beijing. He stayed in Wuhan from January 18th to 20th. When he arrived in Tianjin on the 24th, he developed fever and went to Tianjin Hospital for a fever clinic. At 10 o'clock on January 25, the city expert group confirmed the 10th case in our city, which is a mild case. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable.

At present, a total of 10 cases of pneumonitis infected by new coronavirus have been found in our city, including 7 males and 3 females; 7 severe cases and 3 mild cases; no critical cases. 85 close contacts were tracked, 2 were released from medical observation, and 83 were still receiving medical observation.
