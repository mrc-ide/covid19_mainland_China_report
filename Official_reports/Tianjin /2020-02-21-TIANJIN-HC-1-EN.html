At 04:00 on February 20, 2020, Tianjin reported 1 new confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new critical cases, 0 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, and new discharge 5 cases. among them:

One new case was confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 20, Tianjin had reported a total of 131 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 2 critical cases, 20 severe cases, 3 deaths, and 59 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 56 cases in Baodi District, 15 in Hedong District, 12 in Hebei District, 6 in Heping District, 6 in Nankai District, 6 in Beichen District, 4 in Hexi District, 4 in Ninghe District, and 4 in Dongli District 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Laijin District.

Among the discharged patients, there were 17 cases in Baodi District, 9 cases in Hebei District, 8 cases in Hedong District, 4 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Xiqing District, 2 cases in Hexi District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Nankai District, and 2 cases in Dongli District. There were 1 case in Beichen District, 1 case in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Ninghe District, 1 case in Binhai New District, and 4 cases in Tianjin.

At present, 1986 close contacts have been traced, 1,167 medical observations have been lifted, and 775 are still receiving medical observations.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
