As of 18:00 on February 1, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection has been added to 38 confirmed cases in Tianjin

Date: 2020-02-01 19:37
source: Commission office

As of 18:00 on February 1, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection was added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases increased to 38.

The 38th patient, male, 32 years old, lived in Ninghe District, Tianjin, and had no travel history in Wuhan. He had a close relationship with the 32nd confirmed case in Tianjin and was a close contact. On January 31, the patient was transferred to a centralized isolation medical observation point in Ninghe District. The Ninghe District Center for Disease Control and Prevention organized a nucleic acid test when screening close contacts. The results were positive at both city and district levels. On February 1st, he was transferred to the Haihe Hospital and was diagnosed as the 38th case in Tianjin by the city expert group, which is a common case. At present, the patient's vital signs are stable.

At present, close contacts of relatives of this patient have been isolated for medical observation, and other close contacts are being investigated. The Ninghe District Disease Control Center has terminally disinfected its place of residence.

Up to now, a total of 38 cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection have been found in Tianjin, including 26 males and 12 females; 15 severe cases and 23 common cases, without critical and death cases.




Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
