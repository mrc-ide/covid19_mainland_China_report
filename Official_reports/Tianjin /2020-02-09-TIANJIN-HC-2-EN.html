As of 6:00 on February 9, two new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 90 confirmed cases.

The 89th patient, female, 34 years old, lived in Shigezhuang Town, Baodi District, Tianjin, and was a temporary salesperson in the Men's Wear Zone of Baodi Department Store. He had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on the 8th. The 89th case in Tianjin was diagnosed by the city's expert team on the 9th. It is a common type and has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. The 3 close contacts who have been judged have been isolated for medical observation. , Other close contacts are being investigated, and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 90th patient, male, 29 years old, lived in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin. He had no history of travel or work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or stay in the department store in Baodi. The patient's nucleic acid test result on the 9th was positive. The 90th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city expert group. Its possible source of infection is being further investigated. Isolation medical observation was conducted on the three close contacts who have been identified. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 90 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 50 males and 40 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 9, 2020
