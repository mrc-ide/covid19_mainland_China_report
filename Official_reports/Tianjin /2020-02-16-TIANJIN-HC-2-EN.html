From 18:00 on February 15 to 6:00 on February 16, 2 new cases of coronavirus pneumonia were newly confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 124 confirmed cases.

The 123 patient, male, 74 years old, now lives in Dazhigu Street, Hedong District, Tianjin City, has no travel and work history in Wuhan. He is a close relative of the 95th and 116 confirmed cases. The patient was sent to an isolation point for medical observation on February 10; a total of 4 nucleic acid tests were performed from February 10 to 14, and the fourth nucleic acid test result was positive; on February 16 the city expert group confirmed the diagnosis as The 123rd case in our city is still to be classified. At present, she has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Except for confirmed cases, her close contacts are in centralized isolation and observation.

The 124th patient, female, 66 years old, now lives in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, without a history of travel and work in Wuhan. The patient and her husband went to the Baodi District Department Store for shopping on January 21; attended dinners twice on January 25 and 30; went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a hot consultation on February 15; the nucleic acid test result was positive on February 16 The city's expert group confirmed the 124th case in our city, and the classification is yet to be determined. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. The three close contacts who have been judged are undergoing medical observation in isolation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been terminally disinfected. According to further epidemiological investigations, her husband had fever consciously on February 15 and was sent to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic at 120. He was diagnosed with a suspected case of new coronavirus pneumonia and reported that testing is currently in progress.

As of 14:00 on February 16, a total of 124 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia were reported in Tianjin, including 66 males and 58 females.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 16, 2020
