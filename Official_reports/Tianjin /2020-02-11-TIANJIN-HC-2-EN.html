As of 6:00 on February 11, five new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, and a total of 100 cases were confirmed.

The 96th patient, male, 76 years old, was a relative of the 93th and 97th confirmed cases, and lived in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin. He had no travel or work history in Wuhan, and no shopping history or stay in the Department Store in Baodi District. history. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 10, and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 96th case in Tianjin, which was a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Those without close contact have been terminally disinfected.

The 97th patient, female, 77 years old, was a relative of the 93th and 96th confirmed cases. The patient lived in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin. He had no history of travel or work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi District. The patient had a previous history of hypertension and coronary heart disease. The nucleic acid test result was positive on February 10, and the city expert group confirmed the 97th case in Tianjin, which was a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Those without close contact have been terminally disinfected.

The 98th patient, male, 43 years old, was a close colleague of the 82nd confirmed case. The patient was a middle school teacher in Baodi District and lived in Haibin Street, Baodi District, Tianjin. He had no history of travel or work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi District. The patient underwent 4 nucleic acid tests from February 2nd to 9th. The first 2 results were negative, and the second 2 results were single positive. On February 10th, the nucleic acid test results were positive. The expert team confirmed the diagnosis as Tianjin The 98th case was a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and 12 identified close contacts have been isolated for medical observation. Other close contacts are being investigated.

The 99th patient, female, 31 years old, had a shopping history in the department store of Baodi District (he had visited the department store twice on January 20 and 21), and was the family member of the 64th confirmed patient. He was a close contact. The patient lived in Dazhongzhuang Town, Baodi District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient was tested 4 times from February 3 to 8, and the first 3 times were negative. The 4th test was single positive, and the retest nucleic acid test result was positive on February 10. The diagnosis was confirmed by Tianjin expert group as 99 cases were of ordinary type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observation is performed on the three close contacts who have been identified. Other close contacts are under investigation.

The 100th patient, female, 80 years old, retired, lives in Ruijing Street, Beichen District, Tianjin, and has no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient went to Beichen Hospital for a fever clinic on February 7 and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment. A total of three tests were performed from February 8 to February 10, the first two were negative and the third was positive, and the retest nucleic acid test results were positive on February 10, and the city expert group confirmed the 100th case in Tianjin For ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his possible source of infection is being further investigated. Four identified close contacts have been isolated for medical observation, and other close contacts are under investigation.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 100 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 54 males and 46 females; 5 critically ill, 27 severe, 58 common, 8 discharged and 2 dead. One new death was the 44th confirmed case, male, 75 years old. The patient was admitted on February 2 with 30 years of hypertension, 10 years of cerebral infarction, 18 years of diabetes, and a right nephrectomy for right kidney cancer in March 2012. At 20:33 on February 10, the rescue was invalid, and clinical death was announced. The cause of death was cardiac arrest.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 11, 2020
