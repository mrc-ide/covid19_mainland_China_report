From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 15, 2020, Tianjin reported 2 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 1 new critically ill case, 0 new severe case, 0 new death case, and new discharge. 5 cases. among them:

Two new cases were confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 15th, Tianjin had reported a total of 122 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 5 severe cases, 23 severe cases, 3 death cases, and 37 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 48 cases in Baodi District, 14 cases in Hedong District, 12 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Nankai District, 6 cases in Beichen District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, and 4 cases in Dongli District. 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Laijin District.

Among the discharged patients, there were 9 cases in Baodi District, 7 cases in Hebei District, 7 cases in Hedong District, 2 cases in Heping District, 2 cases in Hexi District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Xiqing District, 1 case in Nankai District, and 1 in Dongli District. There were 1 case in Beichen District, 1 case in Wuqing District, and 2 cases in other places.

At present, 1,467 close contacts have been tracked, 681 have been released from medical observation, and 749 are still receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
