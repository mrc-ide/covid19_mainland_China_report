From 18:00 to 22:00 on February 6, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection was added in Tianjin, with a total of 79 confirmed cases.

The 79th patient, male, 35 years old, lived in Tianjin Binhai New District, took a train to his hometown in Bazhong City, Sichuan Province on January 19, and met with relatives and friends at his local home on January 24. Someone was later diagnosed as new Confirmed pneumonia of coronavirus infection. The patient returned to his home in Binhai New District on January 30. He developed fever on February 4 and accompanied his colleague to take a taxi to Tianjin Fifth Central Hospital for a fever clinic on February 5 and was consulted by experts in the hospital to diagnose a suspected case. The nucleic acid test result was positive on February 6, and was diagnosed by the Tianjin expert group as the 79th case in Tianjin, which was a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and the two close contacts identified are under medical observation in isolation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been disinfected.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 7, 2020
