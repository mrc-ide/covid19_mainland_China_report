Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on February 4, 2020

Date: 2020-02-04 12:20
source: Commission office

From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 3, 2020, Tianjin reported 12 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 0 new critical cases, 0 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, new No additional cases were discharged. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases in each district, there were 1 case in Heping District, 6 cases in Baodi District, 1 case in Nankai District, 1 case in Ninghe District, and 1 case in Xiqing District; 2 cases came from outside Tianjin.

As of 24:00 on February 3, Tianjin has reported a total of 60 confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection, 3 severe cases, 15 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 1 discharged case. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 11 cases in Baodi District, 9 cases in Hedong District, 9 cases in Hebei District, 5 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 3 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Nankai District, 3 cases in Dongli District, and 3 cases in Xiqing District. There were 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Binhai New District, and 6 cases of Laijin in the field. Among the discharged patients, 1 case was in Hongqiao District.

At present, 410 close contacts have been tracked, 23 have been released from medical observation, and 369 are still receiving medical observation.
