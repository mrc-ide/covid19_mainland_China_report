As of 18:00 on February 18, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 128 confirmed cases.

The 128th patient, male, 67 years old, lived in Yuhua Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. On January 24, she went to Baodi Department Store to shop, her husband was the 124th confirmed case in Tianjin. The patient developed symptoms such as nasal congestion, runny nose, and bloating on February 4 and relieved after taking the medicine on her own; symptoms of pharyngeal discomfort, cough, and sputum appeared on the 10th to 13th without medical attention; fever symptoms on the 15th were transferred to The isolation ward of the People's Hospital of Baodi District; the first nucleic acid test result was negative on the 16th; the second nucleic acid test result was positive on the 18th. The city expert group was diagnosed as the 128th case in Tianjin, and the classification was ordinary. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and the identified close contacts have been isolated for medical observation.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 128 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 69 males and 59 females.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 18, 2020
