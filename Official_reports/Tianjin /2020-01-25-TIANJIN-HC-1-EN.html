Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on January 25, 2020

Date: 2020-01-25 09:19
source: Commission office

At 04:00 on January 24, 2020, Tianjin reported 3 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, 3 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 0 new discharged cases.

As of 24 o'clock on January 24, Tianjin has reported a total of 8 confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection, 6 severe cases, 0 deaths, and 0 discharged patients.

At present, 85 close contacts have been traced, 1 has been released from medical observation, and 84 people are still receiving medical observation.
