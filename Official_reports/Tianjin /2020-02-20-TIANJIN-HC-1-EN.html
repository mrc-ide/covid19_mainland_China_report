From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 19, 2020, Tianjin reported 2 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new critical cases, 0 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, and new discharges. 6 cases. among them:

Two new cases were confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 19, Tianjin has reported a total of 130 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 2 critical cases, 21 severe cases, 3 dead cases, and 54 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 55 cases in Baodi District, 15 cases in Hedong District, 12 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Nankai District, 6 cases in Beichen District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, and 4 cases in Dongli District. 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Laijin District.

Among the discharged patients, there were 15 cases in Baodi District, 9 in Hebei District, 7 in Hedong District, 4 in Heping District, 4 in Xiqing District, 2 in Hexi District, 2 in Hongqiao District, 2 in Dongli District, and 1 in Nankai District. There were 1 case in Beichen District, 1 case in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Binhai New District, and 4 cases outside Tianjin.

At present, 1841 close contacts have been traced, 1,055 medical observations have been lifted, and 743 are still receiving medical observations.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 20, 2020
