As of 6:00 on February 4, 6 new cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin and 66 cases were confirmed

Date: 2020-02-04 12:12
source: Commission office

As of 6:00 on February 4, six new cases of coronavirus pneumonia were newly confirmed in Tianjin, and a total of 66 cases were confirmed.

The 61st patient, a 61-year-old male, lived in Hedong District, Tianjin, and was the family member of the 45th confirmed case. He drove to Xiaogan City, Hubei Province, and returned to Tianjin on January 22. The patient developed fever and sputum on January 31 and was taken by his son to the Third Central Hospital for symptomatic treatment. The patient has been in isolation for medical treatment at the hospital since his family's diagnosis was confirmed on February 1. On February 2nd, the symptoms of discomfort worsened than before. At 23:00 on February 3rd, the city expert group confirmed the 61st case in our city, which was a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, the three close contacts identified have been observed in isolation, and the other close contacts are being followed up and investigated, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

The 62nd patient, a 55-year-old male, lives in Hebei District, Tianjin, and is an employee of the Tianjin Motor Bus Section. He has had close contact with several confirmed employees in the same unit. The patient developed fever on January 30, was transferred to the Fourth Central Hospital of the City on February 1, was tested positive for the nucleic acid on February 2, and was confirmed by the city's expert group at 23:00 on February 3 as the 62nd case in our city. For ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. Since being judged as a close contact on January 24, the patient has been isolated at home. At present, no close contact has been determined and his home has been terminally disinfected.

The 63rd patient, male, 66 years old, lived in Heping District, Tianjin, and was the family member of the 27th confirmed case. The patient had a previous history of coronary heart disease and atrial fibrillation. She developed fever and chest tightness on February 1 and was referred to the 983 hospital and the Peace Hospital for treatment. On February 3, at 23 o'clock, the city's expert team confirmed the 63th case in our city. Severe type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. The patient has been isolated for observation since the family was diagnosed on January 28. Currently, a close contact has been identified for medical observation, and other close contacts are continuing to be traced, and their homes are disinfected at the end.

The 64th patient, male, 31 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin, and drove the 51st confirmed case to the Baodi District Department Store (not in the building). The patient developed fever on February 1 and visited the Baodi Dazhong Hospital and the Baodi District People's Hospital on February 2. At 1 o'clock on February 4, the city expert group confirmed the 64th case in our city, which is a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, five close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts are continuing to be tracked down, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

The 65th patient, female, 40 years old, lives in Hexi District, Tianjin, and has no history of tourism and work in Wuhan. He went to Dalian for a business trip from January 14 to 17. (11 participants, no Hubei personnel, no People who have symptoms such as fever). The patient developed symptoms and symptomatic treatment on January 28 after experiencing fever and sore limbs; he was treated at Xiqing Hospital on January 29, and the discomfort was relieved after taking the medicine; chest pain occurred on January 31, and he was treated at the General Hospital of Medical University. He was given anti-infective treatment. He had fever again on February 3, and had a second visit to the General Hospital of Medical University. He was later diagnosed as the 65th case of the city by the expert team of the city, and it was of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, four close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts are continuing to be tracked down, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

The 66th patient, female, 39 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin City, and went to the Baodi District Department Store for shopping twice on January 20th and 23rd. The patient developed fatigue, dizziness, and nausea on January 31. He was treated symptomatically in Dakoutun Hospital, Baodi District, and in the People's Hospital of Baodi District on February 2. He was diagnosed by the city's expert group as the 66th patient on February 4. Cases are of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, 6 close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts are being tracked and checked, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 66 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, including 37 males and 29 females; 1 was discharged and 65 were hospitalized, including 3 critically ill, 17 severe, and 45 common. 185 suspected cases. A total of 410 close contacts were investigated, of which 19 have been diagnosed, 23 have been released from medical observation, and 368 are still receiving medical observation.
