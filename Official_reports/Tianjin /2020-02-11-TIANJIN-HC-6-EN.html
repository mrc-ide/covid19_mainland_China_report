As of 20:00 on February 11, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 105 confirmed cases.

The 105th patient, male, 53 years old, was the brother of the 103rd confirmed case and lived in Xincun Street, Guoyuan, Beichen District, Tianjin. He had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 11, and was confirmed as the 105th case in Tianjin by the city expert group, which is a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observation has been conducted on the nine close contacts who have been identified. Other close contacts are under investigation and their homes have been disinfected.

As of now, Tianjin has reported 105 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 58 males and 47 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 11, 2020
