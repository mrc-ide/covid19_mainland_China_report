On February 18, 2020, from 04:00 to 24:00, Tianjin reported 3 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new critical cases, 0 new severe cases, 0 new death cases, and new discharges. 2 cases. among them:

Three new cases were confirmed in Baodi District.

As of 24:00 on February 18, Tianjin has reported a total of 128 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 4 severe cases, 22 severe cases, 3 death cases, and 48 discharged cases. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 53 cases in Baodi District, 15 cases in Hedong District, 12 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Nankai District, 6 cases in Beichen District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, and 4 cases in Dongli District. 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Laijin District.

Among the discharged patients, there were 12 cases in Baodi District, 8 cases in Hebei District, 7 cases in Hedong District, 4 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Xiqing District, 2 cases in Hexi District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 1 case in Nankai District, and 1 in Dongli District. There were 1 case in Beichen District, 1 case in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Binhai New District, and 3 cases outside Tianjin.

At present, 1,739 close contacts have been traced, 975 people have been released from medical observation, and 722 people are still receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 19, 2020
