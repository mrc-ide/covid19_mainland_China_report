As of 6:00 on February 17, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 125 confirmed cases.

The 125th patient, male, 9 years old, was a close contact of the 93th confirmed case in our city, the son of the 119th and 121st confirmed cases, and now lives in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin City, without Wuhan travel History, the history of shopping at Wubaocheng Department Store. The patient developed fever on February 9; he went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on the 10th, and was transferred to an isolation point for isolation for medical observation. On the 16th, he developed cough symptoms and went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic. The nucleic acid test result was positive on the 17th. The city's expert group confirmed the 125th case in our city, and the classification was ordinary. At present, she has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observations have been made on the identified close contacts, and other close contacts are being investigated.

Up to now, the city has reported a total of 125 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 67 males and 58 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 17, 2020
