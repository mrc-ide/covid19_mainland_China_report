From 23:00 on February 11 to 6:00 on February 12, one new case of new coronary pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 107 confirmed cases.

The 107th patient, male, 49 years old, was the husband of the 93rd confirmed case in our city. He lived in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin, and had no travel or work history in Wuhan. The patient went to the Baodi District People's Hospital with a wife and parents on February 9 for a fever clinic and was admitted to an isolation ward. The nucleic acid test result was positive on the 11th. The expert group of the city confirmed the 107th case in our city, which is a common type. At present, he has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital of the City, and the identified close contacts will be observed in isolation. Other close contacts are being investigated. Zh

As of now, Tianjin has reported a total of 107 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 59 males and 48 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
