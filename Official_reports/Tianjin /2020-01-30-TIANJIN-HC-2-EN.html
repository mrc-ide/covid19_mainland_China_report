As of 14:00 on January 30, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection has been added to the number of confirmed cases in Tianjin

Date: 2020-01-30 19:37
source: Commission office

As of 14:00 on January 30, Tianjin has newly added one confirmed case of pneumonia infected by a new coronavirus, and the number of confirmed cases has increased to 30.

The 30th patient, female, 64 years old, lived in Hongqiao District, Tianjin. The patient traveled with her family to Xiaogan, Hubei on January 19, and returned to Tianjin with her family on the 23rd. She developed respiratory symptoms on the 28th and went to Hongqiao Hospital for treatment. He developed fever on the 29th and went to the Municipal People's Hospital for treatment. The 30th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city expert group, which is a common case. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment.

According to the epidemiological investigation, there are 4 cases of the patient's main close contacts, all of whom have been taken home observation for medical observation. The Hongqiao District Center for Disease Control and Prevention has terminally disinfected its dwelling and tracked and investigated other close contacts.

Up to now, 30 cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection have been found in Tianjin, including 23 males and 7 females; 13 severe cases and 17 common cases; and no critical and death cases.





Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

January 30, 2020
