At 00:00 on February 8, 2020, Tianjin reported 7 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 1 new critically ill case, 0 new severe case, 0 new death case, and new Two more cases were discharged. among them:

The first confirmed case was reported in Wuqing District. Six other cases were confirmed in Baodi District from other districts.

As of 24:00 on February 8th, Tianjin has reported a total of 88 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 6 critical cases, 29 severe cases, 1 death case, and 4 discharged cases. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 29 cases in Baodi District, 10 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 4 cases in Dongli District, 4 cases in Xiqing District, and 3 cases in Nankai District. Cases, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Beichen District, 1 case in Wuqing District, and 6 cases from outside Tianjin; among the discharged patients, 1 case was in Hedong District, 1 case was in Hongqiao District, There was 1 case in Heping District and 1 case in Hebei District.

At present, 687 close contacts have been traced, 115 medical observations have been lifted, and 547 are still receiving medical observations.
