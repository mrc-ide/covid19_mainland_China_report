Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on January 28, 2020

Date: 2020-01-28 09:06
source: Commission office

From January 24th to January 24th, 2020, Tianjin reported 9 new confirmed cases of pneumonia with new coronavirus infection, 6 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 0 new discharged cases. among them:


Hongqiao District and Hexi District are the first confirmed cases reported. Among the newly confirmed cases in each district, there were 2 cases in Hebei District, 3 in Hedong District, 1 in Hongqiao District, and 3 in Hexi District.

As of 24:00 on January 27, Tianjin has reported a total of 23 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 17 severe cases, 0 deaths, and 0 discharged patients. among them:

Of the confirmed cases, 7 were in Hebei District, 5 in Hedong District, 2 in Nankai District, 1 in Xiqing District, 1 in Heping District, 1 in Binhai New District, 1 in Hongqiao District, 3 in Hexi District, and 2 in Laijin. example.

At present, 191 close contacts have been traced, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 184 are still receiving medical observation.
