As of 6:00 on February 15, one new case of new coronavirus pneumonia was confirmed in Tianjin, with a total of 121 confirmed cases.

The 121st patient, male, 31 years old, now lives in Lintingkou Town, Baodi District. He has no history of travel and work in Wuhan, and no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi District. He is the husband of the 119th confirmed case. The patient was transferred to an isolation point on February 10 for isolation medical observation. On the 12th, he went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment. The nucleic acid test result was positive on the 15th and was confirmed by the city's expert group as Tianjin. The 121st confirmed case in the city was of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital and has no close contacts.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 121 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 65 males and 56 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 15, 2020
