As of 6:00 on February 1, 2 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added to the number of confirmed cases to 34

Date: 2020-02-01 13:30
source: Commission office
Views:

As of 6:00 on February 1, two new cases of pneumonia diagnosed with new coronavirus infection have been added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases has increased to 34. There were 154 suspected cases.


The 33rd patient, female, 48 years old, lived in Hebei District, Tianjin, and was a relative of the 17th confirmed case. He was separated from home on January 26. Fever occurred on January 30, and he was diagnosed in the Fourth Central Hospital of the City on January 31. The city expert group confirmed the 33rd case in Tianjin and it was an ordinary case. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable.

The 34th patient, female, 52 years old, lived in Baodi District. Patients are sales staff and have more contacts. A colleague of this patient went to Beijing to purchase from 16th to 17th. He was in close contact with a high fever customer in a jewelry mall in Beijing. He developed fever on the 25th and had a positive nucleic acid test on January 31, waiting for the expert to confirm the diagnosis. The patient developed fever on January 27. He was admitted to Baodi Seaside Hospital and Baodi District People's Hospital. The 34th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city's expert team and was a severe case. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. The Center for Disease Control and Prevention of Baodi District is conducting a close investigation of its close contacts and terminal disinfection of the epidemic point.

As of now, Tianjin has reported 34 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 24 males and 10 females; 15 severe cases and 19 common cases. A total of 232 close contacts were investigated, of which 8 have been diagnosed, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 222 are still receiving medical observation.


Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
