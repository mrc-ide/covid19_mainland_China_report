From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 24, 2020, Tianjin reported 0 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new critical cases, 0 new severe cases, 0 death cases, and new cures. There were 6 discharged patients.

As of 24:00 on February 24, Tianjin had reported a total of 135 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 87 discharged patients, and 3 dead. There are 45 confirmed cases (2 critical cases and 11 severe cases) in hospital. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 60 cases in Baodi District, 15 cases in Hedong District, 12 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 6 cases in Nankai District, 6 cases in Beichen District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, and 4 cases in Dongli District. 4 cases in Xiqing District, 3 cases in Binhai New District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Laijin District.

Among the discharged patients, there were 30 cases in Baodi District, 11 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 5 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 4 cases in Xiqing District, 4 cases in Dongli District, 3 cases in Beichen District, and 3 cases in Binhai New District. There were 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Nankai District, 2 cases in Wuqing District, 1 case in Jinnan District, 1 case in Ninghe District, and 5 cases outside Tianjin.

At present, 2,203 close contacts have been traced, 1510 people have been released from medical observation, and 647 people are still receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 25, 2020
