As of 6:00 on February 3, 6 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection have been added to 54 in Tianjin.

Date: 2020-02-03 10:59
source: Commission office
Views:

As of 6:00 on February 3, Tianjin had six new confirmed cases of pneumonia infected with a new coronavirus, and the number of confirmed cases increased to 54.

The 49th patient, female, 42 years old, lived in Heping District, Tianjin. The patient worked in Wuhan from January 16th to 22nd, returned to Tianjin on January 22nd, developed fever on January 31st, and treated himself symptomatically. He was diagnosed in the General Hospital of Medical University on February 2 and was diagnosed as the 49th case in Tianjin by the expert group of the city. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. Isolation medical observations have been carried out on four of her close contacts, follow-up investigations of other close contacts have been continued, and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

The 50th patient, female, 66 years old, lived in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin. On the afternoon of January 22, she went to the clothing area on the first floor of the department store in Baodi District for shopping. He developed fever on February 2 and was diagnosed in the People's Hospital of Baodi District, Tianjin. On February 3, the 50th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the expert team of the city, which was a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, four close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts are continuing to be tracked down, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

The 51st patient, female, 36 years old, lived in Dazhongzhuang Town, Baodi District, and went to the department store in Baodi District on January 20. The patient developed nasal congestion, runny nose, and cough on January 27. She had symptomatic treatment on her own. She developed fever on January 30. She was seen at Dazhong Hospital of Baodi District, Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital of Baodi District, and People's Hospital of Baodi District on February 1. The Nikkei expert group was diagnosed as the 51st case in Tianjin and was of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, five close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts are continuing to be tracked down, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

The 52nd patient, female, 37 years old, living in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, was the 50th relative of the confirmed case and had close contact. The patient developed fever and sore body on January 30, and treated symptomatically. He developed sputum and fever on the morning of January 31. He was seen at the People's Hospital of Baodi District on January 31 and February 1, respectively. The group was diagnosed as the 52nd case in Tianjin and was of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, four close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts will continue to be tracked down, and their homes will be disinfected at the end.

The 53 patient, female, 46 years old, worked in the men's wear section of the department store in Baodi District from January 1 to 23. The patient developed fever and cough on January 31 and was admitted to the Baodi District Beach Hospital. On February 1, he was admitted to the Tianjin Baodi District People's Hospital. On February 3, the city expert group confirmed the 53rd case in Tianjin, which was a common type. . He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. A total of 3 close contacts have been identified, and other close contacts are continuing to be tracked down and their homes are being disinfected.

The 54th patient, male, 43 years old, was from Wuhan. He lived in Jianghan District, Wuhan City, and was the family member of the 14th and 25th confirmed cases in Tianjin. He was a close contact. The patient flew from Malaysia to Singapore Airport on OD688 from Malaysia Airlines on January 25. Due to the discovery of a fever patient from Wuhan by a passenger on the same flight, he was transferred to a centralized isolation point in Dongli District for medical observation. The patient developed fever on January 27, and was transferred to the People's Hospital of Wuqing District. The fever occurred in the early morning of January 28, and the nucleic acid test was negative. The nucleic acid test was positive in the early morning of February 3, and was confirmed by the municipal expert group on February 3. The 54th case in Tianjin was of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported 54 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 30 males and 24 females; 3 critically ill, 15 severe, 35 common, and 1 discharged; 141 suspected cases. A total of 356 close contacts were investigated, of which 17 have been diagnosed, 15 have been released from medical observation, and 324 are receiving medical observation.
