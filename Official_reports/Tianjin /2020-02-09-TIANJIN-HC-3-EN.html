From February 6 to 18:00 on February 9, one new confirmed case of pneumonia caused by a new coronavirus infection was reported in Tianjin, with a total of 91 confirmed cases.

The 91st patient, male, 21 years old, living in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, was the son of the 82nd confirmed case, and had no history of shopping or staying in the department store in Baodi District. The patient's nucleic acid test result was positive on February 9, and was diagnosed by the city expert group as the 91st case in Tianjin. It is a common type and has now been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment. Isolation medical observation of the two close contacts who have been identified, Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been terminally disinfected.

As of now, Tianjin has reported 91 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 51 males and 40 females.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 9, 2020
