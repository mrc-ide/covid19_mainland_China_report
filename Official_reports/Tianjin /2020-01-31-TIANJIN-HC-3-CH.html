As of 6:00 on January 31, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new type of coronavirus infection in Tianjin has increased to 32

Date: 2020-01-31 10:39
source: Commission office

As of 6:00 on January 31, one new case of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection has been added to Tianjin, and the number of confirmed cases has increased to 32.

The 32nd patient, male, 28 years old, lived in Ninghe District, Tianjin, and had no history of travel or work in Wuhan. The patient had visited Zhanjiang, Nanchang, Beijing and other places before January 20, and returned to Ninghe on the 20th. On the 21st to 23rd, I had dinners and shopping with relatives and friends several times. On January 23, the patient developed fever and other symptoms. He was seen at the Ninghe District Hospital on the 26th. He returned home because of no blood abnormalities and chest radiographs. He continued to infusion at the community health station. On January 30, due to the exacerbation of the disease, he returned to the Ninghe District Hospital for a fever clinic and was confirmed by the city's expert group as the 32nd case in Tianjin, a severe case. The patient has been transferred to Haihe Hospital. The patient's parents and 6 people at the community health station have been isolated for medical observation, and the disease control department is tracking and examining other close contacts according to its movement trajectory.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 32 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 24 males and 8 females; 14 severe cases, 18 general cases, and 155 suspected cases. A total of 232 close contacts were investigated, of which 7 have been diagnosed, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 223 are receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

January 31, 2020
