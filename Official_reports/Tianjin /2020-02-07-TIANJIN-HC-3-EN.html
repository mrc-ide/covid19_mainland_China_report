As of 6:00 on February 7, two new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were added in Tianjin, with a total of 81 confirmed cases.

The 80th patient, female, 51 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin City. On January 23, she went to the women's clothing area on the first floor and the second floor of the department store in Baodi District to shop. The patient was conscious of fatigue and muscle soreness on February 2 and his symptoms were not significantly relieved after taking the medicine on his own. He had fever on February 4 and improved after taking the medicine on his own. On February 5th, the patient's temperature rose again, and accompanied by his family, he drove to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic. The nucleic acid test result was positive on February 7, and the 80th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city expert group, which was a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and the two close contacts identified are under medical observation in isolation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been disinfected.

The 81st patient, a 65-year-old male, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin City. He had a meal with a neighbor five times from January 20 to 31, and was in close contact with the 72 confirmed cases. The patient felt uncomfortable on January 31, relieved by taking the medicine on her own, developed fever on February 4, and drove to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on February 5. The nucleic acid test result was positive on February 7, and the 81st case in Tianjin was confirmed by the city expert group, which was a common type. At present, he has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and the two close contacts identified are under medical observation in isolation. Other close contacts are being investigated and their homes have been disinfected.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 81 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 7, 2020
