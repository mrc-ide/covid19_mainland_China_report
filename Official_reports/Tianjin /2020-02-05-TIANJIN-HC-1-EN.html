From 04:00 to 24:00 on February 4, 2020, Tianjin reported 4 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 1 new critically ill case, 1 new severe case, 0 new death case, new One more case was discharged. among them:

The first confirmed case was reported in Jinnan District. Two new cases were confirmed in other districts in Baodi District and one in Hexi District.

As of 24:00 on February 4th, Tianjin has reported a total of 67 confirmed cases of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection, 4 severe cases, 17 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 2 discharged cases. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 13 cases in Baodi District, 10 cases in Hedong District, 10 cases in Hebei District, 6 cases in Heping District, 4 cases in Ninghe District, 4 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Nankai District, 3 cases in Dongli District, and 3 cases in Xiqing District. There were 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Binhai New District, 1 case in Jinnan District, and 6 cases in Tianjin outside the hospital. Among the discharged patients, 1 case was in Hongqiao District and 1 case was in Hedong District.

At present, 481 close contacts have been tracked, 39 have been released from medical observation, and 422 are still receiving medical observation.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee
