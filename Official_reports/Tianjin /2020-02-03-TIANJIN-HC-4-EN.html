On February 3, 2020, there were 4 new cases of pneumonia diagnosed with new coronavirus infection in Tianjin with a total of 60 confirmed cases

Date: 2020-02-03 23:54
source: Commission office

As of 18:00 on February 3, 4 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection have been added in Tianjin, with a total of 60 confirmed cases.

The 57th patient, male, 29 years old, lived in Niudaokou Town, Baodi District, Tianjin City. On the afternoon of January 23, he went to the shoes and clothing area of the department store in Baodi District to shop without wearing a mask. The patient developed fever on February 1 and was treated symptomatically on his own. He was seen at the People's Hospital of Baodi District on February 2 and was diagnosed by the city's expert group as the 57th case in Tianjin on February 3, which was a common type. He has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, four close contacts have been determined, and other close contacts are continuing to be tracked down, and their homes are being disinfected at the end.

The 58th patient, female, 69 years old, lived in Baodi District, Tianjin. On January 20, she went shopping in the clothing area of the Department Store in Baodi District without wearing a mask. The patient developed fever on January 26 and treated symptomatically on his own. He developed fatigue and stomach pain on February 2. He was diagnosed in Baodi District Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital and Baodi District People's Hospital. On February 3, he was diagnosed by the expert group as the 58th case in Tianjin. , For ordinary type. He has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, three close contacts have been determined, and follow-up investigations of other close contacts will be continued, and their homes will be terminally disinfected.

The 59th patient, male, 9 years old, was from Wuhan. He lived in Jianghan District, Wuhan City, and was a close relative of the 14th and 25th confirmed cases. On January 25, Malaysia Airlines flew OD688 from Sabah, Malaysia to Tianjin Airport. Passengers on the same plane were found to be feverish patients of Wuhan nationality, and were transferred to a centralized isolation point in Dongli District for medical observation. The patient developed cough and fever in the early morning on February 2 and was admitted to Dongli Hospital. On February 3, the 59th case of Tianjin was diagnosed by the expert group of the city, which was a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, it is determined that one of his close contacts has been started, and centralized medical observation has been initiated.

The 60th patient, male, 61 years old, lived in Yangliuqing Town, Tianjin, and was the family member of the first confirmed case. The patient traveled to Wuhan with his wife on January 5 and developed fever on January 25. The nucleic acid test was negative twice. Fever recurred on January 31, and the third and fourth nucleic acid tests were negative from February 1 to 2, and two chest radiographs showed disease progression, and CT showed bilateral lung viral pneumonia. Due to a high degree of suspicion, the nucleic acid test was positive again on February 3, and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 60th case in Tianjin, which was a common type. He has been transferred to the Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. The patient's family and the first patient have been kept in isolation at Xiqing Hospital for observation as a close contact.

As of now, Tianjin has reported a total of 60 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 33 males and 27 females; 3 critically ill, 15 severe, 41 normal, 1 discharged, and no deaths.

Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 3, 2020
