From 22:00 on February 4 to 6:00 on February 5, 2 new cases of coronavirus pneumonia were newly confirmed in Tianjin, and a total of 69 cases were confirmed.

Case 68, female, 64 years old, lives in Baodi District, Tianjin. On the afternoon of January 20, she went shopping in the clothing area on the first floor of the Department Store in Baodi District without wearing a mask. The patient developed fever and diarrhea from January 29th to 30th. She took medication on her own, and her symptoms were not relieved. On February 3rd, she was accompanied by her family to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever diagnosis, and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 68th in Tianjin One case is a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, the three close contacts who have been judged have undergone isolated medical observation, and other close contacts have continued to be tracked and investigated, and their homes have been terminally disinfected.
The 69th patient, female, 76 years old, lives in Baodi District, Tianjin. This patient has not been to the Baodi Department Store and is a relative of the 53rd case. The patient developed fever on February 3, and was sent to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever diagnosis on February 4. Later, the city expert group was diagnosed as the 69th case in Tianjin, which was of ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and his vital signs are stable. At present, the two close contacts who have been judged have undergone isolated medical observation, and other close contacts have been followed up for investigation, and their homes have been terminally disinfected.
Up to now, a total of Tianjin reported confirmed cases of novel coronavirus pneumonia, 69 cases of which 37 cases of male, female 32 cases; 4 cases of severe, heavy 19 cases, 43 cases of common type, cured and discharged two cases, one case of death, woman , 66 years old, January 22 went to the department store shopping Baodi, February 2, patients with fever, cough to the hospital, February 3 into the Haihe hospital treatment. The patient had a history of more than 30 years of type 2 diabetes, 9 years of hypertension, 2 years of coronary bypass surgery , and died in the early morning of February 5 after rescue failure. The cause of death was initially judged: acute left heart failure, acute coronary syndrome.
As of now, there are 198 suspected cases. A total of 523 close contacts were investigated, of which 21 were diagnosed, 45 were released from medical observation, and 457 were receiving medical observation.
