Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tianjin on February 1, 2020

Date: 2020-02-01 08:58
source: Commission office
Views:

At 04:00 on January 31, 2020, Tianjin reported one new confirmed case of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, one new severe case, one new death case, and one new discharged case. among them:

Ninghe District reported the first confirmed case. No new cases were confirmed in other districts.

As of 14:00 on January 31, Tianjin has reported a total of 32 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 14 severe cases, 0 deaths, and 0 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 8 cases in Hebei District, 5 cases in Hedong District, 3 cases in Hexi District, 3 cases in Heping District, 2 cases in Nankai District, 2 cases in Xiqing District, 2 cases in Hongqiao District, 2 cases in Binhai New District, and 1 in Ninghe District. Cases, 4 cases came from outside Tianjin.

At present, 232 close contacts have been traced, 2 have been released from medical observation, and 223 are still receiving medical observation.




Tianjin Municipal Health Committee

February 1, 2020
