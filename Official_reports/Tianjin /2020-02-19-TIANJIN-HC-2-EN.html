From 6:00 on February 19 to 18:00 on February 19, 2 new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Tianjin, and a total of 130 confirmed cases.

The 129th case, male, 90 years old, now lives in Baoping Street, Baodi District, Tianjin, without a history of travel and work in Wuhan. It is the 72nd, 85th (previously visited Baodi Department Store) and 86th in our city Close contacts of confirmed cases. Since February 6, the patient has been treated as a close contact at a centralized isolation point without cough, sputum, fever and other symptoms. The nucleic acid test result was positive on the 19th and was confirmed by the city expert group as the 129th case in Tianjin. For ordinary type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and the epidemiological investigation is ongoing.

The 130th patient, male, 38 years old, now lives in Dazhongzhuang Town, Baodi District, Tianjin City, without a history of travel and work in Wuhan. It is the close contact of the 64th case in our city (the 51st confirmed case was sent to Baodi Department Store) and the 99th confirmed case (in the Baodi Department Store). The patient went to the Baodi District People's Hospital for a fever clinic on February 6 and was admitted to the isolation ward for treatment. On the 19th, the nucleic acid test result was positive. The 130th case of Tianjin was confirmed by the city expert group, which was a common type. He has been transferred to Haihe Hospital for treatment, and the epidemiological investigation is ongoing.

Up to now, Tianjin has reported a total of 130 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, including 71 males and 59 females.
