Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Guangdong Province on February 1, 2020

Time: 2020-02-01 09:06:36 source: this website

As of 24:00 on January 31, the province has reported a total of 520 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. There were 127 new confirmed cases in the province on the 31st. Among the newly confirmed cases, 3 cases were reported in Chaozhou for the first time; 60 cases were in Shenzhen, 31 in Guangzhou, 12 in Zhuhai, 6 in Foshan, 5 in Dongguan, 3 in Huizhou, 2 in Shantou, and 2 in Jiangmen. There were 2 cases in Zhanjiang City and 1 in Shanwei City. 1965 close contacts are under medical observation.

Of the total confirmed cases reported, there were 170 cases in Shenzhen, 137 in Guangzhou, 38 in Foshan, 38 in Zhuhai, 21 in Dongguan, 20 in Huizhou, 18 in Zhongshan, 14 in Shantou, and 13 in Zhanjiang. 10 cases in Yangjiang City, 6 cases in Qingyuan City, 6 cases in Jieyang City, 6 cases in Zhaoqing City, 5 cases in Meizhou City, 4 cases in Shanwei City, 4 cases in Shaoguan City, 3 cases in Chaozhou City, 3 cases in Maoming City, and 3 cases in Jiangmen City 1 case in Heyuan City. There were 254 males and 266 females, aged between 10 months and 86 years.
