Pneumonia situation of new coronavirus infection in Guangdong Province at 12:00 on February 3, 2020

Time: 2020-02-03 16:11:49 source: this website

As of 12:00 on February 3, the province had reported a total of 725 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. At 02:00 on the 3rd, 42 new cases were confirmed in the province. Among the newly confirmed cases, 19 were in Shenzhen, 11 in Guangzhou, 4 in Zhuhai, 3 in Zhaoqing, 2 in Foshan, and 1 in Shantou. 1 case in Qingyuan City and 1 case in Meizhou City. There were 195 suspected cases. There are 2,680 close contacts who are under medical observation.

Among the cumulative confirmed cases, 245 cases were reported in Shenzhen, 200 in Guangzhou, 55 in Zhuhai, 45 in Foshan, 31 in Dongguan, 28 in Huizhou, 25 in Zhongshan, 18 in Shantou, and 14 in Zhanjiang. 10 cases in Zhaoqing City, 10 cases in Yangjiang City, 8 cases in Meizhou City, 7 cases in Qingyuan City, 6 cases in Jieyang City, 5 cases in Shaoguan City, 5 cases in Shanwei City, 4 cases in Maoming City, 4 cases in Jiangmen City, and 4 cases in Chaozhou City. 1 case in Heyuan City. There were 352 males and 373 females, aged between 10 months and 86 years.

As of 12:00 on February 3, of the 711 confirmed cases in the hospital, 22 were critically ill, 58 were severe, and 631 were general. A total of 14 patients were discharged. There were no deaths.
