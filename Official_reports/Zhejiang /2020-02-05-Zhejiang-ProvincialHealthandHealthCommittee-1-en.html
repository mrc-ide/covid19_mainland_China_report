Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Zhejiang Province on February 5, 2020

Release date: 2020-02-05 09:11 Information source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on February 4, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 66 newly diagnosed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection and 15 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, 9 were in Hangzhou, 18 in Ningbo, 24 in Wenzhou, 4 in Jiaxing, 4 in Jinhua, and 7 in Taizhou. Among the newly added cases, 1 was in Hangzhou and 3 were in Ningbo. Cases, 11 cases in Wenzhou.

As of 24:00 on February 4, a total of 895 confirmed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection had been reported in Zhejiang Province. There were 65 severe cases (including 16 critical cases) and a total of 63 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 141 cases in Hangzhou, 120 in Ningbo, 364 in Wenzhou, 9 in Huzhou, 27 in Jiaxing, 30 in Shaoxing, 44 in Jinhua, 15 in Luzhou, 7 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 122 cases in Lishui City and 16 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, there were 12 in Hangzhou, 7 in Ningbo, 22 in Wenzhou, 2 in Jiaxing, 7 in Shaoxing, 3 in Jinhua, 2 in Luzhou, and Zhoushan. 1 case, 7 cases in Taizhou City, 2 cases in Lishui City; among the discharged patients, there were 7 cases in Hangzhou, 4 cases in Ningbo, 28 cases in Wenzhou, 1 case in Huzhou, 3 cases in Shaoxing, 6 cases in Jinhua, and 2 in Zhoushan. There were 11 cases in Taizhou and 1 in Lishui.

At present, a total of 16,360 close contacts have been tracked in the province, 3,955 medical observations have been lifted, and 11,886 people are receiving medical observations.
