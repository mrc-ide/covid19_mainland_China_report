Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Zhejiang Province on February 6, 2020

Release date: 2020-02-06 09:10 Source of information: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on February 5, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 59 newly diagnosed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection and 18 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, 10 were in Hangzhou, 6 were in Ningbo, 32 were in Wenzhou, 3 were in Jiaxing, 3 were in Shaoxing, 3 were in Jinhua, and 2 were in Taizhou. Among the newly-discharged cases, 8 were in Hangzhou. There were 2 cases in Ningbo, 2 cases in Jinhua, 2 cases in Shengzhou, 1 case in Zhoushan, and 3 cases in Taizhou.

As of 24:00 on February 5th, Zhejiang Province had reported a total of 954 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 66 cases of severe cases (including 15 critical cases), and a total of 81 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 151 cases in Hangzhou, 126 in Ningbo, 396 in Wenzhou, 9 in Huzhou, 30 in Jiaxing, 33 in Shaoxing, 47 in Jinhua, 15 in Shengzhou, 7 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 124 cases in Lishui City and 16 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, there were 10 cases in Hangzhou, 7 cases in Ningbo, 24 cases in Wenzhou, 1 case in Huzhou, 2 cases in Jiaxing, 8 cases in Shaoxing, 3 cases in Jinhua, and Quzhou. 2 cases, 1 case in Zhoushan City, 7 cases in Taizhou City, 1 case in Lishui City; among the discharged patients, 15 cases were in Hangzhou City, 6 cases were in Ningbo City, 28 cases were in Wenzhou City, 1 case was in Huzhou City, 3 cases were in Shaoxing City, and 8 were in Jinhua City. There were 2 cases in Luzhou City, 3 cases in Zhoushan City, 14 cases in Taizhou City, and 1 case in Lishui City.

At present, the province has tracked a total of 20191 close contacts, 5,407 medical observations have been lifted, and 13,979 are still receiving medical observations.
