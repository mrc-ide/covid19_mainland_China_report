Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Zhejiang Province on February 14, 2020

Release date: 2020-02-14 09:03 Source of information: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on February 13, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 10 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia and 40 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, 3 were in Hangzhou, 6 in Wenzhou, and 1 in Shaoxing. Among the newly discharged cases, 6 were in Hangzhou, 4 were in Ningbo, 20 were in Wenzhou, 2 were in Jiaxing, and 1 was in Shaoxing. There were 1 case in Jinhua City, 3 cases in Shengzhou City, and 3 cases in Taizhou City.

As of 24:00 on February 13th, Zhejiang Province had reported 1155 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 81 cases of severe cases (30 critical cases), and 367 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 165 cases in Hangzhou, 153 in Ningbo, 496 in Wenzhou, 10 in Huzhou, 42 in Jiaxing, 42 in Shaoxing, 55 in Jinhua, 21 in Shengzhou, 10 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 144 cases in Lishui City and 17 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, there were 18 cases in Hangzhou, 11 cases in Ningbo, 23 cases in Wenzhou, 1 case in Huzhou, 5 cases in Jiaxing, 8 cases in Shaoxing, 5 cases in Jinhua, and Quzhou. 3 cases, 1 in Zhoushan City, 5 in Taizhou City, and 1 in Lishui City; among the discharged patients, 69 were in Hangzhou, 35 were in Ningbo, 140 were in Wenzhou, 4 were in Huzhou, 8 were in Jiaxing, and 16 were in Shaoxing. There were 24 cases in Jinhua City, 8 cases in Shengzhou City, 5 cases in Zhoushan City, 49 cases in Taizhou City, and 9 cases in Lishui City.

A total of 36,046 close contacts were tracked across the province. 1,236 medical observations were lifted that day, and 10,834 were still undergoing medical observations.
