Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Zhejiang Province on January 30, 2020

Release date: 2020-01-30 09:10 Source of information: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on January 29, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 132 new confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, 12 new severe cases, and 1 new discharged case. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 18 cases in Hangzhou, 21 cases in Ningbo, 58 cases in Wenzhou, 2 cases in Jiaxing, 7 cases in Jinhua, 2 cases in Shengzhou, and 24 cases in Taizhou. Among the newly added cases, 3 There were 3 cases in Wenzhou, 2 in Shaoxing, 2 in Jinhua, and 2 in Taizhou. Among the newly-discharged cases, 1 was in Lishui.

As of 24:00 on January 29, Zhejiang Province had reported a total of 428 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 54 severe cases, and 4 discharged patients. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 69 cases in Hangzhou, 41 cases in Ningbo, 172 cases in Wenzhou, 5 cases in Huzhou, 16 cases in Jiaxing, 19 cases in Shaoxing, 20 cases in Jinhua, 10 cases in Shengzhou, 6 cases in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 64 cases in Lishui City and 6 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, there were 17 cases in Hangzhou, 1 in Ningbo, 11 in Wenzhou, 5 in Jiaxing, 6 in Shaoxing, 3 in Jinhua, 1 in Luzhou, and Zhoushan. There were 1 case, 7 cases in Taizhou City, and 2 cases in Lishui City. Among the discharged patients, 3 cases were in Wenzhou City and 1 case in Lishui City.

At present, the province has traced a total of 8011 close contacts, 336 medical observations have been lifted, and 7629 people are receiving medical observations.
