Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Zhejiang Province on February 10, 2020

Release date: 2020-02-10 09:00 Information source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on February 9, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 29 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia and 28 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, 2 were in Hangzhou, 3 in Ningbo, 16 in Wenzhou, 2 in Jiaxing, 3 in Shaoxing, and 3 in Taizhou. Among the newly added cases, 12 were in Hangzhou and 2 were in Ningbo There were 8 cases in Wenzhou, 2 in Jiaxing, 2 in Jinhua, and 2 in Taizhou.

As of 24:00 on February 9th, Zhejiang Province had reported a total of 1092 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia (12 cases in Hangzhou City), 80 cases of severe cases (24 cases were critically ill), and 201 cases were discharged. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, 155 cases were in Hangzhou, 149 in Ningbo, 464 in Wenzhou, 10 in Huzhou, 40 in Jiaxing, 41 in Shaoxing, 53 in Jinhua, 15 in Shengzhou, 7 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 141 cases in Lishui City and 17 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, 15 cases were in Hangzhou, 9 in Ningbo, 28 in Wenzhou, 1 in Huzhou, 6 in Jiaxing, 5 in Shaoxing, 5 in Jinhua, and Zhoushan. 1 case, 9 cases in Taizhou, 1 case in Lishui; among the discharged patients, 41 cases were in Hangzhou, 15 cases were in Ningbo, 78 cases were in Wenzhou, 1 case was in Huzhou, 3 cases were in Jiaxing, 8 cases were in Shaoxing, and 15 were in Jinhua There were 3 cases in Luzhou City, 5 cases in Zhoushan City, 27 cases in Taizhou City, and 5 cases in Lishui City.

A total of 30,795 close contacts were tracked in the province. Of the 2,508 people who were released from medical observation that day, 13,293 were still receiving medical observation.
