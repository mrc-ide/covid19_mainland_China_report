Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Zhejiang Province on February 8, 2020

Release date: 2020-02-08 09:03 Source of information: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on February 7, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 42 newly confirmed cases of pneumonitis infected with new coronavirus and 29 new cases discharged. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, 6 were in Hangzhou, 5 in Ningbo, 17 in Wenzhou, 1 in Huzhou, 2 in Jiaxing, 2 in Shaoxing, 4 in Jinhua, 4 in Taizhou, and 1 in Lishui. ; Among the newly discharged cases, there were 6 cases in Hangzhou, 3 in Ningbo, 13 in Wenzhou, 2 in Shaoxing, 1 in Luzhou, 1 in Zhoushan, and 3 in Taizhou.

As of 24:00 on February 7, a total of 1,048 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to novel coronavirus infection were reported in Zhejiang Province. There were 78 severe cases (including 25 critical cases) and a total of 127 discharged from hospital. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 162 cases in Hangzhou, 141 in Ningbo, 438 in Wenzhou, 10 in Huzhou, 37 in Jiaxing, 36 in Shaoxing, 52 in Jinhua, 15 in Shengzhou, 7 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 133 cases in Lishui City and 17 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, there were 13 cases in Hangzhou, 8 cases in Ningbo, 31 cases in Wenzhou, 1 case in Huzhou, 4 cases in Jiaxing, 6 cases in Shaoxing, 5 cases in Jinhua, and Zhoushan. 1 case, 8 cases in Taizhou, 1 case in Lishui; among the discharged patients, 22 cases were in Hangzhou, 12 cases were in Ningbo, 49 cases were in Wenzhou, 1 case was in Huzhou, 1 case was in Jiaxing, 5 cases were in Shaoxing, and 9 were in Jinhua. There were 3 cases in Chuzhou City, 5 cases in Zhoushan City, 17 cases in Taizhou City, and 3 cases in Lishui City.

A total of 25,936 close contacts were tracked throughout the province. 1,864 medical observations were cancelled on the same day, and 14,526 people were receiving medical observations.
