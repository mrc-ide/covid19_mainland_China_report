Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Zhejiang Province on February 15, 2020

Release date: 2020-02-15 09:00 Source of information: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on February 14, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 7 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia and 42 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, 1 was in Hangzhou, 1 in Ningbo, 3 in Wenzhou, 1 in Jiaxing, and 1 in Taizhou. Among the newly added cases, 4 were in Hangzhou, 11 were in Ningbo, and 19 were in Wenzhou. There were 2 cases in Jiaxing City, 1 in Shaoxing City, 4 in Taizhou City, and 1 in Lishui City.

As of 24:00 on February 14th, Zhejiang Province had reported 1162 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 79 severe cases (29 critical cases), and 409 hospital discharges. among them:

Of the confirmed cases, 166 were in Hangzhou, 154 in Ningbo, 499 in Wenzhou, 10 in Huzhou, 43 in Jiaxing, 42 in Shaoxing, 55 in Jinhua, 21 in Luzhou, 10 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 145 cases in Lishui City and 17 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, there were 16 in Hangzhou, 10 in Ningbo, 26 in Wenzhou, 1 in Huzhou, 5 in Jiaxing, 7 in Shaoxing, 4 in Jinhua, and Luzhou. 3 cases, 1 case in Zhoushan City, 5 cases in Taizhou City, and 1 case in Lishui City; among the discharged patients, there were 73 cases in Hangzhou, 46 cases in Ningbo, 159 cases in Wenzhou, 4 cases in Huzhou, 10 cases in Jiaxing, and 17 in Shaoxing. There were 24 cases in Jinhua City, 8 cases in Shengzhou City, 5 cases in Zhoushan City, 53 cases in Taizhou City, and 10 cases in Lishui City.

A total of 37,017 close contacts were tracked across the province. 1,497 medical observations were lifted that day, and 10,183 were still undergoing medical observations.
