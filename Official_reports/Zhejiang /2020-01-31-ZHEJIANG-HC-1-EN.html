Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Zhejiang Province on January 31, 2020

Release date: 2020-01-31 09:05 Information source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Views:

At 04:00 on January 30, 2020, Zhejiang Province reported 109 new confirmed cases of pneumonia and 5 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 16 cases in Hangzhou, 5 in Ningbo, 55 in Wenzhou, 1 in Huzhou, 4 in Shaoxing, 7 in Jinhua, 2 in Shengzhou, 1 in Zhoushan, and 17 in Taizhou. 1 case in Lishui City; 4 cases were newly discharged in Wenzhou City and 1 case in Zhoushan City.

As of 24:00 on January 30, Zhejiang Province had reported 537 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 50 severe cases (including 10 critical cases), and 9 discharged from hospital. among them:

Among the confirmed cases, there were 85 cases in Hangzhou, 46 in Ningbo, 227 in Wenzhou, 6 in Huzhou, 16 in Jiaxing, 23 in Shaoxing, 27 in Jinhua, 12 in Shengzhou, 7 in Zhoushan, and Taizhou. There were 81 cases in Lishui City and 7 cases in Lishui City. Among the severe cases, 11 were in Hangzhou, 1 in Ningbo, 14 in Wenzhou, 5 in Jiaxing, 5 in Shaoxing, 5 in Jinhua, 1 in Luzhou, and Taizhou. There were 6 cases and 2 cases in Lishui City. Among the discharged patients, 7 cases were in Wenzhou City, 1 in Lishui City, and 1 in Zhoushan City.

At present, 9297 people in close contact have been tracked in the whole province, 422 people have been released from medical observation, and 8801 people are receiving medical observation.
