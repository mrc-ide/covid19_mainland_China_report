Pneumonia epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Yunnan Province
 
Release Date: 2020-02-01
 
From 12:00 to 24:00 on January 31 , Yunnan Province reported 8 new cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection . A total of 91 confirmed cases were reported across the province . Except for one patient who was cured and discharged , they are now receiving isolation treatment at designated hospitals. There were 3 critical cases and 5 severe cases, and there were no deaths. There are currently 228 suspected cases , with 2,642 medical contacts of close contacts . Among the confirmed cases, there were 30 cases in Kunming , 12 cases in Xishuangbanna Prefecture , 7 cases in Yuxi City, 7 cases in Baoshan City, 6 cases in Zhaotong City, 6 cases in Dali Prefecture, 6 cases in Lijiang City, 5 cases in Qujing City, and 4 cases in Honghe Prefecture (including One patient was cured and discharged ), four in Pu'er , three in Dehong, and one in Lincang .
 
Newly diagnosed patients:
 
Case 84 : A 25 -year-old male from Yueqing, Zhejiang, living in Wuhan. On January 23 , he drove through Hunan, Qujing, and Dali by car. He arrived in Gengma County, Lincang City on the 24th . On the 27th , he developed symptoms of dry throat and cough, and went to Gengma Medical Institution for treatment. Report the first confirmed case for Lincang City.
 
Case 85 : A 67 -year-old female from Wuhan, Hubei , who was a close contact with case 81 and traveled to Yunnan with the group. Medical observations of close contacts revealed that Kunming Medical Institution was admitted to hospital for isolation and treatment.
 
Case 86 : A 79 -year-old female from Wuhan, Hubei , who is a close contact of case 81 , traveled to Yunnan with the group. After medical observation of the close contacts, it was found that Kunming Medical Institution was admitted to hospital for isolation and treatment.
 
Case 87 : A 51 -year-old female from Kunming, who lives in Fengning Street, Wuhua District, Kunming. I took a flight from Kunming to Huangshi, Hubei on January 19 , returned to Kunming on January 22 , and developed fever and other symptoms on January 30. I went to Kunming medical institution for treatment and admission.
 
Case 88 : A 3 -year-old girl from Guandu, Kunming, lives in Hailun International Community, Xiaobanqiao, Guandu District, Kunming. Visiting relatives in Guangdong with her mother on December 14 , 2019 , driving from Guangdong to Suizhou, Hubei with her mother on January 16 , 2020 , returning to Luliang, Yunnan on January 24 , returning to Kunming on January 26 , breathing on January 30 Difficulty and fever symptoms, admitted to Kunming Medical Institution on January 31 and admitted to hospital for isolation and treatment.
 
Case 89 : A 39 -year-old female from Wuhan, Hubei. I arrived in Tengchong from Wuhan on January 21 , and stayed in Zhen'an Town. I was quarantined at home after epidemic prevention and inspection. On January 27 , I stayed in Longling County for quarantine. On January 31 , I was admitted to Longling County Medical Institution for isolation and treatment.
 
The 90th case: A 34 -year-old female, born in Kunming, Yunnan, living in Panlong District, Kunming City, was a close contact of case No. 75. She was admitted to Kunming Medical Institution for isolation and treatment on January 31 .
 
Case 91 : A 70 -year-old female from Yuxi, Yunnan, living in Hongta District, Yuxi. He lived with relatives from Wuhan on January 19 and was a close contact with the suspected case (his husband). On January 31 , he was admitted to Yuxi Medical Institution for isolation and treatment.
