Pneumonia epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Yunnan Province

[Index Number: 01510047-20200130-9340] Release Date: 2020-01-30

From 00:00 to 12:00 on January 30, Yunnan reported six new cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection. A total of 76 confirmed cases were reported in the province, of which 2 were critically ill and 6 were severe. There were no deaths. Among them, 23 were in Kunming, 12 in Xishuangbanna, 6 in Yuxi, 6 in Baoshan, 6 in Lijiang, 5 in Qujing, 4 in Zhaotong, 4 in Honghe, 4 in Dali, and 3 in Pu'er. Cases and 3 cases in Dehong Prefecture, all received isolation treatment at designated medical institutions. 234 suspected cases were reported. Close contacts were medically observed 2125 people.
