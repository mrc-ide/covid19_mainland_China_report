Update on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Henan Province as of 24:00 on February 6
Author: Time: 2020-02-07 Views: Source: Font Size: [ Tai Zhong small ]

At 04:00 on February 6, 2020, 63 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by a new coronavirus infection were added in Henan Province, 14 new cases were discharged, and 1 new death was reported. Among the newly confirmed cases, 10 were in Zhengzhou City, 2 in Kaifeng City (including 1 in Lankao County), 3 in Luoyang City, 5 in Pingdingshan City, 3 in Anyang City, 1 in Hebi City, 1 in Jiaozuo City, and Xuchang City. 2 cases, 7 cases in Nanyang City (including 1 case in Dengzhou City), 7 cases in Shangqiu City (including 1 case in Yongcheng City), 12 cases in Xinyang City (including 3 cases in Gushi County), 1 case in Zhoukou City, and 8 cases in Zhumadian City (Including 1 case in Xincai County) and 1 case in Jiyuan Demonstration Area.

As of 24:00 on February 6, a total of 914 confirmed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection were reported in the province, of which 48 were severe cases, 27 were critical cases, 3 were dead cases, and 68 were cured cases. Among them: 112 confirmed cases in Zhengzhou (including 8 in Gongyi City), 20 in Kaifeng (including 5 in Lankao County), 24 in Luoyang, 46 in Pingdingshan (including 1 in Ruzhou), and Anyang 40 cases (including 1 case in Hua County), 11 cases in Hebi City, 41 cases in Xinxiang City (including 4 cases in Changyuan City), 18 cases in Jiaozuo City, 5 cases in Liyang City, 28 cases in Xuchang City, 26 cases in Luohe City, and Sanmenxia City 7 cases, 118 cases in Nanyang City (including 16 in Dengzhou City), 72 cases in Shangqiu City (including 10 in Yongcheng City), 176 cases in Xinyang City (including 17 cases in Gushi County), and 60 cases in Zhoukou City (including Luyi County) 6 cases), 106 cases in Zhumadian City (including 9 cases in Xincai County), and 4 cases in Jiyuan Demonstration Area.

At present, 19,555 close contacts have been tracked. 3,356 people have been released from observation on February 6, and 66 people were suspected. A total of 10,138 people are undergoing medical observation.
