Second patient discharged from Henan
Author: Time: 2020-01-31 Views: Source: Henan Daily Font Size: [ Tai Zhong small ]

After the first patient with pneumonitis infected by a new type of coronavirus was cured in Gongyi City People's Hospital, a patient with pneumonia infected by a new type of coronavirus was successfully discharged at 9 o'clock on January 30. This is the second case in our province and the first cured case in Gongyi City.

Patient Ms. Wang, 30 years old, an employee of a company went to Wuhan on January 13 for a business trip, returned to Zhengzhou by high-speed rail on January 17, and went to Gongyi People's Hospital for a fever on January 20, and was initially diagnosed with suspected new-type coronavirus infection. Pneumonia was immediately treated in isolation and was diagnosed on January 23 by the Henan Provincial Center for Disease Control and Prevention.

Gongyi City People's Hospital Director Qiao Laijun introduced that during the hospitalization of patients, the hospital attaches great importance to the organization of expert consultations, analysis of the condition, and development and guidance of treatment plans. With the careful treatment and careful care of medical staff, Ms. Wang's condition was gradually controlled, and a chest CT review was performed on January 27, suggesting that the left lower lobe virus pneumonia was significantly better than before. According to the requirements of the diagnosis and treatment guidelines, the patient was re-examined twice, and the results of the new coronavirus nucleic acid test were negative, which met the criteria for de-segregation and discharge of pneumonia diagnosed by new coronavirus infection of the National Health and Medical Commission. With the approval of the Provincial Health and Health Commission, the patient was released from isolation and discharged.

"The epidemic has happened. When everyone treats the patient as a pathogen or a source of infection and is far away, you approach me, diagnose, draw blood, sample, kill, care, and encourage. I don't see your appearance , But remembered your clear eyes, you are the retrograde hero in my heart. "Ms. Wang wrote the words of affectionately and wrote her thanks to the medical staff.

A fever clinic was set up on January 19, and after the discovery of the first suspected case of infection with a new type of coronavirus in Gongyi City, Zhou Chengguo, director of the Department of Infectious Diseases at Gongyi People's Hospital, invested in the front line of the fight against epidemics, and has not yet left the hospital. Has been standing for 11 days. He said with emotion: "Although the epidemic situation is severe, it is not terrible. The hospital has set up an epidemic prevention and control office. The infection department originally had only 13 medical staff. This time, the elite staff was drawn from the whole hospital to 32 people. We strictly follow The National Health and Medical Commission's "New Coronavirus Infected Pneumonia Diagnosis and Treatment Scheme" is based on symptomatic treatment of patients' symptoms, and the effect is gradually showing. "During the treatment, Zhou Chengguo's team adopted a three-shift method, in addition to the health care staff 24 hours a day Also pay attention to appease Ms. Wang's emotions and help her build confidence.

"I am a party member, so I should take the lead and rush ahead." Faced with the first result, Zhou Chengguo did not relax. "After Ms. Wang is discharged, we will pay close attention and return to her."
