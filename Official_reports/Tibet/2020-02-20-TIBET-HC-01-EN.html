Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 19, 2020

Author: Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region health Editor: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee Translation: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee share to:

 

As of 24:00 on February 19, there were 0 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases, 0 suspected cases, 1 cured case and 1 confirmed new coronary pneumonia case in our district. 21 consecutiveThere are no new confirmed or suspected cases.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee: Enterprises to do a good job in protecting against new crown pneumonia

1.The factory environment is clean and tidy, and the corridor is free of debris, sputum, and cigarette butts. Strengthen garbage sorting management, collect and clear in time. Discarded masks should be placed in a special trash can covered with plastic bags and covered, and cleaned regularly. Disinfection should be performed before cleaning. Strengthen the cleaning of other garbage containers to ensure that Nissan and Nissan are cleaned.

2.The toilet should be cleaned, cleaned and killed by someone. Squat pits and urinals are free of dung, urine, and basically no odor.

3.Ensure that the canteen is safe and hygienic. The environment of food processing, packaging, storage and other places is clean and tidy to ensure that the ingredients are not contaminated. Raw and cooked food should be stored and processed separately. The processing process is reasonable to prevent cross-contamination of the food to be processed and directly imported food, raw materials and finished products, to ensure that meat and eggs are thoroughly cooked; tableware and drinking utensils should be washed before use Disinfection, cookware, utensils should be washed and kept clean after use; canteen operators should wash their hands and wear clean work clothes, caps, etc. during food processing. Containers, tools and equipment for storing, transporting, and loading and unloading food shall be safe, harmless, kept clean, and prevented from food contamination.

4.Strengthen water quality and sanitation management to ensure the safety of drinking water.

5.Carry out prevention and control of vector organisms such as rats, mosquitoes, flies and cockroaches. The containers containing the remaining items, garbage, and water are sealed and covered, and no fly is breeding. The gutter, sewer, and wall vents are equipped with rat-proof nets, and the gaps between doors and windows are less than 0.6 cm. There are no traces of flies, rats and rat holes, rat dung, rat bite marks in the room, no cockroaches, pods and cockroaches, no fly maggots and breeding in the outdoor environment, no rat and rat traces, basically no fly.

6.Strengthen monitoring of animals of unknown origin. Animals of unknown origin in the employing unit shall be disposed of in a timely manner to prevent employees from coming into contact with animals of unknown health, and all personnel of the employing unit shall avoid contact with wild animals or breeding animals of unknown origin without protection.

7.The unit's collective dormitory and employee residential area (building) should be neatly and orderly arranged, the indoor floor should be clean, and the outdoor should not be piled up. .
