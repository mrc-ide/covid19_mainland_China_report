Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 24, 2020

Author: Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region health Editor: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee Translation: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee share to:

 

cutoff2monthAt 24 o'clock on the 24th, there were 0 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 0 severe cases, 0 dead cases, 0 suspected cases, 1 cured case and 1 confirmed new coronary pneumonia case. No new cases were confirmed or suspected for 26 consecutive days.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health Committee:Focus on taxi travel protection!

1. The interior of the vehicle should be cleaned and disinfected daily before travelling.

2. The vehicle should be equipped with disinfection and personal protective equipment such as masks, disinfection wipes or disposable hand disinfectants.

3. The driver should increase the frequency of cleaning and disinfection of door handles, steering wheels, and handrails in the car, as well as good hand hygiene.

4. When conditions such as natural air temperature and driving speed allow, turn off the air conditioner in the car as much as possible and open the window for ventilation.

5. The driver should wear a mask all the time, and remind passengers on the vehicle to wear a mask and reduce communication. Cover the mouth and nose with a tissue when sneezing, or cover with an elbow.

6. In case of vomiting by passengers, first cover the vomit with disinfectant and absorbent material (or disinfection dry towel), remove the vomit and then disinfect the surface with disinfectant.

7. Carry out publicity on health protection knowledge by means of car radio, posters or slogans on the back of car seats.
