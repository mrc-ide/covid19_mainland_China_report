Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 9, 2020

Author: Editor: Translation: Share to:

As of 24:00 on February 9, 2020, the Tibet Autonomous Region has reported a total of one confirmed case of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. There are 1 confirmed case, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases and 0 discharged cases.

At present, the vital signs of confirmed patients are stable. All close contacts have been released from medical isolation.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee: Guidance on nutrition diet for the general population

1. Food is diverse, with cereals as the mainstay. Daily meals should include cereals, vegetables and fruits, livestock, poultry, fish, eggs, milk, soybeans and other foods. Pay attention to choosing whole grains, mixed beans and potatoes.

2. Eat more fruits and vegetables, milk, soybeans. Make sure you have vegetables in your meals and eat fruits every day. Choose dark fruits and vegetables instead of fresh fruits. Eating a variety of milk and its products, especially yogurt, is equivalent to 300 grams of liquid milk per day. Eat soy products regularly and eat nuts in moderation.

3. Eat fish, poultry, eggs, and lean meat in moderation. Fish, poultry, eggs, and lean meats should be consumed in moderation. Eat less fatty, smoked, and cured meat products. Resolutely put an end to eating wild animals.

4. Less salt and less oil, control sugar and limit wine. Eat a light diet and avoid high-salt and fried foods. Drink plenty of water. Adults should drink 7-8 cups (1500-1700 ml) per day. Drinking plain water and tea is recommended; do not drink or drink less sugary beverages. Adults, such as alcohol, men do not drink more than 25 grams of alcohol a day, women do not exceed 15 grams.

5. Eat dynamic balance and healthy weight. Exercise at home every day to maintain a healthy weight. Do not overeat, do not overeating, control total energy intake and maintain energy balance. Reduce sedentary time and get up every hour.

6. Eliminate waste and promote new food. Cherish food, prepare meals on demand, promote meal sharing and use of chopsticks and spoons. Choose fresh, safe food and proper cooking methods. Separate raw food from cooked food and heat through cooked food twice. Learn to read food labels and choose foods reasonably.
