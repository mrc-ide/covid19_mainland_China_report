Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tibet Autonomous Region on January 30, 2020

Author: Editor: Translation: Share to:


As of 24 o'clock on January 30, 2020, the Tibet Autonomous Region has reported a total of 1 confirmed case of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 0 discharged cases.

Close contacts are currently undergoing medical isolation observation.
