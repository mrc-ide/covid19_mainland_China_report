Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 20, 2020

Author: Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region health Editor: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee Translation: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee share to:

 

As of 24:00 on February 20, there were 0 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases, 0 suspected cases, 1 cured case and 1 confirmed new coronary pneumonia case in our district. There were no new confirmed or suspected cases for 22 consecutive days.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee: During the epidemic, the canteen is safe to eat. Nine guidelines must be kept in mind!
1. Meals scattered or packaged for separate meals. During the epidemic, it is recommended not to concentrate on dining in the unit's canteen. If you have to eat, you can take turns to take meals. In order to reduce the residence time in centralized places, packing is encouraged to bring back to their respective office (or residence) to eat alone.
2. Wear a correct mask before eating. If you go to the cafeteria to eat, make sure that you are healthy and that there are no suspicious symptoms of the new coronavirus pneumonia, and wear a mask properly before removing it.
Keep your hands clean. Wash your hands before eating! Wash your hands! Wash your hands!
4. Eat at the wrong time. Avoid peak meals.
5. Reasonable diet. According to personal characteristics, the diet should be vegetarian, thick and thin, mixed with corn beans, combined with egg and milk, plenty of fruits and vegetables, eat less fat, smoked and cured meat products, eat less high salt and fried food, enough Drinking water can promote the body's metabolism and enhance the body's immunity and resistance.
6. Communicate less while eating. When dining, talk less and concentrate on eating.
7. How to disinfect tableware. The most effective and safest method for disinfecting tableware is thermal disinfection. After washing the tableware, it is preferred to sterilize it by boiling in boiling water for 15-30 minutes; or clean the tableware and steam it in a hot steam generator for 15-30 minutes; Chemical disinfectants suitable for tableware disinfection can be used in accordance with the product instructions.
8. The principle of measuring body temperature in the cafeteria. If it is the internal canteen of the unit, and the diners have performed healthy body temperature monitoring before entering the work area, it is not necessary to take a temperature measurement when going to the canteen to eat. If the canteen is open to the outside world, according to the transmission characteristics of the new coronavirus, in order to avoid cluster sexual transmission and ensure the health of all diners, outside diners need to take a temperature measurement before entering the restaurant.
Keep a distance and avoid the spread of droplets. To eat in the canteen, keep a certain distance between people to avoid the spread of droplets.
