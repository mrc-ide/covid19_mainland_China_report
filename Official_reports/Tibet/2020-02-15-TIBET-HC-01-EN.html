Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 14, 2020

Author: Editor: Translation: Share to:

As of 24:00 on February 14, there were 0 confirmed cases, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases, 0 suspected cases, and 1 cured case in our district. One case of new coronavirus pneumonia was confirmed.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee: Home Prevention Points for the Interim Guide to Preventing New Coronary Pneumonia in Pregnant Women

1. Keep the room air fresh and the temperature appropriate, and open the windows in a timely manner to avoid being too cold or overheating to avoid catching a cold.

2. Maternal towels, bath towels, tableware, bedding and other daily necessities should be used alone to avoid cross infection.

3. Maintain hand hygiene at all times. Wash your hands with hand sanitizer or soap running water before or after meals, or use alcohol-free hand sanitizer. When you are not sure whether your hands are clean, avoid touching your mouth, nose, and eyes. When sneezing or coughing, use your elbow or paper towel Cover your mouth and nose.

4. Maintain a balanced nutrition, light diet, avoid overeating, and do weight control.

5. Avoid visiting relatives and friends, and avoid contact with people with respiratory infections and people who have been in areas with high incidence of epidemics within two weeks.

6. Maternal insists on breastfeeding, and wash hands properly before feeding.

7. Regular life, adequate sleep, drink plenty of water, exercise properly, maintain a good attitude, and strengthen your own resistance.
