Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 6, 2020

Author: Editor: Translation: Share to:

As of 24:00 on February 6, 2020, the Tibet Autonomous Region has reported a total of 1 confirmed case of pneumonitis infected with new-type coronavirus, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases, and 0 discharged cases.

At present, the patient's vital signs are stable, and the close contacts tracked are undergoing medical observation.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee: How to protect yourself in public places?

1. Minimize activities in crowded public places. If you must go, you must do personal protection and wear disposable medical masks properly.

2. When coughing or sneezing, completely cover your nose and mouth with paper towels; throw the used paper towels into closed bins; if you cover with coughing or sneezing with your hands, wash your hands with running water and soap or use alcohol Scrub hands without disinfectant.

3. Avoid touching the mouth, eyes, and nose directly with your hands after touching public goods or facilities.

4. go back home to wash their hands properly, ensure hand hygiene, avoid handling infected.
