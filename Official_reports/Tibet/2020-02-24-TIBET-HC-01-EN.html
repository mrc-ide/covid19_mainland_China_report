Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 23, 2020

Author: Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region health Editor: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee Translation: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee share to:

 

As of 24:00 on February 23, there were 0 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 0 severe cases, 0 dead cases, 0 suspected cases, 1 cured case, and 1 confirmed new coronary pneumonia case. No new cases were confirmed or suspected for 25 consecutive days.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee: Key points for home protection during the Tibetan New Year

1.Reduce activities to crowded public places, especially places with poor air mobility, such as shopping malls, stations, airports,Agricultural MarketWait.

2.The room is kept clean, windows are opened frequently, and ventilation is frequent.

3.Hand hygiene at all times. Reduce contact with public items and parts in public places; return from public places, cover your hands with coughing, before or after meals, wash your hands with hand sanitizer or soap running water, or use alcohol-free hand sanitizer; not sure if your hands are clean Avoid touching the nose, mouth and eyes with your hands; cover your mouth and nose with your elbow clothes when sneezing or coughing.

4.Always wear a mask when going out. When going out to public places, for medical treatment and taking public transportation, you should wear a surgical mask or N95 mask.

5.Maintain good hygiene and health habits. Family members do not share towels, keep their homes and tableware clean, and frequently wash clothes. Do not spit, wrap nose and nose secretions with paper towels, and put them in a covered dustbin. Pay attention to nutrition and exercise.

6.Proactive health monitoring of individuals and family members. Active temperature measurement when you feel consciously feverish. If there is a child in the family, touch the child's forehead sooner or later. If there is fever, take the temperature of the child in time.

Season's greetings and best wishes to the people of all ethnic groups in the region, Tashidler!
