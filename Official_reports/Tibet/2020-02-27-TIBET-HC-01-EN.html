Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 26, 2020

Author: Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region health Editor: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee Translation: Tibet Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee share to:

 

As of 24:00 on February 26, there were 0 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases, 0 suspected cases, 1 cured case and 1 confirmed new coronary pneumonia case. There were no new confirmed or suspected cases for 28 consecutive days.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health Committee:How should the clothes be disinfected after going home?

the first,For daily coats, hang them directly at the door when you go home. Separate the clothes you wear outside and the clothes you wear at home. There is no need to go back every day to disinfect the coat.

second,If you feel that the jacket may be contaminated, for example, go to the hospital, visit the patient, and contact some people with suspicious symptoms, in this case you need to disinfect the jacket. Use physical disinfection as much as possible, and use chemical disinfection if physical disinfection fails. For example, as long as the clothes are resistant to high temperatures, you can choose 56 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes. If you have a dryer, you can also adjust the temperature to above 80 degrees. Drying for 20 minutes can also achieve disinfection.

If the clothing fabric is not resistant to high temperatures, chemical disinfectants can also be used for soaking and disinfecting. These types of disinfectants are more commonly used. One is a phenol disinfectant, the second is a quaternary ammonium salt disinfectant, and the third is represented by 84 disinfectant. Chlorine-containing disinfectants, these three types of disinfectants can be used to disinfect clothes, be sure to operate according to the dosage of the instructions. These three disinfectants have their own disadvantages, so pay attention when using them. Phenolic disinfectants sometimes color chemical fiber materials and may cause them to discolor. Chlorine-containing disinfectants such as 84 Disinfectant may discolor clothing and bleach. Quaternary ammonium salt disinfectants, if used together with anionic surfactants such as washing powder and soap, will fail on both sides, which can neither disinfect nor clean. So we have to choose the disinfectant according to the actual situation.
