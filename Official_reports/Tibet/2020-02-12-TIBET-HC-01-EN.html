The first patient diagnosed with new-type coronavirus pneumonia in our district cured and discharged today

Author: Editor: Translation: Share to:


On February 12 , the first patient with confirmed new type of coronavirus pneumonia in Tibet was cured and discharged from the Third People's Hospital of the autonomous region. After 18 days of intensive treatment, Zhang Moumou 's body temperature returned to normal for more than 1 week. The respiratory symptoms improved significantly, and the respiratory pathogenic nucleic acid tests were negative at two intervals of 24 hours. At the same time, imaging examination showed that pulmonary inflammation was significantly absorbed. The medical treatment expert group assessed that it met the criteria for de-isolation and discharge of confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia.

Patients Moumou, 34 years old, male, Suizhou, Hubei, January 25 because of cough, fever, fatigue and other symptoms go their own autonomous Third People's Hospital for treatment, the woman was admitted to hospital and treated in isolation. He was diagnosed with new coronavirus pneumonia on January 29 .

During the treatment period, the party committee and the government of the autonomous region attached great importance to the treatment and rehabilitation of patients, and made special deployment arrangements on several occasions. They required to stand on the level of maintaining national unity and do a good job of treatment. The Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee has organized experts to hold special meetings to discuss treatment measures and make specific arrangements for patient treatment. The Third People's Hospital of the Autonomous Region has carefully formulated specific personalized treatment plans in accordance with the new coronary pneumonia diagnosis and treatment program specifications, combined with the patient's condition and plateau climate conditions. Nursing work to ensure that patients receive timely and effective treatment.

14:30 Xu , accompanied by medical staff, Zhang Moumou walked out of the isolation ward. With a relaxed expression and a steady movement, he smiled and thanked the medical staff and media reporters waiting outside the ward.

Wang Yunting, secretary of the Party Committee of the Autonomous Region Health and Health Committee, presented Zhang Hou with white Hada, and told him to strengthen nutrition, exercise properly, and maintain a good mood. Zhang Moumou said excitedly: When I learned that I was diagnosed with the new type of coronavirus pneumonia, I felt very stressed and very depressed. The medical staff gave the meticulous care. I sincerely thank the doctors and nurses of the Third People's Hospital for their concern. Help his Tibetan people! May Tibet have a better tomorrow.

Our region's first novel coronavirus in patients diagnosed with pneumonia on February 12 cured, quarantine medical observation 32 close contacts lifted on February 8, effectively examine the health level of technology in our region, revealed the epidemic prevention and control stage Sexual effectiveness, the first time in 31 provinces (autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the central government) in China to clear the confirmed cases and suspected cases. This achievement is not due to factors such as high temperature, lack of oxygen, and strong ultraviolet rays. It is because we resolutely implement the decision-making and deployment of the Party Central Committee with Comrade Xi Jinping as the core. Under the correct leadership of the district party committee and government, scientific research, judgment, Reasonable scheduling and strict prevention of deadlocked results.
