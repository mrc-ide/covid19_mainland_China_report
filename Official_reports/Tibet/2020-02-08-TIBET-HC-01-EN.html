Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 7, 2020

Author: Xinhua Editor: Xinhua translation: Xinhua Published:

As at 4:00 on February 2 2020 7, the Tibet Autonomous Region reported a total of novel coronavirus infection in one case confirmed cases of pneumonia, severe cases 0 cases, deaths 0 cases, 0 cases of patients discharged from hospital.

Currently, Zhang diagnosed patients with stable vital signs. The 32 close contacts of Zhang, the first confirmed case of pneumonia infected with new-type coronavirus in our district , have passed 14 days of isolation medical observation at designated isolation points in Lhasa, Shigatse, Shannan, and Naqu. its symptoms, met the criteria for the lifting of medical observation, quarantine medical observation on February 8 officially released.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health Committee: During the epidemic, such a "home"!

1. Pay attention to physical health, do not visit, do not party;

2. Ventilate frequently, wash hands often, do not share towels;

3. Regular cleaning and not over disinfection;

4. Exercise moderately, don't sit still for long;

5. Eat regularly and do n’t overeating;

6. Go to bed early and get up early, don't overdraw your body;

7. Read more, study more, don't waste time;

8. Be rational and not panic.
