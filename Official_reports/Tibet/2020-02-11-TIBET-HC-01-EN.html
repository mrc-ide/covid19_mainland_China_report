Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Tibet Autonomous Region on February 10, 2020

Author: Editor: Translation: Share to:

As of 24:00 on February 10, 2020, the Tibet Autonomous Region has reported a total of one confirmed case of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. There are 1 confirmed case, 0 severe cases, 0 death cases and 0 discharged cases.

At present, the vital signs of confirmed patients are stable. All close contacts have been released from medical isolation.

 

Tips from the Tibet Autonomous Region Health Committee: Citizens' rights and obligations in the prevention and control of epidemic situations

In accordance with the Law on the Prevention and Treatment of Infectious Diseases, the State supports and encourages units and individuals to participate in the prevention and treatment of infectious diseases. People's governments at all levels shall improve relevant systems to facilitate units and individuals to participate in propaganda and education, epidemic report, volunteer service and donation activities for the prevention and control of infectious diseases.
The state and society should care and help patients with infectious diseases, carriers of pathogens and patients with suspected infectious diseases so that they can be treated in a timely manner. No unit or individual shall discriminate against patients with infectious diseases, carriers of pathogens and patients with suspected infectious diseases.
Infectious disease patients, pathogen carriers, and patients suspected of infectious diseases shall not engage in work that is prone to the spread of the infectious disease as prohibited by laws, administrative regulations and the health administrative department of the State Council before curing or excluding suspected infectious diseases.
Residents 'committees and villagers' committees shall organize residents and villagers to participate in community and rural infectious disease prevention and control activities.
All units and individuals in the territory of the People's Republic of China must accept preventive and control measures such as investigations, inspections, collection of samples, and isolation and treatment of infectious diseases by disease prevention and control agencies and medical institutions, and provide relevant information truthfully.
When any unit or individual finds a patient with an infectious disease or a patient suspected of having an infectious disease, it shall promptly report to the nearby disease prevention and control institution or medical institution.
