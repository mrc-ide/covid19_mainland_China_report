Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guizhou province from 12 to 24 March 2020

release time:                 -Times & emsp;->print this page Close this page                 Font:small in Big  Eye protection:

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 10, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia in the province and 4 new cured cases, of which: 2 in Guiyang, 1 in Zunyi, and 1 in Bijie.

As of 24:00 on March 10, the province has reported 146 cases of new coronavirus pneumonia. Among them, 51 cases had a history of staying in Wuhan, 14 cases had a history of staying in other areas outside Wuhan, and 15 cases had a history of staying in areas outside Hubei; 133 cases were cured and discharged, and 11 cases were treated in the hospital (10 cases of mild and ordinary, severe 1 Example), 2 deaths; 74 males and 72 females; the oldest was 87 years old and the youngest was 1 month (confirmed time); 36 cases in Guiyang (31 cases were cured, 1 case died), and 32 cases in Zunyi city (29 cases were cured) Example), 10 cases in Liupanshui City (8 cases cured, 1 death), 4 cases in Anshun City (4 cases cured), 23 cases in Bijie City (21 cases cured), 10 cases in Tongren City (10 cases cured), Qiandongnan 10 cases (10 cases were cured), 17 cases in Qiannan Prefecture (16 cases were cured), 4 cases in Qiannan Prefecture (4 cases were cured). There are 2 suspected cases.

A total of 2,574 close contacts have been tracked, 2,489 medical observations have been lifted, and 13 are still receiving medical observations.

Note: When quoted by the media, please mark "Information comes from the official website of Guizhou Health and Health Committee".
