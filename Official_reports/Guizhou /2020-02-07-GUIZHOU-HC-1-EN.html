Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Guizhou Province at 0-12 on February 7, 2020

release time: Print this pageClose this page Font: Small , medium and large eyesight protection color:

At 02:00 on February 7, 2020, there were 4 newly confirmed cases of pneumonia of the new type of coronavirus infection in the province, and 0 new cases were cured and discharged. Among them: 4 new cases were confirmed in Bijie City.

As of 12:00 on February 7, the province has reported 81 cases of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus infection. 36 cases had a history of staying in Wuhan, 4 cases had a history of staying in Hubei other than Wuhan, and 6 cases had a history of staying in Hubei. Six patients were discharged from the hospital, 64 were in stable condition, 3 were critically ill, 7 were critically ill, and 1 died; 41 were male and 40 were female; the oldest was 80 years old and the youngest was 1 month (confirmation time); 18 were from Guiyang ( 1 case has been cured and discharged), 12 cases in Zunyi City, 9 cases in Liupanshui, 4 cases in Anshun City, 17 cases in Bijie City, 6 cases in Tongren City (2 cases have been cured and discharged), 5 cases in Qiandongnan Prefecture, 7 cases in Qiannan Prefecture ( 3 cases have been cured and discharged), 3 cases in Qianxinan Prefecture. There are 54 suspected cases.

At present, 1285 close contacts have been tracked, 564 have been released from medical observation, and 678 are still receiving medical observation.

The information of the four new patients is as follows:

Patient 1: Li Moumou, male, 55 years old, currently lives in Baihua Village, Supu Town, Qianxi County, Bijie City. He was diagnosed with a confirmed case on February 7 and is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Qianxi County People's Hospital with a stable condition.

Patient 2: Chen Moumou, male, 5 years old, currently lives in Baihua Village, Supu Town, Qianxi County, Bijie City. He was diagnosed with a confirmed case on February 7 and is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Qianxi County People's Hospital with a stable condition.

Patient 3: Li Moumou, male, 44 years old, currently lives in Baihua Village, Supu Town, Qianxi County, Bijie City. He was diagnosed with a confirmed case on February 7 and is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Qianxi County People's Hospital with a stable condition.

Patient 4: Wang Moumou, female, 50 years old, currently lives in Baihua Village, Supu Town, Qianxi County, Bijie City. He was diagnosed with a confirmed case on February 7 and is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Qianxi County People's Hospital with a stable condition.
