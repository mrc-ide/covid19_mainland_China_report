Briefing on COVID-19 in Jiangxi Province on 27 February 2020

Issued by the Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 28 February 2020

　　Between 00:00 hrs and 24:00 hrs on 27 February, Jiangxi province reported one new confirmed case and 36 new cured and discharged cases.

　　This new conformed case was reported in Nanchang. Of the new cured and discharged cases, 5 in Nanchang, 11 in Jiujiang, 3 in Pingxiang, 3 in Xinyu, 1 in Yingtan, 2 in Ganzhou,1 in Yichun, 8 in Shangrao and 2 in Ji’an.

　　As of 24:00 on 27 February, a total of 935 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 790 cured and discharged cases and 1 fatality. Up till now, there are still 144 confirmed and hospitalized cases including 5 severe cases with no suspected case.

　　Of the 144 confirmed and hospitalized cases, 32 were in Nanchang, 27 in Jiujiang, 3 in Jingdezhen, 10 in Pingxiang, 12 in Xinyu, 6 in Yingtan, 12 in Ganzhou, 10 in Yichun, 14 in Shangrao, 7 in Ji’an and 11 in Fuzhou.

　　The total 935 confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (230 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Jingdezhen (6 cases), Pingxiang (33 cases), Xinyu (130 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases) and Ganjiang New District(1 case), respectively.

　　Of the 790 cured and discharged cases, 198 were in Nanchang, 91 in Jiujiang, 3 in Jingdezhen, 23 in Pingxiang, 118 in Xinyu, 12 in Yingtan, 63 in Ganzhou, 96 in Yichun, 109 in Shangrao, 15 in Ji’an, 61 in Fuzhou and 1 in Ganjiang New District.

　　To date, 26401 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 25417 have been released from medical observation while 984 are still under medical observation.
