2020年3月8日江西省新型冠状病毒肺炎疫情情况

发布时间：2020-03-09    
【字体：大 中 小】    打印本页

　　2020年3月8日0-24时，江西省无新增新型冠状病毒肺炎确诊病例，新增出院病例5例。

　　新增出院病例中，南昌市1例、九江市1例、新余市3例。

　　截至3月8日24时，江西省现有住院确诊病例11例。累计报告新型冠状病毒肺炎确诊病例935例，其中出院病例923例，死亡病例1例。现有重症病例1例。现有疑似病例0例。

　　现有住院确诊病例（11例）中，南昌市5例、九江市5例、上饶市1例。鹰潭市、景德镇市、宜春市、萍乡市、赣州市、吉安市、抚州市、新余市等8个设区市和赣江新区住院确诊病例已“清零”。75个县（市、区）住院确诊病例已“清零”，16个县（市、区）继续保持无确诊病例报告。

　　累计确诊病例（935例）中，南昌市230例、九江市118例、景德镇市6例、萍乡市33例、新余市130例、鹰潭市18例、赣州市76例、宜春市106例、上饶市123例、吉安市22例、抚州市72例、赣江新区1例。

　　出院病例（923例）中，南昌市225例、九江市113例、景德镇市6例、萍乡市33例、新余市130例、鹰潭市18例、赣州市75例、宜春市106例、上饶市122例、吉安市22例、抚州市72例、赣江新区1例。

　　目前追踪到密切接触者26834人，解除医学观察26547人，尚有287人正在接受医学观察。

　　

　　江西省卫生健康委员会

　　2020年3月9日 

　　

 

Briefing on COVID-19 in Jiangxi Province on 8 March 2020

Issued by the Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 9 March 2020

　　Between 00:00 hrs and 24:00 hrs on 8 March, no new confirmed case was reported in Jiangxi province, and five new cases including one in each of Nanchang and Jiujiang and three in Xinyu , were cured and discharged from hospital.

　　As of 24:00 hrs on 8 March, a total of 935 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 923 cured and discharged cases and one fatality. Up till now, there are still 11 confirmed and hospitalized cases including one in severe condition,with no suspected case.

　　Of the 11 confirmed and hospitalized cases, five were in each of Nanchang and Jiujiang and one in Shangrao. All the confirmed and hospitalized cases in eight municipalities of Yingtan, Jingdezhen, Yichun, Pingxiang, Ganzhou, Ji’an,Fuzhou and Xinyu as well as Ganjiang New District have been cured and discharged from hospital. Meanwhile, the confirmed and hospitalized cases in 75 counties (including county-level cities and districts) have been reduced to zero, with no new confirmed case reported in 16 counties (including county-level cities and districts).

　　The total 935 confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (230 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Jingdezhen (six cases), Pingxiang (33 cases), Xinyu (130 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases) and Ganjiang New District (one case), respectively.

　　Of the 923 cured and discharged cases, 225 were in Nanchang, 113 in Jiujiang, six in Jingdezhen, 33 in Pingxiang, 130 in Xinyu, 18 in Yingtan, 75 in Ganzhou, 106 in Yichun, 122 in Shangrao, 22 in Ji’an, 72 in Fuzhou and one in Ganjiang New District.

　　To date, 26834 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 26547 have been released from medical observation while 287 are still under medical observation.
