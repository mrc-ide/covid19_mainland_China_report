Commendation Order of Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control Command of New Coronavirus Infection in Jiangxi Province

Published: 2020-01-27 Source: Jiangxi novel coronavirus infection pneumonia epidemic prevention and control emergency command [Font: Big Middle Small Print

Each member unit of the district, city, county (city, district) people's government, the Ganjiang New District Management Committee, and the provincial new-type coronavirus-infected pneumonia epidemic prevention and control emergency command department

On the morning of January 27, 2020, 2 cases of pneumonia patients diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus infection in our province were carefully diagnosed and treated by medical staff of the First Affiliated Hospital of Nanchang University and the Second People's Hospital of Pingxiang City, and reached the new crown of the National Health and Health Commission. Virus-infected pneumonia confirmed cases were released from isolation and discharged. The Provincial Emergency Command for Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control of New Coronavirus Infections of the Province decided to commend the first Affiliated Hospital of Nanchang University and the Second People's Hospital of Pingxiang City.

After the epidemic, the vast number of medical staff across the province gave up their vacations and opportunities for reunion and took the initiative to invite them into the epidemic prevention and control campaign. A large number of excellent medical staff are fighting the epidemic front every day. The headquarters expressed condolences and respect to the vast number of medical staff who fought on the front lines. It is hoped that the medical staff of the province will continue to practice the professional spirit of respecting life, saving lives and helping wounds, dedication, and boundless love in the new era, stick to their jobs, do their own protection, and go all out to do a good job in medical treatment of pneumonia patients with new type of coronavirus infection. Work, resolutely win the battle and hard battle of epidemic prevention and control, and make greater contributions to protecting the lives and health of the people in the province.

Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control Emergency Command of New Coronavirus Infection in Jiangxi Province

January 27, 2020
