Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Jiangxi Province on February 24, 2020

Release time: 2020-02-25
[Font:Big in small】    print this page

0 On February 24, 2020, at 04:00 and no new cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were confirmed in Jiangxi Province, and 37 new cases were discharged.

Among the newly discharged cases, 2 were in Nanchang, 5 in Jiujiang, 6 in Xinyu, 4 in Yingtan, 4 in Ganzhou, 8 in Yichun, 7 in Shangrao, and 1 in Fuzhou.

As of 24:00 on February 24, there were 251 confirmed hospitalized cases in Jiangxi Province. A total of 934 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were reported, of which 682 were discharged and 1 died. There are 18 severe cases. There are 0 suspected cases.

Among the confirmed hospitalized cases (251 cases), there were 45 cases in Nanchang (1 reduction), 56 cases in Jiujiang City, 3 cases in Jingdezhen City, 17 cases in Pingxiang City, 21 cases in Xinyu City, 8 cases in Yingtan City, and 22 cases in Ganzhou City. There were 22 cases in Yichun City, 26 cases in Shangrao City, 10 cases in Ji'an City (1 case with nuclear increase), 20 cases in Fuzhou City, and 1 case in Ganjiang New District.

Among the cumulative confirmed cases (934 cases), 229 cases were in Nanchang, 118 in Jiujiang, 6 in Jingdezhen, 33 in Pingxiang, 130 in Xinyu, 18 in Yingtan, 76 in Ganzhou, 106 in Yichun, and Shangrao. There were 123 cases in Ji'an City, 22 cases in Ji'an City, 72 cases in Fuzhou City, and 1 case in Ganjiang New District.

Among the discharged patients (682 cases), 184 cases in Nanchang (1 case with nuclear increase), 62 cases in Jiujiang City, 3 cases in Jingdezhen City, 16 cases in Pingxiang City, 109 cases in Xinyu City, 10 cases in Yingtan City, 53 cases in Ganzhou City, Yichun There were 84 cases in Shanghai, 97 cases in Shangrao City, 12 cases in Ji'an City (one reduction), and 52 cases in Fuzhou City.

At present, 26,106 close contacts have been traced, 24,333 medical observations have been lifted, and 1,773 people are still receiving medical observations.

　　

Jiangxi Provincial Health Committee

February 25, 2020

 

Briefing on COVID-19 in Jiangxi Province on 24 February 2020

Issued by the Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 25 February 2020

　　Between 00:00 hrs and 24:00 hrs on 24 February, no new confirmed case was reported in Jiangxi province, and 37 new cases were cured and discharged from hospital including two in Nanchang, five in Jiujiang, six in Xinyu, four in Yingtan, four in Ganzhou, eight in Yichun, seven in Shangrao and one in Fuzhou.

　　As of 24:00 hrs on February 24, a total of 934 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 682 cured and discharged cases and one fatality. Up till now, there are still 251 confirmed and hospitalized cases including 18 severe ones with no suspected case.

　　Of the 251 confirmed and hospitalized cases, 45 were in Nanchang (with one case reduced after verification), 56 in Jiujiang, three in Jingdezhen, 17 in Pingxiang, 21 in Xinyu, eight in Yingtan, 22 in Ganzhou, 22 in Yichun, 26 in Shangrao, ten in Ji’an (with one case increased after verification), 20 in Fuzhou and one in Ganjiang New District.

　　The total 934 confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (229 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Jingdezhen (6 cases), Pingxiang (33 cases), Xinyu (130 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases) and Ganjiang New District (one case), respectively.

　　Of the 682 cured and discharged cases, 184 were in Nanchang (with one case increased after verification), 62 in Jiujiang, three in Jingdezhen, 16 in Pingxiang, 109 in Xinyu, ten in Yingtan, 53 in Ganzhou, 84 in Yichun, 97 in Shangrao, 12 in Ji’an (with one case reduced after verification) and 52 in Fuzhou.

　　To date, 26106 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 24333 have been released from medical observation while 1773 are still under medical observation.
