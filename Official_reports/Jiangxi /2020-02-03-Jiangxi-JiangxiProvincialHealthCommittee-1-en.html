Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Jiangxi Province on February 2, 2020

Release time: 2020-02-03
[Font: Big In Small】 Print

At 04:00 on February 2, 2020, Jiangxi Province reported 59 newly diagnosed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection, and 8 new cases were cured and discharged.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 14 were in Nanchang, 13 in Shangrao, 9 in Jiujiang, 7 in Xinyu, 5 in Yichun, 5 in Fuzhou, 3 in Pingxiang, 2 in Ganzhou, and 1 in Ji'an. . Among the newly added cured cases, 3 were in Nanchang, 3 in Shangrao, and 2 in Jiujiang.

As of 24:00 on February 2nd, Jiangxi Province had reported a total of 391 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection (1 case confirmed from Ganzhou, the state's nuclear reduction), of which 34 were severe cases and 18 were cured and discharged.

Among the confirmed cases, 103 were in Nanchang, 64 in Jiujiang, 50 in Xinyu, 36 in Yichun, 35 in Ganzhou, 32 in Shangrao, 29 in Fuzhou, 18 in Pingxiang, 13 in Ji'an, and Yingtan. There were 8 cases in the city and 3 cases in the city of Jingdezhen. Among the cured and discharged patients, there were 4 cases in Nanchang, 4 cases in Shangrao City, 3 cases in Xinyu City, 2 cases in Jiujiang City, 1 case in Jingdezhen City, 1 case in Pingxiang City, 1 case in Ganzhou City, 1 case in Yichun City, and 1 case in Ji'an City.

At present, 10,477 close contacts have been traced, 1174 people have been released from medical observation, and 9303 people are still receiving medical observation.

Jiangxi Provincial Health Committee

February 3, 2020
