Briefing on COVID-19 in Jiangxi province on 18 February 2020

Issued by the Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 19 February 2020

　　Between 00:00 hrs and 24:00 hrs on 18 February, Jiangxi province reported one new confirmed case and 52 new cured and discharged cases.

　　This new confirmed case was reported in Nanchang. Of the new cured and discharged cases, 19 were in Nanchang, 12 in Xinyu, eight in Shangrao, four in Ganzhou, three in Jiujiang, three in Yichun, one in Piangxiang, one in Ji’an and one in Fuzhou.

　　As of 24:00 hrs on 18 February, a total of 934 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 362 cured and discharged cases and one fatality. Up till now, there are still 571 confirmed and hospitalized cases (including 26 severe ones) and six suspected cases. 

　　The confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (230 cases), Xinyu (129 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases), Pingxiang (33 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Jingdezhen (six cases) and Ganjiang New District (one case), respectively. Of the cured and discharged cases, 114 were in Nanchang, 58 in Xinyu, 47 in Shangrao, 33 in Jiujiang, 33 in Yichun, 29 in Fuzhou, 24 in Ganzhou, 11 in Ji’an, seven in Pingxiang, three in Jingdezhen and three in Yingtan.

　　To date, 25104 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 21775 have been released from medical observation while 3329 are still under medical observation.

　　Of the 6 suspected cases, three were in Fuzhou, two in Yingtan and one in Jiujiang.
