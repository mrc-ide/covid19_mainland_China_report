Epidemic of new coronavirus pneumonia reported in Anhui Province on February 15

  • Date: 2020-02-15 09:20
  • Source of information: Anhui Health Commission

At 04:00 on February 14, 2020, Anhui Province reported 16 new confirmed cases, 13 new suspected cases, and 32 new cured cases.

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 4 cases in Bengbu, 4 in Fuyang, 2 in Hefei, 2 in Lu'an, 1 in Huaibei, 1 in Luzhou, 1 in Ma'anshan, and 1 in Anqing.

Among the new suspected cases, 6 were in Bengbu, 3 in Fuyang, 1 in Luzhou, 1 in Huainan, 1 in Lu'an, and 1 in Ma'anshan.

Among the newly cured cases, 5 were from Fuyang, 5 from Lu'an, 4 from Bengbu, 3 from Hefei, 3 from Luzhou, 3 from Luzhou, 3 from Wuhu, 3 from Anqing, 1 from Suzhou, 1 from Ma'anshan, and 1 from Chizhou. example.

As of 24:00 on February 14, a total of 950 confirmed cases were reported in Anhui Province, 198 cases were cured and discharged, 6 cases were fatal, 20,646 people were in close contact with medical observation, and 4,570 were in medical observation.

Among the confirmed cases, 168 cases were in Hefei, 153 in Bengbu, 150 in Fuyang, 107 in Luzhou, 82 in Anqing, 63 in Lu'an, 39 in Suzhou, 35 in Ma'anshan, 31 in Wuhu, 28 in Tongling, 26 in Huaibei, There were 23 cases in Huainan, 17 cases in Chizhou, 13 cases in Luzhou, 9 cases in Huangshan, and 6 cases in Xuancheng.

As of 24:00 on February 14th, 746 confirmed cases were being treated in hospital in Anhui Province, including 14 critically ill cases.

34 cases are expected to be discharged on February 15, including 7 in Lu'an, 5 in Hefei, 6 in Luzhou, 4 in Huaibei, 4 in Fuyang, 3 in Wuhu, 2 in Bengbu, 1 in Huainan, 1 in Ma'anshan, and 1 in Anqing. example.

Brief analysis of the epidemic situation: As of 24:00 on February 14th, of the 950 confirmed cases, the youngest was 9 months and the oldest was 91 years old, mainly distributed between 20-60 years old. Among them, 16 cases were under 10 years old, accounting for 1.7%; 40 cases were 10 to 19 years old, accounting for 4.2%; 132 cases were 20 to 29 years old, accounting for 13.9%; 193 cases were 30 to 39 years old, accounting for 20.3%; 40 to 49 years old, 228 Cases, accounting for 24.0%; 213 cases aged 50 to 59, accounting for 22.4%; 88 cases aged 60 to 69, accounting for 9.3%; 28 cases aged 70 to 79, accounting for 2.9%; 12 cases aged 80 and above, accounting for 1.3%.
