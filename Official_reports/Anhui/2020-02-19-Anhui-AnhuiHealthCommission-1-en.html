Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia reported in Anhui Province on February 19

At 04:00 on February 18, 2020, Anhui Province reported 4 new confirmed cases, no new suspected cases, and 68 new cured cases.

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 1 case in Bengbu, 1 case in Fuyang, 1 case in Lu'an, and 1 case in Ma'anshan.

        Among the newly added discharged cases, 21 were in Hefei, 12 in Luzhou, 9 in Bengbu, 6 in Fuyang, 5 in Anqing, 4 in Wuhu, 3 in Huainan, 2 in Huaibei, 2 in Lu'an, 2 in Ma'anshan, and 1 in Suzhou. Case, Tongling 1 case.

As of 24:00 on February 18, Anhui Province had reported a total of 986 confirmed cases, a total of 361 cured and discharged patients, a total of 6 deaths, a total of 24,600 close medical contacts, and 3,602 people still under medical observation.

The cumulative confirmed cases include 173 cases in Hefei, 159 in Bengbu, 155 in Fuyang, 108 in Luzhou, 83 in Anqing, 69 in Lu'an, 41 in Suzhou, 37 in Maanshan, 33 in Wuhu, 29 in Tongling, 27 in Huaibei, There were 27 cases in Huainan, 17 cases in Chizhou, 13 cases in Luzhou, 9 cases in Huangshan, and 6 cases in Xuancheng.

As of 24:00 on February 18th, 619 cases of confirmed cases were treated in hospital in Anhui Province, of which 9 were critical cases.

On February 19, 63 patients were expected to be discharged, 17 in Fuyang, 11 in Chenzhou, 10 in Bengbu, 7 in Lu'an, 5 in Anqing, 4 in Hefei, 3 in Suzhou, 3 in Huangshan, 1 in Huainan, and 1 in Ma'anshan. 1 case of Tongling.

Warm reminder: At present, with the in-depth implementation of the precise classification and control strategy in our province, according to the epidemic situation, the market continues to improve, and some supermarkets in some areas have gradually resumed normal operations. The provincial integrated command for epidemic prevention and control requires that localities strengthen health management in densely populated places such as shopping malls and supermarkets, conscientiously implement specific measures such as temporary closures, control of staff numbers, shortening of business hours, and promotion of remote services to prevent the spread of the new crown pneumonia epidemic and make every effort Protect the health and safety of consumers and employees.
