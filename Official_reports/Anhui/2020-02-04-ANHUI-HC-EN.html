Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection reported in Anhui Province on February 4

  • Source of information: Anhui Health Commission

At 04:00 on February 3, 2020, Anhui Province reported 72 new confirmed cases, 57 new suspected cases, 1 new critical case, and 7 newly discharged patients.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 6 were in Hefei, 3 in Huaibei, 9 in Luzhou, 2 in Suzhou, 6 in Bengbu, 14 in Fuyang, 1 in Huainan, 3 in Luzhou, 10 in Lu'an, 5 in Wuhu, and 1 in Tongling. There were 4 cases in Chizhou and 8 cases in Anqing. All of them were in stable condition.

Among the new suspected cases, 5 were in Hefei, 5 in Huaibei, 6 in Luzhou, 1 in Suzhou, 2 in Bengbu, 4 in Fuyang, 4 in Huainan, 1 in Luzhou, 18 in Lu'an, 2 in Ma'anshan, and 2 in Wuhu. And 7 cases of Anqing.

Among the newly added cured cases, 3 were from Hefei, 2 from Luzhou, 1 from Tongling, and 1 from Chizhou.

As of 24:00 on February 3, Anhui Province had reported a total of 480 confirmed cases, 14 cases were cured and discharged, and there were no deaths. Of the confirmed cases, 81 were in Hefei, 11 in Huaibei, 48 in Luzhou, 23 in Suzhou, 43 in Bengbu, 79 in Fuyang, 8 in Huainan, 11 in Luzhou, 30 in Lu'an, 28 in Ma'anshan, 21 in Wuhu, and Xuan There were 4 cases in the city, 19 cases in Tongling, 10 cases in Chizhou, 55 cases in Anqing, and 9 cases in Huangshan.

As of 24:00 on February 3, 466 confirmed cases were treated in hospital in Anhui Province, of which 5 were critical cases.

Six patients will be cured and discharged on February 4, including three in Fuyang and three in Anqing.

A total of 1,099 medical observations have been made in close contact.

During the epidemic, if you find that there is still slaughter of live poultry in the bazaar, the price of masks is extremely long, and the prices of vegetables are raised by traders, you can call the Market Supervision Administration Tel: 12315

If you find that there are crowded entertainment business places such as Internet cafes, KTVs, cinemas, and travel agencies, you can call the city's cultural market to report special telephones: 12318

If you find that the hot spring bath, swimming pool and life beauty pedicure are still open, you can call the health supervision telephone: 0551-63628215 0551-63624699.
