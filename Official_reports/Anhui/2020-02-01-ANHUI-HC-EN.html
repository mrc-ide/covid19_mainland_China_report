Update on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection reported in Anhui Province on February 1

  • Date: 2020-02-01 08:58
  • Source of information: Anhui Health Commission
  • Click : 3023 times
  • Font: Big Small ]
  • Print
  • close

At 04:00 on January 31, 2020, Anhui Province reported 60 new confirmed cases and 77 new suspected cases.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 9 were in Hefei, 4 in Huaibei, 5 in Luzhou, 2 in Suzhou, 1 in Bengbu, 9 in Fuyang, 2 in Luzhou, 4 in Lu'an, 8 in Ma'anshan, 1 in Wuhu, and 2 in Tongling. There were 13 cases in Anqing, all of which were stable.

Among the new suspected cases, 7 were in Hefei, 3 in Huaibei, 11 in Luzhou, 5 in Suzhou, 6 in Bengbu, 8 in Fuyang, 18 in Lu'an, 5 in Ma'anshan, 1 in Wuhu, 2 in Chizhou, and 11 in Anqing. .

As of 24:00 on January 31, Anhui Province had reported a total of 297 confirmed cases, 3 critical cases, 3 cumulative discharged patients, and no deaths. Among them, 59 were confirmed in Hefei, 6 in Huaibei, 25 in Luzhou, 12 in Suzhou, 16 in Bengbu, 47 in Fuyang, 6 in Huainan, 6 in Luzhou, 13 in Lu'an, 22 in Ma'anshan, and 15 in Wuhu There were 4 cases in Xuancheng, 16 cases in Tongling, 4 cases in Chizhou, 37 cases in Anqing and 9 cases in Huangshan.

As of 24:00 on January 31, 294 confirmed cases were being treated in hospital in Anhui Province. On February 1, 2 patients will be cured and discharged, 1 in Hefei and 1 in Luzhou.

A total of 5,967 close medical contacts were made.
