Epidemic of new coronavirus pneumonia reported in Anhui province on February 13

  • Date: 2020-02-13 08:47
  • Source of information: Anhui Health Commission
  • Click: 1861times
  • Font: 【 Big Small ]
  • Print
  •  close

At 02:00 on February 12, 2020, Anhui Province reported 22 new confirmed cases, 38 new suspected cases, 3 new critical cases, 1 new death case, and 20 new cured cases. .

Among the newly confirmed cases, 4 were in Hefei, 1 in Huaibei, 3 in Luzhou, 2 in Suzhou, 5 in Bengbu, 4 in Fuyang, 1 in Luzhou, 1 in Lu'an, and 1 in Tongling.

Among the new suspected cases, 4 were in Hefei, 3 in Huaibei, 7 in Luzhou, 3 in Suzhou, 9 in Bengbu, 6 in Lu'an, 2 in Ma'anshan, 3 in Chizhou, and 1 in Anqing.

Among the newly cured cases, 6 were discharged from Hefei, 1 from Huaibei, 5 from Luzhou, 4 from Suzhou, 2 from Maanshan, and 2 from Tongling.

As of 24:00 on February 12, a total of 910 confirmed cases (1 case of Huainan nuclear reduction) have been reported in Anhui Province, 128 cases have been cured and discharged, 5 cases have died, and 19,784 people have been in close contact with medical observation.

Of the cumulative confirmed cases, 161 were in Hefei, 25 in Huaibei, 100 in Luzhou, 38 in Suzhou, 146 in Bengbu, 142 in Fuyang, 23 in Huainan (1 case with nuclear reduction), 13 in Luzhou, 60 in Lu'an, and 32 in Maanshan. There were 31 cases in Wuhu, 6 cases in Xuancheng, 28 cases in Tongling, 16 cases in Chizhou, 80 cases in Anqing, and 9 cases in Huangshan.

As of 24:00 on February 12, 777 confirmed cases were treated in hospital in Anhui Province, of which 11 were critically ill cases.

Thirty-eight cases will be cured and discharged on February 13. This is the first time since the outbreak that the number of cases cured and discharged on the same day exceeded the number of confirmed cases the day before. Among them, 14 were in Anqing, 4 in Luzhou, 7 in Hefei, 1 in Tongling, 2 in Suzhou, 1 in Wuhu, 6 in Fuyang, 2 in Maanshan, and 1 in Bengbu.

Death case introduction: patient Wu Moumou, female, 69 years old. He was admitted to the Department of Respiratory Diseases of the Second Affiliated Hospital of Mengyi on January 27, and was tested positive for new coronavirus on January 28. He was transferred to the Infectious Diseases Hospital of Bengbu City for treatment. New crown pneumonia intensive treatment base hospital). The admission diagnosis was neo-pneumonitis (severe) with acute respiratory failure and type 2 diabetes. After admission, the hospital repeatedly organized multi-disciplinary consultations with provincial and hospital expert groups, and went all out to provide rescue, high-flow oxygen inhalation, antiviral, anti-infective, immunoglobulin, traditional Chinese medicine and symptomatic support and other comprehensive treatments. Rescued to death on February 12.
