Anhui Province Reports Epidemic Situation of New Crown Pneumonia on February 17

  • Date: 2020-02-17 08:25
  • Source of information: Anhui Health Commission
  • Click: 32183 times
  • Font: [Big small 】
  • print
  • shut down

At 04:00 on February 16, 2020, Anhui Province reported 11 new confirmed cases, 9 new suspected cases, and 34 new cured discharged cases.

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 3 cases in Lu'an, 2 in Hefei, 1 in Huaibei, 1 in Suzhou, 1 in Bengbu, 1 in Fuyang, 1 in Huainan, and 1 in Anqing.

Among the newly added suspected cases, 3 were in Hefei, 2 in Wuhu, 1 in Huaibei, 1 in Suzhou, 1 in Bengbu, and 1 in Fuyang.

Among the newly added and discharged cases, 10 were in Luzhou, 6 in Hefei, 5 in Fuyang, 4 in Bengbu, 2 in Suzhou, 2 in Wuhu, 2 in Chizhou, 1 in Huainan, 1 in Lu'an, and 1 in Anqing.

As of 24:00 on February 16th, Anhui Province had reported a total of 973 confirmed cases, 266 cured and discharged patients, 6 dead cases, 22,820 close medical contacts, and 3,860 people still in medical observation.

The cumulative confirmed cases include 171 cases in Hefei, 158 in Bengbu, 153 in Fuyang, 108 in Luzhou, 83 in Anqing, 68 in Lu'an, 40 in Suzhou, 36 in Maanshan, 31 in Wuhu, 28 in Tongling, and 27 in Huaibei. There were 25 cases in Huainan, 17 cases in Chizhou, 13 cases in Luzhou, 9 cases in Huangshan, and 6 cases in Xuancheng.

As of 24:00 on February 16th, 701 confirmed cases were treated in hospital in Anhui Province, including 12 critically ill cases.

27 cases were expected to be discharged on February 17, including 8 cases in Bengbu, 5 in Lu'an, 4 in Fuyang, 3 in Suzhou, 2 in Hefei, 2 in Maanshan, 2 in Huangshan, and 1 in Tongling.

A brief analysis of the epidemic situation: In the past week, the number of newly diagnosed cases of new crown pneumonia in Anhui Province has continued to decline, from 51 reported on February 9 to 11 on February 16. At the same time, the number of newly-discharged discharged patients increased significantly, and 207 patients were discharged from the 9th to the 16th. On the whole, the situation in the province's epidemic prevention and control situation has changed positively.

According to the development of the epidemic situation and the need for prevention and control, Anhui Province takes the county as a unit, according to the number of new cases in the county (city, district) in the past 3 days, the number of new cases in the last 7 days, the total number of cases, the number of clustered outbreaks, Local cases accounted for the risk level assessment, and divided the county level of the province into five risk levels: low risk, low risk, medium risk, high risk, and extremely high risk. Differentiated and precise prevention and control strategies were implemented in different areas. The results of the assessment were issued by the Office of the Comprehensive Command for Epidemic Prevention and Emergency Response of Anhui Province. The risk level assessment is carried out dynamically.
