A total of 5,868 close contacts were tracked, 1,079 medical observations have been lifted, and 4,789 people are still receiving medical observations.

Experts believe that at present, the overall situation of the city's epidemic situation is relatively stable, and imported cases are still the main cases, and local cases account for about 20%. The next few days will be a critical period for epidemic prevention and control, and citizens must not take it lightly. Preventing large-scale local spread and spread of imported cases is the key to future prevention and control. The trend of the epidemic is closely related to prevention and control measures and the coordination of citizens.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Commission has suggested that the new coronavirus is mainly transmitted through respiratory droplets and contact, and measures such as patient isolation and close contact isolation for medical observation are convenient to effectively control the epidemic.
