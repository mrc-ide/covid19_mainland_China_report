Situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing as of 24:00 on February 8, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-09 Source: Publicity United Front Office Views: Edit: Office review: Publicity United Front
Font size: small medium large üñ®

At 12:00 to 24:00 on February 8, 2020, Chongqing reported 18 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia (referred to as new coronary pneumonia), 4 new cases of severe cases, 4 cases of severe cases and 4 cases of ordinary cases, and severe cases became severe. There were 3 cases of type I, 1 case of severe cases converted to severe cases, 1 case of newly added severe cases, and 8 cases of discharged patients.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 6 were in Wanzhou District, 3 in Jiangbei District, 3 in Changshou District, 1 in Hechuan District, 2 in Yongchuan District, 1 in Tongliang District, 1 in Dianjiang County, and 1 in Wuxi County. Among the cases of increased weight, there was 1 case in Wanzhou District, 1 in Hechuan District, 1 in Taonan District, and 1 in Fengjie County. Among the severe cases converted to ordinary cases, 1 in Laoshan District, 1 in Kaizhou District, 1 in Zhongxian, 1 case in Fengjie County; 1 case in Jiangbei District, 1 case in Jiulongpo District, and 1 case in Yubei District; 1 case in Zhongjie County; 1 case in Wanzhou District One case was newly added: one in Wanzhou District, one in Yuzhong District, one in Jiulongpo District, one in Liangping District, two in Zhong County, and two in Yunyang County.

As of 24:00 on February 8th, Chongqing had reported a total of 446 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia; there were 29 severe cases, 20 severe cases, 2 death cases, and 39 discharged patients. A total of 14,765 close contacts have been traced, 8073 people have been released from medical observation, and 6,692 people are still receiving medical observation. Details are as follows:

Experts believe that at present, the new cases in Wanzhou District and other places are mainly imported second- and third-generation cases. The main urban area and the western part of Chongqing are mainly community-infected cases. Strengthen community investigation and close contact tracing and isolation to stop the epidemic. The path of spread is particularly important. Especially after resumption of work and resumption of labor, the increase in staff turnover and the strengthening of control over close contacts of confirmed cases and suspected cases is the top priority of epidemic prevention and control.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee has suggested that the community is the first line of defense for epidemic prevention and control. Only by implementing preventive and control measures from household to person, group prevention and control, and stable prevention and control can we effectively prevent the spread of the epidemic. The general public is requested to fully understand and actively support community investigation and prevention and control, and proactively cooperate with prevention and control measures such as community closed management. All close contacts should be medically observed for 14 days.
