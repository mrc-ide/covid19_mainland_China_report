A total of 7,327 close contacts have been tracked, 1,581 medical observations have been lifted, and 5,746 people are still receiving medical observations.

Experts believe that with the increasing number of second-generation cases, the epidemic situation is more complicated and severe. The second-generation cases are mostly caused by family transmission, and family cluster epidemics need to be paid close attention to. Early detection, early isolation, early treatment of the source of infection, and cut-off of the transmission route are crucial. This determines the development trend of the future epidemic situation, and prevention and control measures must not be relaxed.

Tips of the Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee: For your health and the health of others, you must wear a mask when you go out, especially in public confined spaces. Try to avoid going to crowded public places, not visiting relatives and friends, not visiting, not gathering, Party. Take good daily health protection, return from public places, cough your hands, cover your hands, before or after meals, wash your hands with hand sanitizer or soap running water, or use alcohol-free hand sanitizer; avoid using your hands when you are not sure whether your hands are clean Contact mouth, nose, and eyes. In case of fever, cough, etc., please wear a mask and consult a doctor immediately.
