March 8 Chongqing City New Crown Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control Network News Briefing
Release time: 2020-03-08 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a news conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia network in Chongqing, and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases6 cases

The Municipal Health and Health Committee reported that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city from 04:00 to 7 March, and 6 new cases were cured and discharged. As of March 7, there were 44 confirmed cases in the city (including 3 severe cases and 1 critically ill case), 6 death cases, 526 cured cases, and 576 confirmed cases. A total of 3,527 suspected cases have been ruled out, and 5 cases are still being investigated. A total of 23,654 close contacts have been tracked, 23,412 have been released from medical observation, and 242 are still receiving medical observation. 44 patients were treated in 4 centralized hospitals.

Continue to do a good job of partitioning and grading for precise prevention and control

Experts believe that there are no new confirmed cases in our city for 12 consecutive days and no new confirmed cases in 38 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days. The number of existing districts and counties with zero confirmed cases has increased to 22, and the situation of epidemic prevention and control continues to improve. . However, with the resumption of work and production and the restoration of transportation, the movement of people and crowds of people may bring the risk of epidemic transmission. The current prevention and control tasks are still arduous and arduous, and we must not relax. It is still necessary to continue to grasp the precise prevention and control of zoning and classification to prevent the occurrence of cluster epidemics.

Effectively strengthen epidemic prevention and control and medical treatment

The Municipal Health and Health Committee reported that since the outbreak, our city has closely focused on the requirements of "four early", "four concentrations" and "increasing treatment and cure rates, reducing infection rates and mortality rates," and effectively strengthening epidemic prevention and control and medical treatment jobs.

In terms of epidemic prevention and control,Focusing on the "four key points" of key areas, key populations, key places and key units, categorizing by districts, and implementing precise policies, effectively and orderly control the spread of the epidemic.For key areas such as high-risk areas,Emphasis is placed on the consultation and discussion of thematic conferences, expert guidance and special persons to supervise.Key groups such as returnees, close contacts, etc.Pay attention to giving play to the functions of the community, do a good job of migrating people, and strive not to miss one household or one person; clarify responsible persons, strictly implement standards and procedures for lifting isolation, handle abnormal situations well, and regulate close contact management.For airports, stations, terminals, shopping malls and other key places,Take detailed measures such as physical temperature screening, disinfection and ventilation, and personal protection to standardize the disposal process.For key units such as prisons and mental health institutions,Special documents were issued to strengthen the implementation of personnel access control, screening, protection, and referral and consultation systems.

In terms of medical treatment,Strengthen the overall coordination, improve the working mechanism, and deepen and implement the "four enhancements" of medical treatment management.Strengthen the unified command of the city medical treatment team,The municipal medical treatment team has uniformly formulated medical treatment technical guidelines, uniformly deployed the city's medical human resources, equipment, scarce protective supplies and drugs to ensure the needs of front-line medical treatment.Strengthening the main responsibility of designated hospitals,The main person in charge of the party and government of designated hospitals will be the first person responsible for medical treatment and strengthen overall planning and coordination; a deputy head of business will be involved in medical treatment throughout the process, and the logistics and human resources in the hospital will be enhanced.Strengthen the responsibility system of city-level expert team leaders,The leader of the municipal medical treatment expert group is in charge of the medical business of the four centralized treatment hospitals, organizes consultations, case discussions, on-site inspections, and rescue treatment, and supervises the implementation of various tasks.Strengthening the integrated operation mechanism of medical prevention and prevention,Strengthen the linkage of data and information to ensure the accuracy of the data; strengthen the linkage of policies and measures to ensure that there is no disconnection between prevention and control and medical treatment; strengthen the linkage of work to ensure the overall advancement of epidemic prevention and control and treatment.
