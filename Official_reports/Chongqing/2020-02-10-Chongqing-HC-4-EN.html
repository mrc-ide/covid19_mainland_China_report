good news! Nine confirmed cases were discharged in Chongqing, and a total of 60 have been discharged.
Release time: 2020-02-10 Source: Publicity United Front Office Views: Edit: Office review: Publicity United Front Office
Font size: small medium large ðŸ–¨

Mr. Lu, 40 years old, was seen at the Zhongxian People's Hospital on January 25. On January 29, he was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital. After consultation with the expert group, he was diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia.

Qin, male, 44 years old, was admitted to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital on January 24, and was diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia after consultation with an expert group.

Zou, male, 39 years old, was seen at Kaixian People's Hospital on January 19. On January 30, he was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital. After consultation with the expert group, he was diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia.

Ren Mou, male, 51 years old, was seen at the First People's Hospital of Wanzhou District on January 19. On January 24, he was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital. After consultation with the expert group, he was diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia.

Cui, female, 39 years old, was seen at the Shizhu County People's Hospital on January 29. He was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital on February 1 and was diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia after consultation with the expert group.

Luo, male, 32 years old, was seen at the Shizhu County People's Hospital on January 29. He was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital on February 1. After consultation with the expert group, he was diagnosed with new coronavirus pneumonia.

Wang, male, 45 years old, was seen at Yunyang County People's Hospital on January 28. He was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital on February 1. After consultation with the expert group, he was diagnosed with new coronavirus pneumonia.

Wang, female, 16 years old, was seen in Shanghai Hospital of Wanzhou District on January 27. He was transferred to Chongqing Three Gorges Central Hospital on February 1 and was diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia after consultation with the expert group.

Li, male, 31 years old, was seen at the Laoshan District People's Hospital in Chongqing on January 27, and was subsequently diagnosed with a new type of coronavirus pneumonia. On January 30, he was transferred to Yongchuan Hospital, Chongqing Medical University.

The above nine patients were carefully diagnosed and treated by the medical staff of the designated medical institution. The expert group evaluated them and found that they met the latest standards for the removal and discharge of new cases of confirmed coronavirus pneumonia from the National Health and Health Commission, and were discharged today.

At present, a total of 60 patients with new-type coronavirus pneumonia have been cured and discharged.
