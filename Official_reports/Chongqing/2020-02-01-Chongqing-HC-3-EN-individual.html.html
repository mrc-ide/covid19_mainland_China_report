good news! One more confirmed patient was discharged from Chongqing today, and two patients are currently discharged
Release time: 2020-02-01 Source: Publicity United Front Office Views: Edit: Office review: Publicity United Front Office
Font size: small medium large

Sun Moumou, male, 46 years old, developed fever, cough and other symptoms on January 22, and then went to Fengdu County People's Hospital for treatment and was confirmed to be a confirmed patient with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Under the careful diagnosis and treatment of medical staff, the patient's condition gradually improved, and respiratory symptoms improved markedly. The two new coronavirus nucleic acid tests were negative. After being evaluated by medical experts at Chongqing and county levels, they were in line with the latest version of the new type of coronation of the National Health and Health Commission. Virus-infected pneumonia cases were released from isolation and discharged today, and cured today.

This is the second patient diagnosed with pneumonitis infected with a new coronavirus cured in Chongqing.
