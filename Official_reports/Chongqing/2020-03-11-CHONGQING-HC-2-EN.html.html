Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 11
Release time: 2020-03-11 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing (forty-sixth session), and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases5 cases

The Municipal Health and Health Committee informed that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city from 00:00 to 20:00 on March 10, and 5 new cured cases were discharged. As of March 10, there were 23 confirmed cases (no severe and critical cases) in the hospital, 6 deaths, 547 cured cases, and 576 confirmed cases. A total of 3529 suspected cases have been ruled out, and 3 cases are still being investigated. A total of 23,680 close contacts have been tracked, 23,577 have been released from medical observation, and 103 people are still receiving medical observation.

Staged results of epidemic prevention and control

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that experts believe that since the outbreak of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, our city has implemented strict prevention and control measures of "foreign import prevention and internal non-proliferation" and extensively mobilized the whole society to participate. After hard work, there are no communities and Local outbreaks, all districts and counties have no new confirmed cases for 15 consecutive days, the number of districts and counties with zero confirmed cases has increased to 28, and the number of confirmed cases has decreased to 23. There are no severe and critical cases, the epidemic prevention and control work has achieved periodic results, and the situation of prevention and control continues to improve.

Risk of spread of epidemic input due to movement and concentration of personnel still exists

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that experts emphasized that with the accelerated recovery of production and living order, the number of returnees from Chongqing at home and abroad will increase, and the risk of spread of epidemic input caused by the movement and gathering of people will still exist. We must be cautious at the beginning, pay close attention to various prevention and control measures, strictly prevent new imported risks and local disseminated risks, and resolutely prevent the epidemic from rebounding. It is necessary to adhere to the strategy of zoning and differentiated prevention and control, do a good job of dynamic research and analysis of the epidemic situation, implement precise strategies, and scientifically prevent and control. Continue to do a good job of preventing and controlling public places such as airports, terminals, shopping malls, implement measures for party and government agencies, enterprises and institutions, and strengthen prevention and control in key places such as nursing homes, mental health institutions, welfare homes, and prisons.

Experts remind that New Coronary Pneumonia is an infectious disease mainly caused by respiratory droplets and contact transmission. The general public should strengthen self-protection so as not to gather or get together; develop good hygiene habits, wear masks, wash hands frequently, and avoid dirty hands. Touch the mouth, eyes and nose, not spitting; keep your home or workplace clean and ventilated. At the same time, self-health monitoring should be done well. If fever, cough, fatigue and other symptoms occur, please wear a mask and see a doctor in time.
