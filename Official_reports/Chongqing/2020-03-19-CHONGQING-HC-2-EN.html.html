Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March
Release time: 2020-03-19 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big 🖨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing (56th session), and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the situation.

The risk of outbreak import and transmission cannot be ignored

The Municipal Health and Health Committee reported that as of 24:00 on March 18, all districts and counties in our city had no new confirmed cases for 23 consecutive days, and no confirmed cases or suspected cases have been confirmed in the hospital. The spread of the epidemic is accelerating, and the large-scale population movement brought about by the resumption of economic activities. The risks of importing and spreading the epidemic cannot be ignored. Therefore, the string of epidemic prevention and control cannot be relaxed, and the vigilance of the epidemic cannot be reduced. We must continue to work hard and continue to strengthen the quarantine at entry ports, strictly prevent the import of overseas epidemics, implement the district-level precision prevention and control measures, and cut off the transmission channels. Prevent the risk of local infection.

Outbreak prevention and control and basic medical care"Grab with both hands"

According to the Municipal Health and Health Committee, since the outbreak of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, the city's three-level health service network, which is mainly composed of counties and villages, has responded quickly. The primary health service system that combines prevention and treatment has played a very important role in preventing and controlling the epidemic and ensuring daily medical services. Role, held the first line of defense.

The first is to carry out community investigations.During the epidemic, all the staff at the basic medical and health institutions gave up their vacations and stayed on the job. They formed special classes with the police, village cadres, and grid staff, and divided 1,063 grids in township (street) medical and health institutions. The community and the door of the house carried out a pull-net, carpet-type rolling community investigation, and a total of 17,501 people with suspicious symptoms were found, and 283 cases were confirmed.

The second is to be a "three divisions and one member."The city's 8,187 family doctor teams carried out health follow-ups by phone, WeChat, and family doctor signing an APP to guide personal protection, household disinfection and other matters, do psychological counseling, popularize prevention and control information and protection knowledge, and be a good “family health Teachers, frequent therapists of common diseases, rehabilitators in the recovery period, and guides for referrals to doctors, "so that early detection, early reporting, early isolation, and early treatment of epidemics can be achieved.

The third is to do a good job of health management of key populations.Focus on the health management of the elderly, pregnant women, children, the disabled, as well as key populations such as patients with hypertension, diabetes, tuberculosis and severe mental disorders, and poor households who set up a file; they must not use information means for basic diseases or mobility problems Key personnel for effective communication provide on-site services; for patients with chronic diseases who have “clear diagnosis, stable condition, and need to take drugs for a long time”, they can prescribe medication for up to 3 months at a time according to the situation, reducing the number of visits to residents and preventing staff gathering.

The fourth is to take overall planning into consideration, and "emphasis on both sides" of epidemic prevention and control and basic medical treatment.Established prevention and control technical service team and resumption health service team to provide prevention and control technical guidance and health certification services for reworked enterprises and migrant workers. A total of 68400 industrial enterprises above designated size and service industry companies provided outbreak prevention and control guidance and issued health certificates. 4.278 million copies of certification. While doing a good job of preventing and controlling the epidemic, gradually restore daily medical services. Up to now, a total of 39 districts and counties (including Liangjiang New District, Wansheng Economic Development Zone, and High-tech District) have resumed the diagnosis and treatment services of grass-roots medical and health institutions; and 2 districts and counties are gradually restoring grass-roots clinics and clinics.
