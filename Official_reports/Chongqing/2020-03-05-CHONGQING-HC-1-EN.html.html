Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 5
Release time: 2020-03-05 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ğŸ–¨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing (forty sessions), and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city about the epidemic situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases12 cases

The Municipal Health and Health Committee informed that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia and 12 new cured patients discharged from hospital at 04:00 on March 4. As of 24:00 on March 4, there were 68 confirmed cases in the city (including 3 severe cases and 2 critical cases), 6 deaths, 502 cured cases, and 576 confirmed cases. example. A total of 3523 suspected cases have been ruled out, and 2 cases are still being investigated. A total of 23,612 close contacts have been traced, 23,318 medical observations have been lifted, and 294 are still receiving medical observations. 68 patients were treated in 4 centralized hospitals.

Resolutely prevent epidemic rebound

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that experts believe that as of March 4, there were no new confirmed cases in our city for 9 consecutive days, and no new confirmed cases in 30 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days. The number has increased to 16, and the situation of epidemic prevention and control continues to improve. However, we are facing new changes in epidemic prevention and control such as resumption of work and resumption of production, restoration of the order of daily life, and an increase in the number of people returning to Chongqing. We must strengthen the management and control of people coming to Chongqing from key areas in accordance with the requirements of different risk levels for prevention and control, and do a good job abroad. Chongqing personnel health monitoring and service management, do a good job of dynamic management of rework and return to work, and resolutely prevent the epidemic rebound.

Gradually restore daily medical services

According to the Municipal Health and Health Committee, in order to implement the municipal Party committee and municipal government's deployment of district-level precision prevention and control, the Municipal Health and Health Committee adhered to the "double grasp" of epidemic prevention and control and daily medical services, strengthened measures, and strengthened the quality of medical services and the management of medical safety. Continuously meet the basic medical needs of the masses.

On February 25, the Municipal Health and Health Committee held a special conference to study and strengthen the management of medical services during the outbreak, to meet the basic medical treatment needs of the masses, and to guide districts and counties to gradually restore daily medical services in accordance with the principle of district policy. As of March 4, a total of 221 public hospitals in the city resumed daily medical services (recovery rate 96.09%), and 502 private hospitals resumed daily medical services (recovery rate 81.36%).

During this period, the Municipal Health and Health Committee guided various types of medical institutions, including general hospitals, traditional Chinese medicine hospitals, maternal and child health hospitals, grass-roots medical institutions, and social medical institutions to implement the main responsibilities, formulate work implementation plans, and refine the implementation of pre-inspection and triage. , Fever, general outpatient, emergency, general hospitalization, emergency surgery, elective surgery, medical technical logistics, and other specific work requirements to ensure that no cluster epidemic in hospitals and medical staff infections do not occur. At the same time, the system of graded diagnosis and treatment, "Internet + medical treatment", etc. was implemented, non-emergency appointment registration and appointment inspection were implemented, and patients were guided to order clinics in order to reduce staff gathering. Optimize the outpatient and emergency pre-trial triage process, strictly implement the "one person, one diagnosis and one room", and reduce cross infection. Insist on ensuring the medical needs of different patients, attach importance to and strengthen the regular maintenance treatment of patients with renal failure and tumors, do a good job in the timely treatment of critically ill patients, and strengthen the daily medical service supply of key groups such as pregnant women, children, and chronic patients.

Vigorously carry out patriotic health"Six Actions"

According to the Municipal Health and Health Committee, since the outbreak of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, our city has given full play to the traditional advantages of patriotic sanitation and organized the implementation of cleanliness of the environment of institutions, enterprises and institutions, cleanup of the community and rural environment, comprehensive improvement of the market environment, cleanliness of other key places, and cleanup of the home environment Patriotic health â€œsix major actionsâ€ such as popularization of science and popularization, strengthen regular cleaning and disinfection of public goods and public areas, standardize the prevention and control of vector organisms in key areas, carry out cleaning of densely populated places and the surrounding environment, and regularly clear garbage and waste , Actively promote the centralized slaughter of live birds, cold chain transportation, white bar chilled fresh market and other work, effectively improve the public health environment. At the same time, actively advocate the masses to maintain good living habits, guide the masses to develop civilized health habits and healthy lifestyles such as hand washing, more ventilation, and not eating wild animals, comprehensively improve awareness of prevention and control and self-protection capabilities, and do a good job of individuals and families Members' health monitoring has played an important role in preventing and controlling the epidemic situation of new coronary pneumonia.
