Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on February 25, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-25 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

At 04:00 on February 24, 2020, Chongqing reported 1 new confirmed case of new coronary pneumonia, and 14 new cases were cured and discharged.

The newly confirmed cases were 1 in Jiulongpo District; among the newly cured cases, 6 were in Wanzhou District, 1 in Hechuan District, 1 in Qijiang District, 2 in Kaizhou District, 1 in Fengjie County, 1 in Wushan County, and There was 1 case in Xi County and 1 case in High-tech Zone.

As of 24:00 on February 24, there were 221 confirmed cases in hospital in Chongqing (including 19 severe cases and 8 critical cases), a total of 6 deaths, a total of 349 discharged patients cured, and a cumulative report of 576 example. A total of 23,350 close contacts have been tracked, 22,447 medical observations have been lifted, and 903 people are still receiving medical observations. Details are as follows:

Experts believe that as of February 24, the number of newly diagnosed cases in our city has maintained a single digit for the 11th consecutive day. There are no new confirmed cases in 19 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days, and no new confirmed cases in 7 districts and counties for 7 consecutive days The positive situation of epidemic prevention and control is expanding. However, with the gradual restoration of production and living order, the number of people returning to Chongqing will gradually increase, and the possibility of personnel movement and gathering will increase, especially the gathering of people brought by the market. There is a potential risk of further spread of the epidemic, and epidemic prevention and control is facing a rebound Great pressure.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee reminded that the current epidemic situation is still severe and complex, and prevention and control are at the most critical stage. We must be highly vigilant to paralyzed thoughts, war exhaustion, fluke, and relaxation mentality, or we will have serious consequences and even lose our previous achievements. The implementation of zoning and classification prevention and control is not to relax the prevention and control, but to ensure that both the epidemic prevention and control and economic development are right. The general public is still insisting on going out, wearing face masks, washing hands frequently, not gathering meals, and not gathering. If you have suspicious symptoms such as fever, cough, or fatigue, consult your doctor promptly.
