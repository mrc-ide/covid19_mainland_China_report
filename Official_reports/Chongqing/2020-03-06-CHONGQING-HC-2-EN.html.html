Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 6
Release time: 2020-03-06 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big 🖨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing (forty-first session), and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases10 cases

The Municipal Health and Health Commission reported that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city at 04:00 on March 5th. There were no new cases on the 10th day, and 10 new cases were cured and discharged. As of 24:00 on March 5, there were 58 confirmed cases in the city (including 4 severe cases and 1 critically ill case), a total of 576 confirmed cases were reported, 512 cured cases were discharged, and 6 were dead cases. example. A total of 3523 suspected cases have been ruled out, and 4 suspected cases are still being investigated. A total of 23,620 close contacts have been tracked, 23,351 have been released from medical observation, and 269 are still receiving medical observation. Fifty-eight patients were admitted to the four centralized hospitals.

Resolutely prevent the resurgence of the epidemic

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that experts believe that as of March 5, there were no new confirmed cases in our city for 10 consecutive days, and no new confirmed cases in 34 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days. A total of 18; 512 patients were discharged from the hospital with a discharge rate of 88.9%. The situation of epidemic prevention and control continued to improve. However, with the resumption of work and production, the restoration of the order of daily life, the increase in the number of people returning to Chongqing, and the increase in the flow and gathering of people, it has brought new pressure on the prevention and control of the epidemic, and the risk of a rebound in the epidemic has always existed. The vigilance of the epidemic cannot be reduced, and the requirements for prevention and control cannot be reduced. We must continue to pay close attention to various prevention and control measures and resolutely prevent the resurgence of the epidemic.

Continuous dispatch1636 members of 18 medical teams went to Hubei

The Municipal Health and Health Committee emphasized that General Secretary Xi Jinping pointed out that Hubei and Wuhan are the top priorities for epidemic prevention and control, and the decisive place to win the fight against epidemic prevention and control. Wuhan wins Hubei, Hubei wins national victory. Our city moved by hearing the orders. Health administrative departments and medical institutions at all levels talk about politics and take the overall situation into consideration, overcome difficulties, and take urgent action and deployment. The majority of medical workers have stepped forward to settle their homes and take care of everyone. The decision-making and deployment of the Central Government and the State Council will give the city's strength to fully support Hubei Province in carrying out the prevention and control of new crown pneumonia and medical treatment.

According to the introduction of the Municipal Health and Health Committee, in accordance with the requirements of Secretary Chen Min'er, "Hubei needs urgently, do everything Chongqing can do", since January 26, the city has dispatched 18 batches of 1,636 medical teams to Hubei. 6th in the country. The 1,636 team members are located at the front lines of fighting the epidemic in 16 hospitals and 7 CDCs in Wuhan, Xiaogan, Jingmen and Tianmen, Hubei Province. Among them, there are 7 batches of 812 members in Xiaogan City, including doctors, nurses, disease control and management personnel; 9 batches of 815 members in Wuhan City, including doctors, nurses and management personnel; in Jingmen and Tianmen City, each batch has 9 diseases Control staff. The scale of the support for the Hubei medical team is the largest support operation for the outsourcing of medical personnel in the city's history, involving a total of 13 city-level medical institutions in the city and 89 medical institutions in 37 districts and counties.

In supporting Hubei to carry out the treatment of new crown pneumonia, the medical team of our city has effectively implemented the "four mornings", strengthened medical treatment and epidemic situation treatment, and improved the treatment rate and cure rate. In accordance with the "four concentration" principle, we have made great efforts to provide medical treatment for severely ill patients. Strengthening nosocomial infection control, further reducing the case fatality rate and infection rate, making a "Chongqing contribution" to defending the people's health in Hubei and decisively fighting the epidemic, and using actual actions to practice "respect life, save lives, be dedicated, and love big" "Professionalism. As of 24:00 on March 4, the Hubei Medical Team was responsible for 4,346 beds, accumulatively managing 4,152 patients, including 390 critically ill patients, 95 critically ill patients, a total of 1,616 discharged patients, and a total of 3,334 nursing patients. There were 8,198 virus samples, and 251 cases were completed for epidemiological investigation. Among them, the Xiaogan Medical Team was responsible for 1,479 beds and 1,257 patients were managed, of which 173 were critically ill, 47 were critically ill, 771 were discharged, and 1,257 were nursing patients; 2867 were supported by the Wuhan Medical Team A total of 2,895 patients were managed, including 217 critically ill patients, 48 critically ill patients, 845 discharged from hospital, and 2,077 nursing patients. Recently, the two supporting medical teams in Hubei, including the two medical teams in Hubei and Wang Jie, were awarded the National Health and Health System Advanced Collective for Prevention and Control of the National Health and Health System, the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security, and the State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine. ,outstanding person.

Continue to maintain first-level response

The person in charge of the Municipal Health and Health Committee said in response to a reporter's question that since late February, more than a dozen provinces and cities across the country have successively adjusted the response level to public health emergencies, and some citizens are more concerned about how the city will adjust. In fact, the city's epidemic prevention and control work is always based on the overall epidemic situation and the central requirements, Chongqing's actual situation, to carry out timely research and judgment, dynamic adjustment. At present, the city continues to maintain a first-level response on the basis of organizing relevant experts to conduct a comprehensive and comprehensive evaluation of the epidemic situation. This is mainly based on the following considerations.

The first is to continue to maintain a high degree of vigilance against the epidemic. General Secretary Xi Jinping emphasized that in order to clearly understand the complexity of the current epidemic prevention and control and the economic and social development situation, strengthening the epidemic prevention and control must be prudent, the vigilance against the epidemic cannot be reduced, and the requirements for prevention and control cannot be reduced. Combining with the actual situation of our city, we will continue to maintain a first-level response at this time, which will help the city's epidemic prevention and control work continue to be carried out.

Second, the city's external defense input tasks are still heavy. The central government pointed out that the epidemic prevention and control tasks in Hubei and Wuhan are still arduous and arduous. It is necessary to continue to maintain the prevention and control strategies of internal proliferation prevention and external prevention output, and strictly control the Lihan and Lihe channels. 53 towns in 9 districts and counties of our city are bordered by Hubei Province. The mountains and rivers are connected, the humanities are intimate with each other, and the economic exchanges are frequent. It is necessary to continue to do a good job of preventing and controlling the epidemic from being imported into our city. At the same time, the epidemic situation in some countries and regions has been severe recently. The city is more open to the outside world and there is also greater pressure to prevent overseas imports. Therefore, maintaining a first-level response is conducive to continuing to do a good job in preventing and controlling epidemic import at home and abroad.

Third, the task of non-proliferation in the city cannot be relaxed. With the resumption of work and production in industrial enterprises and business service enterprises, government agencies and institutions go to work, business activities increase, and the risk of spreading the epidemic caused by personnel mobility increases. Continue to maintain a first-level response, which is conducive to strengthening the management and control of people who come to Chongqing from key areas, do a good job of health monitoring and service management of overseas people who enter Chongqing, and do a good job of dynamic management and community management of reworkers and returnees.

At the same time, in accordance with the central government's decision-making and deployment on “outlining priorities, taking overall planning into consideration, classified guidance, and partitioned strategies,” according to the guidance of the State Council ’s joint prevention and control mechanism, the city will implement hierarchical classification prevention and control from February 19, with districts and counties as the The units are divided into low-risk, medium-risk, and high-risk districts and counties, and differentiated prevention and control is implemented. During the implementation process, the city's epidemic prevention and control headquarters made dynamic adjustments to the list of risky districts and counties on the basis of scientific research and judgment.

The Central Leadership Group response to the epidemic situation requires that other regions of the country except Hubei should adjust the emergency response level according to the actual situation, combine with the county's differentiated prevention and control, accurately implement various prevention and control measures after resumption and production, and reduce unnecessary crowds. Orderly restore normal order of production and life. At present, the situation of epidemic prevention and control in our city continues to improve, and efforts are being made to promote the resumption of production and production of enterprises to normal production and operation, and the recovery of production and living order is accelerating. In the next step, the municipal epidemic prevention and control headquarters will obey the overall situation of national epidemic prevention and control, and according to the actual situation in Chongqing, scientifically judge and adjust the public health emergency response level in a timely manner.
