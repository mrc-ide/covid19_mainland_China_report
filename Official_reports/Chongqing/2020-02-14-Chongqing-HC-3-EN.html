good news! Another 11 confirmed patients were discharged from Chongqing, and a total of 139 patients have been discharged.
Release time: 2020-02-14 Source: Publicity United Front Office Views: Edit: Office review: Publicity United Front
Font size: small medium large üñ®

good news! Eleven confirmed patients were discharged from Chongqing, including 4 in Wanzhou District, 1 in Luannan District, 2 in Zhongxian County, 1 in Yunyang County, 1 in Fengjie County, and 2 in Shizhu County.

The above 11 patients, after careful diagnosis and treatment by designated hospitals and medical staff, were evaluated by the expert group and were deemed to meet the latest standards for the removal and discharge of new cases of confirmed coronavirus pneumonia from the National Health and Health Commission.

At present, a total of 139 patients with new-type coronavirus pneumonia have been cured and discharged.
