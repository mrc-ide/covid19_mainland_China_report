Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on February 27, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-27 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

At 04:00 on February 26, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Chongqing, and 12 new cases were cured and discharged.

Among the newly cured cases, there were 4 cases in Wanzhou District, 2 cases in Jiangbei District, 1 case in Shapingba District, 1 case in Changshou District, 1 case in Wuxi County, 1 case in Pengshui County, and 2 cases in Liangjiang New District.

As of 24:00 on February 26, Chongqing has 186 confirmed cases in hospital (including 12 severe cases and 6 critically ill cases), a total of 6 deaths, 384 cured and discharged patients, and 576 confirmed cases. example. A total of 23,441 close contacts have been tracked, 22,701 medical observations have been lifted, and 740 are still receiving medical observations. Details are as follows:

Experts believe that as of February 26, there were no new confirmed cases in our city for two consecutive days, and the number of districts and counties with zero confirmed cases in the hospital increased to 10, and the epidemic situation is positive. However, we have to be sober to see that there may be confirmed cases among suspected cases, close contacts, and asymptomatic infections that are currently being investigated; there are still community-infected cases in newly confirmed cases recently, suggesting that there may be "walking Source of infection "; with the gradual resumption of work and production, and the increase in the number of people returning to Chongqing, the aggregate and import risks have increased significantly. We must overcome the war exhaustion, implement all kinds of prevention and control measures, and promote the continued improvement of the epidemic situation.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee has suggested that various localities and departments should strictly implement measures for prevention and control of zoning and classification. Communities and grass-roots medical and health institutions shall implement grid-based, carpet-type management in accordance with the requirements of the community prevention and control work plan, and do a good job in registering and investigating returnees. All enterprises and institutions should earnestly implement the management of employees during work and rest periods and implement various prevention and control measures, and make every effort to reduce the occurrence of cluster epidemics.
