Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 9
Release time: 2020-03-09 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference (44th session) on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing, and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases1 case

The Municipal Health and Health Committee informed that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city and one new case of discharged hospitals. As of March 8, there were 43 confirmed cases in the hospital (including 3 severe cases and 1 critically ill case), 6 death cases, 527 cured cases, and 576 confirmed cases. A total of 3,527 suspected cases have been ruled out, and 4 cases are still being investigated. A total of 23,668 close contacts have been traced, 23,477 medical observations have been lifted, and 191 are still receiving medical observations. 43 patients were treated in 4 centralized hospitals.

Preventing the spread of epidemic input

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that experts believe that as of March 8, there were no new confirmed cases in our city for 13 consecutive days, and no new confirmed cases in 40 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days. The number has increased to 22, and the situation of epidemic prevention and control continues to improve. However, with the resumption of work and production by government agencies, institutions and institutions, the normal order of production and living, the increase in the flow of personnel, the increase of imported risks and aggregated risks, the city's epidemic prevention and control is still facing considerable risks and pressures. We must be highly vigilant against paralyzed thoughts, war exhaustion, fluke, and renewed mentality, continue to strictly implement differentiated prevention and control measures, and accurately and orderly promote resumption of work and production and market. It is necessary to strengthen the classified management of communities, improve the scientificness and accuracy of management measures, and ensure that community personnel are well managed and mobile. It is necessary to strictly prevent the import risk of the epidemic, increase the service management of returnees and returnees and return to work in key areas at home and abroad, and prevent the spread of the epidemic.

Communicating Reports on Epidemic Prevention and Control7 advanced collectives

According to the Municipal Health and Health Committee, after the epidemic, the majority of medical workers in the city have a small family for everyone. Some went in the opposite direction and worked hard, always staying on the front line of prevention, treatment and treatment; some said goodbye to their families and offered their help, and Xingye made an emergency trip to Hubei.
