Situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing as of 12:00 on February 10, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-10 Source: Publicity United Front Office Views: Edit: Office review: Publicity United Front Office
Font size: small medium large ğŸ–¨

At 02:00 on February 10, 2020, Chongqing reported 5 new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia and 9 new discharged cases.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 1 was in Wanzhou District, 2 in Banan District, and 2 in Dazu District; among the newly discharged cases, 3 were in Wanzhou District, 1 in Laoshan District, 1 in Kaizhou District, 1 in Zhong County, There were 1 case in Yang County and 2 cases in Shizhu County.

As of 12:00 on February 10, Chongqing had reported a total of 473 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia; 34 severe cases, 22 severe cases, 2 deaths, and 60 discharged patients.
