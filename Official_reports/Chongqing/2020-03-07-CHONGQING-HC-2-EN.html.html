March 7 Chongqing City New Crown Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention Work Network News Ventilation Society Outbreak Report
Release time: 2020-03-07 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big 🖨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a news conference on the network of Chongqing New Crown Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control, and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the epidemic situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases8 cases

According to reports, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city from 04:00 to 6 March, and 8 new cases were cured and discharged. As of March 6, there were 50 confirmed cases in the city (including 3 severe cases and 1 critically ill case), 6 death cases, 520 cured cases, and 576 confirmed cases. A total of 3,524 suspected cases have been ruled out, and 7 cases are still being investigated. A total of 23,640 close contacts have been tracked, 23,375 have been released from medical observation, and 265 are still receiving medical observation. Fifty patients were admitted to the four centralized hospitals.

Resolutely prevent cluster outbreaks

Experts believe that as of March 6, there were no new confirmed cases in our city for 11 consecutive days, and no new confirmed cases in 37 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days. The number of existing districts and counties with zero confirmed cases has increased to 20. 520 patients were discharged from the hospital with a cure rate of 90.3%. The situation of epidemic prevention and control continued to improve. However, with the resumption of work and production by government agencies, institutions and institutions, the risk of spreading the epidemic caused by the increase in the flow and concentration of personnel is increasing. We must continue to be highly vigilant and sober-minded, strictly implement the requirements for precise prevention and control of zoning and classification, and improve the pertinence and effectiveness of epidemic prevention and control. It is necessary to increase the service management of key populations such as returnees from Chongqing in key areas at home and abroad, continue to do a good job of community management, and strictly prevent imported risks; strengthen the management of key places such as prisons, nursing homes, and welfare homes, and resolutely prevent cluster epidemics occur.

Suspected cases will be prolonged

According to the report, in accordance with the requirements of the National New Coronavirus Diagnosis and Treatment Program (Seventh Edition), "suspected cases have been negative for two consecutive new coronavirus nucleic acid tests (sampling time interval of at least 24 hours) and 7 days after onset Globulin M (IgM) and immunoglobulin G (IgG) are still negative to rule out suspected case diagnosis. " With this requirement, the elimination of suspected cases takes longer than in the past.

Outbreak prevention and control and daily healthcare services"Grasping with both hands"”

The Municipal Health and Health Committee stated that in accordance with the work requirements of the Municipal Party Committee and the Municipal Government, the people's lives and health are first and foremost, and medical care services for key populations such as pregnant women and newborns are attached great importance to prevent and control epidemics. Health care services are "two-handed" and "two-handed" to ensure the safety of mothers and infants.

In terms of epidemic prevention and control, efforts were made to build a rescue system.Fever clinics set up in the city, designated treatment hospitals in districts and counties, and centralized treatment hospitals in districts have fully considered maternal health and treatment work.Give full play to the role of experts,Establish a multidisciplinary expert linkage mechanism for obstetrics, pediatrics, respiratory, critical illness, infection, etc. The expert advisory group is responsible for the city's technical guidance; the on-site expert group provides on-site guidance to the centralized treatment hospital, participates in the rescue and consultation of severe and critically ill patients, and conducts tours. guide.Improve emergency response procedures,Formulate work plans, put forward detailed requirements and disposal methods from 12 aspects such as emergency treatment, protection, neonatal treatment, postpartum management, etc., and guide the midwifery organization to carry out the work, strengthen the hospital's sense prevention and control, and minimize cross infection.

In terms of safeguarding the demand for normal medical services, strengthen daily diagnosis and treatment management,Fever clinics and emergency obstetrics and gynecology are available 24 hours. Each midwifery organization optimizes the outpatient process and waiting environment in a timely manner to ensure "one person, one clinic and one room" in outpatient clinics; sets up single wards and independent channels for obstetrics, reserves sufficient isolation wards, and implements "one person, one companion" for hospitalization.Focus on health coaching,Develop guidelines for maternal health during epidemic prevention and control, and further clarify birth inspection items, inspection time, and protection requirements for the four periods of early pregnancy, second trimester, third trimester, postpartum, and puerperium to ensure the safety of mothers and infants.Strengthen mission guidance,The midwifery agency issues epidemic prevention and control measures through WeChat, APP, phone, video, online pregnant women's school, etc., provides online counseling, guides pregnant women to do home self-monitoring and personal protection, and guides pregnant women to reasonably arrange the inspection period according to their own circumstances.
