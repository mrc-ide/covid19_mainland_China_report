Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 25

date:2020-03-25
Biginsmall

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing (sixtieth session), and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the epidemic situation.

No new confirmed cases of imported new coronary pneumonia

The Municipal Health and Health Committee reported that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia locally in Chongqing and no new confirmed imported cases of new coronary pneumonia from 04:00 on March 24th. As of March 24, there were no confirmed cases or suspected cases in Chongqing; 2 cases of overseas imported confirmed cases were reported, and they were all treated in the hospital. A total of 24,038 close contacts have been traced, 23,696 medical observations have been lifted, there are 342 people are undergoing medical observation. There were no newly confirmed cases in the city for 29 consecutive days, and all districts and counties were low-risk areas. The situation of epidemic prevention and control continued to improve, and overall efforts to promote epidemic prevention and control and economic and social development have achieved positive results.

Further work on epidemic prevention and control and medical treatment

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that the current pandemic situation is spreading globally, and the risk of importing from overseas continues to increase. At the same time, the risk of rebounding from the epidemic caused by the increase in the flow of people and gathering still exists. Strategies to further do a good job in epidemic prevention and control and medical treatment, and promote the comprehensive and rapid restoration of production and living order.

The first is to implement detailed measures to prevent and control the import of overseas epidemics. Strengthen port quarantine, strengthen health declaration, temperature detection, nucleic acid detection and isolation management of returnees from Chongqing, conduct epidemiological investigations and close contact tracing of confirmed imported cases in a timely manner, and effectively implement closed-loop management to ensure that The outbreak was controlled within a limited scope.

The second is to implement key measures for internal defense rebound. We will strengthen the screening of fever clinics in medical institutions, and do a good job in the prevention and control of communities, enterprises, and schools, as well as the health management of personnel. Once an epidemic situation is discovered, it should be reported in a timely manner, accurately controlled, and dealt with decisively to prevent the epidemic from spreading.

The third is to strengthen medical treatment of confirmed cases and improve the cure rate. Recently, our city reported 2 confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia. The Municipal Health and Health Committee dispatched medical treatment specialists for the first time. After consultation with the two-level expert team in the urban area, both cases were mild cases of neocoronary pneumonia, which have been converted Entered the city public health medical treatment center for symptomatic treatment. Currently, there are no fever symptoms, other symptoms are relieved, and the condition is stable.
