Epidemic situation briefing on press conference on prevention and control of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 18
Release time: 2020-03-18 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a press conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic in Chongqing (55th session), and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the epidemic situation.

No new confirmed cases, the situation of epidemic prevention and control continues to improve

The Municipal Health and Health Committee reported that as of 24:00 on March 17, there were no new confirmed cases in all districts and counties of the city for 22 consecutive days. There are no confirmed cases or suspected cases in the hospital. The situation of epidemic prevention and control continues to improve and the order of production and living is improving Speed up recovery.

Increased imported risks

The Municipal Health and Health Committee said that experts pointed out that the current situation of epidemics abroad is severe and complex, and the import risks are increasing. At the same time, with the gradual resumption of work and production, the risk of epidemic transmission caused by the increase in the movement and gathering of people in the city cannot be ignored.

Experts believe that we must continue to tighten the string of epidemic prevention and control, and take strict prevention of imported risks abroad as the top priority of the current epidemic prevention and control work. We must closely monitor the situation of epidemics abroad, strengthen the accurate management of returnees from overseas, and implement them. Measures such as health declaration, health screening, etc., to perform nucleic acid detection and isolation observation for returnees from severely affected countries and regions, so that early detection and early identification are required; it is necessary to implement district-level precision prevention and control, strengthen community prevention and control, and strictly prevent Risk of local infection in the city.

Actively use Chinese medicine to participate in public prevention and medical treatment

The Municipal Health and Health Committee introduced that since the outbreak of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, the Chongqing health system has thoroughly studied and implemented the important directive spirit of General Secretary Xi Jinping on integrating traditional Chinese and western medicine to treat new crown pneumonia, and actively used it in accordance with the requirements of the Party Central Committee, the State Council and the requirements of the Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government. Chinese medicine participates in public prevention and medical treatment, practically guarantees daily medical services, and fully protects the health of the people.

Establish and improve the mechanism of coordinated treatment of traditional Chinese and western medicine, coordinate the deployment of 16 traditional Chinese medicine experts into the municipal medical treatment expert group, and participate in the combined diagnosis and treatment of traditional Chinese and western medicine in 4 hospitals.

In epidemic prevention and control, multi-channel and multi-form publicity of Chinese medicine prevention and control knowledge was introduced to guide the people to use traditional Chinese medicine methods to prevent epidemic diseases. Traditional Chinese medicine hospitals at all levels have brewed traditional Chinese medicine "Big Pot Soup", made epidemic-proof sachets, and issued more than 300,000 pairs of Chinese medicine for prevention. Chinese and Western medicine cooperated with the "war epidemic", jointly conducted consultations, rounds, and case discussions, and jointly formulated diagnostic and treatment plans to maximize the role of coordinated treatment, improve the cure rate, and reduce the mortality rate. As of March 17, the rate of Chinese medicine participation in the treatment was 92.53%, and 84.91% of the cured patients were discharged through the cooperative treatment of Chinese and Western medicine. Simultaneously start 6 scientific research projects such as "Research and Application of Traditional Chinese Medicine in the Clinical Treatment of New Coronavirus Pneumonia", and put forward the prevention and treatment theories such as "full-course righting" and "spleen tonic in lung and spleen". Developed and marketed two in-hospital Chinese medicinal preparations, "Pu Pu Toxie Mix" and "Ma Xing Jiedu Mix".

A total of 327 people from 28 Chinese medicine hospitals in the city sent Hubei medical teams, accounting for 20%. Among them, there are 161 people in Wuhan and 166 people in Xiaogan. They are involved in Chinese medicine, breathing, severe illness, infection, and nursing. The participation rate of traditional Chinese medicine was about 68%, and about 77% of the patients who were discharged were treated with integrated Chinese and western medicine.

At the same time, various Chinese medicine hospitals have adopted methods such as increasing the number of online sources and home delivery of medicines to facilitate the medical treatment of the masses, and have increased the number of online appointments by 54,000 and the delivery of nearly 9,000 medicines. At present, the opening rate of clinical departments in public TCM hospitals at or above Grade II in the city has exceeded 95%, and the rate of reinstatement of frontline medical staff has exceeded 97%.
