Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 22, 2020
Release time: 2020-03-22 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

At 04:00 on March 21, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Chongqing.

As of 24:00 on March 21, there were no confirmed cases in hospital in Chongqing, with a total of 6 deaths, 570 cases cured and discharged, and a total of 576 confirmed cases reported.
