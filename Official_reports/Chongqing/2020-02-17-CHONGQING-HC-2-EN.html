Situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing as of 24:00, February 16, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-17 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ğŸ–¨

At 12:00 to 24:00 on February 16, 2020, Chongqing reported 4 newly diagnosed cases of new coronary pneumonia and 20 newly discharged cases.

Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 2 cases in Wanzhou District, 1 in Jiangbei District, and 1 in Changshou District. Among the newly cured cases, 5 were in Yuzhong District, 1 in Dadukou District, 1 in Shapingba District, 1 in Jiulongpo District, There was 1 case in Nan'an District, 2 cases in Yubei District, 1 case in Qijiang District, 1 case in Dazu District, 1 case in Laoshan District, 1 case in Tongliang District, 3 cases in Rongchang District, 1 case in Kaizhou District, and 1 case in Dianjiang County.

As of 24:00 on February 16, there were 339 confirmed cases in hospital in Chongqing (including 37 cases of severe cases and 13 cases of critical cases), 5 cases of deaths, 207 cases of discharged patients, and 551 confirmed cases example. A total of 22,026 close contacts have been traced, 17,825 medical observations have been lifted, and 4201 people are still receiving medical observations. Details are as follows:

Experts believe that recently, the number of newly diagnosed cases in our city has been decreasing for 7 consecutive days, and the number of newly diagnosed cases has been less than 10 in the last 3 days. number. From February 11th, both "increased cases" and "in-hospital cases" have been reduced, indicating that the early epidemic prevention and control measures have achieved significant results. However, with the increase in the number of workers returning to work and production, the epidemic prevention and control is still facing the severe challenge of â€œforeign defense import and internal proliferation preventionâ€. There have been imported cases of returning to Chongqing in non-key areas. It is warned that during the return to Chongqing, the return of personnel will put new pressure on epidemic prevention and control.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee reminded that all enterprises should conscientiously implement epidemic prevention and control measures such as protection, epidemic response, and personal protection before and after resumption and production, and must strictly implement isolation medical observation for reworkers outside the city for at least 14 days. Measures were taken to train all employees on epidemic prevention and control knowledge before returning to Chongqing, establish a body temperature detection system and an illness reporting system, and monitor the temperature of commuters every morning and evening. Once abnormalities such as fever and cough are found, report immediately and deal with them in a timely manner. At the same time, it implements closed management to ensure the safety of the work scene; the canteen promotes the time-sharing and meal-sharing system; minimizes meetings and does not convene gatherings; rationally arranges work positions, maintains staff distance, and reduces staff gathering.
