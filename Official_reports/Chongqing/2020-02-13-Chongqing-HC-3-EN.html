Situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing as of 24:00 on February 12, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-13 Source: Publicity United Front Office Views: Edits: Office Review: Publicity United Front Office
Font size: small medium large 🖨

At 12:00 to 24:00 on February 12, 2020, Chongqing reported 9 new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 1 new severe case, 1 severe case to normal case, 2 severe case to critically severe case, and 2 severe case. 2 cases were converted into severe cases, and 15 new cases were discharged.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 1 in Wanzhou District, 1 in Nan'an District, 1 in Changshou District, 1 in Hechuan District, 3 in Qijiang District, 1 in Fengjie County, and 1 in Liangjiang New District; the newly added heavy cases were Wuxi County1 1 case was changed from severe case to ordinary case in Kaizhou District; 1 case was converted from severe case to critical case in Hechuan District and 1 case was in Nanan District; 1 case was converted to severe case in Wanzhou District and 1 in Fengjie County Among the newly-discharged cases, 1 in Wanzhou District, 2 in Dadukou District, 2 in Jiulongpo District, 1 in Nan'an District, 1 in Yubei District, 5 in Changshou District, 1 in Qijiang District, and 1 in Laoshan District. 1 case in Liangjiang New District.

As of 24:00 on February 12, a total of 518 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia had been reported in Chongqing; there were 29 severe cases, 24 severe cases, 3 deaths, and 102 discharged cases. A total of 18,925 close contacts have been tracked, 11,755 have been released from medical observation, and 7,170 are still undergoing medical observation. Details are as follows:

Experts believe that as the investigation of suspected cases continues to increase throughout the city, the range of close contacts will also expand, which will indirectly protect the susceptible people. The proportion of suspected to be diagnosed and the proportion of close contacts to be diagnosed are decreasing. The epidemic prevention and control will play a positive role in the coming week. However, among the newly added cases, the proportion of third-generation infection cases has increased significantly. Tracing to the source also found that two cases of fourth-generation infection cases appeared for the first time in our city on February 10, the chain of infection extended, and the difficulty of prevention and control further increased.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee reminded: At present, the situation of epidemic prevention and control is still severe and complicated, and the task of preventing and spreading is very difficult. In order to find and lock the source of infection more quickly, it is necessary to strengthen carpet inspection and grid management, focusing on those who come to Chongqing, especially those who come back to Chongqing in epidemic-stricken areas, those who have been in close contact with confirmed cases, and those who purchase drugs such as cough and fever. Carry out in-depth inspections; For those who have been diagnosed, organize relevant communities and units to cooperate in conducting trace tracing, timely grasp close contacts, and implement medical observation and other measures one by one; practically strengthen the management of real estate communities, strictly implement the entry and exit of vehicles, personnel registration and temperature detection Give full play to the role of village (community) residents' self-governing organizations, and guide and supervise residents not to get together, not to dine together, not to go out, and to seek medical treatment immediately if symptoms such as fever and cough occur.
