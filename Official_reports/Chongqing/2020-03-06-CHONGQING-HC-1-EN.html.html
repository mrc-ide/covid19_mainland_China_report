Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in Chongqing on March 6, 2020
Release time: 2020-03-06 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

From 04:00 to March 24, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Chongqing, and 10 new cases were cured and discharged.

Among the newly added cured cases, 2 were in Shapingba District, 1 in Nan'an District, 1 in Changshou District, 2 in Hechuan District, 1 in Nanan District, 1 in Kaizhou District, and 2 in Dianjiang County.

As of 24:00 on March 5, there were 58 confirmed cases in hospital in Chongqing (including 4 cases of severe cases and 1 case of critical cases), 6 cases of deaths, 512 cases of discharged patients cured, and 576 confirmed cases. example. A total of 23,620 close contacts have been tracked, 23,351 have been released from medical observation, and 269 are still receiving medical observation. Details are as follows:

Experts believe that as of March 5, there were no new confirmed cases in our city for 10 consecutive days, and no new confirmed cases in 34 districts and counties for 14 consecutive days. The number of existing districts and counties with zero confirmed cases increased to 18, and the epidemic situation The situation of prevention and control has continued to improve. However, with the resumption of work and production, the restoration of the order of daily life, the increase in the number of people returning to Chongqing, and the increase in the flow and gathering of people, it has brought new pressure on the prevention and control of the epidemic, and the risk of a rebound in the epidemic has always existed. The vigilance of the epidemic cannot be reduced, and the requirements for prevention and control cannot be reduced. We must continue to pay close attention to various prevention and control measures and resolutely prevent the resurgence of the epidemic.

The Chongqing Municipal Health and Health Committee reminds: everyone is the first person responsible for their own health, and they should consciously bear their personal responsibility for epidemic prevention and control, and assist, cooperate and obey the prevention and control work organized by government departments. During the period of the epidemic, the general public is still required to strengthen personal protection and insist on going out less, wearing masks, washing hands frequently, not gathering meals, and not gathering. If you are going to a place with relatively dense personnel and poor air movement, wear a mask and keep a distance of more than 1 meter from others, and avoid touching public items with your hands. If you have suspicious symptoms such as fever, cough, and fatigue, please go to a fever clinic in time.

Starting from March 7, the epidemic situation of Chongqing New Crown Pneumonia was adjusted from 7 am daily to 9 am daily. The related epidemic situation was analyzed at the press conference on the prevention and control of Chongqing New Crown pneumonia epidemic every day. Release, if there is no press conference, release in other ways.
