Situation of pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Chongqing as of 24:00 on February 2, 2020
Release time: 2020-02-03Source : Propaganda United Front Office Views: Edit: Office review: Propaganda United Front Office
Font size: small medium large 🖨

At 12:00 to 24:00 on February 2, 2020, Chongqing reported 25 newly diagnosed pneumonia cases of new coronavirus infection, 3 fewer severe cases, and 1 new death. Gaoxin District is the first confirmed case to be reported.

Among the newly confirmed cases, 5 were in Wanzhou District, 1 in Fuling District, 3 in Jiangbei District, 2 in Jiulongpo District, 2 in Nan'an District, 1 in Banan District, 1 in Minjiang District, 1 in Dazu District, Tongliang. 3 cases, 1 case in Taonan District, 1 case in Rongchang District, 1 case in Kaizhou District, 1 case in Fengjie County, 1 case in Wushan County, 1 case in High-tech Zone; among the reduced heavy cases, 1 case was in Changshou District and 2 cases were in Jiangjin District ; One new death was in Wanzhou District.

As of 24:00 on February 2, 300 cases of pneumonia diagnosed with new coronavirus infection have been reported in Chongqing; there are 23 severe cases, 6 severe cases, 2 death cases, and 7 discharged cases.

Among the confirmed cases, 49 were in Wanzhou District, 2 in Qianjiang District, 2 in Fuling District, 9 in Yuzhong District, 6 in Dadukou District, 16 in Jiangbei District, 2 in Shapingba District, 13 in Jiulongpo District, and 6 in South Bank District. 12 cases in Yubei District, 2 cases in Banan District, 11 cases in Changshou District, 3 cases in Jiangjin District, 9 cases in Hechuan District, 3 cases in Yongchuan District, 5 cases in Qijiang District, 5 cases in Dazu District, 8 cases in Laoshan District, Tongliang There were 7 cases in district, 3 cases in Taonan district, 4 cases in Rongchang district, 17 cases in Kaizhou district, 3 cases in Liangping district, 1 case in Wulong District, 1 case in Chengkou County, 5 cases in Fengdu County, 12 cases in Dianjiang County, 15 cases in Zhongxian County, 18 cases in Yunyang County, 9 cases in Fengjie County, 7 cases in Wushan County, 11 cases in Wuxi County, 10 cases in Shizhu County, 1 case in Xiushan County, 12 cases in Liangjiang New District, and 1 case in High-tech Zone; Among the cases, there were 5 cases in Wanzhou District, 2 cases in Dadukou District, 1 case in Jiangbei District, 1 case in Yubei District, 2 cases in Changshou District, 1 case in Jiangjin District, 1 case in Qijiang District, 2 cases in Laoshan District, and 1 case in Kaizhou District. 1 case in Fengdu County, 1 case in Dianjiang County, 3 cases in Zhongxian County, and 2 cases in Fengjie County; among the existing critical cases, 1 case in Jiangbei District, 1 case in Jiulongpo District, 1 case in Tongliang District, and Kaizhou District 1 case, 1 case in Liangping District, 1 case in Zhongxian; among the death cases, 1 in Wanzhou District Jiulongpo 1 case; discharge cases, one case Yongchuan, 1 Fengdu, 1 YunYangXian, Wushan County three cases, one case XiuShanXian.

A total of 8,429 close contacts have been tracked, 1958 people have been released from medical observation, and 6471 people are still receiving medical observation.

Experts believe that at present, new cases, local infection cases, and clustered cases are increasing rapidly in the main city of our city. There are 14 community infection cases found in 10 districts and counties, and there is a potential risk of community spread. As people return to work after the holidays, a large number of people will gather again, and the task of epidemic prevention and control is very difficult. In view of the seriousness of the current epidemic situation, the whole society must participate in this "war epidemic" together, and everyone must do a good job of self-prevention and control to reduce the number of trips, no gathering, and reduce the flow of security.

The Chongqing Municipal Commission of Health and Health has suggested that if there are suspicious symptoms such as fever (underarm temperature ≥37.3 ℃), cough, etc., you should wear a protective mask and go to the designated hospital in the area for diagnosis and treatment. Try to avoid taking public transportation. Actively cooperate with doctors to conduct relevant investigations and Diagnosis and treatment.
