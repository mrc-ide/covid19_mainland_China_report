March 15 Chongqing City New Crown Pneumonia Epidemic Prevention and Control Network News Ventilation Society Outbreak Report
Release time: 2020-03-15 Source: United Front Office Page views:Times Edit: Office Review: United Front Office
font size: small in Big ðŸ–¨

Today, the Chongqing Municipal Government Information Office held a news conference on the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia network in Chongqing, and the Municipal Health and Health Commission notified the city of the situation.

No new confirmed cases, new cured discharged cases3 cases

The Municipal Health and Health Commission reported that there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia and 3 new cases of cured discharge from our city at 04:00 on March 14. As of March 14, a total of 6 death cases have been reported in our city, and a total of 576 confirmed cases have been reported. A total of 3,532 suspected cases have been ruled out, and no doubt the cases are yet to be investigated. A total of 23,692 close contacts have been tracked, 23,624 have been released from medical observation, and 68 are still receiving medical observation.

At 11:00 on March 15, the last confirmed case in our city was cured and discharged from the municipal public health medical treatment center. At present, there are no confirmed cases in the city, and a total of 570 patients have been discharged from hospital, with a cure rate of 98.96%.

Prevent imported cases from spreading abroad and spread locally

Experts believe that with the rapid development of overseas epidemics, it is necessary to strengthen the service management of returnees from Chongqing in countries and regions with severe epidemics, and strictly prevent imported cases from spreading abroad and spreading locally. It is necessary to implement detailed and precise measures for district-level grading prevention and control, effectively achieve the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic and economic and social development, continually consolidate the effectiveness of the prevention and control of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, and promote the stable restoration of production and living order.

Experts reminded that the general public still need to protect themselves, do not gather, wear masks, wash hands frequently, and often ventilate.

Citywide689 dental clinics resume medical services

It is understood that, as the epidemic prevention and control work progresses in an orderly manner, the Municipal Health and Health Commission adjusts measures in a timely manner, and in accordance with the zoning classification and scientific and orderly principles, all districts and counties will gradually resume routine dental treatment services to meet the people's normal medical needs. Strengthen fine-grained management of oral medical institutions, promote Internet medical services, implement appointment registration and appointment inspections, guide patients to visit in different time periods, and reduce staff gathering; implement an outpatient pre-examination and triage system and increase the screening of suspected patients with new coronary pneumonia; Do a good job of medical protection, strictly implement "one person, one diagnosis and one room", prevent cross infection in the hospital, and effectively protect the normal medical order. At present, 689 dental medical institutions in the city have resumed medical services, and 32,000 patients have been treated since March 1.
