From March 29th, Beijing, from 00:00 to 12:00, one newly diagnosed imported case was reported, and one was discharged.
Date: 2020-03-29                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

On March 29th, from 00:00 to 12:00, a new case of newly diagnosed imported coronary pneumonia was reported, and it was imported in the United States. One patient was discharged from the hospital. As of 12:00 on March 29, a total of 161 overseas confirmed confirmed cases were reported, and 16 cases were cured and discharged.
From 09:00 to 12:00 on March 29, no new confirmed cases were reported locally. As of 12:00 on March 29, a total of 416 locally confirmed cases were reported, 396 cases were cured and discharged, and the cure discharge rate was 95.2%.
