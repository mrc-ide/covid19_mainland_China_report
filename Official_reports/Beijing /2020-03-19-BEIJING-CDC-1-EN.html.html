21 new imported cases of new coronary pneumonia were added on March 18
Date: 2020-03-19                 Source: Beijing Centers for Disease Control and Prevention

According to the Beijing Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, from 00:00 to 24:00 on March 18, our city reported 21 confirmed imported cases of neocoronary pneumonia, of which 7 were from Spain, 7 from the United Kingdom, 3 from Hungary, and 2 from One case was from Austria, one case was from Brazil, and one case was from Luxembourg, not involving the Beijing metropolitan area.
Recently, the international epidemic has spread rapidly. Here, we sincerely remind people outside the country to do personal protection. If they come into contact with cases or symptoms, they should seek medical treatment in the local area promptly, consciously accept isolation and observation, avoid traveling with illness, reduce contact with the outside world, and reduce the risk of spread of infectious diseases.
The immigrants who come to Beijing must take the initiative to report the health truthfully and truthfully in accordance with the law, cooperate with the health quarantine of the customs department, and report any symptoms such as fever to the customs without concealing the illness and implement centralized quarantine medical observation for 14 days as required.
As the temperature rises, remind friends of the public to wear masks when they go out, less outdoor activities, low density indoor offices, and maintain a one-meter distance in interpersonal communication. Do not travel to countries or regions with epidemics in the near future.
