Three new cases of overseas imported confirmed cases were reported from 14:00 to 24:00 on March 16, Beijing
Date: 2020-03-17                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 14:00 to 24:00 on March 16th, there were no newly reported local confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city, there were no local suspected cases, and 8 cases were discharged from the hospital. As of 24:00 on the 16th, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 366 cases were cured and discharged, and the cure discharge rate was 88.2%.
From 14:00 to 24:00 on March 16, three newly diagnosed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, including two in Spain and one in the United States. From 00:00 to 24:00 on the 16th, 7 new suspected cases were reported, including 3 in the United Kingdom and 1 in the United States, France, the Netherlands, and Egypt. There were 14 close contacts. As of 24:00 on the 16th, a total of 40 imported confirmed cases were reported abroad, and 2 were cured and discharged.
As of 24:00 on March 16, a total of 455 confirmed cases were reported, 368 were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 36 suspected cases. A total of 3884 close contacts have been identified, of which 410 are still in isolation medical observation. 14 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng District, 72 in Chaoyang District, 63 in Haidian District, 43 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, and 1 case in Yanqing District. There were no cases in Pinggu District and 25 cases came from other places in Beijing.
Thirteen districts in the city have had no new confirmed cases for more than 14 consecutive days, specifically no reported cases in Pinggu District since the outbreak, 53 days in Yanqing District, 43 days in Mentougou District, 39 days in Huairou District, 37 days in Shunyi District and Miyun District 34 days, 32 days in Shijingshan District, 32 days in Daxing District, 29 days in Fangshan District, 28 days in Changping District, 26 days in Xicheng District, 26 days in Tongzhou District, and 15 days in Haidian District.
