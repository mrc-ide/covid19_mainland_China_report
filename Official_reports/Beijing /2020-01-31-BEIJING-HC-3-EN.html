From 24 o'clock on January 30 to 14:00 on 31st, 7 new cases of pneumonia infected with new coronavirus were added in this city
2020-01-31 17:00:39 Date: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 24 o'clock on January 30 to 14 o'clock on 31 January, 7 new cases of pneumonia infected with new coronavirus were added to the city, 6 of which had contact history in Hubei and other provinces, 5 were close contacts of confirmed cases, and 4 were People with contact history in Hubei and other provinces are close contacts of confirmed cases. All have been sent to designated medical institutions at the municipal and district levels for treatment.
