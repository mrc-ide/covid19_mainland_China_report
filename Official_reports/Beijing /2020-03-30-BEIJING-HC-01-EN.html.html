Yesterday, one newly imported case was reported in Beijing, but no new case was reported locally
Date: 2020-03-30                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 29, there were no new reports of confirmed cases of imported new coronary pneumonia overseas. Yesterday, a total of 1 new confirmed imported case and 1 suspected case were imported. As of 24:00 on March 29, a total of 161 imported confirmed cases and 16 discharged patients were cured.
