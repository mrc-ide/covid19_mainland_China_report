8 new cases of newly diagnosed new pneumonia in Beijing were cured and discharged
Date: 2020-02-20                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 09:00 to 24:00 on February 19th, two new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in this city, both of whom are close contacts of confirmed cases, and have been sent to designated medical institutions for treatment. There were 25 new suspected cases and 125 existing suspected cases. Sixteen new close contacts were added, and a total of 2091 close contacts were identified, of which 1,419 were released from medical observation. Eight patients were cured and discharged.

As of 24:00 on February 19, a total of 395 confirmed cases have been diagnosed in this city. Among them, 153 were discharged and 4 died. 12 cases in Dongcheng District, 53 cases in Xicheng District, 58 cases in Chaoyang District, 61 cases in Haidian District, 40 cases in Fengtai District, 14 cases in Shijingshan District, 3 cases in Mentougou District, 16 cases in Fangshan District, 19 cases in Tongzhou District, 10 cases in Shunyi District, There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, 1 case in Yanqing District, and 26 cases came from overseas. No cases have been reported in Pinggu District.
