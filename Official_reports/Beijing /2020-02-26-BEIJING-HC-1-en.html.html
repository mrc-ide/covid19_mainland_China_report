No new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Beijing, 20 patients cured and discharged
Date: 2020-02-26                 Source: Beijing Municipal Health Committee

From 00:00 to 24:00 on February 25, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Beijing. There were 21 new suspected cases and 57 existing suspected cases. 87 new close contacts were added, and a total of 2574 close contacts were identified, of which 1784 cases have been released from medical observation. Twenty patients with new crown pneumonia were discharged from Ditan Hospital, You'an Hospital, the Fifth Medical Center of the General Hospital of the PLA, Changping District Hospital, Puren Hospital, Fengtai District Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital, and Fangshan District Tuberculosis Prevention Hospital. There are 10 males and 10 females; the youngest is 3 years old and the oldest is 85 years old.

As of 24:00 on February 25, Beijing had a total of 400 confirmed cases. 235 patients were discharged, 4 died, and 12 were critically ill. 13 cases in Dongcheng District, 53 cases in Xicheng District, 61 cases in Chaoyang District, 62 cases in Haidian District, 41 cases in Fengtai District, 14 cases in Shijingshan District, 3 cases in Mentougou District, 16 cases in Fangshan District, 19 cases in Tongzhou District, and 10 cases in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, 1 case in Yanqing District, and 25 cases came from overseas. No cases have been reported in Pinggu District.

Of the 400 confirmed cases, 189 were male cases, accounting for 47.2%, and 211 were female cases, accounting for 52.8%. The age range was 6 months to 94 years, of which 14 were under 5 years old, accounting for 3.5%, and 6 to 17 years old 13 cases, accounting for 3.2%, 263 cases aged 18 to 59, accounting for 65.8%, 110 cases aged 60 and above, accounting for 27.5%.
