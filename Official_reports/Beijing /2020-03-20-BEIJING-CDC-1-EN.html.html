6 new cases of new imported pneumonia were imported on March 19
Date: 2020-03-20                 Source: Beijing Centers for Disease Control and Prevention

According to the Beijing Municipal Center for Disease Control and Prevention, from 09:00 to 24:00 on March 19, our city reported 6 confirmed imported cases of neocoronary pneumonia, of which 5 were from the United Kingdom and 1 from the United States, and did not involve the Beijing community.

Recently, the international epidemic has spread rapidly, and the prevention and control of overseas import risks is the focus of current epidemic prevention and control. Here, I sincerely remind overseas visitors to Beijing to strictly abide by relevant laws and regulations on epidemic prevention and control, implement the full process and closed management of entry from Beijing ports, declare personal health information in accordance with the law, and cooperate with Beijing Customs quarantine and medical staff to implement various measures . Medical inspectors are required to implement full-process health prevention and control measures and truthfully report travel and medical histories in accordance with the law.

Remind friends, do not travel to countries or regions with epidemic situation in the near future, to avoid the risk of infection during the trip.
