Beijing, March 27, 00:00 to 12:00 no new confirmed cases were reported and 2 patients were discharged from the hospital
Date: 2020-03-27                 Source: Beijing Municipal Health Committee

From 00:00 to 12:00 on March 27, there were no new reports of newly confirmed cases of imported new coronary pneumonia. Two patients were cured and discharged. As of 12:00 on March 27, a total of 153 overseas confirmed confirmed cases and 14 discharged patients were cured.
From 02:00 to 12:00 on March 27, there were no new reports of locally confirmed cases. As of 12:00 on March 27, a total of 416 locally confirmed cases were reported, 394 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 94.7%.
