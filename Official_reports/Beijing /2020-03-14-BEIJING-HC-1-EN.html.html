Yesterday, no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia were reported in Beijing. One new case was imported overseas, and 7 cases were cured and discharged.
Date: 2020-03-14                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 00:00 to 24:00 on March 13, no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia were reported locally. One new suspected case was reported.
One newly-reported confirmed case of new coronary pneumonia was reported overseas, and it was an overseas imported case from the United States. There were 4 suspected cases, including 1 in Italy, 2 in the United States, and 1 in Spain. There were 14 close contacts. As of 24 o'clock on March 13th, 22 cases of confirmed overseas imported cases were reported.
Seven of the discharged patients were cured yesterday, including 3 males and 4 females. The youngest was 37 years old and the oldest was 78 years old.
As of 24:00 on March 13, a total of 415 confirmed cases were diagnosed locally, 349 were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 32 suspected cases. A total of 3,785 close contacts have been identified, of which 466 are still in isolation medical observation. 14 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng District, 72 in Chaoyang District, 63 in Haidian District, 43 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, and 1 case in Yanqing District. There were no cases in Pinggu District and 25 cases came from other places in Beijing.
Twelve districts in the city have had no new confirmed cases for more than 14 consecutive days, specifically no reported cases in Pinggu District since the outbreak, Yanqing District for 50 days, Mentougou District for 40 days, Huairou District for 36 days, Shunyi District for 34 days, and Miyun District. 31 days, 29 days in Shijingshan District, 29 days in Daxing District, 26 days in Fangshan District, 25 days in Changping District, 23 days in Xicheng District, and 23 days in Tongzhou District.
