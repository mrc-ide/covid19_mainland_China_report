Relevant information of imported confirmed cases abroad
Date: 2020-03-20                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

On March 19, six newly diagnosed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported in our city, which mainly have the following characteristics:

1. The source of confirmed cases is concentrated, involving two countries, including five in the United Kingdom and one in the United States.

2. A total of 4 flights were involved, 3 in Emirates EK306, 1 in SU204, 1 in CA126, 1 in CZ346 of China Southern Airlines.

3. Domicile locations are: 2 cases in Shandong, 1 case in Shanghai, 1 case in Zhejiang, 1 case in Hainan, and 1 case in Macau, China.

4. In terms of occupational distribution, there were 5 cases of foreign students and 1 company designer. Zh

A typical case is described below.

Wen Moumou, female, 34 years old, Shandong native, working in Yancheng, Jiangsu, a British trainee. Travel to Britain on January 18. On March 3, symptoms such as headache and cough occurred, and the body temperature was 38 ° C. On the 7th and 8th, he went to the emergency department of the local hospital. He departed from the United Kingdom via Dubai on the 17th, took the Emirates EK306 and arrived in Beijing on the 18th. Reported previous fever and other symptoms, immediately sent to Beijing Xiaotangshan Hospital, diagnosed as confirmed cases on the 19th, clinical classification is ordinary.

Once again, everyone is reminded that overseas imported cases are still increasing. It is recommended that overseas personnel should go to the local medical institution in time for symptoms such as fever and cough, follow the relevant requirements for the prevention and control of local infectious diseases, and avoid long-distance travel. Comply with relevant regulations on epidemic prevention and control in various places, actively declare personal health information in accordance with the law, cooperate with the health screening of customs quarantine personnel, and reduce the risk of infectious disease transmission.
