One new case of overseas imported confirmed cases was reported from 00:00 to 12:00 on March 19th in Beijing
Date: 2020-03-19                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From March 19th to midnight, one new imported case was reported and imported for the United States. As of 12:00 on March 19, a total of 65 imported confirmed cases were reported abroad, and 6 cases were cured and discharged.
From 00:00 to 12:00 on March 19, no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia were reported. One case was cured and discharged. As of 12:00 on March 19, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 374 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 90.1%.
