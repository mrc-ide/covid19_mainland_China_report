2 new reports of confirmed overseas imported cases were reported from 12:00 to 24:00 on March 17, Beijing
Date: 2020-03-18                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 17th, there were no newly reported local confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city, no local suspected cases, and 3 discharged patients were cured. As of 24:00 on the 17th, a total of 415 locally diagnosed cases were reported, 369 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 88.9%.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 17, two newly diagnosed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, including one in Spain and one in the United Kingdom. There were 11 suspected cases, including 5 in the United Kingdom, 3 in Spain, and 1 each in Luxembourg, Canada, and Brazil. One case was cured and discharged. As of 24:00 on the 17th, a total of 43 imported confirmed cases and 4 cured cases were reported.
As of 24:00 on March 17, a total of 458 confirmed cases were reported, 373 were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 48 suspected cases. A total of 3921 close contacts have been identified, of which 307 are still in isolation medical observation. 14 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng, 72 in Chaoyang District, 63 in Haidian District, 43 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, and 1 case in Yanqing District. There were no cases in Pinggu District and 25 cases came from other places in Beijing.
There have been no new confirmed cases in 14 districts in the city for more than 14 consecutive days, specifically no reported cases in Pinggu District since the outbreak, 54 days in Yanqing District, 44 days in Mentougou District, 40 days in Huairou District, 38 days in Shunyi District, and Miyun District. 35 days, 33 days in Shijingshan District, 33 days in Daxing District, 30 days in Fangshan District, 29 days in Changping District, 27 days in Xicheng District, 27 days in Tongzhou District, 16 days in Haidian District, and 14 days in Fengtai District.
