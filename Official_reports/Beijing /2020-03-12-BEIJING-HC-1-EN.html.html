Yesterday, no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia were reported in Beijing, 8 were cured and discharged
Date: 2020-03-12                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 01:00 to 24:00 on March 11, no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia were reported in Beijing. Five new suspected cases were reported, of which 2 were imported from Italy, 1 from the United Kingdom, 1 from Spain, and 1 from the city. There were 31 close contacts, including 23 from overseas. Eight discharged patients were cured and discharged from two designated hospitals in the urban area. There are 4 men and 4 women, the youngest is 27 years old and the oldest is 57 years old.
As of 24:00 on March 11, a total of 435 confirmed cases, 334 cured cases and 8 dead cases were cured. There are 29 suspected cases.
