Beijing reported 4 new imported cases yesterday, no new cases reported locally
Date: 2020-03-29                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 28, 3 newly confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, including 1 in Spain, Belgium, and Thailand each. Yesterday, a total of 4 newly confirmed cases were imported. As of 24:00 on March 28, a total of 160 imported confirmed cases were reported abroad, and 15 cases were cured and discharged.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 28, there were no newly reported locally confirmed cases, and 1 case was cured and discharged. As of 24:00 on March 28, a total of 416 locally confirmed cases were reported, 396 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 95.2%. Zh
