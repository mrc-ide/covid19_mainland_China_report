Yesterday, no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia were reported in Beijing, and 10 new imported cases were reported overseas
Date: 2020-03-23                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 22, 8 newly confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, including 2 in Spain, 2 in the United Kingdom, 2 in France, 1 in the United States, and 1 in Pakistan. A total of 10 newly diagnosed imported cases and 15 suspected cases were imported yesterday. As of 24:00 on March 22, a total of 107 imported confirmed cases were reported overseas and 8 cases were cured and discharged.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 22, no new confirmed cases were reported locally. As of 24:00 on March 22, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 392 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 94.5%.
As of 24:00 on March 22, a total of 522 confirmed cases were reported, 400 were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 55 suspected cases. A total of 4,164 close contacts have been identified, of which 381 are still in isolation medical observation.
The city has not reported any new confirmed cases locally for 16 consecutive days. Specifically, there have been no reported cases since the outbreak in Pinggu District, 59 days in Yanqing District, 49 days in Mentougou District, 45 days in Huairou District, 43 days in Shunyi District, 40 days in Miyun District, 38 days in Shijingshan District, 38 days in Daxing District, and 35 in Fangshan District.
