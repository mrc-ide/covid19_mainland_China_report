One new case of overseas imported confirmed cases was reported from 00:00 to 12:00 on March 17, Beijing
Date: 2020-03-17                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 00:00 to 12:00 on March 17, there were no new cases of local confirmed new coronary pneumonia reported in Beijing. As of 12:00 on March 17, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 366 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges reached 88.2%.
One newly-reported confirmed case was reported as an imported case in the United Kingdom. There were 4 suspected cases, including 2 in Spain, 1 in Egypt, and 1 in the United Kingdom. One case was cured and discharged. As of 12:00 on March 17, a total of 41 imported confirmed cases were reported abroad, and 3 were discharged.
