Introduction of overseas epidemic situation and 3 cases of confirmed imported cases abroad
Date: 2020-03-18                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

According to data from the World Health Organization, as of March 17, the new crown pneumonia epidemic has affected 159 countries and regions, and the number of cases outside China has reached 97,996. On the same day, 11,487 new cases were reported, and 88 countries have formed local transmission.
The increase in the number of confirmed cases in the last seven days on six continents from last week is: 24% in Asia, 329% in Europe, 787% in North America, 490% in South America, 360% in Africa, and 317% in Oceania. In the past 14 days, the proportion of confirmed cases in all continents in overseas cases is: Asia (excluding China) accounts for 27%, Europe accounts for 69%, North America accounts for 2.8%, South America accounts for 0.6%, Africa accounts for 0.2%, and Oceania accounts for 0.4% .
As of March 17, according to the number of confirmed cases abroad, the top 10 countries were: 27,980 cases in Italy, 14,991 cases in Iran, 9,191 cases in Spain, 8,320 cases in South Korea, 6,573 cases in France, 6012 cases in Germany, 3503 cases in the United States, and Switzerland There were 2200 cases, 1547 cases in the UK, and 1413 cases in the Netherlands.
As of March 17, 35 countries worldwide have declared a state of emergency: Italy, Spain, Czech Republic, France, Hungary, Portugal, Slovakia, Switzerland, Austria, Romania, Bulgaria, Latvia, Estonia, Serbia, Cyprus, Poland, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Kazakhstan, Armenia, Palestine, Lebanon, South Korea, Philippines, El Salvador, United States, Costa Rica, Argentina, Peru, Panama, Colombia, Venezuela, Guatemala, Sudan, South Africa, Libya.
On March 17, the city reported 3 confirmed imported cases abroad, and the relevant situation is now introduced as follows.
1. Huang Moumou, female, 22 years old, registered in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, and studied in London, England. Headache, sore throat and other symptoms occurred on March 6. She went to the clinic on the 10th and purchased a throat spray in accordance with the doctor's advice. On the 14th, she developed a dry cough. On the 15th, he flew from London to Moscow by SU262 and flew back to Beijing from Moscow by SU204. He reported the health declaration card on board and had symptoms such as sore throat. After arriving in Beijing at 10:00 on the 16th, he was transferred to a designated hospital for screening. On the 17th, he was diagnosed with confirmed cases.
Du Moumou, male, 41 years old, registered in Xicheng District, Beijing. From March 1 to 14, from Beijing to Madrid, Spain, and Sao Paulo, Brazil. During his stay in Madrid, he went out to the supermarket to shop without a mask. He arrived in Beijing in the early morning of the 14th, and was identified as a high-risk person for centralized medical observation. On the 16th, he developed fever and other symptoms. His temperature was 39.2 ° C. He was transferred to a designated hospital for screening. On the 17th, a confirmed case was diagnosed.
3. Xie, female, 27 years old, registered in Nanchong City, Sichuan Province, studying in Manchester, England. She had fever and other symptoms on March 2nd, self-measured body temperature was 36.2 ° C, she did not seek treatment, and symptoms of nasal congestion occurred on the 5th. On the 15th, take the Singapore Airlines SQ051 from the UK to Singapore, and on the 16th, take the Singapore Airlines SQ802 to Beijing. Xu arrived in Beijing at 15:00 on the 16th, reported sore throat at the customs, and went to the designated hospital for screening. On the 17th, he was diagnosed with confirmed cases.
The above cases are light or common and are treated in designated hospitals. Thirty-seven close contacts have been tracked, and all have undergone centralized isolation medical observations as required.
Here, residents outside the country are advised to take personal protection. If they are exposed to cases or symptoms, they should seek medical treatment in the local area promptly, and accept measures such as isolation and observation to avoid traveling with illness, reduce contact with the outside world, and prohibit boarding with symptoms to avoid The risk of spread of infectious diseases. When returning to China, health declaration should be proactively and truthfully carried out in accordance with the requirements and cooperate with the health quarantine of the customs department. If you have fever and other symptoms, you should report to the customs on your own initiative, and you cannot hide the condition. After returning to Beijing, implement centralized isolation medical observation for 14 days as required.
