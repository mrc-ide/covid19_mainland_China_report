One new case of newly diagnosed pneumonia was reported in Beijing yesterday, and five patients were cured
Date: 2020-03-10                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 09:00 to 24:00 on March 9, a new confirmed case of new coronary pneumonia was reported in Beijing, which was an imported case from the United Kingdom. Nine suspected cases were newly reported, of which 7 were imported overseas; 19 were in close contact, of which 17 were imported overseas. Five discharged patients were cured and discharged from two designated hospitals in the urban area. Among them were 1 male and 4 females, the youngest was 28 years old and the oldest was 68 years old.

As of 24:00 on March 9, a total of 429 confirmed cases, 320 discharged cases and 8 dead cases were cured. There are 36 suspected cases.
