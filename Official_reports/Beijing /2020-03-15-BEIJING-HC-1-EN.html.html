Yesterday, no new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia were reported in Beijing, 5 cases were imported abroad, and 4 cases were cured and discharged.
Date: 2020-03-15                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 04:00 to 24:00 on March 14th, there were no new cases of local confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city. One new suspected case was reported.
Five newly diagnosed cases of newly imported new coronary pneumonia were reported, including three in Spain, one in Italy and one in Thailand. There were 8 suspected cases, including 3 in Spain and 1 in the United States, Norway, Japan, Italy, and the United Kingdom. There were 12 close contacts. As of 24:00 on March 14, 27 cases of imported confirmed cases were reported.
Yesterday, 4 cases were discharged from hospital, all of which were women, the youngest was 11 years old and the oldest was 57 years old.
As of 24:00 on March 14th, a total of 415 confirmed cases were diagnosed locally, 353 were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 37 suspected cases. A total of 3,797 close contacts have been identified, of which 475 are still in isolation medical observation. 14 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng District, 72 in Chaoyang District, 63 in Haidian District, 43 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, and 1 case in Yanqing District. There were no cases in Pinggu District and 25 cases came from other places in Beijing.
Twelve districts of the city have had no new confirmed cases for more than 14 consecutive days, specifically no reported cases in Pinggu District since the outbreak, 51 days in Yanqing District, 41 days in Mentougou District, 37 days in Huairou District, 35 days in Shunyi District, and Miyun District. 32 days, 30 days in Shijingshan District, 30 days in Daxing District, 27 days in Fangshan District, 26 days in Changping District, 24 days in Xicheng District, 24 days in Tongzhou District.
