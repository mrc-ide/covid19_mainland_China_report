11 new cases of overseas imported confirmed cases were reported from 00:00 to 12:00 on March 18th in Beijing
Date: 2020-03-18                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 00:00 to 12:00 on March 18, 11 newly diagnosed imported cases were reported, including 5 in Spain, 4 in the UK, 1 in Brazil, and 1 in Luxembourg. There were 3 suspected cases, including 2 in Austria and 1 in Hungary. One case was cured and discharged. As of 12:00 on March 18, a total of 54 overseas confirmed confirmed cases were imported and 5 cases were cured and discharged.
There are no new reports of confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia. 4 cases were cured and discharged. As of 12:00 on March 18, a total of 415 locally diagnosed cases were reported, 373 cases were cured and discharged, and the cure discharge rate was 89.9%.
