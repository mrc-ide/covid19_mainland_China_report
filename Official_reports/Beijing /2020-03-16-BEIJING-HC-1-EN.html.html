Yesterday, no new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia were reported in Beijing, 4 cases were imported overseas, and 5 cases were cured and discharged.
Date: 2020-03-16                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 00:00 to 24:00 on March 15, there were no newly reported local confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in our city, and no new suspected local cases. Three of them were close contacts, all of whom were turned across provinces and cities.
Four newly diagnosed cases of newly imported new coronary pneumonia were reported, including two in Spain and two in Italy. Seven suspected cases, including three in Spain, three in the United Kingdom, and one in Italy. There were 67 close contacts. As of 24:00 on March 15, a total of 31 confirmed imported cases were reported overseas.
Five cases were discharged yesterday, including 3 men and 2 women. The youngest was 21 years old and the oldest was 58 years old.
As of 24:00 on March 15th, a total of 415 cases were confirmed locally, 358 cases were cured and discharged, and 8 cases died. There are 39 suspected cases. A total of 3867 close contacts have been identified, of which 441 are still in isolation medical observation. 14 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng District, 72 in Chaoyang District, 63 in Haidian District, 43 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, and 1 case in Yanqing District. There were no cases in Pinggu District and 25 cases came from other places in Beijing.
There are no new confirmed cases in 13 districts in the city for more than 14 consecutive days, specifically no reported cases in Pinggu District since the outbreak, 52 days in Yanqing District, 42 days in Mentougou District, 38 days in Huairou District, 36 days in Shunyi District, and Miyun District. 33 days, 31 days in Shijingshan District, 31 days in Daxing District, 28 days in Fangshan District, 27 days in Changping District, 25 days in Xicheng District, 25 days in Tongzhou District, and 14 days in Haidian District.
