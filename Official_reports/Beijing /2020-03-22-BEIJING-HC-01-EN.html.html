Analysis of imported cases outside Beijing
Date: 2020-03-22                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

Beijing has not reported any newly confirmed local cases for 15 consecutive days. As of 24:00 on March 21, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 391 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharge was 94.2%.
Yesterday, a total of 13 newly confirmed imported cases and 8 suspected cases were imported overseas. As of 24:00 on March 21, a total of 97 imported confirmed cases were reported overseas, and 7 cases were cured and discharged. The 97 overseas imported cases have the following characteristics:
I. Source of input cases: 30 cases in the UK, 27 cases in Spain, 18 cases in Italy, 6 cases in the United States, 3 cases in Austria, 3 cases in Hungary, 3 cases in France, 3 cases in Iran, 1 case in Thailand, 1 case in Brazil, 1 case in Luxembourg, 1 case in the Netherlands. The proportion of imported cases in the United Kingdom, Spain, Italy, and the United States accounted for 84%.
2. Age distribution of cases: The age range is 1 to 69 years, of which 1 case is 2 years old and below, accounting for 1%; 8 cases is 3 years to 17 years, accounting for 8.3%; 87 cases are 18 to 59 years, accounting for 89.7% ; 1 case aged 60 years and over, accounting for 1%.
Case Gender: Male 33, female 64, male to female ratio 1: 1.9.
Case registration: 5 cases of foreign nationality; 92 cases of domestic household registration, of which 21 were in Zhejiang and 13 in Shandong.
V. Case occupation: 51 cases of international students, accounting for 52.6%; 14 cases of housework and unemployment, accounting for 14.4%; 7 cases of employees, accounting for 7.2%; 25 cases of other personnel, accounting for 25.8%.
6. Classification of cases: 4 cases were severe, 46 cases were ordinary, and 47 cases were light.
The epidemic situation abroad is severe and complicated, and the import risks brought about by the rapid spread of the international epidemic continue to increase. Preventing the import of overseas epidemics is the focus of current prevention and control. Sincerely remind overseas Chinese and overseas Chinese who have fever, cough and other symptoms, they should go to the local medical institution in time to consciously abide by the local requirements for the prevention and control of infectious diseases, avoid long-distance travel and reduce the risk of infection.
Those who come to Beijing must strictly abide by the relevant laws and regulations on epidemic prevention and control, implement the full process and closed management of entry personnel from Beijing ports, declare personal health information in accordance with the law, cooperate with Beijing Customs quarantine and medical staff to implement various measures, and report travel truthfully History and medical history.
