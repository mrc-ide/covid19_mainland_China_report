No new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Beijing, 6 cured and discharged
Date: 2020-03-03                 Source: Beijing Municipal Health Committee

Between 00:00 and 24:00 on March 2, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Beijing. 14 new suspected cases and 7 close contacts were reported. Six discharged patients were cured and discharged from two designated hospitals in the urban area. There are 2 men and 4 women, the youngest is 34 years old and the oldest is 56 years old.

As of 24:00 on March 2nd, a total of 414 confirmed cases, 288 discharged patients were cured, and 8 died. There are 37 suspected cases. A total of 2735 close contacts were identified, of which 273 were still under medical observation. 13 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng, 71 in Chaoyang, 63 in Haidian, 42 in Fengtai, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, 1 case in Yanqing District, 25 cases came from overseas, and 2 cases came from overseas.
