10 new cases of new coronary pneumonia confirmed in Beijing, 13 cured and discharged
Date: 2020-02-27                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From February 06:00 to 24:00 on February 26, 10 new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Beijing, all of whom were close contacts of the confirmed cases. They belonged to a united epidemic of imported units from other provinces and have been sent to designated medical institutions for treatment. There were 10 new suspected cases and 46 existing suspected cases. One new death was reported. There were 84 new close contacts, and 2658 close contacts were identified, of which 1852 cases have been released from medical observation. Thirteen patients with new crown pneumonia were discharged from Ditan Hospital, You'an Hospital, the Fifth Medical Center of the General Hospital of the PLA, Hui People's Hospital, Haidian Hospital, and Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine Hospital of Fengtai District. There are 5 males and 8 females; the youngest is 17 years old and the oldest is 69 years old.

As of 24:00 on February 26, a total of 410 cases were confirmed in Beijing, 248 were discharged, and 5 died. 13 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng District, 70 in Chaoyang District, 62 in Haidian District, 42 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, 1 case in Yanqing District, and 25 cases came from overseas. No cases have been reported in Pinggu District.

Of the 410 confirmed cases, 196 were male cases, accounting for 47.8%, and 214 were female cases, accounting for 52.2%. The age range was 6 months to 94 years, of which 14 cases were under 5 years old, accounting for 3.4%, and 6 to 17 years old. 13 cases, accounting for 3.2%, 273 cases aged 18 to 59, accounting for 66.6%, 110 cases aged 60 and above, accounting for 26.8%.
