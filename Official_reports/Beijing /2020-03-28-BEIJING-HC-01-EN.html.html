Yesterday, 3 newly imported overseas cases were reported in Beijing, but no new cases were reported locally
Date: 2020-03-28                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 27, 3 newly confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, all of which were imported cases from the United States. One patient was discharged from the hospital. A total of 3 newly confirmed imported cases were imported yesterday. As of 24:00 on March 27, a total of 156 overseas confirmed confirmed cases and 15 discharged patients were reported.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 27, no new confirmed cases were reported locally. One new case was suspected yesterday. As of 24:00 on March 27, a total of 416 locally confirmed cases were reported, 394 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 94.7%. Zh
