4 new cases of new pneumonia in Beijing, 5 cured and discharged
Date: 2020-03-07                 Source: Beijing Municipal Health Committee

From 00:00 to 24:00 on March 6, 4 new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Beijing, and 3 cases were imported from Italy; 1 was a confirmed case in our city, and the case was treated as a suspected case on February 6 In the hospital, 3 nucleic acid tests were negative during the hospitalization. On February 20, due to limited home isolation conditions, the patient was observed at the centralized isolation point for 14 days, and rechecked on March 5. The nucleic acid test was positive. Comprehensive lung imaging was performed by an expert. For confirmed cases. The designated hospitals have admitted more than 4 confirmed cases. 13 new suspected cases and 99 close contacts were reported. Five patients were cured and discharged from two designated hospitals in the urban area. There are 2 men and 3 women, the youngest is 31 years old and the oldest is 62 years old.

As of 24:00 on March 6, a total of 426 confirmed cases, 303 cured cases and 8 dead cases were cured. There are 35 suspected cases. A total of 3,545 close contacts have been identified, of which 470 are still in isolation medical observation. 14 confirmed cases in the Middle East, 53 in Xicheng District, 72 in Chaoyang District, 63 in Haidian District, 43 in Fengtai District, 14 in Shijingshan District, 3 in Mentougou District, 16 in Fangshan District, 19 in Tongzhou District, and 10 in Shunyi District There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, and 1 case in Yanqing District. There were no cases in Pinggu District, 25 cases came from abroad, and 11 cases were imported abroad.

Twelve districts in the city have had no new confirmed cases for more than 14 consecutive days. Specifically, there have been no reported cases in Pinggu District since the outbreak. Yanqing District is 43 days, Mentougou District is 33 days, Huairou District is 29 days, Shunyi District is 27 days, and Miyun District 24 days, 22 days in Shijingshan District, 22 days in Daxing District, 19 days in Fangshan District, 18 days in Changping District, 16 days in Xicheng District, and 16 days in Tongzhou District.

Of the 426 confirmed cases, 205 were male cases, accounting for 48.1%, and 221 were female cases, accounting for 51.9%; the age range was 6 months to 94 years, of which 14 were under 5 years, accounting for 3.3%, and 6 to 17 years 14 cases, accounting for 3.3%, 287 cases aged 18 to 59, accounting for 67.4%, 111 cases aged 60 and above, accounting for 26%.
