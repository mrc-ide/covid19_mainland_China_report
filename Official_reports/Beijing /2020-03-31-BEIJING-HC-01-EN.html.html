Yesterday, 3 newly imported overseas cases were reported in Beijing, but no new cases were reported locally
Date: 2020-03-31                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 30, 3 newly confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, including 2 in Serbia and 1 in Spain. One patient was discharged from the hospital. A total of 3 newly confirmed imported cases were imported yesterday. As of 24:00 on March 30, a total of 164 confirmed imported cases and 20 discharged patients have been reported.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 30, no new confirmed cases were reported locally. As of 24:00 on March 30, a total of 416 locally confirmed cases were reported, 396 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 95.2%. Zh
