10 new cases of overseas imported confirmed cases were reported from 12:00 to 24:00 on March 18th in Beijing
Date: 2020-03-19                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 18, 10 newly diagnosed imported cases were reported, including 3 in the UK, 3 in Hungary, 2 in Spain, and 2 in Austria. One case was cured and discharged. As of 24:00 on March 18, a total of 64 imported confirmed cases were reported overseas and 6 cases were cured and discharged. There are 40 suspected cases.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 18, no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia were reported. As of 24:00 on March 18, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 373 cases were cured and discharged, and the cure discharge rate was 89.9%. There are 1 suspected cases. No new confirmed cases have been reported locally in our city for 12 days.
As of 24:00 on March 18, a total of 479 confirmed cases were reported, 379 cases were cured and discharged, and 8 cases died. There are 41 suspected cases. A total of 3,975 close contacts have been identified, of which 344 are still in isolation medical observation.
There have been no new confirmed cases in 15 districts of the city for more than 14 consecutive days. Specifically, there have been no reported cases since the epidemic in Pinggu District, 55 days in Yanqing District, 45 days in Mentougou District, 41 days in Huairou District, 39 days in Shunyi District, 36 days in Miyun District, 34 days in Shijingshan District, 34 days in Daxing District, and 31 in Fangshan District. Days, 30 days in Changping District, 28 days in Xicheng District, 28 days in Tongzhou District, 17 days in Haidian District, 15 days in Fengtai District, and 14 days in Chaoyang District.
