From 12:00 on January 28th to 12:00 on 29th, 11 new cases of pneumonia infected with new coronavirus were added in this city
2020-01-30 21:19:12 Date: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 on January 28th to 12:00 on 29th, 11 new cases of pneumonitis infected with new coronavirus were reported in this city, 7 had contact history in Hubei and other provinces, 7 were close contacts of confirmed cases, and 1 was not completed Epidemiological investigation, 4 of them had close contact with both Hubei contact history and confirmed cases. All have been sent to municipal designated medical institutions for treatment.

As of 12:00 on January 29, the city has accumulated 102 confirmed cases. 2 cases in Dongcheng District, 9 cases in Xicheng District, 19 cases in Chaoyang District, 23 cases in Haidian District, 8 cases in Fengtai District, 2 cases in Shijingshan District, 1 case in Mentougou, 7 cases in Tongzhou District, 2 cases in Shunyi District, 10 cases in Daxing District, There were 8 cases in Changping District, and 11 cases came from overseas. No cases have been found in Fangshan District, Pinggu District, Huairou District, Miyun District, and Yanqing District.
