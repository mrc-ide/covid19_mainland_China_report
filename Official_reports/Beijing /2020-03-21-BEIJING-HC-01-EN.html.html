Eight new cases of overseas imported confirmed cases were reported from 12:00 to 24:00 on March 20th in Beijing
Date: 2020-03-21                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 20, 8 newly confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, including 5 in the UK and 1 in France, Italy, and the Netherlands. A total of 14 newly confirmed imported cases and 6 suspected cases were imported yesterday. As of 24:00 on March 20, a total of 84 imported confirmed cases and 7 discharged patients were reported.
From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 20, there were no newly reported locally confirmed cases, and 2 cases were cured and discharged. As of 24:00 on March 20, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 385 cases were cured and discharged, and the rate of cured discharges was 92.8%. No new confirmed cases have been reported locally in the city for 14 days.
As of 24:00 on March 20, a total of 499 confirmed cases were reported, 392 cases were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 41 suspected cases. A total of 4033 close contacts have been identified, 330 of which are still under observation in isolation.
There have been no new confirmed cases in the districts of the city for more than 14 consecutive days. Specifically, there have been no reported cases since the outbreak in Pinggu District, 57 days in Yanqing District, 47 days in Mentougou District, 43 days in Huairou District, 41 days in Shunyi District, 38 days in Miyun District, 36 days in Shijingshan District, 36 days in Daxing District, and 33 in Fangshan District.
