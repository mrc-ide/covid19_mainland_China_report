5 cases of newly confirmed overseas imported cases were reported from 12:00 to 24:00 on March 19th in Beijing
Date: 2020-03-20                 Source: Beijing Municipal Commission of Health

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 19, 5 newly confirmed cases of newly imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported, all of which were from the UK. A total of 6 newly diagnosed imported cases and 6 suspected cases were imported yesterday. As of 24:00 on March 19, a total of 70 imported confirmed cases were reported overseas, and 6 cases were cured and discharged.

From 12:00 to 24:00 on March 19, no new confirmed cases were reported, and 4 cases were cured and discharged. As of 24:00 on March 19, a total of 415 locally confirmed cases were reported, 378 cases were cured and discharged, and the cure discharge rate was 91.1%. No new confirmed cases have been reported locally in our city for 13 days.

As of 24:00 on March 19, a total of 485 confirmed cases were reported, 384 cases were cured and discharged, and 8 died. There are 44 suspected cases. A total of 4001 close contacts have been identified, of which 331 are still in isolation medical observation.

There have been no new confirmed cases in 15 districts of the city for more than 14 consecutive days. Specifically, there have been no reported cases since the outbreak in Pinggu District. Yanqing District has 56 days, Mentougou District 46 days, Huairou District 42 days, Shunyi District 40 days, Miyun District 37 days, Shijingshan District 35 days, Daxing District 35 days, and Fangshan District 32. Days are 31 days in Changping District, 29 days in Xicheng District, 29 days in Tongzhou District, 18 days in Haidian District, 16 days in Fengtai District, and 15 days in Chaoyang District.
