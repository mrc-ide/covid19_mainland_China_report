No new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Beijing, 11 cured and discharged
Date: 2020-02-23                 Source: Beijing Municipal Health Committee

From 02:00 to 24:00 on February 22, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Beijing. There were 35 new suspected cases and 100 existing suspected cases. Nineteen new close contacts were added, and a total of 2452 close contacts were identified, of which 1655 have been removed from medical observation. Eleven patients with new crown pneumonia were discharged from Ditan Hospital, the Fifth Medical Center of the General Hospital of the People's Liberation Army, Beijing Puren Hospital, the designated hospital in Shijingshan District, and the People's Hospital of Daxing District. There are 4 males and 7 females; the youngest is 6 months and the oldest is 78 years old.

As of 24:00 on February 22, a total of 399 cases were confirmed in Beijing. 189 patients were discharged, 4 died, and 17 were critically ill. 12 cases in Dongcheng District, 53 cases in Xicheng District, 60 cases in Chaoyang District, 62 cases in Haidian District, 41 cases in Fengtai District, 14 cases in Shijingshan District, 3 cases in Mentougou District, 16 cases in Fangshan District, 19 cases in Tongzhou District, and 10 cases in Shunyi District. There were 29 cases in Changping District, 39 cases in Daxing District, 7 cases in Huairou District, 7 cases in Miyun District, 1 case in Yanqing District, and 26 cases came from overseas. No cases have been reported in Pinggu District.

Of the 399 confirmed cases, 189 were male cases, accounting for 47.4%, and 210 were female cases, accounting for 52.6%; the age range was 6 months to 94 years, of which 14 were under 5 years old, accounting for 3.5%, and 6 to 17 years old. There were 13 cases, accounting for 3.2%, 262 cases aged 18 to 59, accounting for 65.7%, and 110 cases aged 60 and above, accounting for 27.6%.
