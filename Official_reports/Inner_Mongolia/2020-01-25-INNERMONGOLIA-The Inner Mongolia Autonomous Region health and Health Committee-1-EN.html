Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region on January 24, 2020
/
Published: 2020-01-25 10:33:59 nuclear draft: Cheng Li test editor: Huang Lihua editor: Chia-Yu Source: / Office of Emergency Font Size: [ small Zhong Tai ] Print [mistake]
// /
/

At 04:00 on January 24, 2020, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region reported one new confirmed case of pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus, and four new suspected cases of pneumonia infected by a new coronavirus.

//

The suspected case in Erlianhot, Xilinguole League was confirmed, and the first confirmed case was reported for Xilinguole League. There were 4 new suspected cases of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus, including 1 in Hulunbuir, 1 in Chifeng, and 2 in Ordos.

//

As of 24:00 on January 24, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region had reported two confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, one in Manzhouli City and one in Xilinguole League. There were 5 suspected cases of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus, 1 in Hulunbuir, 1 in Chifeng, 1 in Xilinguole League, and 2 in Ordos. All received isolation treatment at designated hospitals. At present, 49 close contacts have been traced and are under medical observation. (Contributed by Lu Yakai)
