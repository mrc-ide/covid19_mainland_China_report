Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region on January 23, 2020
/
Published: 2020-01-24 11:00:08 nuclear draft: Liu Wei editor: Huang Lihua Editor: Emergency Office Source: / Committee of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region health Font Size: [ small Zhong Da ] Print [I want to correct wrong]
// /
/

At 04:00 on January 23, 2020, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region reported one new confirmed case of pneumonitis infected by a new type of coronavirus, and two new suspected pneumonia cases infected by a new type of coronavirus.

//

Manzhouli City was diagnosed with a suspected case. Lemou, a 30-year-old male, lives in Wuhan and is engaged in clothing business. After arriving in Manzhouli by car on the 21st, he went directly to the Emergency Department of Manzhouli People's Hospital and was tested by the Inner Mongolia Comprehensive Disease Control Center A laboratory review confirmed that the case was a confirmed case of pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus and the first confirmed case reported in Inner Mongolia.

//

Two new suspected cases of pneumonia caused by Coronavirus infection in Xilinguole League: 1 case in Erlianhot City, female patient, 27 years old, working in Wuhan for a long time, January 22, taking the K3 train from Beijing Station to Moscow and arriving in Erlianhot At the station, the customs conducted a medical investigation and found that the patient was currently undergoing isolation treatment at the designated hospital. The laboratory test results of the patient were being reviewed in accordance with the regulations. Two persons with close contact with Chinese nationality were identified and medical observation was isolated; one in Xilinhot, A female patient, 58 years old, worked in Wuhan. On January 16, she came to Xilinhot from Wuhan to visit her relatives. On January 23, she went to Xilin Golmeng Central Hospital for a hot clinic. At present, the patient has received isolation treatment at a designated hospital. Patient experiments Laboratory test results are being reviewed in accordance with regulations. Three close contacts have been identified and they have been isolated for medical observation.

//

As of 24:00 on January 23, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region had reported one confirmed case of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. Two suspected cases of pneumonia caused by the new coronavirus have been traced to 20 close contacts and are undergoing medical observation.

//

The Health and Health Committee of the Autonomous Region attaches great importance to the prevention and control of pneumonia outbreaks of new coronavirus infections, and immediately established a leading group for epidemic prevention and control to implement a 24-hour on-duty system for the entire health industry; complete preparation of health emergency supplies; Medical institutions and disease prevention and control institutions strengthen staff training, comprehensively strengthen the allocation of power for pre-screening, triage and fever, standardize the monitoring, screening, diagnosis and treatment of suspicious cases, and implement early detection, early reporting, and early isolation Early treatment and centralized treatment measures; implement various measures to prevent nosocomial infections, strengthen village, Gacha, and community prevention and control measures, implement comprehensive prevention and control measures based on prevention of importation, and delay the introduction of epidemic conditions. The patriotic health campaign has been carried out intensively throughout the region, health science popularization has been strengthened, the environment has been rectified, the farmer's market management has been done, and joint prevention and control measures have been implemented.

//

At present, it is in the season of high incidence of respiratory infectious diseases in winter and spring. Individuals must do good health protection, wash their hands frequently, pay attention to maintaining indoor sanitation and air circulation, and minimize activities to public places with no air circulation or dense crowds. Those who have returned from Wuhan recommend to take the initiative to observe the home medicine for 14 days. If respiratory symptoms or fever occur during this period, immediately put on a mask and go to the hospital for a fever clinic to actively inform the doctor of Wuhan's travel history. (Contributed by Lu Yakai)
