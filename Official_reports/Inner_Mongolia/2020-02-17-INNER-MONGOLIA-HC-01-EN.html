Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region from 9 to 17 February 2020
/
Release time: 2020-02-17 09:30:42Checkout: Liu Wei     Editor review: Huang LihuaEdit:Emergency officeArticle source: / Emergency Office Browse times:14553 timesprint this page    [I want to correct it]
///
/

From February 9th to February 9th, 2020, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region reported 2 new cases of newly diagnosed coronary pneumonia, 3 new suspected cases, 1 new discharged case, 1 severe to normal, and 1 critical to severe 1 Cases, excluding 3 suspected cases.

//

Among them: 2 newly confirmed cases were 2 in Xilinhot City, Xilinguole League.

//

Two new confirmed cases in Xilinhot City, Xilinguole League, were suspected cases on February 14 and 15, respectively. On February 14th, the suspected cases were closely related to the previously confirmed case Yin Mou, and passed the laboratory of Xilinguole Disease Control Center The test was positive and combined with the clinical symptoms, it was confirmed by the expert group on the 16th and was confirmed to be a confirmed case of new coronary pneumonia. Related epidemiological investigations are ongoing.

//

3 new suspected cases were 2 cases of Moli Dawa Banner in Hulunbuir and 1 case in Huolinguole in Tongliao.

//

As of 9:00 on February 17, 72 cases of newly diagnosed new coronary pneumonia, 8 cases of discharged patients, 4 cases of severe cases, and 4 cases of critical cases were reported in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

//

Among these 72 confirmed cases:

//

1. 11 cases of Ordos City (9 cases of Dalat Banner, 1 case of Dongsheng District, 1 case of Etokqian Banner)

//

2. 11 cases in Baotou City (7 cases in Tumoteyou Banner, 3 cases in Kundulun District, 1 case in Donghe District)

//

3. 9 cases in Chifeng City (2 cases in Yuanbaoshan District, 2 cases in Songshan District, 1 case in Linxi County, 1 case in Hongshan District, 3 cases in Wengniute Banner)

//

4. 8 cases of Bayannaoer City (2 cases in Wuyuan County, 2 cases in Linhe District, 4 cases in Wulatezhong Banner)

//

5 cases of Xilinguole League (4 cases in Xilinhot City, 1 case in Erlianhot City, 3 cases in Duolun County)

//

6 cases in Hohhot (3 cases in Yuquan District, 2 cases in Xincheng District, 1 in Saihan District)(Example, 1 case in Muslim district)

//

7. 6 cases in Tongliao City (5 cases in Huolinguole City and 1 case in Economic Development Zone)

//

8. 6 cases in Hulunbuir City (4 cases in Moli Dawa Banner, 1 case in Manzhouli City, and 1 case in Yakeshi City)

//

9. 3 cases in Wulanchabu City (2 in Huade County, 1 in Siziwang Banner)

//

10. 2 cases in Haibo Bay District, Wuhai City

//

11. A case in Ulanhot, Xing'an League

//

Among the confirmed cases, 8 were discharged (1 case in Dongsheng District, Ordos City, 1 case in Etok Front Banner, 1 case in Dalate Banner, 1 case in Siziwang Banner in Ulanqab, 1 case in Erlianhot City, Xilinguole League, and Chifeng City). 1 case in Songshan District, 1 case in Linxi County, 1 case in Manzhouli City, Hulunbuir City; 4 cases in severe cases (1 case in Yuanbao District, Chifeng City, 1 case in Wengniute Banner, 1 case in Linhe District, Bayannaoer City, Tongliao City 1 in Huolinguole City), 1 severe case was converted into a common case in Kundulun District, Baotou; 4 cases were critical cases (1 in Kundulun District, Baotou, 1 in Tuyou Banner, and Linhe in Bayannaoer 1 case in the district, 1 case in Moridawa Banner in Hulunbuir City), and 1 case in critical condition was converted into a severe case in Yuanbaoshan District of Chifeng City.

//

17 suspected cases (6 in Moridawa Banner in Hulunbuir, 1 in Hailar District, 2 in Tumoteyou Banner in Baotou City, 1 in Donghe District, 1 in Jiuyuan District, 2 in Duolun County, Xilinguole League, 2 cases in Saihan District of Hohhot City and 2 cases in Huolinguole City of Tongliao City). All received isolation treatment at designated hospitals. Three suspected cases were excluded: one in Wuda District of Wuhai City and two in Yuanbaoshan District of Chifeng City. At present, 745 close contacts are still receiving medical observations. 112 medical observations were lifted that day.

//

The Party Committee and the government of the autonomous region attach great importance to the epidemic prevention and control work in our region, and various tasks are being carried out in an orderly manner in accordance with the unified deployment of the national epidemic prevention and control work. (Contributed by Huang Lihua)
