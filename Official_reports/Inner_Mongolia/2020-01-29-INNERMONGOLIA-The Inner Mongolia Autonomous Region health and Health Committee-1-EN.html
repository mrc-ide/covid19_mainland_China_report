From 00:00 on January 28, 2020 to 10:00 on January 29, 2020, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region reported 3 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis infected by new coronavirus, 1 new severe case, 1 new critical case, and new suspected 2 cases. Among them: Among the 3 newly confirmed cases, 2 in Hohhot and 1 in Bayannaoer, both were the first confirmed cases in the two cities; 1 new critical case in Hulunbeier and 1 severe case in Xilinguole League; new suspect cases Of the 2 cases, 1 was in Ordos and 1 in Bayannaoer; 1 was suspected in Hulunbuir and 1 was suspected in Xilinguole League.
