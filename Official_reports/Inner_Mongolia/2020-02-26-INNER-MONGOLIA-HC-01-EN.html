Epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region from 7:00 February to 7:00 February 26, 2020
/

7 From February 25, 2020 to 7:00, February 26, 2020, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region reported no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, no new suspected cases, one new discharged case, and two suspected cases excluded.

//

One new case of discharge was one in Wengniute Banner, Chifeng City. 2 suspected cases were excluded as 2 in Saihan District, Hohhot.

//

As of 7:00 on February 26, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has reported a total of 75 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia, 40 confirmed cases, 35 discharged patients, 3 severe cases, and 4 critical cases.
