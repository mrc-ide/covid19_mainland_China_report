Information release of new coronavirus pneumonia in Hunan Province

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-17 08:39

At 04:00 on February 16, 2020, Hunan Province reported 2 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 1 new severe case, 0 new deaths, and 37 new discharges. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, one was in Hengyang and one in Loudi.

Among the newly added severe cases, one was in Changsha.

Among the newly discharged cases, 15 were in Changsha, 4 in Zhuzhou, 1 in Xiangtan, 5 in Shaoyang, 3 in Yueyang, 3 in Changde, 2 in Yiyang, 1 in Yongzhou, and 2 in Huaihua 1 case in Loudi City.

As of 24:00 on February 16, Hunan Province had reported 1006 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 138 severe cases, 52 existing severe cases, 3 deaths, 467 discharged cases, and 536 treated in hospital . among them:

Among the confirmed cases, 241 were in Changsha, 48 in Hengyang, 78 in Zhuzhou, 35 in Xiangtan, 101 in Shaoyang, 154 in Yueyang, 79 in Changde, 5 in Zhangjiajie, 59 in Yiyang, and Quzhou. There were 39 cases in Yongzhou City, 43 cases in Yongzhou City, 40 cases in Huaihua City, 76 cases in Loudi City, and 8 cases in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.

Among the current severe cases, 15 were in Changsha, 2 in Hengyang, 5 in Zhuzhou, 5 in Xiangtan, 7 in Shaoyang, 2 in Yueyang, 4 in Changde, 4 in Yiyang, and 2 in Luzhou. 2 in Yongzhou and 4 in Loudi.

Among the deaths, one was in Changsha, one in Shaoyang, and one in Yueyang.

Among the discharged patients, there were 88 cases in Changsha, 28 in Hengyang, 33 in Zhuzhou, 15 in Xiangtan, 64 in Shaoyang, 54 in Yueyang, 31 in Changde, 4 in Zhangjiajie, 27 in Yiyang, and Chenzhou. There were 22 cases in Beijing, 28 cases in Yongzhou, 32 cases in Huaihua, 35 cases in Loudi, and 6 cases in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.

At present, 26,092 close contacts have been traced, 22,262 medical observations have been lifted, and 3,830 people are still receiving medical observations.
