Information release of new coronavirus pneumonia in Hunan Province

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-14 08:53

   
At 04:00 on February 13, 2020, Hunan Province reported 20 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 3 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 40 new discharged cases. among them:
Among the newly confirmed cases, 10 were in Changsha, 1 in Hengyang, 2 in Zhuzhou, 2 in Xiangtan, 1 in Shaoyang, 1 in Yueyang, 1 in Changde, 1 in Luzhou, and 1 in Loudi. .
Among the newly added severe cases, one was in Yueyang City, one in Yiyang City, and one in Yongzhou City.
Among the newly discharged cases, there were 6 cases in Changsha, 2 in Hengyang, 7 in Zhuzhou, 8 in Shaoyang, 2 in Yueyang, 2 in Changde, 5 in Yongzhou, 5 in Huaihua, and 3 in Loudi. .
As of 24:00 on February 13, Hunan Province had reported a total of 988 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 130 cases of severe cases, 55 cases of severe cases, 2 deaths, and 352 discharged patients. among them:
Of the confirmed cases, 238 were in Changsha, 46 in Hengyang, 77 in Zhuzhou, 34 in Xiangtan, 100 in Shaoyang, 149 in Yueyang, 78 in Changde, 5 in Zhangjiajie, 59 in Yiyang, and Chenzhou. There were 39 cases in Yongzhou City, 43 cases in Yongzhou City, 39 cases in Huaihua City, 73 cases in Loudi City, and 8 cases in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
Among the current severe cases, there were 17 cases in Changsha, 2 in Hengyang, 6 in Zhuzhou, 4 in Xiangtan, 4 in Shaoyang, 3 in Yueyang, 4 in Changde, 5 in Yiyang, and 2 in Luzhou. 3 in Yongzhou, 1 in Huaihua and 4 in Loudi.
Among the deaths, one was in Shaoyang and one was in Yueyang.
Among the discharged patients, there were 63 cases in Changsha, 25 in Hengyang, 21 in Zhuzhou, 12 in Xiangtan, 41 in Shaoyang, 44 in Yueyang, 26 in Changde, 3 in Zhangjiajie, 22 in Yiyang, and Chenzhou. There were 15 cases in the city, 23 in Yongzhou, 26 in Huaihua, 25 in Loudi, and 6 in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
At present, 24,971 close contacts have been traced, 19,494 people have been released from medical observation, and 5,477 people are still receiving medical observation.
