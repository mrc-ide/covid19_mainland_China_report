Twelve more confirmed patients were discharged

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-05 16:00

Today, the news of the epidemic prevention and control in our province spread good news again, and another 12 confirmed patients with pneumonia infected with a new type of coronavirus were cured and discharged. So far, 34 confirmed patients have been discharged from our hospital.

On the morning of the same day, four confirmed patients were discharged from the Changsha Public Health Center. Patient Chen Moumou, male, 53 years old, Shen Moumou, female, 47 years old, two of them are husband and wife, returned to Changsha on January 18, and transferred to treatment center on January 24; Peng Moumou, male, 46 years old, Working in the catering industry in Wuhan, he returned to Changsha for treatment at Xiangya Hospital on January 25, and was transferred to a treatment center that day; Zhou, male, 29 years old, was transferred to treatment center on January 25.

At 11:00 am, the first batch of 3 confirmed patients in Changde City were cured and discharged. The patients Sun, Yang, and Tan were either regular members of Wuhan or had travel history in Wuhan. They all developed fever, dry cough, shortness of breath and other symptoms when they became ill, and were diagnosed after admission.

At 9 am, a 4-year-old confirmed patient was discharged from Zhuzhou Central Hospital. This is the youngest patient discharged from Zhuzhou. Patient Chengcheng (pseudonym) lives with his parents in Wuhan and was sent to his grandparents' home in Zhuzhou years ago. On January 27, Chengcheng started having a fever and was taken to Zhuzhou Central Hospital. He was diagnosed on January 29.

At 9 am, the first confirmed patient in Shaodong City was discharged from Shaodong People's Hospital. Patient Lee, female, 54 years old, was admitted to the hospital on January 23 for shortness of breath, cough, and general weakness, and was diagnosed on January 25.

At 5 pm, the first confirmed patient in Xiangxiang City was discharged. The patient Yang Moumou, male, came to Xiangxiang from Wuhan to visit relatives before the Spring Festival. He was diagnosed on January 26 and was immediately sent to the Baishuitang infectious disease area of Xiangxiang People's Hospital for treatment.

The first severely confirmed patient in Huaihua City was discharged. Patient Lu Moumou, female, 55 years old, suffered from "cough, sputum with chills and fever for 7 days", and went to the First Affiliated Hospital of Hunan Medical College for medical treatment on January 23, and was subsequently diagnosed.

A patient was discharged from Loudi Central Hospital. Patient Zhu Moumou, female, 35 years old, has been engaged in art training in Wuhan for a long time. She returned to Loudi on January 13 and developed sore throat and cough on the 21st. She was admitted to the hospital for treatment on the 23rd and was diagnosed on January 27th.

It is understood that during the isolation and treatment of the 12 patients who were discharged this time, the relevant hospitals provided targeted treatments such as anti-inflammatory, anti-viral, and infection prevention according to the patient's condition and physical conditions. .
