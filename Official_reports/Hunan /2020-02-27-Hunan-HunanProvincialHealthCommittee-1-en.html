Information release of new coronavirus pneumonia in Hunan Province

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-27 08:41

At 04:00 on February 26, 2020, Hunan Province reported 1 new confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia, 0 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 14 new discharged cases. among them:

Among the newly confirmed cases, one was in Yongzhou.

Among the newly discharged cases, 5 were in Changsha, 2 in Zhuzhou, 1 in Yueyang, 1 in Changde, 2 in Shengzhou, 1 in Yongzhou, 1 in Huaihua, and 1 in Loudi.
