Information release on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Hunan Province

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-05 08:46

At 04:00 on February 4, 2020, Hunan Province reported 68 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 4 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 13 new discharges. among them:
Among the newly confirmed cases, 16 were in Changsha, 4 in Hengyang, 4 in Zhuzhou, 2 in Xiangtan, 13 in Shaoyang, 6 in Yueyang, 6 in Changde, 5 in Yiyang, and 2 in Luzhou. 4 cases in Yongzhou City, 5 cases in Loudi City, and 1 case in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
Among the newly added severe cases, one was in Changsha, two in Shaoyang, and one in Yongzhou.
Among the newly discharged cases, 4 were in Changsha, 1 in Hengyang, 1 in Zhuzhou, 1 in Xiangtan, 1 in Shaoyang, 3 in Changde, 1 in Huaihua, and 1 in Loudi.
As of 24:00 on February 4th, Hunan Province had reported a total of 661 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 77 cases of severe cases, 57 cases of severe cases, 0 deaths, and 35 discharged patients. among them:
Of the confirmed cases, 164 were in Changsha, 39 in Hengyang, 38 in Zhuzhou, 23 in Xiangtan, 68 in Shaoyang, 89 in Yueyang, 56 in Changde, 3 in Zhangjiajie, 40 in Yiyang, and Chenzhou. There were 24 cases in Beijing, 30 cases in Yongzhou, 35 cases in Huaihua, 45 cases in Loudi, and 7 cases in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
Among the existing severe cases, there were 17 cases in Changsha, 2 in Hengyang, 2 in Zhuzhou, 3 in Xiangtan, 10 in Shaoyang, 4 in Yueyang, 3 in Changde, 5 in Yiyang, and 2 in Yongzhou 6 cases in Huaihua City, 2 cases in Loudi City, and 1 case in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
Among the discharged patients, there were 6 cases in Changsha, 5 in Hengyang, 2 in Zhuzhou, 4 in Xiangtan, 2 in Shaoyang, 3 in Changde, 1 in Yiyang, 1 in Shengzhou, 5 in Yongzhou, and Huaihua. There were 2 cases in the city, 3 cases in Loudi City, and 1 case in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
At present, 14,840 close contacts have been traced, 4955 medical observations have been lifted, and 9885 people are still receiving medical observations.
