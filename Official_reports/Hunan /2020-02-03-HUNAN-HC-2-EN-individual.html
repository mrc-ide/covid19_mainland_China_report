Eight confirmed patients discharged from hospital in Hunan a day

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-03 12:27

On February 2nd, there was good news on the front line of the epidemic prevention and control in our province, and another 8 patients with pneumonia diagnosed with new-type coronavirus were carefully treated by medical staff to reach the discharge standard and were cured and discharged. So far, 16 confirmed patients have been discharged from our province.

At 9:46 am on the same day, three patients with pneumonia infected with new-type coronavirus in Hengyang City were cured and discharged from the First Affiliated Hospital of Nanhua University. This is the first batch of confirmed patients discharged from Hengyang City. All three patients were from Hengyang and all had contact history in Wuhan. Among them, Wang Moumou, male, 29 years old, had traveled to Wuhan with fever and chills. He was admitted to the hospital on January 21; Jiang Moumou, 52 years old, worked in Wuhan with fever and headache for 6 days. She was admitted to hospital on January 22; Shen, female, 38 years old, worked in Wuhan South China Seafood Market for about one month, and was admitted to hospital on January 23 for fever and cough for one week.

On the morning of the same day, one patient was discharged from the hospital in Longshan County People's Hospital in the western Hunan Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture. This is the first patient diagnosed with new-type coronavirus-infected pneumonia discharged from Xiangxi. Patient Qiu Moumou, female, 27 years old, developed fever, dry cough, headache and other symptoms after returning to Longshan from Wuhan on January 21. She was admitted to the hospital on January 23 and was diagnosed as a patient with pneumonitis infected by a new type of coronavirus.

In the afternoon of the same day, three patients with pneumonia infected with a new coronavirus were discharged from the hospital in Xiangtan Central Hospital. This is the first batch of confirmed patients discharged from Xiangtan City. Among them, Ms. Li was a native of Xiangtan. She had purchased goods in Wuhan on January 11. She returned to the city on the 13th and developed symptoms such as cough and fever. She went to the Xiangtan Central Hospital for treatment on the 24th and was diagnosed with pneumonitis patients with a new coronavirus infection. Mr. Song settled in Wuhan and returned to Xiangtan by car on January 19. He developed cough, sore throat, and other discomforts. He went to the Xiangtan Central Hospital for treatment on the 24th, and was later diagnosed with a pneumonia patient with a new coronavirus infection. Ms. Nie has a working history in Wuhan. Because of fever and fatigue for 10 days, he was admitted to the hospital on January 29 and was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus.

At 16:15 on the same day, a confirmed patient treated at Youxian People's Hospital in Zhuzhou City was cured and discharged. This is the first patient diagnosed with new-type coronavirus-infected pneumonia discharged from Zhuzhou City. Patient Ms. Chen, 56 years old, from Lianxing Street, Youxian County, who runs a small hotel in Wuhan City. She returned to Youxian County on January 16 and developed symptoms such as fever and diarrhea. She went to the Youxian People's Hospital on January 20, January 24. He was diagnosed with a pneumonia case of a new coronavirus infection.

It is understood that during the isolation and treatment of the eight patients who were discharged this time, the relevant hospital attached great importance to the establishment of an expert group, and according to the patient's condition and physical conditions, given targeted treatments such as anti-virus and infection prevention, and organized medical staff for 24-hour escort They also conducted psychological counseling.
