Information release on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Hunan Province

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-02 09:26

 
At 04:00 on February 1, 2020, Hunan Province reported 74 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 2 new severe cases, 0 new deaths, and 5 new discharged cases. among them:
Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 26 cases in Changsha, 4 in Hengyang, 3 in Zhuzhou, 3 in Xiangtan, 8 in Shaoyang, 8 in Yueyang, 3 in Changde, 1 in Zhangjiajie, and 3 in Yiyang. There were 5 cases in Shengzhou City, 4 cases in Yongzhou City, 3 cases in Huaihua City, and 3 cases in Loudi City.
Among the newly added severe cases, one was in Changsha and one in Yiyang.
As of 24:00 on February 1, Hunan Province had reported a total of 463 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, 56 severe cases, 0 deaths, and 8 discharged patients. There are 44 severe cases, including:
Among the confirmed cases, there were 112 cases in Changsha, 30 in Hengyang, 25 in Zhuzhou, 16 in Xiangtan, 49 in Shaoyang, 53 in Yueyang, 42 in Changde, 3 in Zhangjiajie, 27 in Yiyang, and Quzhou. There were 18 cases in Beijing, 22 cases in Yongzhou, 31 cases in Huaihua, 29 cases in Loudi, and 6 cases in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.     
Among the current severe cases, 9 cases were in Changsha City, 6 cases in Zhuzhou City, 1 case in Xiangtan City, 7 cases in Shaoyang City, 2 cases in Yueyang City, 4 cases in Changde City, 3 cases in Yiyang City, 2 cases in Yongzhou City, and 8 cases in Huaihua City. 1 in Loudi City and 1 in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.   
At present, 9,599 close contacts have been traced, 1,353 medical observations have been lifted, and 8246 are still receiving medical observations.
