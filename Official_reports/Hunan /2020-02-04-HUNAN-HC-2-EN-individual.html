Eight patients with new type of coronavirus pneumonia were cured and discharged in Hunan

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-02-04 11:36

From January 30, the first-line reports of Hunan Province's epidemic-resistant epidemic have been circulating. As of the morning of February 4, a total of 30 confirmed patients with pneumonia infected with a new coronavirus have been discharged from hospital. They were: 6 cases in Changsha, 2 in Loudi, 1 in Shaoyang, 1 in Huaihua, 5 in Yongzhou, 1 in Luzhou, 4 in Hengyang, 1 in Xiangxi Prefecture, 2 in Zhuzhou, 3 in Xiangtan, 1 in Yiyang, and 3 in Changde. example.
