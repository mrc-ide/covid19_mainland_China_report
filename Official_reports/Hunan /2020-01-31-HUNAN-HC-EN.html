Information release on pneumonia of new coronavirus infection in Hunan Province

Hunan Provincial Health Committee Time: 2020-01-31 09:39

At 04:00 on January 30, 2020, Hunan Province reported 55 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 10 new severe cases (6 of which were previously confirmed cases), and 0 new death cases. Two new cases were discharged. among them:
Among the newly confirmed cases, 11 were in Changsha, 5 in Hengyang, 1 in Zhuzhou, 1 in Xiangtan, 7 in Shaoyang, 8 in Yueyang, 7 in Changde, 3 in Yiyang, and 2 in Luzhou. 2 cases in Yongzhou City, 3 cases in Huaihua City, 4 cases in Loudi City, 1 case in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture; among the newly added severe cases, 1 case was in Changsha City, 1 case was in Hengyang City, 1 case was in Xiangtan City, 1 case was in Yueyang City, and 1 was in Changde City. There were 1 case in Yiyang City, 1 case in Yongzhou City, 2 cases in Huaihua City, and 1 case in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture. Among the newly discharged cases, 1 case was in Changsha City and 1 case was in Luzhou City.
As of 24:00 on January 30, Hunan Province had reported a total of 332 confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection, a total of 48 severe cases, 34 existing severe cases, 0 deaths, and 2 discharged patients. among them:
Of the confirmed cases, 68 were in Changsha, 25 in Hengyang, 19 in Zhuzhou, 10 in Xiangtan, 36 in Shaoyang, 37 in Yueyang, 37 in Changde, 1 in Zhangjiajie, 20 in Yiyang, and Chenzhou. There were 12 cases in the city, 16 in Yongzhou, 26 in Huaihua, 20 in Loudi, and 5 in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
Among the current severe cases, there were 6 cases in Changsha, 1 in Hengyang, 3 in Zhuzhou, 5 in Shaoyang, 2 in Yueyang, 3 in Changde, 3 in Yiyang, 2 in Yongzhou, and 7 in Huaihua. 1 in Loudi City and 1 in Xiangxi Autonomous Prefecture.
At present, 5,990 close contacts have been traced, 388 people have been released from medical observation, and 5602 people are still receiving medical observation.
