Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Hainan Province at 0-24 on January 31, 2020

source: Release Date: 2020-02-01 share to: [Font: small medium large ]

At 04:00 on January 31, 2020, Hainan Province reported 8 new confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 1 new severe case, 0 new death cases, and 0 new discharged cases; Among the confirmed cases, there were 2 cases in Haikou City, 1 in Sanya City, 1 in Luzhou City, 1 in Lingshui County, 1 in Ledong County, and 2 in Lingao County. Among the newly added cases, 1 was in Sanya City.

As of 24:00 on January 31, Hainan Province had reported 57 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, 9 severe cases, 1 death case, and 1 discharged case. Among the confirmed cases, there were 11 cases in Haikou, 15 in Sanya, 6 in Shengzhou, 4 in Qionghai, 8 in Wanning, 1 in Dongfang, 2 in Chengmai County, 3 in Lingao County, Ling There were 3 cases in Shui County, 1 case in Qiongzhong County, 1 case in Ding'an County, 1 case in Changjiang County, and 1 case in Ledong County.

As of 24:00 on January 31, 1,549 close contacts have been traced, 489 medical observations have been lifted, and 1060 are still receiving home or intensive medical observations.
