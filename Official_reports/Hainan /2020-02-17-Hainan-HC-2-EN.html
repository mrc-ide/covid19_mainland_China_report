Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Hainan Province from 0-24 on February 16, 2020

source: Release Date: 2020-02-17 share to: [Font: small  Chinese  Big】

At 00: 00-24: 00 on February 16, 2020, Hainan Province reported 0 new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia，There were 7 new severe cases, 0 new deaths and 13 new discharges. Among the newly added severe cases, 1 was in Haikou, 5 in Sanya, and 1 in Wanning. Among the newly discharged cases, 3 were in Haikou, 5 were in Luzhou, 2 were in Qionghai, and 1 was in Wanning. 1 case in Ding'an County and 1 case in Baoting County.

As of 24:00 on February 16, Hainan Province had reported a total of 162 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, 19 severe cases, 4 deaths, and 52 discharged patients. Among the confirmed cases, there were 33 cases in Haikou, 54 in Sanya, 15 in Shengzhou, 3 in Wenchang, 6 in Qionghai, 13 in Wanning, 3 in Dongfang, 9 in Chengmai County, and Lingao There were 6 cases in county, 7 cases in Changjiang County, 4 cases in Lingshui County, 3 cases in Ding'an County, 3 cases in Baoting County, 2 cases in Ledong County, and 1 case in Qiongzhong County.

As of 24:00 on February 16th, 5,010 close contacts were tracked, 3,877 medical observations have been lifted, and 1,133 are still receiving centralized medical observations.
