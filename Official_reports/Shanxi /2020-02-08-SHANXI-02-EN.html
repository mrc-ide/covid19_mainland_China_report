At 7 o'clock on January 23, 2020 , I drove from Wuhan to Pingyao and stopped for half an hour to eat and eat in Xiaogan Service Area, Hubei.

At 16:30 on February 5, 2020 , he went to the Pingyao County People's Hospital for a fever clinic.
