Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Shanxi Province on February 20, 2020

Release time: 2020-02-20 01:16:00 Publisher: Editor of this article Source of article: Original of this website BrowseTimes

From 09:00 to 24:00 on February 19, 2020, Shanxi Province did not report any new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, and a total of 131 confirmed cases. 7 new cases were cured and discharged,A total of 68 patients were discharged from the hospital. There are currently 63 cases of in-patient isolation and treatment, of which 5 are critically ill and 1 is critically ill. 1 new suspected case and 11 existing suspected cases. At present, a total of 4010 close contacts have been tracked. 105 people were released from medical observation on the same day, and a total of 312 people were receiving medical observation.
