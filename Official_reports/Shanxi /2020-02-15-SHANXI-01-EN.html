Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Shanxi Province on February 15, 2020

Published: 2020-02-15 01:58:10 Publisher: Edited by this article Source: Original created by this site Browse Times

2020 at 0:00 on February 14 to 24, the new report Shanxi Province confirmed cases of novel coronavirus pneumonia in one case, a total of 127 cases of confirmed cases. Add cured in 2 patients recovered, total 38 cases were cured. There are currently 89 cases in hospital isolation treatment , of which 6 are critically ill and 4 are critically ill . There were 12 new suspected cases and 62 existing suspected cases. A total of 3,838 close contacts have been tracked . Of the 231 people who were released from medical observation on that day , a total of 727 people are receiving medical observation.

 

Unit: Example

Note: According to the requirements of the National Health and Health Commission, statistics are based on the county where the hospital is located when the case is diagnosed

At present, the newly confirmed cases in our province are at a low level, and they are cured and discharged every day. The number of cases treated in the hospital is slowly decreasing, and there are still new suspected cases every day. Experts remind: Although the epidemic situation of new crown pneumonia in our province is in a stable period, it cannot be blindly optimistic, and the situation of prevention and control is still grim.
