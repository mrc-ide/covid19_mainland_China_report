6 new cases confirmed in Shanxi Province on February 6

Release time: 2020-02-07 22:36:01 Publisher: Edited by this article Source: Original created by this site Browse Times

Case 1: Male, 24 years old. The current address is Rongchen Jiayuan, Ji County, Linfen City.

On the afternoon of January 22, 2020, take bus 849 in Taiyuan to Taiyuan South Station. At 15:42, take the train D9205 from Taiyuan South to Linfen West (seat number 02.16C) and arrive at Linfen West Station at 17:07. Returned to Jixian's home from Linji Expressway in a private van with a total of 8 people.

On January 27, 2020, she developed fever, sore throat, and expectoration. She took self-purchased drugs.

On January 30, 2020, she went to the Jixian People's Hospital for treatment. Her temperature was 37.5 ° C, her blood tests were normal, and she was instructed to observe and treat at home.

At 10:42 on February 2, 2020, due to no remission, he went to the Jixian People's Hospital for a fever clinic.

On February 4, 2020, the nucleic acid test was positive, and he was transferred to Linfen Third People's Hospital (municipal designated medical institution) for treatment.

The diagnosis was confirmed on February 6, 2020.

Case 2: Male, 74 years old. The current address is Ludong Community (Honglou Ganxiu) in Xiushui Town, Yu County, Yangquan City.

At 2:30 on the morning of January 24, 2020, the son of the patient (diagnosed on February 5) came to his home.

At 22:25 on February 4, 2020, 120 was sent from home to the Yuxian People's Hospital for isolation medical observation.

Those diagnosed with asymptomatic infection on February 6, 2020.

Case 3: Female, 35 years old, current address is Xiangle Village, Xiangle Town, Pingyao County.

Live in Xiangle Town Police Station, Pingyao County before January 21, 2020.

On January 21, 2020, he returned to live with his husband (back to Pingyao from Wuhan) and visited his mother at 16:30 on the same day.

Attend the family banquet twice at 12 o'clock on January 22, 2020 and 23 o'clock on 23 January 2020.

At 14:00 on January 24, 2020, go to Wanfulong Supermarket in Xiangle Village.

Fever symptoms occurred at 23:00 on February 3, 2020.

At 19:00 on February 4, 2020, the 120 ambulance of the Pingyao County People's Hospital received a fever clinic from the County People's Hospital.

The diagnosis was confirmed on February 6, 2020.

Case 4: Male, 64 years old, currently living in the middle village of Zhengzhuang Town, Qinshui County.

From January 24th to 29th, 2020, he went to Liu's home in the village (diagnosed on February 2nd) at home.

On February 5, 2020, she developed fever and chest discomfort and went to the Qinshui County People's Hospital for treatment.

The diagnosis was confirmed on February 6, 2020.

Case 5: Male, 66 years old, current address is Xinghua North Community, Huifeng Street, Jiancao District.

On January 20 and 2020, his grandson and son (diagnosed on February 4) returned from Wuhan and lived with him. The patient lived in Taiyuan for a long time and had no history of going out within 14 days before the onset of illness.

On the morning of January 29, 2020, he walked to Shanxi Wanfeng Hospital with his wife (diagnosed on February 4) and was hospitalized.

On February 6, 2020, he was transferred to the Fourth People's Hospital of Taiyuan for hospitalization and confirmed diagnosis.

Case 6: Male, 12 years old. The current address is Xinghua North Community, Huifeng Street, Jiancao District.

Take the Wuhan-Taiyuan HU7630 flight to Taiyuan on January 20, 2020. His aunt and aunt drove home to Grandpa (diagnosed on February 6).

Medical observation at home on February 5, 2020.

On February 6, 2020, he was transferred to the Fourth People's Hospital of Taiyuan for hospitalization and confirmed diagnosis.
