(January 27, 2020) Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Infection in Shanxi Province

Release time: 2020-01-27 05:25:07 Publisher: Edited by this article Source: Original created by this site Browse Times

From 06:00 to 24:00 on January 26, 2020 , Shanxi Province reported 4 new confirmed cases of pneumonia due to new coronavirus infection. Among them: Shuozhou City, Linfen City, and Luliang City are the first confirmed cases reported. Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 2 cases in Shuozhou City, 1 in Linfen City and 1 in Luliang City.

As of 24:00 on January 26, 13 cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection have been reported in 9 cities in Shanxi Province, including 2 in Taiyuan, 2 in Shuozhou, 2 in Yuncheng, 2 in Jinzhong, and Datong. There was 1 case in Shanghai, 1 case in Changzhi, 1 case in Yangquan, 1 case in Linfen, and 1 case in Luliang.

At present, 312 close contacts have been traced, and 54 people have been released from medical observation.

 

The prevention and control of pneumonia caused by new coronavirus infection is a major political task to protect the lives and health of the people and maintain social harmony and stability. At present, in accordance with the spirit of the Provincial Party Committee's special meeting, the entire province is carrying out the "five strictures and five preventions" work of strict control of the entrance, strict control of infections, strict control of the spread of spreads, strict control of out-of-control gathering, and publicity of panic prevention. Measures, carefully implemented various measures of "anti-input, non-proliferation, and anti-export", further strengthened "prevention, control, and governance", resolutely curb the spread of the epidemic, and effectively and effectively fight the epidemic prevention and control.

Experts remind: since January 8, 2020, people coming to and from Wuhan in Wuhan must actively cooperate with townships, streets, community registration and screening, and health screening of medical staff at primary medical institutions to protect them and minimize contact with other people. Opportunities to take the initiative to do their own health management. For those who are identified as close contacts, please keep in mind your social responsibilities, take the initiative to cooperate with relevant departments for home medical observation, and be responsible for the health of yourself, your family and the public.
