A total of 165 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection in Shaanxi
/
source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-02-05 10:19 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

As of 10:00 on February 5th, 23 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new-type coronavirus infection were newly added in Shaanxi (the first confirmed cases were found in pregnant women), and 41 new suspected cases were added. Among the newly confirmed cases, 10 were in Xi'an, 4 in Xianyang, 3 in Weinan, 3 in Hanzhong, 2 in Ankang, and 1 in Shangluo. A total of 165 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection were reported in Shaanxi (two were cured and discharged), of which 100 were imported cases, 58 were close contacts, and 7 cases had no clear contact history. Among the cumulative confirmed cases, there were 70 cases in Xi'an, 10 in Baoji, 15 in Xianyang, 7 in Tongchuan, 9 in Weinan, 7 in Yanan, 1 in Yulin, 19 in Hanzhong, 19 in Ankang, Six cases were in Shangluo City, one in Yangling Demonstration Area, and one in Hancheng City. There are 215 suspected cases in the province. There were 800 new close contacts in the province, and a total of 7,040 people, all of whom focused on medical observation. There were 449 new close contacts released, and a total of 1,752 close contacts were lifted.

Patient 1, female, 53 years old, currently lives in Yanliang District, Xi'an. And the 7 patients diagnosed on February 3 were married. Isolated for observation on January 28, and symptoms appeared on February 1. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 2, female, 35 years old, currently lives in Lintong District, Xi'an. On January 20, I shopped at Xi'an Colorful Shopping Mall. On February 2, I developed symptoms. I went to a clinic in Fujia Village, Xiekou Street, Lintong District, and then went to the Lintong District People's Hospital for treatment. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 3, female, 50 years old, now living in Yanta District, Xi'an, was diagnosed as the sister of Patient 3 on January 31. He was isolated for observation on January 31, and symptoms appeared that night. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 4, male, 66 years old, currently lives in Xincheng District, Xi'an City, and has had multiple dinners. Symptoms occurred on February 1st, and he went to the Second People's Hospital of Shaanxi Province that day. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 5, male, 33 years old, now lives in Xixian New District, and has a dinner experience with patient 7 confirmed on February 1. Symptoms occurred on the 24th. On the 25th, he went to a clinic in Changning Town, Wugong County, Xianyang City. On the 31st, he went to the First Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Medical College. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the First Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Medical College, his condition is stable.

Patient 6, female, 67 years old, currently lives in Xiqiao District, Xi'an City, and has a history of shopping at Colorful Shopping Mall. Symptoms occurred on January 29 and were taken to Tangdu Hospital on February 3. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Tangdu Hospital, his condition is stable.

Patient 7, male, 50 years old, currently lives in Weiyang District, Xi'an. From Hubei Province to Xi'an on January 24, I was observed at home on the same day. Symptoms appeared on February 3, and the same day went to Tangdu Hospital. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Tangdu Hospital, his condition is stable.

Patient 8, male, 47 years old, now lives in Xincheng District, Xi'an, and runs a shop in Colorful Shopping Mall. Symptoms appeared on January 23, and symptoms worsened on the 25th to the Xi'an Electric Power Central Hospital. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the Second Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University, his condition is stable.

Patient 9, female, 43 years old, currently lives in Xincheng District, Xi'an, and has a husband and wife relationship with Patient 8. Symptoms appeared on January 28, and went to the Xi'an Electric Power Central Hospital for the same day. On February 2, he was transferred to the Second Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the Second Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University, his condition is stable.

Patient 10, female, 77 years old, now lives in Weiyang District, Xi'an. From January 27, Guangzhou to Xi'an, Guangdong, symptoms appeared on the 31st, and went to the Third Hospital of Xi'an on the same day. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 11, male, 40 years old, born in Wuhan, Hubei Province, was diagnosed with the son of patient 3 on February 2. From January 22 to Wuhan, Wugong County, Xianyang City, symptoms appeared on the 28th, and went to the People's Hospital of Wugong County that day. On the 31st, he was transferred to Wugong People's Hospital for isolation and treatment. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Xianyang Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 12, male, 9 years old, born in Wuhan, Hubei Province, is the son of patient 11. He was isolated for observation on January 31, and transferred to the People's Hospital of Wugong County for observation on February 1. The new coronavirus nucleic acid test was positive on February 5. Currently in isolation and treatment at Xianyang Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 13, male, 68 years old, currently living in Sanyuan County, Xianyang City, has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on February 3, and she went to Sanyuan County Hospital the same day. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment in Sanyuan County Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 14, female, 59 years old, currently lives in Sanyuan County, Xianyang City, and has a husband-wife relationship with patient 13 and has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on February 1, and she went to the Sanyuan County Hospital on February 3. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment in Sanyuan County Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 15, female, 39 years old, currently lives in Zhenba County, Hanzhong City, and has a husband-wife relationship with 14 on February 4. Symptoms appeared on January 27, and went to the Zhenba County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital on the 28th. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. He is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at the No. 20121 Hospital in Hanzhong City, and his condition is stable.

Patient 16, male, 24 years old, currently living in Nanzheng District, Hanzhong City, has no clear history of exposure. On January 22, symptoms of fever, sore throat, and diarrhea appeared in Xi'an. At the same day, he went to the Chang'an Hospital for treatment and took medicine by himself. He arrived in Hanzhong City on the 24th, and his symptoms worsened on the 26th. He went to the 321 Hospital of Hanzhong City that day. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. He is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at the No. 20121 Hospital in Hanzhong City, and his condition is stable.

Patient 17, female, 44 years old, currently living in Xixiang County, Hanzhong City, and had a dinner experience with patients 3 and 4 diagnosed on January 27. He was isolated for observation on January 29, and the positive result of the new coronavirus nucleic acid test was positive on February 1, and he was transferred to Xixiang County People's Hospital on February 2. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the Second People's Hospital of Hanzhong City, his condition is stable.

Patient 18, male, 48 years old, now living in Baihe County, Ankang City, has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on February 2. On February 3, the symptoms aggravated and went to the Cangshang Town Health Center for treatment. He was then transferred to Baihe County Hospital for treatment. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Ankang Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 19, female, 4 years old, currently living in Hanbin District, Ankang City, has a history of travel in Wuhan. Symptoms occurred on January 31, and they were quarantined on February 1 for observation. On February 2, the symptoms worsened and they were transferred to Hanbin District Third Hospital for isolation and treatment. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Ankang Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 20, male, 64 years old, now living in Linwei District, Weinan City, with no clear history of exposure. Symptoms occurred on January 30th, and went to the Second Hospital of Weinan City on February 3rd. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the Second Hospital of Weinan City, his condition is stable.

Patient 21, female, 64 years old, now living in Linwei District, Weinan City, was the mother of Patient 4 on February 3. Isolated for observation on January 30, and transferred to Weinan Central Hospital on February 4. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Weinan Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 22, female, 37 years old, Linwei District, Weinan City, was the daughter of patient 7 on January 31. On January 30, he was isolated for observation. On the 31st, he developed symptoms and was transferred to the Weinan Central Hospital for treatment on February 1. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Weinan Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 23, female, 33 years old, now living in Danfeng County, Shangluo City, was diagnosed with the infants of patients 1 and 2 on February 4 and was 8 months pregnant. She was quarantined and observed on February 2 and continued to have a dry cough. February 5 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Shangluo Central Hospital, his condition is stable.

Another patient was confirmed on February 4th. After further epidemiological investigation, it was found that he had contact with patient 10 confirmed on January 31st.
