Our province steadily carries out health supervision on pneumonia epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection
/
source: Comprehensive Supervision Bureau Release time: 2020-02-01 10:00 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

Recently, the Supervision Bureau of the Provincial Health and Health Committee has carefully studied and implemented General Secretary Xi Jinping's important instructions on the prevention and control of pneumonia epidemic of new coronavirus infections, implemented the province's decision-making and deployment of epidemic prevention and control, remembered its duties and missions, insisted on moving the gate forward, and regularly analyzed Situation, study response measures, clarify work priorities, strengthen training and guidance, increase inspection efforts, take multiple measures simultaneously, and solidly carry out health supervision during epidemic prevention and control. As of January 30, the province has trained 13,113 supervisors, 6,696 supervisors dispatched outbreaks, 1,569 vehicles, inspected 2,553 medical institutions, 4,158 public places such as hotels and restaurants, and 465 disinfectant manufacturers. A total of 2,606 law enforcement documents were issued, and 828 publicity activities in the form of media were conducted.

Combining with the current situation of epidemic prevention and control, the Comprehensive Supervision Bureau requires the supervisory administrative departments and supervisory agencies at all levels to follow the laws and regulations such as the "Infectious Diseases Prevention and Control Law", according to the work deployment, the principle of helping without chaos, and not offside, and insist that the supervisors The bottom line of not being infected during epidemic surveillance is to take guidance, help, reminders, warnings and other methods to help medical workers take good isolation and protection measures.
