begin->No new coronary pneumonia in Shaanxi Province end->
/
source: begin->Provincial Health and Health Commission end-> Release time: 2020-03-07 08:15 Font: [Big in small 】
/
/
begin->

From March 6th to March 8th, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Shaanxi and no new suspected cases.

As of 8:00 on March 7, Shaanxi had reported a total of 245 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia (226 were cured and discharged, 1 died), of which 116 were imported cases, 114 were close contacts, and 15 were cases without clear contact history. The cumulative confirmed cases include 120 cases in Xi'an, 13 in Baoji, 17 in Xianyang, 8 in Tongchuan, 15 in Weinan, 8 in Yan'an, 3 in Yulin, 26 in Hanzhong, 26 in Ankang, There were 7 cases in Shangluo City, 1 case in Yangling Demonstration Area, and 1 case in Hancheng City.

There are 0 suspected cases in the province. There were 2 new close contacts in the province (co-investigated by other provinces), with a total of 18,941, all of which were focused on medical observation. 49 new close contacts were released, and a total of 18,683 close contacts were lifted.
