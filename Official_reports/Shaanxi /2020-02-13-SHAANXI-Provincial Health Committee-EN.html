A total of 229 new cases of new coronary pneumonia have been confirmed in Shaanxi
/
source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-02-13 10:01 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

As of 10:00 on February 13, 4 new cases of new coronary pneumonia were confirmed in Shaanxi, and 53 new suspected cases were added. Among the newly confirmed cases, 4 were in Xi'an. Shaanxi has reported a total of 229 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia (42 cases were cured and discharged), of which 115 were imported cases, 100 were close contacts, and 14 cases had no clear contact history. Among the confirmed cases, there were 114 cases in Xi'an, 13 in Baoji, 17 in Xianyang, 8 in Tongchuan, 14 in Weinan, 8 in Yan'an, 3 in Yulin, 21 in Hanzhong, 22 in Ankang, There were 7 cases in Shangluo City, 1 case in Yangling Demonstration Area, and 1 case in Hancheng City. There are 304 suspected cases in the province. There were 829 new close contacts in the province, with a total of 13,562, all of which were focused on medical observation. A total of 590 new close contacts were released, and a total of 7,498 close contacts were lifted.

Patient 1, male, 58 years old, currently lives in Xi'an High-tech Zone. From Shanghai to Xi'an on January 19, symptoms appeared on February 2 and self-administration was not effective. Symptoms worsened on February 7 and went to Xi'an High-tech Hospital for treatment. On February 8, he was transferred to Xi'an Chest Hospital. New crown pneumonia was diagnosed on February 13. Isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.

Patient 2, male, 50 years old, now living in Xincheng District, Xi'an City, with confirmed patients in the unit building (confirmed patient 13 on February 2). From Wenzhou City, Zhejiang Province to Xi'an City on January 9th, I had work experience in Colorful Mall from January 10th to January 14th, symptoms occurred on January 28th, and the unit building where I lived was isolated on February 2nd, and transferred on the same day He went to Huashan Hospital for treatment, and was transferred to Xi'an Chest Hospital that night. New crown pneumonia was diagnosed on February 13. Isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.

Patient 3, male, 89 years old, now lives in Yanta District of Xi'an City and has a history of close contact with the confirmed patient 8 on February 8. Symptoms appeared on February 9, and she was treated in isolation at the First Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University on February 10. New crown pneumonia was diagnosed on February 13. Currently in isolation and treatment at the First Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University.

Patient 4, female, 81 years old, now lives in Beilin District, Xi'an, and has a history of close contact with patient 7 confirmed on February 8. On February 5th, he was isolated for observation. On February 6th, he experienced symptoms of anorexia. On February 11th, he was transferred to the Shaanxi Provincial People's Hospital for treatment. New crown pneumonia was diagnosed on February 13. Currently in isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.
