A total of 56 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by 10 new coronavirus infections in Shaanxi
/
source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-01-29 11:00 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

As of 10:00 on January 29, 10 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new-type coronavirus infection and 5 new suspected cases were added in Shaanxi. Among the newly confirmed cases, 3 were in Xi'an, 1 in Baoji, 1 in Xianyang, 1 in Yan'an, 2 in Hanzhong, and 2 in Ankang. A total of 56 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection were reported in Shaanxi, including 18 in Xi'an, 4 in Baoji, 5 in Xianyang, 5 in Tongchuan, 3 in Weinan, 3 in Yanan, 6 in Hanzhong, There were 10 cases in Ankang City and 2 cases in Shangluo City. There were 389 new close contacts in the province, and a total of 1,288, all of which were focused on medical observation.

Patient 1, female, 3 years old, Wuhan City, Hubei Province. From Wuhan to Yanchuan County on January 21, symptoms appeared on the 27th, and they went to the Yanchuan County People's Hospital on the same day. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in isolation and treatment at Yanchuan County People's Hospital, her condition is stable.

Patient 2, female, 46 years old, from Xixiang County, Hanzhong City, currently living in Yanta District, Xi'an City, has a history of contact with patient 4 diagnosed on January 27. Symptoms appeared on the 22nd, and he went to the Shaanxi Provincial Infectious Diseases Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital) on the 23rd. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in isolation and treatment at Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital), the condition is stable.

Patient 3, male, 66 years old, from Xincheng District, Xi'an, has no clear history of exposure. Symptoms occurred on January 18th, and he went to the Fifth Hospital of Xi'an for the same day. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in isolation and treatment at Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital), the condition is stable.

Patient 4, male, 49 years old, from Xixiang County, Hanzhong City, had contact history with confirmed cases 3 and 4 on January 27. Symptoms occurred on the 21st, and went to the Xixiang County People's Hospital on the 26th. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in isolation and treatment at Xixiang County People's Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 5, male, 36 years old, from Xixiang County, Hanzhong City, had contact history with confirmed cases 3 and 4 on January 27. On the 27th, he went to the Xixiang County People's Hospital for treatment. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th and is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at the People's Hospital of Xixiang County with a stable condition.

Patient 6, male, 29 years old, from Ziyang County, Ankang City, has long been working in Wuhan. On January 19, he returned to Ziyang County, Ankang City from Wuhan City. He developed symptoms on the 24th and went to the People's Hospital of Ziyang County on the 26th. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in Ziyang County People's Hospital for isolation and treatment, the condition is stable.

Patient 7, male, 26 years old, born in Xunyang County, Ankang City, has been working in Wuhan for a long time. On January 18, I returned to Xunyang County, Ankang City from Wuhan. On the 20th, symptoms appeared. On the same day, I went to a clinic in Zhaowan Town, Xunyang County. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in Xunyang County Hospital for isolation and treatment, the condition is stable.

Patient 8, male, 46 years old, from Qishan County, Baoji City, works in Wuhan. On January 13, he returned to Qishan County, Baoji City from Wuhan City. On the 21st, he developed symptoms. On the 22nd, he went to the clinic in Xiaoying Village, Yongchuan Town, Qishan County. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in isolation and treatment at Qishan County Hospital, her condition is stable.

Patient 9, female, 45 years old, from Xiaogan City, Hubei Province. On January 22, from Xiaogan City, Hubei Province to Yanliang District, Xi'an City. Symptoms appeared on the 23rd, and he went to the People's Hospital of Yanliang District that day. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on the 29th. Currently in isolation and treatment at Yanliang District People's Hospital, her condition is stable.

Patient 10, male, 27 years old, native of Chunhua County, Xianyang City, a student at a university in Wuhan. January 19 from Wuhan to Chunhua County, Xianyang City. On the 26th, his mother went to Chunhua County Hospital for fever. After investigation, it was found that she had a history of living in Wuhan, and samples were collected for testing. The laboratory test on the 29th was positive for the new coronavirus. Currently, it is isolated in Chunhua County Hospital, and no related symptoms have appeared so far.
