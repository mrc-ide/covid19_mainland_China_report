A total of 173 new cases of pneumonia diagnosed by new coronavirus infection in Shaanxi
/
source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-02-06 10:01 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

As of 10:00 on February 6, 8 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new-type coronavirus infection and 71 new suspected cases were newly added in Shaanxi. Among the newly confirmed cases, 4 were in Xi'an, 1 in Hanzhong, 2 in Ankang, and 1 in Yulin. Shaanxi reported a total of 173 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection (6 cases were cured and discharged), of which 101 were imported cases, 65 were close contacts, and 7 cases had no clear contact history. Among the cumulative confirmed cases, there were 74 cases in Xi'an, 10 in Baoji, 15 in Xianyang, 7 in Tongchuan, 9 in Weinan, 7 in Yanan, 2 in Yulin, 20 in Hanzhong, and 21 in Ankang. Six cases were in Shangluo City, one in Yangling Demonstration Area, and one in Hancheng City. There are 238 suspected cases in the province. There were 615 new close contacts in the province, with a total of 7,655, all of which were focused on medical observation. A total of 641 new close contacts were released, and a total of 2,393 close contacts were lifted.

Patient 1, female, 83 years old, now lives in Lianhu District, Xi'an, and has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on February 2 and after being proactively notified to the community, he was transferred to the Xi'an Central Hospital. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 2, male, 48 years old, now lives in Weiyang District, Xi'an, and visited Northwest Business Center on January 19 and January 22. Symptoms occurred on January 29, and went to Tangcheng Hospital on February 3. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 3, female, 49 years old, currently lives in Beilin District, Xi'an, and works at the Renmenle supermarket in Dongmen. Symptoms occurred on January 31, and she went to the Second People's Hospital of Shaanxi Province on February 3. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 4, female, 66 years old, currently lives in Lianhu District, Xi'an, and has dinner experience with people from Wuhan who come to Shaanxi. Symptoms occurred on January 27, and were transferred to Daxing Hospital in Lianhu District on February 2 for treatment. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 5, female, 62 years old, now living in Zhenba County, Hanzhong City, has a history of contact with 14 patients diagnosed on February 4. Symptoms appeared on January 27, and went to the Zhenba County Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital on the 28th. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Hanzhong Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 6, female, 27 years old, now living in Baihe County, Ankang City, has been working in Wuhan for a long time. The daughter of patient 18 was confirmed on February 5. From Wuhan to Baihe County, Ankang City on January 20, and was transferred to Baihe County Hospital for isolation on February 4, CT of the patient's chest showed a right lower lobe infection and thickened pleura. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Baihe County Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 7, female, 51 years old, now lives in Baihe County, Ankang City, and has a confirmed husband-wife relationship with patient 18 on February 5. The specimen was collected on February 4 and tested by the laboratory of Ankang Disease Control Center, and the result was positive for the new coronavirus nucleic acid. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Baihe County Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 8, male, 60 years old, from Dingbian County, Yulin City, has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms occurred on January 28. He went to a clinic in Zhangyexian Town, Dingbian County, Yulin City, and went to Dingbian County Hospital on February 4. February 6 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Dingbian County Hospital, the condition is stable.
