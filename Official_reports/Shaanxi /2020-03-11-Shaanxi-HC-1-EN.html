begin->No new coronary pneumonia in Shaanxi Province end->
/
source: begin->Provincial Health and Health Commission end-> Release time: 2020-03-11 08:13 Font: [Big in small 】
/
/
begin->

From 8 am to 8 am on March 10, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Shaanxi and no new suspected cases. As of 8:00 on March 11, Shaanxi had reported a total of 245 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia (227 patients were cured and discharged, and 1 died). There are 0 suspected cases in the province. There were 1 new close contacts (co-investigation) in the province, with a total of 18,958, all of which were focused on medical observation. 14 new close contacts were released, and a total of 18,923 close contacts were lifted.
