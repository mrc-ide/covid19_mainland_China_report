begin->Shaanxi has no new coronary pneumonia for 21 consecutive days end->
/
source: begin->Provincial Health and Health Commission end-> Release time: 2020-03-12 08:51 Font: [Big in small 】
/
/
begin->

From March 11 to 8:00 on March 12, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia in Shaanxi, 0 new suspected cases, and 1 death. As of 8:00 on March 12, a total of 245 confirmed cases of new coronary pneumonia were reported in Shaanxi (232 patients were cured and discharged, 2 died). There are 0 suspected cases in the province. There were 2 new close contacts (co-investigation) in the province, with a total of 18,960, all of which were focused on medical observation. 7 new close contacts were released, and a total of 18930 close contacts were lifted.

Yesterday, a critically ill patient with new coronary pneumonia in our province died unfortunately. This is the second case of death in our province. The patient was confirmed case 10, which was announced on February 2 and was a 77-year-old male from Wuhan City, Hubei Province. He had a history of hypertension and emphysema. The patient was quarantined from Sanya, Hainan, to Xi'an on January 27. On January 29, symptoms were transferred to Tangdu Hospital for treatment. On January 31, he was reported to be seriously ill, and on February 2 he was diagnosed with severe neo-coronary pneumonia. Based on the changes in the patient's condition, the hospital has repeatedly organized multi-disciplinary consultations with provincial experts and experts outside the province to adjust the treatment plan and provide continuous ventilator-assisted breathing, extracorporeal membrane oxygenation, plasma exchange, continuous hemodiafiltration, and installation of temporary heart Pacemakers and other treatments, but due to secondary pulmonary fibrosis, multiple organ failure, and underlying diseases after the onset of the patient, the condition continued to worsen, and all efforts were made to rescue the death.


end->
