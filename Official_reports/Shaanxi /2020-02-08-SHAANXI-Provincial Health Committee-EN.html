A total of 195 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection in Shaanxi
/
source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-02-08 10:01 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

As of 10:00 on February 8, 11 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new-type coronavirus infection were added in Shaanxi, and 92 new suspected cases were added. Among the newly confirmed cases, there were 8 cases in Xi'an, 1 in Xianyang, 1 in Yan'an, and 1 in Yulin. A total of 195 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection were reported in Shaanxi (17 were cured and discharged), of which 108 were imported cases, 76 were close contacts, and 11 were cases without clear contact history. Among the confirmed cases, there were 88 cases in Xi'an, 12 in Baoji, 16 in Xianyang, 8 in Tongchuan, 9 in Weinan, 8 in Yan'an, 3 in Yulin, 21 in Hanzhong, 21 in Ankang, There were 7 cases in Shangluo City, 1 case in Yangling Demonstration Area, and 1 case in Hancheng City. There are 321 suspected cases in the province. There were 768 new close contacts in the province, with a total of 9,077, all of which were focused on medical observation. There were 749 new close contacts released, and a total of 3837 closed contacts.

Patient 1, female, 69 years old, now lives in Xincheng District, Xi'an City, and was diagnosed as a husband and wife on February 5. On February 4th, she was observed under quarantine. On the same day, she developed symptoms and was transferred to the Shaanxi Provincial Infectious Diseases Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital) for treatment. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment in Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital).

Patient 2, male, 46 years old, now living in Yanliang District, Xi'an City, has a history of confirmed case contact. Symptoms appeared on February 4th, and he went to the Yanliang District People's Hospital for treatment. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment in Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital).

Patient 3, female, 43 years old, now living in Yanliang District, Xi'an, has a history of confirmed case contact. Symptoms appeared on February 2, and she went to the People's Hospital of Yanliang District on February 3. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment in Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital).

Patient 4, female, 57 years old, currently lives in Beilin District, Xi'an. On January 27, he went to the Xi'an Dongguan Nanjie Community Hospital for medical treatment. From January 30 to January 31, he went to the Yanta District Chinese Medicine Hospital for treatment and infusion. On February 4, he had a fever of 37.2 ° C and an upset stomach. He went to the Traditional Chinese Medicine Hospital of Yanta District, Xi'an again. CT results: multiple ground glass shadows in both lungs. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.

Patient 5, female, 65 years old, now living in Lianhu District, Xi'an, was diagnosed as the mother of patient 7 on February 1. From Wuhan to Xi'an on January 19, symptoms appeared on January 30, and he was transferred to Xi'an Chest Hospital on February 1. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.

Patient 6, female, 66 years old, currently lives in Xi'an High-tech Zone and has dinner experience with suspected patients. Symptoms appeared on February 1, and he went to the First Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University on February 5. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the First Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University.

Patient 7, female, 31 years old, currently lives in Xi'an High-tech Zone. There is a returnee from Wuhan in the same unit of the patient's residence. Symptoms appeared on the evening of January 31. He went to the Xi'an High-tech Hospital on February 4 and was transferred to the Xi'an Chest Hospital on the same day. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.

Patient 8, male, 22 years old, currently living in Yanta District, Xi'an, a student at a university in Wuhan. From Wuhan to Xi'an on January 14th, I had a dinner with people on January 19th, symptoms occurred on January 31st, I went to the Fourth Hospital of Xi'an City on the same day, and was transferred to Xi'an Chest Hospital. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Isolation treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital.

Patient 9, female, 56 years old, currently lives in Yanchuan County, Yan'an City, and was diagnosed with husband and wife 19 on January 31 in a marital relationship. Because there were returnees from Wuhan in his home, he was quarantined and observed from January 23 to January 29. He developed symptoms on January 31 and was transferred to Yanchuan County People's Hospital for treatment. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Yanchuan County People's Hospital.

Patient 10, male, 57 years old, currently lives in Sanyuan County, Xianyang City, and has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on January 31, and went to Sanyuan County Hospital on February 6. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Xianyang Central Hospital.

Patient 11, female, 27 years old, currently living in Dingbian County, Yulin City, was the daughter of a confirmed patient on February 6. From January 15 through Wuhan to Ningxia Yinchuan and Lingwu to Dingbian County, Yulin City, symptoms appeared on January 22, which relieved after self-administration. On February 6, he was admitted to the hospital as a suspected patient. February 8 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Yulin North Hospital.

Another 10 patients were confirmed on February 4th. After further epidemiological investigation, it was found that they had visited the Northwest Business Center on January 20th.
