A total of 116 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection in Shaanxi
/
source: Provincial Health and Health Committee Release time: 2020-02-02 10:53 Font: 【 Tai Zhong small ]
/
/

As of 10:00 on February 2nd, 15 new cases of pneumonia diagnosed with new-type coronavirus infection have been added to Shaanxi, 1 new severe patient, and 77 new suspected cases. Among the newly confirmed cases, 8 were in Xi'an, 1 in Baoji, 1 in Xianyang, 1 in Tongchuan, 2 in Hanzhong, and 2 in Ankang. In Shaanxi, 116 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection were reported, of which 82 were imported cases, 31 were close contacts, and 3 were cases without clear contact history. Among the cumulative confirmed cases, there were 47 cases in Xi'an, 7 in Baoji, 9 in Xianyang, 6 in Tongchuan, 5 in Weinan, 7 in Yanan, 1 in Yulin, 15 in Hanzhong, 16 in Ankang, There were 2 cases in Shangluo City and 1 in Hancheng City. There are 201 suspected cases in the province. There were 830 new close contacts in the province, and a total of 4,394, all of which were focused on medical observation. There were 264 new close contacts and 809 close contacts.

Patient 1, male, 64 years old, born in Qishan County, Baoji City, was the cousin of patient 8 who was diagnosed on January 29. Symptoms occurred on January 29, and she was quarantined at the Qishan County Hospital on the 30th. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Qishan County Hospital, her condition is stable.

Patient 2, male, 74 years old, born in Wangyi District, Tongchuan City, was the father of patient 4 on January 25. On January 25, he was isolated for observation. On the 27th, he developed symptoms and was transferred to the Central Hospital of Tongchuan Mining Bureau for treatment on the same day. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment in the Central Hospital of Tongchuan Mining Bureau, and her condition is stable.

Patient 3, female, 62 years old, from Wugong County, Xianyang City, has been living in Wuhan for a long time. On January 22, from Wuhan to Wugong County, Xianyang City, symptoms appeared on the 24th, and symptoms worsened on the 31st and went to the People's Hospital of Wugong County. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the People's Hospital of Wugong County, his condition is stable.

Patient 4, female, 5 years old, from Hanbin District, Ankang City, has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on January 22, and symptoms worsened on the 27th at Ankang Central Hospital. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Ankang Central Hospital, the condition is stable.

Patient 5, female, 51 years old, from Hanbin District, Ankang City, has a history of contact with returnees from Wuhan. Symptoms appeared on January 19th, and symptoms worsened on the 28th to the Third Hospital of Hanbin District. He was diagnosed with pneumonitis associated with a new coronavirus on February 2 and is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at the Ankang Central Hospital with a stable condition.

Patient 6, female, 68 years old, from Suizhou City, Hubei Province. From Suizhou City, Hubei Province to Hanzhong City on January 18, symptoms appeared on the 28th, and on the 31st, he went to the Second People's Hospital of Hanzhong City for treatment. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the Second People's Hospital of Hanzhong City, his condition is stable.

Patient 7, male, 70 years old, from Suizhou City, Hubei Province, and patient 6 was married. From Suizhou City, Hubei Province to Hanzhong City on January 18, and his wife to the Second People's Hospital of Hanzhong City on the 31st, it was found that he had a history of living in Suizhou City, Hubei Province, and his samples were tested. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at the Second People's Hospital of Hanzhong City, his condition is stable.

Patient 8, male, 39 years old, teacher of a university in Wuhan. From Wuhan to Xi'an on January 19, the symptoms appeared on the 28th, and went to the Ninth Hospital of Xi'an on the 29th. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital), the condition is stable.

Patient 9, male, 48 years old, Wuhan City, Hubei Province. From Sanya, Hainan to Xi'an on January 27, they were isolated on the same day. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Tangdu Hospital, his condition is stable.

Patient 10, male, 72 years old, the father of patient 9, a native of Wuhan City, Hubei Province. From Sanya, Hainan to Xi'an on January 27, they were isolated on the same day. Symptoms occurred on the 29th and were transferred to Tangdu Hospital for treatment on the same day. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Tangdu Hospital, his condition is stable.

Patient 11, female, 73 years old, mother of patient 9, was born in Wuhan, Hubei Province. From Sanya, Hainan to Xi'an on January 27, they were isolated on the same day. Symptoms occurred on the 31st and were transferred to Tangdu Hospital for treatment on the same day. He was diagnosed with pneumonia due to a new coronavirus on February 1. Currently in isolation and treatment in Tangdu Hospital, his condition is critical.

Patient 12, female, 34 years old, born in Beilin District, Xi'an, was the daughter of patient 4 who was diagnosed on February 1. Symptoms occurred on January 22, and that night he went to the Second Affiliated Hospital of Xi'an Jiaotong University. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. She is currently undergoing isolation and treatment at Xi'an Chest Hospital, and her condition is stable.

Patient 13, female, 50 years old, now lives in Xincheng District, Xi'an City, and has a history of contact with Hubei nationals doing business in Shaanxi. Symptoms occurred on January 31. At the early morning of February 1, he went to Kunlun Hospital of Shaanxi Health Medical Group Co., Ltd. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation treatment at Kunlun Hospital of Shaanxi Health Medical Group Co., Ltd., his condition is stable.

Patient 14, male, 36 years old, from Ankang City, Shaanxi Province, currently lives in Gaoling District, Xi'an. From Wuhan to Xi'an on January 21, symptoms appeared on the 26th, and went to the Changqing Oilfield Staff Hospital on the 28th. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Shaanxi Infectious Disease Hospital (Xi'an No. 8 Hospital), the condition is stable.

Patient 15, female, 56 years old, native of Pingdingshan City, Henan Province, has been living in Baqiao District of Xi'an City for a long time, and no clear exposure history has been found. Symptoms appeared on January 22, and went to the Xi'an Traditional Chinese Encephalopathy Hospital on the 29th. February 2 was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. Currently in isolation and treatment at Tangdu Hospital, his condition is stable.
