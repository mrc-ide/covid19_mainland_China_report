Yesterday there were no new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia in Shanghai, and 6 new cases were imported overseas
/

/ ( 2020-03-30)/

/
/
/ /

3At 04:00 on the 29th of the month, through the port joint prevention and control mechanism, six confirmed cases of imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported.

Case 1 is from Jiangsu, China, visiting relatives in the United States, departing from the United States on March 26, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 27. After entering the country, he was quarantined and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and The results of imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 2 is from Guangdong, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from the United Kingdom on March 23, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 24 after transiting through Germany. After entering the country, he was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical symptoms , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 3 is from Beijing, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from the United Kingdom on March 24, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 26 after transiting through Thailand. After entering the country, he was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical symptoms , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 4 is from Anhui, China, and lives in the United Kingdom. He left the United Kingdom on March 24 and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on the same day. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 5 is from Xinjiang, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from the United Kingdom on March 25, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 26 after transiting through Malaysia. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation . Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 6 is from Shanghai, China, studying in the United States, departing from the United States on March 25, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 27 after transiting in South Korea. After entering the customs, she was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical symptoms , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

6All imported imported confirmed cases have been transferred to designated medical institutions for treatment. Twenty-six people who have been in close contact with the flight have been tracked. Centralized isolation observation has been implemented.

3At 04:00 on the 29th, there were no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia.

As of 24:00 on March 29, a total of 159 overseas imported confirmed cases have been reported, 7 cases have been cured and discharged, and 152 cases have been treated in the hospital (including 2 severe cases and 1 critical case). There are currently 19 overseas suspected imported cases being investigated. in. A total of 339 locally diagnosed cases were reported, 327 were cured and discharged, 5 died, 7 were treated in the hospital (6 of them were critically ill), and there were currently 0 suspected local cases.
