No new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia on March 25 in Shanghai
/

/ ( 2020-03-26)/

/
/
/ /

From 04:00 to March 24th, through the port joint prevention and control mechanism, 18 confirmed cases of imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported. As of 24:00 on March 25, a total of 112 overseas imported confirmed cases have been reported, and there are currently 20 suspected overseas imported cases under investigation.

Case 1 is of British nationality. It departed from the United Kingdom on March 20 and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22 after transiting through Thailand. After entering the country, it was isolated for observation, combining epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests and The results of imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 2 is a British national. He left the United Kingdom on March 21 and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22 after transiting in Singapore. After entering the country, he was quarantined and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests and The results of imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 3 was born in Shanghai, China, studying in the United States, departing from the United States on March 23, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on the same day. After entering the country, he was isolated for observation, combining epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests and imaging Examination results etc. were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 4 is from Shanghai, China, traveling in Malaysia, departing from Malaysia on March 22, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on the same day. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 5 is of U.S. nationality. It departed from the United States on March 21 and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22 after transiting through Taipei. After entering the country, it was isolated and observed. It combined epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests and The results of imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 6 is a Fujian national of China, travelling in the UK, departing from the UK on March 21, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22 after transiting through Russia. After entering the country, she was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical symptoms , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 7 is from Zhejiang Province, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from the United Kingdom on March 20, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 21 after transiting through Russia. After entering the country, he was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical symptoms , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 9 is from Shanghai, China, studying in the United States, departing from the United States on March 22, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 23, and was isolated for observation after entering the customs. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and The results of imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 10 is a Canadian, living in the United States, departing from the United States on March 21, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 23 after transiting through Japan. After entering the country, she was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, Laboratory tests and imaging findings were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 11 is a native of Chongqing, China, studying in the United States, departing from the United States on March 21, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22 after transiting through Taipei. After entering the customs, she was isolated for observation and symptoms occurred during the period. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

18 overseas confirmed imported cases have been transferred to designated medical institutions for treatment, 73 close contacts with the flight have been tracked, and centralized isolation observation has been implemented.


At 04:00 on March 25th, there were no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia. As of 24:00 on March 25, a total of 339 locally confirmed cases had been reported, and there were currently 0 suspected local cases.

At 04:00 on March 25th, no new cure was discharged. As of 24:00 on March 25, a total of 330 patients were discharged from hospital and 5 died. There are 116 cases in the hospital (including overseas imported 109 cases), of which 108 cases are stable, 2 cases are severe, and 6 cases are critical.
