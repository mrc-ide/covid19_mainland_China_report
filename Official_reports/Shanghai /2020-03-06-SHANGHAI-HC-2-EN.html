Today at 0-12 hours, three new imported cases of new coronavirus pneumonia confirmed in Shanghai
/

/ ( 2020-03-06)/

/
/
/ /

2020At 02:00 on March 6, 2010, 2 suspected cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were ruled out in Shanghai; 3 cases were cured and discharged; 3 confirmed cases were newly added, and they were imported cases.

3Of the confirmed cases, 2 were from Ningxia, China, and 1 from Gansu, China. They both studied in Iran. They all departed from Tehran, Iran, and arrived in Shanghai in the evening of March 2 and in the early morning of March 3 after transit through Bangkok, Thailand. Pudong International Airport will be sent to the centralized isolation point for observation after entering the customs. On the evening of March 3, I went to a designated medical institution to watch. On March 6, a confirmed case was diagnosed based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory test results, and imaging studies.

There are 111 cases of foreigners coming to Shanghai, 4 cases of overseas importers, and 227 permanent residents in this city. Details are as follows:
