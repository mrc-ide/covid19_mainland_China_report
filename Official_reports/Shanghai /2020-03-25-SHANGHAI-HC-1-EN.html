Yesterday there were no new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia in Shanghai
/

/ ( 2020-03-25)/

/
/
/ /

3At 04:00 on the 24th of the month, through the port joint prevention and control mechanism, 19 confirmed cases of imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported. As of 24:00 on March 24, a total of 94 overseas imported confirmed cases were reported, and there are currently 14 suspected overseas imported cases under investigation.

Case 1 is of Italian nationality, traveling in Thailand, departing from Thailand on March 21, and arriving at Shanghai Hongqiao Airport on March 22 after transiting through Macau. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 2 is from Shanghai, China, studying in the UK, departing from the UK on March 20, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22 after transiting through Russia. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation. . Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 3 is a Cantonese from China, studying in the UK, departing from the UK on March 19, and arriving at Shanghai Hongqiao Airport on March 22 after transiting from Hong Kong. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for observation. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 4 is of Spanish nationality. He departed from Spain on March 21st and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 22nd after connecting with Russia. After entering the country, he was isolated and observed. He combined epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests and The results of imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 5 is from Beijing, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from the United Kingdom on March 19, arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 21 after transiting through France, and being isolated for observation after entering customs. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical symptoms , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

3At 04:00 on the 24th, there were no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia. As of 24:00 on March 24, a total of 339 locally diagnosed cases have been reported, and there are currently 0 suspected local cases.


3At 04:00 on the 24th, one new case was cured and discharged, which was an imported case in Italy. One patient died, 81 years old. He had previous history of liver and kidney dysfunction, coronary heart disease, and atrial fibrillation. He was critically admitted upon admission. He was given ventilator, ECMO (external membrane pulmonary oxygenation), and CRRT (continuous renal replacement therapy). ) Treatment, died with all-out rescue.


As of 24:00 on March 24, a total of 330 patients were discharged from hospital and 5 died. There are 98 cases in the hospital (including overseas imported 91 cases), of which 90 cases are stable, 2 cases are severe, 6 cases are critical.
