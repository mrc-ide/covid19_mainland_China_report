Yesterday, there were no new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia in Shanghai. One patient was discharged from the hospital, one died, and 14 cases were imported overseas.
/

/ ( 2020-03-22)/

/
/
/ /

3From 04:00 to 24:00 on the 21st, 14 confirmed cases of imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported through the port joint prevention and control mechanism. As of 24:00 on March 21, a total of 56 overseas confirmed imported cases have been reported, and there are currently 26 suspected overseas imported cases under investigation.

Case 1 and Case 2 are brothers, Israeli nationals, traveling in the United States, departing from Los Angeles, United States on March 18, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 19 after transiting through Taipei. They were isolated and observed after entering customs. Comprehensive epidemic Medical history, clinical manifestations, laboratory tests, and imaging findings are diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 3 is a U.S. national, traveling in the United States, departing from Minnesota, the United States on March 17, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 19 after transiting through Tokyo, Japan. After entering the country, he was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical Manifestations, laboratory tests, and imaging findings were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 4 is a Macao Chinese national, travelling in the UK, departing from London, UK on March 17, and arriving at Shanghai Hongqiao Airport on March 19 after transiting through Hong Kong. After entering the country, she was isolated and observed. Comprehensive epidemiological history and clinical manifestations , Laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 5 is from Canton, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from London, UK on March 18, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 19, and was isolated for observation after entering customs. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory The results of tests and imaging examinations were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 6 is from Zhejiang Province, China, studying in the UK, departing from London, UK on March 18, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 19, and was observed under quarantine after entering the country. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 7 is from Hubei, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from London, UK on March 18, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 19 after transiting through Frankfurt, Germany. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution Watch. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 8 is a Canadian, studying in the UK, departing from London, UK on March 18, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 19 after transiting through Tokyo, Japan. Due to symptoms, he was sent to a designated medical institution for admission Concept. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Cases 9 and 10 are husband and wife, Chinese from Zhejiang, working in Spain, departing from Madrid, Spain on March 17, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 18 after connecting with Hong Kong. Medical history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 11 is from Canton, China, studying in the UK, departing from London, UK on March 17, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 18, and was placed under centralized observation after entering customs. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, experiments Laboratory tests and imaging findings were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 12 is from Shanghai, China, studying in the United Kingdom, departing from London, UK on March 14, and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 15 after transiting through Helsinki, Finland. After entering the customs, she was isolated for observation and symptoms occurred during the period. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 13 is from Shanghai, China, studying in the UK, departing from London, UK on March 19, arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 20 after transiting through Changi, Singapore, and being isolated for observation after entering customs. Comprehensive epidemiological history , Clinical manifestations, laboratory tests and imaging findings, etc., diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 14 is of German nationality, visiting relatives in Switzerland, departing from Zurich, Switzerland on March 19, arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 20 after transiting through Bangkok, Thailand, and being isolated for observation after entering customs. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical Manifestations, laboratory tests, and imaging findings were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

14All imported imported confirmed cases have been transferred to designated medical institutions for treatment, and 120 people who have been in close contact with the flight have been tracked. Centralized isolation observation has been implemented.

3At 04:00 on the 21st, there were no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia. As of 24:00 on March 21, a total of 338 locally confirmed cases have been reported, and there are currently 0 suspected local cases.

3At 04:00 on the 21st of May, one patient was cured and discharged, and one died. The fatal case was a male, 75 years old, with bladder cancer. He had respiratory failure after admission on February 1 and was critically ill. He failed to rescue after all efforts and died on the morning of March 21. As of 24:00 on March 21, a total of 327 patients were discharged and 4 died. There are 63 cases in the hospital (including overseas imported 55 cases), of which 55 cases are stable, 1 case is severe, and 7 cases are critical.
