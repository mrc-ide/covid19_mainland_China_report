Today at 0-12, no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia in Shanghai
/

/ ( 2020-02-19)/

/
/
/ /

2020At 0-12 on February 19, 2000, 29 suspected cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were ruled out in Shanghai; 9 new cases were cured and discharged; 1 new death; and no new confirmed cases. The fatal case was a 79-year-old female with a history of hypertension and cerebral infarction, combined with multiple organ dysfunction, a critical condition, which was ineffective after all rescue efforts.

As of 12:00 on February 19, 1909 suspected cases have been ruled out in Shanghai, and 333 confirmed cases have been found. Among the confirmed cases, there were 173 males and 160 females; the oldest was 88 years old and the youngest was 7 months old; 144 had a history of living or traveling in Hubei, 33 had a history of living or traveling outside Hubei, and 156 had a history of contact with related cases; There were 110 foreigners coming to Shanghai and 223 of the city's resident population.
