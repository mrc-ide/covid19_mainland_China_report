Jilin Provincial Health and Health Committee's report on the pneumonia situation of new coronavirus infection

  • source:
  • Release time: 2020-02-01
  • Font: Tai Zhong Small

At 04:00 on January 31, 3 new cases were confirmed in the province. As of 24:00 on January 31, a total of 17 confirmed cases have been reported in Jilin Province, 1 case has been cured and discharged from Changchun City, and 16 confirmed cases are currently being treated in hospital isolation. There were 6 cases in Changchun City, 3 cases in Jilin City, 2 cases in Yanbian Prefecture, 2 cases in Songyuan City, 1 case in Tonghua City, 1 case in Siping City, and 1 case in Gongzhuling City. All 831 close contacts of the confirmed cases were under observation in isolation.

Case 1, male, born in 1941, is a close contact with previously diagnosed cases in Changchun. Currently lives at 694 Chongzhi Road, Chaoyang District, Changchun City.

Case 2, male, born in 1986, was imported from Wuhan. He returned to Changchun from Wuhan on CA8223 flight (seat number 20C) on January 20, and became ill on January 27. He currently lives in Zhonghai Lanting District, Changchun High-tech Zone.

Case 3, male, born in 1973, was imported from Wuhan. On January 14, we took flight CZ6651 (seat number 39H) and returned to Changchun from Wuhan. Onset on January 22, he drove to Jilin City that day. On January 23, take the C1010 train (seat number 3, 15A) and return to Changchun. Currently living in Dongcheng International Garden Community, Boxue Road, Jingyue District, Changchun City.

On January 31, 6 suspected cases were newly added and 6 cases were ruled out. Existing 10 suspected cases have all been isolated for medical observation. Epidemiological investigations and laboratory tests are currently underway.

Remind the general public that if you are returning from an outbreak province such as Hubei, or if you are taking a public transportation with the above confirmed cases, you should take the initiative to cooperate with the local community to conduct screening and registration, cooperate with professionals to carry out medical observation, and do your own health Management, to avoid poorly ventilated, crowded places, once acute respiratory symptoms such as fever, cough, etc., immediately report to the staff of the CDC, go to the local designated medical institution for a hot clinic.

Winter and spring are the seasons with high incidence of respiratory infectious diseases, as well as the period with high incidence of viral pneumonia. It is hoped that the general public will do a good job of personal hygiene protection, wear masks when going out, gather less, pay attention to personal hygiene, and maintain indoor environmental hygiene and air circulation.
