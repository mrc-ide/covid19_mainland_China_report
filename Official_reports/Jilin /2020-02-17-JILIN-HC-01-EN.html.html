Jilin Provincial Health and Health Committee's report on the epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia (announced on February 17, 2020)

/
    /
  • Time: 2020-02-17
    • /
  • source:Jilin Provincial Health Committee
    • /
  • Font display:smallinBig
    • /
/
/

0 At 04:00 on February 16, there were no new confirmed cases in the province. 2 cases were cured and discharged (2 cases in Changchun City).

/

As of 24:00 on February 16th, the province has reported a total of 89 confirmed cases and 30 hospitalized patients (16 in Changchun, 3 in Jilin, 2 in Yanbian, 4 in Siping, 1 in Liaoyuan, and Songyuan). 1 case, 2 cases in Gongzhuling City, 1 case in Meihekou City, 1 case died (Siping City), and 58 cases have been diagnosed and treated in hospital. There were 29 cases in Changchun City, 2 cases in Jilin City, 3 cases in Yanbian Prefecture, 9 cases in Siping City, 3 cases in Tonghua City, 1 case in Baicheng City, 6 cases in Liaoyuan City, 1 case in Songyuan City and 4 cases in Gongzhuling City.

/

Of the confirmed cases treated in the hospital, 52 were general cases, 3 were severe cases (2 in Changchun City, 1 in Songyuan City), and 3 were critical cases (1 in Changchun City, 1 in Baicheng City, and 1 in Gongzhuling City). example). 3,759 close contacts of the above confirmed cases, 3,120 medical observations have been lifted, and 639 medical observations are being quarantined or at home in designated places. A total of 13 confirmed patients were actively screened for nucleic acid testing among close contacts.

/

0 On February 16th, 00:00, out of the original 37 suspected cases in the province, 10 were excluded; 9 new suspected cases were added; 36 existing suspected cases were all isolated and treated, and the diagnosis is currently being further confirmed.

/

Recently, the return to work is returning to the peak and personnel mobility has increased. The general public should pay attention to avoid crowded places, do personal protection, wash hands frequently, wear face masks, and go out less frequently. Do not gather and protect the health of yourself and your family.

/

Remind the general public that if you are returning from an outbreak province, you should take the initiative to go to the local community for screening and registration and cooperate with medical professionals to carry out medical observation. Once acute respiratory symptoms such as fever and cough occur, please go to a local designated medical institution See a doctor.
