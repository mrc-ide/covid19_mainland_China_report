Jilin Provincial Health and Health Committee's report on the pneumonia situation of new coronavirus infection

  • source:
  • Release time: 2020-02-03
  • Font: Tai Zhong Small

At 02:00 on February 2, 8 new cases were confirmed in the province, including 4 in Changchun, 3 in Siping, and 1 in Jilin. As of 24:00 on February 2, a total of 31 confirmed cases have been reported in the province, 1 case has been cured and discharged from Changchun City, and 30 cases have been diagnosed and treated in the hospital. There were 16 cases in Changchun City, 4 cases in Jilin City, 4 cases in Siping City, 2 cases in Yanbian Prefecture, 2 cases in Songyuan City, 1 case in Tonghua City, and 1 case in Gongzhuling City. Of the confirmed cases treated in the hospital, 25 were general cases, 4 were severe cases, and 1 was a critical case. 1181 people who were in close contact with the above confirmed cases were in isolation or at home with 1,110 medical observations, and 71 medical observations have been lifted.

Case 1, female, born in 1972. The sister of the confirmed case notified by Jilin Province on January 30. The address is 694 Chongzhi Road, Chaoyang District, Changchun City.

Case 2, female, born in 1940. It is the mother of a confirmed case notified in Jilin Province on January 30. The address is 694 Chongzhi Road, Chaoyang District, Changchun City.

Case 3, male, born in 1996. The son of case 1. The address is 694 Chongzhi Road, Chaoyang District, Changchun City.

Case 4, male, born in 1977. Close contacts of confirmed cases in Panjin City, Liaoning Province. On January 20th, I went to the Sun City Store in Jiran Province to attend the annual meeting of store managers in Jilin Province. On January 21, C1007 train (16 cars, 06F) arrived from Changchun City to Dunhua City. Return to Changchun by car on January 22. The address is Xicheng International Mansion, Luyuan District, Changchun City.

Case 5, female, born in 1986. I went to Guangdong for tourism, and ended the tour on January 23 by taking flight CZ3749 (56A) from Zhuhai to Changchun Longjia Airport and returning to Jilin by car. The address is Zhongyu Ziyu River City, Fengman District, Jilin City.

Case 6, male, born in 1990. On January 18, take Z352 train (No. 56 from No. 3 car) to Hohhot from Baotou city, take DR6562 flight (10F) from Hohhot to Shenyang city, transfer to T261 train (No. 16 car No. 36) to return to Siping. The address is Phase II of Hongtai First City, Tiexi District, Siping City.

Case 7, male, born in 1938 (critically ill), onset on January 30. Her daughter is a returnee from Wuhan, and she is not sick. The address is the Civil Defense Office of Tiexi District, Siping City.

Case 8, male, born in 1987. It is a close contact of the confirmed cases of Siping City notified by Jilin Province on January 27. The address is Harmonious Home in Tiexi District, Siping City.

At 02:00 on February 2nd, the province's original 14 suspected cases were converted into 8 confirmed cases, 1 case was excluded, and 12 new cases were added. The existing 17 suspected cases have all been isolated and treated, and the diagnosis is currently being further confirmed. .

Recently, the number of confirmed and suspected cases in our province has gradually increased, which is in line with the national epidemic situation judged by experts. As of now, all confirmed cases and suspected cases found are under control. Our province will continue to increase the active search and investigation in rural areas and communities. We hope that the general public will not panic, and do personal hygiene protection according to the requirements of the health and health department, go out and wear masks, gather less, pay attention to personal hygiene, and maintain indoor environmental hygiene and air circulation.

Remind the general public that if you are returning from the province where the epidemic occurred, or if you are traveling with the above confirmed cases and who are in close contact with the same community, you should take the initiative to go to the local community for screening and registration, and cooperate with professionals to carry out medicine Observation. Once acute respiratory symptoms such as fever and cough occur, please go to a local designated medical institution for a fever clinic.
