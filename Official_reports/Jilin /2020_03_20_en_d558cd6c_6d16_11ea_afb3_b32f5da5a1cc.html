Jilin Provincial Health and Health Committee's report on the epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia (published on March 20, 2020)

/
    /
  • Time: 2020-03-20
    • /
  • source:Jilin Provincial Health Committee
    • /
  • Font display:smallinBig
    • /
/
/

0 At 04:00 on March 19th, there were no new confirmed cases and no new suspected cases in the province. Zero reports for 25 consecutive days.

/

As of 24:00 on March 19th, the province has reported a total of 93 confirmed cases, 92 cases were cured and discharged, and 1 died.

/

Remind the general public that clearing does not mean that the epidemic is over. The current situation of epidemics abroad is severe. Please do not relax your vigilance, increase your awareness of prevention, do a good job of personal protection, and protect the health of yourself and your family.
