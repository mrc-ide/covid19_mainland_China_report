A total of 86 new cases of new coronavirus pneumonia confirmed in Gansu

Release time: 2020-02-11 21:31:24 Traffic:

February 10 20:00 to February 11 20:00, Gansu was no new confirmed case of novel coronavirus pneumonia. 3 new patients were discharged. As of February 11 At 20:00 , Gansu reported a total of 86 confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia, a total of 24 patients were cured and discharged, a total of 2 died, and 60 were treated in designated hospitals. 19 cases of Lanzhou City, 10 cases of Tianshui City, six cases of 8 cases of Pingliang, Dingxi City, Gannan five cases, Longnan 4 cases, Silver City 4 cases, Qingyang two cases, one case of Zhangye City, Linxia one case . There are 7 severe cases and 1 severe case.

There were 5 new suspected cases, 3 in Pingliang and 2 in Lanzhou. Existing suspected cases in 17 cases, five cases of Lanzhou City, Pingliang four cases, three cases of Gannan, Dingxi City set three cases, two cases of Tianshui City.

At present, a total of 3,426 close contacts have been tracked, 2,495 people have been released from medical observation, and the remaining 931 people are receiving medical observation.
