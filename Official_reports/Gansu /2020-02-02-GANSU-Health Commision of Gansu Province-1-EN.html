Gansu added 2 new patients with pneumonitis infected with new coronavirus

Release time: 2020-02-02 21:05:35 Traffic:

On February 2, after consultation with provincial and municipal experts, a patient with pneumococcal infection of imported new coronavirus in Longxi County, Dingxi City, met discharge standards and was discharged from hospital. On the same day, the first hospital of Lanzhou University and the Lanzhou Pulmonary Hospital each discharged one patient with pneumonitis infected by a new type of coronavirus. The total number of discharged patients cured in the province was 3.

Pan a patient is given cases of pneumonia in Longxi County, Dingxi City First People's Hospital admitted a new type of coronavirus infection, on January 22 confirmed and admitted to hospital. On February 2nd, he was consulted by provincial experts and met discharge standards and was discharged.
Patient Li Moumou, living in Wuhan for a long time, was diagnosed with a new coronavirus-infected pneumonia patient at the First Hospital of Lanzhou University on January 22. On February 2, it was evaluated by the expert group and met discharge standards and was discharged.
Patient Wu Moumou was diagnosed with a new coronavirus-infected pneumonia patient at Lanzhou Pulmonary Hospital on January 24. After being evaluated by the expert group on February 2, it met the discharge criteria and was discharged.
