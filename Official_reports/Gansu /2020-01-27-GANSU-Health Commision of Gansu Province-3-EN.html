A total of 4 cases of pneumonia infected with new coronavirus confirmed in Gansu Province

Release time: 2020-01-27 21:54:59 Visits:

As of 24 January 24, the province has a total of confirmed cases of pneumonia novel coronavirus infection in 4 cases.

January 24 2 new confirmed cases, have been treated in isolation, and quarantine of close contacts and carry out medical observation track.

A female patient, 33 years old, worked in Wuhan. 18, and her husband and their children took the opportunity to leave Wuhan City, Dhahran Yongdeng County in-laws home that night no obvious incentive to fever, cough, sputum, self-test the highest temperature 39 , accompanied by general malaise. On the 23rd , he went to the First Hospital of Lanzhou University for treatment, and was admitted to the hospital for isolation and treatment. After the nucleic acid test in the laboratory of the Provincial Center for Disease Control and Prevention, the new coronavirus was positive. According to the "Pneumonitis Diagnosis and Treatment Scheme for New Coronavirus Infection (Trial Version)", combined with the patient's epidemiological history, clinical manifestations, and laboratory test results, it was determined to be a confirmed case of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection . The patient is currently in a stable condition.

Patient Ma Moumou, female, 20 years old, lives in Wuhan. On the 18th , he returned to Linxia to visit relatives. The next day he experienced general soreness, dry throat, dry cough, headache, and dizziness. He went to Linxiazhou People's Hospital for isolation and treatment on the 24th . Based on the epidemiological history, clinical manifestations, laboratory tests, and nucleic acid test results, the patient was diagnosed with pneumonitis infected by a new coronavirus. An epidemiological investigation has been carried out. Seventeen close contacts have been traced, all of which have been observed at home.
