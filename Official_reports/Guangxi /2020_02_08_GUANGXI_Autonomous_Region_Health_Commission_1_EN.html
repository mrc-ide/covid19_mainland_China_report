Epidemiological situation of new coronavirus infection in Guangxi on February 7, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-08 08:00:36 Click:
32506
Source: Autonomous Regional Health Commission
/ /
/
/
/ /
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/


February 0-24 on the 7th, the district added a total of 11 cases of pneumonia confirmed cases of novel coronavirus infection, where one case Nanning, Guilin 1 case, 1 case of Beihai, Fangchenggang City, 1 case, 1 case of Qinzhou 1 case in Yulin City, 4 cases in Hechi City, and 1 case in Laibin City. There were 123 new suspected cases and 295 existing suspected cases.

A total of 183 confirmed cases and 17 discharged patients have been reported. There are 7 severe cases (2 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 3 in Fangchenggang, 1 in Guigang), and 8 critical cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 2 in Fangchenggang, and Yulin). 1 case, 3 cases in Hechi City), no deaths.

Among the confirmed cases, 32 were in Nanning, 20 in Liuzhou, 29 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 32 in Beihai, 16 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 7 in Guigang, 9 in Yulin, There were 3 cases in Baise City, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 15 cases in Hechi City, and 3 cases in Laibin City.

A total of 8,877 close contacts have been tracked, and 4,072 are still under medical observation.

Today is the fifteenth day of the first lunar month. Under the circumstances of the epidemic, for the health of you, me and him, every family and individual should consciously take up the responsibility of prevention and control, and further block the spread and spread of the epidemic. Experts recommend that you keep indoor air circulation in your home, open more windows and ventilate, add and remove clothes in a timely manner, and avoid colds and colds; keep your hands hygienic, wash your hands before and after meals, sneeze, cough, and clean your nose. Do not spit; wear masks when you go out, try not to visit visitors or go to public places to avoid meal gathering activities; arrange your diet reasonably, drink plenty of water and intake enough vitamins, and eat plenty of high-quality protein, sugars and traces Elemental foods, such as lean meat, eggs, fresh vegetables, fruits, etc .; actively carry out personal fitness and physical exercise to make the body qi and blood flow, enhance physical fitness; to maintain adequate rest and sleep, do not stay up all night playing cards, playing video games Wait, otherwise it will reduce the body's immunity and become susceptible to illness.

I wish you all a peaceful, healthy and beautiful Lantern Festival!
