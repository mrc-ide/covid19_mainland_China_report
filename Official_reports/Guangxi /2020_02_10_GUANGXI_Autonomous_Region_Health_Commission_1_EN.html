Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 9, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-10 08:00:07 Click:
34296
Source: Autonomous Regional Health Commission
/ /
/
/
/ /
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/


0-24 February 9th, the district added a total of confirmed cases of novel coronavirus pneumonia in 15 cases, of which two cases Nanning, Liuzhou two cases, one case of Guilin, Beihai two cases, one case of Fangchenggang, Guigang There were 1 case in city, 2 cases in Hechi city, and 4 cases in Laibin city. 81 new suspected cases were added.

Currently, 210 confirmed cases and 18 discharged cases have been reported in the district. There are 243 suspected cases, 5 severe cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Beihai, 1 in Guigang, 2 in Hechi), and 9 critical cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, There were 2 cases in Fangchenggang City, 1 case in Yulin City, and 4 cases in Hechi City), and a total of 1 death case (1 case in Du'an County, Hechi City).

Of the confirmed cases, 37 were in Nanning, 22 in Liuzhou, 30 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 35 in Beihai, 17 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 9 in Yulin, There were 3 cases in Baise City, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 21 cases in Hechi City, and 11 cases in Laibin City.

A total of 11025 close contacts have been tracked, and 3911 people are undergoing medical observation.

Whether on the way to work or at work, the risk of spread of the epidemic is brought about by the movement or gathering of people. Experts recommend that you pay attention to the personal protection of wearing a mask in a public environment, and promote green travel such as walking and cycling to work. If you use public transportation, try to avoid touching the items on the car and keep a distance from others; encourage individuals to enter the work unit (place ), Take the initiative to cooperate with receiving body temperature measurement and inquiries; promote the use of box-type elevators with poor air circulation, avoid touching the surface of the elevator or rubbing eyes with the hand pressing the elevator key; Use remote collaboration, online training, video conferencing and other ways to work; advocate that the unit's canteen (restaurant) requires employees to pack back to the office to dine alone. If you need to eat in the canteen, try to avoid face-to-face dining, dining talk, and group dining. At the same time, daily disinfection of public transportation, elevators, conference rooms, canteens and other public places.
