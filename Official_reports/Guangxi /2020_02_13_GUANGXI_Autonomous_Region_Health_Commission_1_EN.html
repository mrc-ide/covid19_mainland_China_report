Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 12, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-13 08:00:06 Source: Autonomous Region Health Committee
/ /
/
/
/ /
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

0-24 February 12, the district was no new confirmed case of novel coronavirus pneumonia; new suspected cases, 81 cases; new cases were cured two cases (one case Nanning, Liuzhou one case); new death 1 case (1 case in Beihai City).

A total of 222 confirmed cases and 33 discharged patients have been reported. There are 251 suspected cases and 7 severe cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Beihai, 1 in Fangchenggang, 1 in Guigang, 1 in Yulin, and 2 in Hechi), and 7 in critical cases (Nanning 1 case, 1 case in Guilin City, 1 case in Fangchenggang City, 4 cases in Hechi City) , and 2 cases of deaths (1 case in Hechi City and 1 case in Beihai City).

Of the confirmed cases , 44 were in Nanning, 22 in Liuzhou, 31 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 39 in Beihai, 17 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 9 in Yulin, There were 3 cases in Baise City, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 21 cases in Hechi City, and 11 cases in Laibin City .

A total of 13,075 close contacts have been tracked, and 3,256 people are undergoing medical observation.

At present, the region is at a critical period of superimposed personnel movements during the Spring Festival return and epidemic prevention and control entering a state of stalemate. In order to effectively protect the lives and health of the people, February 11, the prevention and control of pneumonia epidemic of new-type coronavirus infection in the autonomous region The headquarters of the working leading group issued a notice to further strengthen the prevention and control of epidemics in communities and rural areas, requiring all places to sink prevention and control measures, move key points forward, adhere to people-centeredness, and strengthen "ten strict" management of urban and rural grassroots units.

  "Ten strict" in community epidemic prevention and control: Strictly implement closed management of residential quarters, strictly control outsiders and vehicles in the district, strictly control personnel from or passing through the epidemic area, strictly strengthen close contact control and fever management, and strictly public space environment Sanitation management, strict rental house management, strict implementation of basic living and protection guarantees, strict compliance with citizen protection standards, strict implementation of prevention and control propaganda measures, and strict implementation of party member responsibilities.

"Ten strict" prevention and control of rural epidemics: Strict personnel flow control, strict implementation of closed village and village management, strict implementation of epidemic prevention and control reporting system, strict control of village and public places, strict control of village and village environment, and strict implementation of migrant workers returning to the wrong peaks Travel guidance measures, strictly implement epidemic prevention and control in rural bazaars, strictly strengthen supervision of rural market operators, strictly implement prevention and control propaganda measures, and strictly implement organizational safeguard measures.

An epidemic is an order, and prevention and control is a responsibility. In order to win this new crown pneumonia epidemic prevention and control fight, you, me, and him must be involved.
