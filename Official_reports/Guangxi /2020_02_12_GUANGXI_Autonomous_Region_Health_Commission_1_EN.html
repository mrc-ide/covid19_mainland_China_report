Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 11, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-12 08:00:02 Click:
54635
Source: Autonomous Regional Health Commission
/ /
/
/
/ /
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

February 0-24 on the 11th, the district added a total of confirmed cases of novel coronavirus pneumonia in 7 cases, 3 cases of Nanning, Guilin one case, Beihai three cases. 106 new suspected cases were added.

A total of 222 confirmed cases and 31 discharged cases have been reported in the district. There are 248 suspected cases, 6 severe cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Beihai, 1 in Fangchenggang, 1 in Guigang, 2 in Hechi), and 8 critical cases (1 in Nanning, One case was in Guilin, one in Fangchenggang, one in Yulin, and four in Hechi) , and one cumulative death was reported (one in Duan County, Hechi).

Of the confirmed cases , 44 were in Nanning, 22 in Liuzhou, 31 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 39 in Beihai, 17 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 9 in Yulin, There were 3 cases in Baise City, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 21 cases in Hechi City, and 11 cases in Laibin City .

A total of 12,519 close contacts have been tracked, and 3803 people are undergoing medical observation.

The family is one of the important elements in the comprehensive prevention and control of the spread and spread of the new crown pneumonia in the community. Experts suggest that in daily work, life, and study, family members should avoid going to areas where the disease is endemic, not visiting or less visiting, and reducing activities to crowded public places. Do not touch, buy and eat wild animals (ie game), and avoid visiting markets that sell live animals (poultry, seafood, wildlife, etc.). Keep your room clean, always open windows, and ventilate. Return from public places, cover your hands with coughs, before and after meals, and wash your hands with soap or running soap. If you go out for medical treatment, take elevators, public transportation, farmer's markets and other public places, you should wear medical surgical masks. Family members do not share towels, wash clothes frequently, pay attention to nutrition, exercise regularly, and do not spit. Do a good job of personal and family member health monitoring. If suspicious symptoms of new coronary pneumonia such as fever, cough, sore throat, chest tightness, and fatigue appear, you should avoid taking public transportation and seek medical treatment or treatment in a timely manner.  

Common disinfection methods at home: 1. Physical disinfection. Such as boiling or using high temperature sterilization box (cabinet), mostly used for disinfection of tableware and tea set. 2. Chemical disinfection. Such as chlorine disinfectant, clothing disinfectant, etc., mostly used on the surface of objects (such as countertops, door handles, telephones, switches, wash basins, toilets, etc.), floor and general fabric disinfection (note: chlorine disinfectant has Bleaching effect).
