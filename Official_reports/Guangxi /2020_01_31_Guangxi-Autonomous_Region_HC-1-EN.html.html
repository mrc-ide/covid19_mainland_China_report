Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Guangxi on January 30, 2020

/
Date: 2020-01-31 08:01:51 Click:
21754
Source: Autonomous Regional Health Commission
/ /
/
/
/ /
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

30th January 0-24, the district added a total of confirmed cases of pneumonia novel coronavirus infection in 9 cases, including one case in Liuzhou, two cases of Fangchenggang City, Yulin City, two cases, one case of Hechi City, 1 Nanning , 2 cases in Beihai City.

At present, 87 confirmed cases and 2 discharged patients have been reported. There are 5 severe cases, 2 critical cases, no deaths and no medical staff infections.

Among the confirmed cases: 16 in Nanning, 11 in Liuzhou, 18 in Guilin, 4 in Wuzhou, 17 in Beihai, 7 in Fangchenggang, 4 in Yulin, 2 in Baise, and 6 in Hechi. There was 1 case in Qinzhou and 1 case in Hezhou. Among the discharged patients, 1 was in Wuzhou and 1 was in Fangchenggang.

A total of 1,913 close contacts have been tracked, and 1,564 are still undergoing medical observation.
