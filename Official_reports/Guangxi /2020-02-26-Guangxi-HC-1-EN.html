Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 25, 2020

/
Release date: 2020-02-26 08:00:00 Source: Health and Health Committee of the Autonomous Region
/ /
/
/
//
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

2No new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia in our district from 04:00 to 20:00 on May 25; 2 new suspected cases;Nine new cases were cured and discharged (2 in Nanning, 3 in Beihai, 1 in Yulin, 1 in Baise, and 2 in Hezhou); there were no new deaths.

At present, the district has reported a total of 252 confirmed cases, 138 discharged patients, and 2 deaths (1 in Hechi City and 1 in Beihai City). There are 112 confirmed cases, all of which are being treated in hospital, of which6 severe cases (1 in Guilin, 2 in Fangchenggang, 3 in Hechi), and 5 critical cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Beihai, 2 in Fangchenggang, and 1 in Hechi);There are 10 suspected cases.

Of the cumulative confirmed cases,55 in Nanning, 24 in Liuzhou, 32 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 44 in Beihai, 19 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 11 in Yulin, 3 in Baise, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 28 cases in Hechi City, 11 cases in Laibin City。

Close contacts were tracked today 39People, 985 people are currently undergoing medical observation.

Trading markets such as farm products, flowers, birds, insects, and fish, and live poultry (livestock) are high-risk places with densely populated populations and are likely to spread infectious diseases. With the epidemic going on, how to do a good job of blocking the spread of the new crown pneumonia epidemic? Experts suggest that we focus on the three key links of "one clear, two eliminations and three kills." "One clear" is mainly cleaning, to achieve "one day a day cleaning, one week a week cleaning, one month a month cleaning", that is, the farmers' trading market operators, after the market closes every day, the fish scales, viscera, feces, Remove feathers, scraps, other garbage and other dirt. Wash the countertops, floors, drains and surrounding floors of the store. Rinse the disinfected slaughtering tools, countertops, cutting boards, etc. after the market is closed every week. Equipment, cages, feeding utensils, fish ponds (boxes), transportation tools, sewers, the ground around the store, excreta, etc. are thoroughly cleaned; after the market is closed every month, the cages (fences) and stall floors where live birds (livestock) are stored , Wall, store environment, etc. for thorough cleaning. "Second elimination" is to use 10% chlorine-containing disinfectant to spray and disinfect daily, weekly and monthly cleaned tools, cages, countertops, floors, and sewers. "Three kills" refers to the combination of physical and chemical methods to kill vector organisms such as rats, mosquitoes, flies and cockroaches, and control their density within the nationally prescribed standards. At the same time, strengthen scientific management, Nissan clean up garbage, and purify and beautify the environment.
