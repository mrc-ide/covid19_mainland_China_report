Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 19, 2020

/
Release date: 2020-02-20 08:02:29 Source: Health and Health Committee of the Autonomous Region
/ /
/
/
//
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

2At 00:00 on the 19th of May, one new case of new coronavirus pneumonia was confirmed in our district (one in Fangchenggang City); 17 new suspected cases;11 new cases were cured and discharged (5 in Nanning, 2 in Liuzhou, and 4 in Hechi); no new deaths were reported.

At present, a total of 245 confirmed cases, 87 discharged patients, and 2 deaths (1 in Hechi City and 1 in Beihai City) have been reported in the district. There are 156 confirmed cases currently in the hospital, of which3 severe cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 1 in Hechi), 8 critical cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 1 in Beihai, 4 in Fangchenggang, and 1 in Hechi ). There are 65 suspected cases.

Of the cumulative confirmed cases,There were 54 cases in Nanning, 24 in Liuzhou, 31 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 44 in Beihai, 19 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 11 in Yulin, 3 in Baise, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 23 cases in Hechi City, 11 cases in Laibin City。

147 close contacts were tracked today, and 1381 are currently undergoing medical observation.

How can parents help children protect against new coronavirus infections during the epidemic? Experts recommend that you keep in mind the protection strategy of "one prevention, two washing, three connections, four cleansing and five rests". The first is household daily protection. For babies or newborns who are not suitable for wearing masks, parents should take the initiative to wear masks, do not kiss their children, do not exhale or pant, do not share tableware and drinking utensils with their children, and do not use mouth blowing to cool food before feeding; Parents should change their clothes and shoes immediately after going out, thoroughly wash and clean before touching the children; if the children must go out, they should wear masks. The second is to strictly regulate hand washing. Urge children to wash their hands and face frequently, do not touch or rub their eyes with hands; wash their hands before eating, after urinating, and after touching unclean objects. The third is to strengthen room ventilation. Ventilate each room alternately 2 to 3 times a day, about 30 minutes each time. The fourth is household cleaning and disinfection. Mobile phones used by parents are cleaned and disinfected daily, not for children to play and watch; children's toys and learning and daily necessities can be boiled and sterilized with boiling water for 30 minutes, and those that are not resistant to heat can be exposed to the sun; keep the ground clean and dry. Mop the floor with chlorine disinfectant 1 or 2 times a day. Fifth, appropriate activities and rest. Children should not watch TV or play electronic products for a long time. While studying or completing school assignments, properly arrange exercise or participate in household cleaning labor, and ensure that sleep is regular and adequate.
