Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 24, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-25 08:00:00 Source: Health and Health Committee of the Autonomous Region
/ /
/
/
//
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

2At 04:00 on the 24th of January, one new case of new coronavirus pneumonia was confirmed in our district (1 case in Hechi City); 3 new suspected cases;Twenty-two cases were cured and discharged (5 in Nanning, 6 in Guilin, 7 in Beihai, 2 in Fangchenggang, 1 in Guigang, and 1 in Hechi); no new deaths were reported.

At present, the district has reported a total of 252 confirmed cases, 129 discharged patients, and 2 deaths (1 in Hechi City and 1 in Beihai City). There are 121 confirmed cases, all of which are being treated in hospital, of which6 severe cases (1 in Guilin, 2 in Fangchenggang, 3 in Hechi), and 5 critical cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Beihai, 2 in Fangchenggang, and 1 in Hechi);There are 21 suspected cases.

Of the cumulative confirmed cases,55 in Nanning, 24 in Liuzhou, 32 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 44 in Beihai, 19 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 11 in Yulin, 3 in Baise, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 28 cases in Hechi City, 11 cases in Laibin City。

Close contacts tracked today40People, 1037 people are under medical observation.

With the orderly progress of resumption of work and resumption of production and movement of urban and rural residents to buy daily necessities, how should various shopping malls do a good job of blocking the transmission channels of the new crown pneumonia epidemic? Experts suggest that one should carry out preventive disinfection. Suspend the services of places such as mother and infant rooms, children's play, indoor entertainment, etc., and regularly disinfect chlorine-containing disinfectants on public equipment or surfaces (such as checkout counters, counters, elevator buttons, door handles, rest areas, etc.) and the ground, Toilets are equipped with hand sanitizer or disposable sanitizer. Second, we must keep the air flowing and clean. Ensure the normal operation of the exhaust equipment, open the window for ventilation, and try not to use the central air conditioner (if used, the air conditioner filter should be thoroughly cleaned and disinfected), maintain environmental sanitation, and clean up the garbage in a timely manner. Third, we must widely publicize the knowledge of prevention and control. Through video scrolling or posting notices at prominent locations such as supermarket entrances, stairways, elevators, etc., remind staff and customers to wear masks and wash their hands after returning home. Fourth, we must reduce the risk of close contact. When shopping in line for payment, customers should be 1 meter away, and cashiers should pay by scanning code to speed up the settlement. Fifth, we must strengthen individual health management. At the entrance of the mall, use a fast infrared body temperature detector to detect the temperature of employees and customers. If the body temperature is greater than or equal to 37.30, they should report or assist the hot spot clinic at the nearest designated hospital.
