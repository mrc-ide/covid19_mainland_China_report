Epidemic situation of new coronavirus pneumonia in Guangxi on February 22, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-23 08:00:03 Source: Health and Health Committee of the Autonomous Region
/ /
/
/
//
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/

2No new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia in our district from 0-24 on April 22; 12 new suspected cases;Five new cases were cured and discharged (3 in Nanning and 2 in Yulin); there were no new deaths.

At present, the district has reported a total of 249 confirmed cases, 104 discharged cases, and 2 deaths (1 in Hechi City and 1 in Beihai City). There are currently 143 confirmed cases, all of which are being treated in hospital, of which6 severe cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 1 in Fangchenggang, 3 in Hechi), 7 critical cases (1 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 1 in Beihai, and 3 in Fangchenggang 1 case in Hechi City);There are 58 suspected cases.

Of the cumulative confirmed cases,55 in Nanning, 24 in Liuzhou, 32 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 44 in Beihai, 19 in Fangchenggang, 8 in Qinzhou, 8 in Guigang, 11 in Yulin, 3 in Baise, 4 cases in Hezhou City, 25 cases in Hechi City, and 11 cases in Laibin City。

Close contacts tracked today34Of these, 1274 people are undergoing medical observation.

During the implementation of closed management, how can urban and rural residents reduce the risk of virus infection when they go out to purchase necessities? Experts recommend that young people in the family go out as far as possible to avoid people with low immunity, such as the elderly and children; they should wear masks throughout the shopping process, preferably walking, cycling or private transportation, and less public transportation; Choose well-ventilated, relatively low-traffic malls and supermarkets, and consciously cooperate with staff to accept temperature detection and scan codes; make a purchase list at home before shopping, do not hang out and chat in the store, and shorten the time to buy goods Keep items more than 1 meter away from people, especially when you check out in line. Do n’t go from person to person, you can choose code scanning or self-service cash register to check out to reduce unnecessary coin pollution and waiting time; you should do it thoroughly after returning home Clean and reduce the risk of virus transmission.
