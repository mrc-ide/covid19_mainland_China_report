Five confirmed coronavirus-infected pneumonia patients cured and discharged

On February 3, five confirmed cases of pneumonia infected with a new coronavirus were cured in Guangxi and released from hospital. So far, a total of 7 confirmed patients with pneumonia infected with new coronavirus have been discharged from our district .

Four of the five discharged patients were cured and discharged from Nanxishan Hospital of the autonomous region, and one was discharged from the Third People's Hospital of Wuzhou. They are carefully after a local hospital for treatment and care, anti-infection, anti-virus, enhance immunity and symptomatic treatment of a variety of methods such as TCM, her condition improved, no fever has reached more than 3 days, 2 times by nucleic acid testing were negative . After being reviewed by experts at the autonomous region level, they were discharged according to the discharge standards. The hospital urged patients to do home observation and follow-up after discharge. Details are as follows:

Hu Moumou, female, 33 years old, went from Wuhan to Guilin on January 18th, and went to the Yangshuo County People's Hospital for a fever and cough on January 24th. The test was positive for a new type of coronavirus and was transferred to January 27th. Autonomous Region Nanxishan Hospital.
