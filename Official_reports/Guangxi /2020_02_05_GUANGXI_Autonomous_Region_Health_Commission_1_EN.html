Epidemic situation of new coronavirus infection in Guangxi on February 4, 2020

/
Date: 2020-02-05 08:00:30 Click:
Source: Autonomous Regional Health Commission
/ /
/
/
/ /
/
/
/
/
/ /
/
/
/


At 04:00 on February 4th, 11 new cases of pneumonia confirmed by new coronavirus infection were newly added in our district, including 1 in Nanning, 2 in Liuzhou, 2 in Guilin, 2 in Beihai, and 2 in Fangchenggang. 1 case in Qinzhou City and 1 case in Hechi City.

At present, the district has reported a total of 150 confirmed cases and 10 discharged cases. There are 10 severe cases (2 in Nanning, 1 in Guilin, 5 in Fangchenggang, 1 in Guigang, 1 in Hechi), and 4 critical cases (2 in Guilin, 1 in Yulin, and Hechi). 1 case), no deaths.

Among the confirmed cases, there were 28 cases in Nanning, 17 in Liuzhou, 26 in Guilin, 5 in Wuzhou, 29 in Beihai, 13 in Fangchenggang, 4 in Qinzhou, 5 in Guigang, 8 in Yulin, There were 2 cases in Baise City, 4 cases in Hezhou City, and 9 cases in Hechi City.

A total of 6,469 close contacts have been tracked, and 3,972 people are undergoing medical observation.

In order to control the spread of the pneumonia epidemic of the new coronavirus infection, the autonomous region requires the returnees of government agencies and institutions in the region to take the lead: "six must be six and no," that is, return to work must actively report, health monitoring and inspection must cooperate, ventilation and disinfection must Diligence, physical discomfort must promptly seek medical treatment, personal protection must be strengthened, moderate physical exercise must be maintained; do not participate in gathering activities, do not visit visitors, do not make rumors, do not blindly panic, do not blindly buy drugs and stockpiles, do not touch and eat wild animals.
