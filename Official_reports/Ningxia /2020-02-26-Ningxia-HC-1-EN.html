Epidemic Situation of New Coronavirus Pneumonia in the Region on February 25

Publisher: Health and Health Committee of Ningxia Hui Autonomous RegionRelease time: February 26, 2020 08:53Source: Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Command for New Coronavirus PneumoniaViews:Person-times

On February 26, Ningxia's headquarters in response to the epidemic of new-type coronavirus pneumonia issued the latest bulletin on new-type coronavirus pneumonia in the region.

At 04:00 on February 25, 2020, there were no new confirmed cases of new coronavirus pneumonia in the district, and 3 new cases were discharged (1 in Yongning County, 1 in Tongxin County, and 1 in Hongsibao District).

As of 24:00 on February 25, the whole districtThere are 10 confirmed cases and 61 discharged cases.Whole areaA total of 71 confirmed cases were reported,among themYinchuan33 cases (13 in Xingqing District, 8 in Jinfeng District, 6 in Xixia District, 5 in Yongning County, and 1 in Lingwu City),Shizuishan1 case (Dawukou District),Wuzhong28 cases (4 cases in Litong District, 4 cases in Qingtongxia City, 16 cases in Tongxin County, 4 cases in Hongsibao District),Guyuan5 cases (2 in Yuanzhou District and 3 in Longde County),Zhongwei3 cases (2 in Shapotou District, 1 in Zhongning County),Ning Dong1 case (Ningdong Town). Suspected case5example。

At present, a total of 3942 medical observations have been lifted and 452 medical observations have been concentrated.
