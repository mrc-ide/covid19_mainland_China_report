Epidemic situation of pneumonia in new district coronavirus infection

Publisher: Ningxia Health and Medical Commission Release time: January 29, 2020 10:57 Source: Ningxia Health Committee Views: Person-times

At 00:00 on January 28, 2020, the district reported one new confirmed case of pneumonia and a new suspected case.

As of 24:00 on January 28, the district has reported a total of 12 confirmed cases of pneumonitis with new coronavirus infection, including 7 in Yinchuan, 3 in Wuzhong, 1 in Zhongwei, and 1 in Guyuan; 2 suspected cases, of which There was 1 case in Wuzhong City and 1 case in Zhongwei City.
