Yesterday, from 12 to 24, two new cases of imported new coronavirus pneumonia were confirmed in Shanghai
/

/ ( 2020-03-16)/

/
/
/ /

At 12:00 to 24:00 on March 15, 2020, 9 suspected cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were ruled out in Shanghai; two new confirmed cases were imported overseas cases discovered through the joint prevention and control mechanism.

Case 1 is of Italian nationality. It departed from Amsterdam, the Netherlands on March 10, and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 11 after transiting through Paris, France. After entering the country, it was collected and observed in isolation. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory test results, and imaging studies were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

Case 2 is from Shanghai, China, working and living in the United Kingdom. He set off from London, UK on March 12, and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 13, and was placed under quarantine for observation. Comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory test results, and imaging studies were diagnosed as confirmed cases.

As of 24:00 on March 15th, 355 confirmed cases have been found in Shanghai. Of the confirmed cases, 186 were male and 169 were female; the oldest was 88 years old and the youngest was 7 months old; 145 had a history of living or traveling in Hubei, 34 had a history of living or traveling outside Hubei, and 159 had a history of contact with related cases. 4 cases had a history of residence in Iran, 9 cases had a history of residence in Italy, 1 case had a history of residence in the United States, 1 case had a history of residence in France, 1 case had a history of residence in Spain, and 1 case had a history of residence in the United Kingdom; There were 17 input personnel and 227 permanent residents in this city.
