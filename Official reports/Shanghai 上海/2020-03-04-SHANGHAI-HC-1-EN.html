Yesterday at 12-24, no new confirmed cases of coronavirus pneumonia in Shanghai
/

/ ( 2020-03-04)/

/
/
/ /

2020At 12 to 24 on March 3, 2015, five suspected cases of new coronavirus pneumonia were excluded from Shanghai; no new confirmed cases were found.

As of 24:00 on March 3, a total of 2,445 suspected cases had been ruled out in Shanghai, and 338 confirmed cases were found. Of the confirmed cases, 176 were male and 162 were female; the oldest was 88 years old and the youngest was 7 months old; 145 had a history of living or traveling in Hubei, 34 had a history of living or traveling outside Hubei, and 158 had a history of contact with related cases. An epidemiological investigation is underway; 111 cases are coming to Shanghai from abroad, and 227 are permanent residents in this city. Details are as follows:
