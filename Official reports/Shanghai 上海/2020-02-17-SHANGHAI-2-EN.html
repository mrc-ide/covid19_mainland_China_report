New confirmed case of new coronavirus pneumonia in Shanghai

/

( 2020-02-17)

/
/
/

/

2020At 02:00 on February 17, 2000, 23 suspected cases of new-type coronavirus pneumonia were ruled out in Shanghai; 21 cases were cured and discharged; 1 confirmed case was added, and the city was a resident population. Details are as follows:

/

 

/

/


/

 

/

As of 12:00 on February 17, Shanghai had ruled out 1772 suspected cases and found 332 confirmed cases. Among the confirmed cases, there were 173 males and 159 females; the oldest was 88 years old and the youngest was 7 months old; 144 had a history of living or traveling in Hubei, 33 had a history of living or traveling outside Hubei, and 155 had a history of contact with related cases; There were 109 foreigners coming to Shanghai and 223 of the city's resident population. Details are as follows:

/

 

/

/


/

 

/

At present, 152 patients are in stable condition, 14 patients are critically ill, 4 patients are seriously ill, 161 patients are cured and discharged, and 1 patient has died. There are still 126 suspected cases under investigation.
