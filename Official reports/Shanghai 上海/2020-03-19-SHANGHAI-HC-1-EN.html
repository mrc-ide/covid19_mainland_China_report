Yesterday, there were no new confirmed cases of local neo-coronary pneumonia in Shanghai, 2 cases were imported overseas, and 1 case was cured and discharged.
/

/ ( 2020-03-19)/

/
/
/ /

3At 04:00 on the 18th of the month, two confirmed cases of imported neocoronary pneumonia were reported through the port joint prevention and control mechanism. As of 24:00 on March 18, a total of 25 imported imported confirmed cases were reported, and there are currently 17 suspected imported imported cases under investigation.

Case 1 was from Inner Mongolian of China, visiting relatives in the United Kingdom. He set off from London, England on March 14 and arrived at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 15 after transiting through Hong Kong. He was quarantined and observed after entering customs, and symptoms occurred during the period. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

Case 2 is from Zhejiang Province, China, studying in the UK, departing from London, UK on March 15 and arriving at Shanghai Pudong International Airport on March 16 after transiting from Changi, Singapore. Due to symptoms, he was sent to designated medical care upon entry. Watch the organization. Based on comprehensive epidemiological history, clinical symptoms, laboratory tests, and imaging findings, the diagnosis is confirmed.

2All imported imported confirmed cases have been transferred to designated medical institutions for treatment. A total of 27 close contacts with the flight have been tracked. Centralized isolation observation has been implemented.

3At 04:00 on the 18th, there were no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia. As of 24:00 on March 18, a total of 338 locally confirmed cases had been reported, and one suspected local case was currently under investigation.

3At 04:00 on the 18th of January, one new case was discharged from the hospital and was imported from Iran. As of 24:00 on March 18, a total of 326 patients were discharged from hospital and 3 died. There are 34 cases in the hospital (including 24 cases imported abroad), of which 24 cases are stable, 2 cases are severe, and 8 cases are critical.
