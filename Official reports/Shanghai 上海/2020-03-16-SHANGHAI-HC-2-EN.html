/

/
/

3At 02:00 on the 16th, there were no new confirmed cases of imported neocoronary pneumonia. As of 12:00 on March 16, a total of 17 imported imported confirmed cases have been reported, and 51 existing suspected imported imported cases are under investigation.

3At 02:00 on the 16th, there were no new confirmed cases of local new coronary pneumonia. As of 12:00 on March 16, a total of 338 locally confirmed cases have been reported, and 12 suspected local cases are currently under investigation.

3At 02:00 on the 16th of January, one new patient was discharged from the hospital. As of 12:00 on March 16, a total of 325 patients had been discharged and 3 died. There are 27 cases in the hospital (17 cases imported abroad), of which 18 cases are stable, 1 case is severe, and 8 cases are critical.
